Insurance Act
Motor Vehicle Insurance Policy Limits Regulation
Prescribed limits under section 151 of the Act
1
In respect of motor vehicle liability insurance policies issued or
renewed on and after January 1, 1985, the prescribed amounts under
section 151 of the Act are
(a) at least $200 000 for each of subsections (1) and (2),
(b) $180 000 for subsection (3) (a),
(c) $20 000 for subsection (3) (b),
(d) at least $200 000 for the first reference to a prescribed
amount in subsection (4), and
(e) at least $20 000 for the second reference to a prescribed
amount in subsection (4).
[Provisions of the
Insurance Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 226, relevant to the enactment of this
regulation: sections 151 and 192]
