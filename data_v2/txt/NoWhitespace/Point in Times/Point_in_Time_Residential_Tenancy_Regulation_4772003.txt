8

[starting dispute resolution]

(a)
for an application for a rent increase above the regulated limit,
$200 plus $5 for each rental unit, to a maximum of $500;
(b)
for a monetary application if the amount sought is in excess of $5
000, $100;
(c)
for any other application, $50.

34

regulation fails to provide submissions or to appear when required
by the notice or under section 33 (4), as applicable, the director
may proceed without further notice to make an order under section
94.1 of the Act in respect of the person.
