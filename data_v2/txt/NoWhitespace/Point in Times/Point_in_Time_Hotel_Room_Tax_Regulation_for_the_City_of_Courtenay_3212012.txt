Hotel Room Tax Act; Provincial Sales Tax Act
Hotel Room Tax Regulation for the City of Courtenay
Definition
1
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Hotel Room Tax Act
.
Applicability of tax
2
March 1, 2013 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of the
Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the City of
Courtenay.
Prescribed rate of tax payable
3
The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the Act is 2% of the
purchase price of accommodation purchased in the City of
Courtenay.
Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax
4
The City of Courtenay may spend the amount paid to it out of the
revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing, programs and
projects.
Repeal
5
This regulation is repealed effective March 1, 2018.
