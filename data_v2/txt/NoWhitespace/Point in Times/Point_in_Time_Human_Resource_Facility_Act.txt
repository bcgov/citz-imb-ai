Section 2.1 (1) BEFORE repealed by 2004-60-108(a), effective July 1,
2005 (BC Reg 231/2005).
(1)  In this section:
"assistance"
includes a grant, loan, guarantee or indemnity and includes
assistance under section 1 or 2;
"human resource facility agreement"
means an agreement made by the minister or any agent of the
government with a person who has received or is to receive
assistance from the government to acquire, develop or operate a
human resource facility;
"human resource facility"
means land and improvements, all or part of which provide or are
intended to provide any of the services referred to in section 1,
and includes land and improvements designated as a human resource
facility;
"human resource purposes"
means the purposes listed in section 1.
