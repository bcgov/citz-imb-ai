Section 6 BEFORE repealed by 2000-23-45, effective March 31, 2002.
Annual report
6
(1)  The minister must make and submit to the Lieutenant
Governor in Council an annual report of the work performed by the
ministry.
(2)  The report must be laid before the Legislative Assembly
during the first session in the calendar year next following the
end of the fiscal year for which it was made.
