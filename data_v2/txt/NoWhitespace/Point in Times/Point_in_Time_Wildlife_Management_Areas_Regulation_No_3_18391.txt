Green Mountain Wildlife Management Area
1
The following area of land, under the administration of the
Minister of Environment, is designated as Green Mountain Wildlife
Management Area and then as a Critical Wildlife Management Area:
That part of Block 1392, Dunsmuir District, lying within the
following described boundaries:
Commencing at a point on the westerly boundary of Block 1392, said
point shown as O.I.P. 5 on plan of Block 1392 on file in the
Surveys and Lands Branch, Ministry of Lands and Parks, Victoria;
thence 75° 00'00" a distance of 1 100 metres more or less to
Triangulation Station "Green", being the summit of Green Mountain;
thence 160° 00'00" a distance of 1 000 metres more or less to a
point on the southerly boundary of Block 1392, said point being
900.0 metres more or less west of the most easterly southeast
corner; thence east to said corner; thence following the easterly,
northerly and westerly boundaries of Block 1392 in a northerly,
westerly, northerly, westerly, northerly, westerly, northerly,
westerly, southerly, westerly, southerly, easterly, southerly and
easterly direction to the aforementioned point of commencement.
[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]
