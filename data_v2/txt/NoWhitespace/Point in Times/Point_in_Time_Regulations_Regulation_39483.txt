Section 7 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 189/2017, effective November
1, 2017.
Reference to consolidated regulations of British Columbia
7
A reference in an enacting regulation to a previously enacted
regulation that at the time of the enactment of the enacting
regulation is published in the looseleaf Consolidated Regulations
of British Columbia shall, unless a contrary intention appears in
the enacting regulation, be construed to be a reference to the
previously enacted regulation as published in that consolidation.
[en. B.C. Reg. 45/85, s. 3.]
Section 8 definition of "enacting authority", paragraphs (a) and (b)
BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.
(a)

regulation to be revised, or a person designated by him, or
(b)
in the case of a regulation that can be enacted by the Lieutenant
Governor in Council, the minister responsible for administration of
the enactment that is authority for enacting the regulation to be

Submission to registrar
11
If the enacting authority approves the revised regulation he shall
endorse it as approved and deposit it with the registrar, and the
Act, except sections 2 and 3 (2), applies to the revised
regulation the same as if the regulation had been originally
enacted.
