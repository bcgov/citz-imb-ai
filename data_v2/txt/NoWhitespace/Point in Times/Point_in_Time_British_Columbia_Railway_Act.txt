(BC Reg 493/2003).
Lease or sale of lines
20
With the prior approval of the Lieutenant Governor in Council, the
company may lease or sell the lines and property of the company to
any other company.

Government ownership of share capital
29
(1)  The government may continue to hold all the issued
shares in the share capital of the company, however acquired.
(2)  The shares may be registered in the name of the minister
who administers this Act.
(3)  The Lieutenant Governor in Council may exercise all the
powers and perform all the functions of shareholders in the
company.
