Hotel Room Tax Act; Provincial Sales Tax Act
Hotel Room Tax Regulation for the District of East Kootenay
(Radium Area)
Definition
1
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Hotel Room Tax Act
.
Applicability of tax
2
July 31, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of the
Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the Village
of Radium Hot Springs and in the portions of the East Kootenay
Regional District Electoral Area G shown in Schedules A and B,
attached to the Regional District of East Kootenay – Additional
Hotel Room Tax Bylaw No. 1973, 2007 (Radium Area).
Prescribed rate of tax payable
3
The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the Act is 2% of the
purchase price of accommodation purchased in the Village of Radium
Hot Springs and in the portions of the East Kootenay Regional
District Electoral Area G shown in Schedules A and B, attached to
the Regional District of East Kootenay – Additional Hotel Room Tax
Bylaw No. 1973, 2007 (Radium Area).
[am. B.C. Reg. 158/2012, s. 2.]
Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax
4
The East Kootenay Regional District may spend the amount paid to
it out of the revenue collected from the tax for tourism
marketing, programs and projects.
[am. B.C. Reg. 158/2012, s. 2.]
Repeal
5
This regulation is repealed effective August 1, 2017.
[am. B.C. Reg. 158/2012, s. 3.]
