Title and publication of revision
6
(1)  A general revision may be published with the title
Revised Statutes of British Columbia and may include in the title
the year of its publication.
(2)  A limited revision may be given a chapter number as if
it were enacted in the current session of the Legislative Assembly
or, if the Legislative Assembly is not then in session, in the
next session, and the limited revision may be published in the
volume of Acts enacted in that session.
