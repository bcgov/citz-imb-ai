Section 6 BEFORE re-enacted by 2007-9-70, effective June 21, 2007
(BC Reg 226/2007).
Powers of inquiry
6
The director, or other person authorized in writing by the
director for the purpose, may make inquiries with regard to the
assets and liabilities of a debtor who applies for assistance
under this Act or in respect of whom a referral has been made by a
court, and for that purpose the director
(a) may examine under oath
(i)  the debtor, the debtor's employees and agents, the
debtor's creditors, and
(ii)  any person who the director considers has any knowledge
of the affairs of the debtor, and
(b) has all the power and authority of a commissioner under
sections 12, 15 and 16 of the
Inquiry Act
.
