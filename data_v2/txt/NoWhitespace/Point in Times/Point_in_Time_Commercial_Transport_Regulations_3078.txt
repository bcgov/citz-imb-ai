Section 1.01 definition of "farm vehicle" BEFORE amended by BC Reg
76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.
"farm vehicle"
means a commercial vehicle owned and operated by a farmer, rancher
or market gardener, the use of which is confined to purposes
connected with his farm, ranch or market garden, including use for
pleasure and is not used in connection with any other business in
which the owner may be engaged;

regulation after the expiration of the term of the permit.
