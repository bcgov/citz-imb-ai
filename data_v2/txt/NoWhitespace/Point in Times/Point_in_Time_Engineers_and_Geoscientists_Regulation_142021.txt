Section 2 definition of "professional engineering licensee" BEFORE
amended by BC Reg 32/2023, effective February 10, 2023.
"professional engineering licensee"
means a registrant who is authorized by the regulatory body to use
the title "professional engineering licensee".

Section 5 (1) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 32/2023, effective February
10, 2023.
(1)
For the purposes of section 51 (1) (b)
[reserved practice]
of the Act, the practice of professional engineering, as it relates
to matters that, having regard to the protective purposes, require
the experience or technical knowledge of a professional engineer or
professional engineering licensee, is a reserved practice that may
only be carried out by or under the supervision of a registrant.
