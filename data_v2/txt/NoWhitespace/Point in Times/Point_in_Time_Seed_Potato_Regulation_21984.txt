Procedure at meeting
6

for the meeting, who shall decide all questions of order and shall
call for nominations of candidates for membership on the
committee.

Voting
9
Where more candidates are nominated than are required to fill the
vacancies on the committee, the chairman shall, with the consent
of the majority of owners present, declare when nominations are
closed and shall then proceed to take the votes by ballot of the
owners.
Section 10 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30,

Tied vote
10
In the event of a tied vote the chairman shall call for another
vote on the candidates who are tied.

Section 20 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 112/2014, effective June 18,
2014.
Vacancy
20
(1)
In the event of any casual vacancy in the membership of the
committee the remaining members of the committee shall constitute
the committee until the vacancy is filled.
(2)
Where a member of the committee is absent from the Province for 6
months or fails to attend to his duties for a like period, the
other members of the committee may declare his office vacant.
(3)
A member of the committee ceases to hold office where
(a)
he is convicted of an offence under the Act or regulations,
(b)
by notice in writing to the committee he resigns his office, or
(c)
his office is declared vacant by the other members of the
committee.
(4)
Where a vacancy occurs in the membership of the committee by
reason of death or through a member ceasing to hold office, the
remaining members of the committee, if constituted a quorum, may,
subject to the approval of the minister, fill the vacancy, and the
person appointed to fill the vacancy shall hold office for the
unexpired term of the member whose office he fills.

Section 26 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30,

Banking
26
The committee shall deposit all money received by it in a bank,
credit union or trust company and shall not disburse any money
other than by means of cheques signed by the chairman and
secretary treasurer.
