Insurance Corporation Act
Permitted Cost of Services Regulation
Emission inspection centres
1
For the purpose of section 17 (1) of the
Insurance Corporation Act
, the permitted cost of services provided by the Insurance
Corporation of British Columbia to operate emissions inspection
centres anywhere in British Columbia under Division 40 of
B.C. Reg. 26/58, the Motor Vehicle Act Regulations, is the
entire amount of the fee it collects under Item 13 of the
Schedule to B.C. Reg. 334/91, the Motor Vehicle Fees
Regulation, for each vehicle emission inspection or vehicle
emission reinspection.
Note:
this regulation repeals B.C. Reg. 311/92.
