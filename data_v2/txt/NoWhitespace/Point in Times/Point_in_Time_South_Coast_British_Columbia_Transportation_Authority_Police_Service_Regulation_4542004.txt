Powers, duties and functions of the board of the SCBCTAPS
2
The powers, duties and functions of the board of the SCBCTAPS are
those set out in section 4.2 (2) (c) of the
Police Act

Section 3 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 231/2022, effective November 25,

Geographical area
3
The geographical area within which the SCBCTAPS and its designated
constables must operate is the transportation service region.
[am. B.C. Reg. 257/2008, s. 2 (c).]
