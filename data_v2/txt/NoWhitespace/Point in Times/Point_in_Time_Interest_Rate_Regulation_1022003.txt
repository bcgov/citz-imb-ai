Land Tax Deferment Act
Interest Rate Regulation
Interest rate
1
For the purposes of sections 8 and 9 of the
Land Tax Deferment Act
, the interest rate for the six-month period beginning April 1,
2003, shall be 2.50 percent per annum.

this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 235/2002.
