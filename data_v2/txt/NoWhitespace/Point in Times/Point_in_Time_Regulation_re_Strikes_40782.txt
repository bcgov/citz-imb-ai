Livestock Identification Act
Regulation re Strikes
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 112/2014, June 18, 2014]
Application of regulation during strikes
1
During the period and for each period that inspectors are on
strike, sections 20 (1), (2) and (4), 22, 23 (1), 24 (1), 28 (2)
and (3) and 28.1 (1) and (2) of the Livestock Identification
Regulation, B.C. Reg. 69/81, do not apply.
[en. B.C. Reg. 112/2014, App. s. 9.]

Application of regulation during strikes
1
During the period and for each period that Inspectors are on
strike, sections 20 (1) and (2), 22 (1) and (2), 23 (1), 24 (1)
and 28 (2) and (3) of B.C. Reg. 69/81, the Livestock Brand
