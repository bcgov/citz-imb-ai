Section 1 definition "plant pathologist" BEFORE amended by BC Reg
4/2010, effective January 14, 2010.
"plant pathologist"
means an inspector who is a plant pathologist employed by the
Ministry of Agriculture and Food of the Province or the Agriculture
and Agri-Food Canada.
Section 3 (1) and (2) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective
March 30, 2022.
(1)
Where, on inspection, an inspector discovers or suspects the
presence of little cherry disease in a plant, he shall notify a
plant pathologist.
(2)
Where, after examination of a plant, a plant pathologist determines
or suspects that the plant has little cherry disease, he may request
the grower to destroy the plant.
