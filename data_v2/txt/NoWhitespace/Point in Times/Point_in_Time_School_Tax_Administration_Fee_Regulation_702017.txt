Administration fee
1
The administration fee allowed to each municipality for the
purposes of section 124 (10) of the
School Act
is $2 000 plus 0.1% of the current year school tax amount
contained in the notice issued to the municipal collector under
section 119 (6) (b) of the
School Act
.
