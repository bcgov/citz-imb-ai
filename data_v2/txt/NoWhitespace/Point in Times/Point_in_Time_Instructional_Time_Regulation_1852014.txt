School Act
Instructional Time Regulation
Definition
1
In this regulation,
"board"
includes a francophone education authority.
Deemed provision of hours of instruction
2
A board that has scheduled for a school in its district a number
of hours of instruction for a day between September 2, 2014 and
September 30, 2014 is deemed to have provided the scheduled number
of hours of instruction for that day and to have complied with
section 87.03 of the
School Act
if the school is closed as a result of a strike or lockout in
respect of some or all of the employees of the board assigned to
the school.
