Industry Training Authority.

Other prescribed requirements — basic tax credit for individuals
5
For the purposes of section 118(1)(d) of the Act, the prescribed
requirement is that, while the individual was registered in an
eligible recognized program, the individual was not
(a) enrolled under section 3 of the
School Act
,
(b) registered under section 13 of the
School Act
, or
(c) attending an independent school, as defined in the
Independent School Act
, or an educational institution operated by the government of
Canada or by a band, as defined in the
Indian Act
(Canada).
