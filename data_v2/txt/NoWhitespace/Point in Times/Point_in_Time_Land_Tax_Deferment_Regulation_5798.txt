Fire insurance required
5
For the purpose of section 5 (1) (e) and (2.1) (e) of the Act, the
eligible property must be supported by a valid fire insurance
policy issued by an insurer authorized under the
Financial Institutions Act
, of an amount not less than the current assessed value determined
by the British Columbia Assessment Authority for all residential
class improvements to that property.
[en. B.C. Reg. 328/2004; am. B.C. Reg. 79/2009, s. 1.]

Minimum equity required
6
For the purpose of section 5 (5) (a) of the Act,
(a) in respect of a deferral agreement referred to in section
5 (1) of the Act, the percentage is 75% of the actual value of the
eligible property determined by the assessor,
(b) in respect of a deferral agreement referred to in section
5 (2.1) of the Act, the percentage is 85% of the actual value of
the eligible property determined by the assessor, and
(c) in respect of a deferral agreement referred to in section
5 (2.3) of the Act, the percentage is 85% of the actual value of
the eligible property determined by the assessor.
[en. B.C. Reg. 79/2009, s. 2; am. B.C. Reg. 121/2010, s. 2.]

Form 2
(section 3 (section 6 (2) of the Act))
Description of property:
..............................................................................................................................
I/We, as the owner/owners of the property identified in the
application (the "Property"), agree with Her Majesty the Queen in
right of British Columbia (the "Province"), as represented by the
minister responsible for the administration of the
Land Tax Deferment Act
, to defer property taxes on the Property as follows:
• I/We authorize the Province:
• to obtain the Parcel Identifier (PID) Number(s), registered owner
information and legal description of the Property, or the details of
the manufactured home that correspond with the Property and to
endorse that information on this agreement with the intent that the
endorsement will form part of this agreement, and in the case of the
manufactured home, endorse the information on the financing
statement,
• to obtain the value of any charges registered against the title on
the Property from the registered chargeholders in order to verify
the minimum equity requirements for tax deferment,
• to verify the information provided by accessing relevant records
held by the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia, Home Owner
Grant Administration, Land Title and Survey Authority, Personal
Property Registry, Manufactured Home Registry, BC Assessment and
other sources as required, and
• to register this agreement as a lien and charge against my/our
interest in the Property in favour of the Province, noted on every
Certificate of Title pertaining to the Property, or in the case of a
manufactured home, to register a financing statement at the Personal
Property Registry as a lien against my/our interest in the
manufactured home in favour of the Province.
• I/We hereby agree to the following Terms and Conditions:
• I/We will repay all deferred taxes, interest, administration fees
and charges accrued under this agreement that are outstanding at the
date of termination of this agreement.
• I/We will notify the Province in the event of the death of an
owner or when the Property is sold, transferred or otherwise
disposed of.
• I/We will not assign any interest in this agreement or any right
or benefits under this agreement.
• The Province will pay the property taxes to the collector of the
jurisdiction in which the Property is located.

Description of property:
..............................................................................................................................
We, the undersigned owner/owners (the "Owner") of the property
identified in the application (the "Property") and the undersigned
spouse (the "Spouse") of the/an Owner, acknowledge that:
• the Owner made an agreement (the "Deferment Agreement") under the
Land Tax Deferment Act
(the "Act"), with Her Majesty the Queen in right of British Columbia
(the "Province"), as represented by the minister responsible for the
administration of the Act, permitting the Owner to defer property
taxes on the Property;
• the Deferment Agreement has been deposited at the Tax Deferment
Office and bears Agreement Number ....................., as assigned
to it by the Province;
• the Spouse is married to the Owner, .........................
[name of applicable owner]
, or is living with that Owner in a marriage-like relationship, and
has been living in that relationship for a continuous period of at
least 2 years;
• the Spouse has read both the entire Deferment Agreement and the
most recent statement of account setting out the amount outstanding
under it;
• we have requested the minister responsible for the administration
of the Act to enter into this Amending Agreement and wish to amend
the Deferment Agreement to add the Spouse as a party to it.
Therefore, we agree with the Province as follows:
• The Deferment Agreement is amended to add the Spouse as a
signatory to it, by adding the name of the Spouse in one of the
spaces provided for an owner's name.
• The Spouse agrees to be bound by and to perform, satisfy and
discharge all of the obligations, liabilities and duties under the
Deferment Agreement, as hereby amended, as if he/she were an
original signatory to it. Without limiting the generality of the
foregoing, the Spouse, together with the Owner, shall be liable for
all taxes, interest, administration fees and charges that, at any
time prior to the time at which this Amending Agreement takes
effect, have been or may be deferred under the Deferment Agreement.
• This Amending Agreement shall be read together with the Deferment
Agreement, and we confirm that, except as modified by this Amending
Agreement, all covenants, terms and conditions in the Deferment
Agreement shall remain unchanged, unmodified and in full force and
effect.
• This Amending Agreement will take effect when a transfer, to the
Spouse, of a portion of the interest of the Owner,
........................................
[name of applicable owner]
, is registered in the land title office or given effect by the
filing of a notice of transfer under the
Manufactured Home Act
.
We authorize the Province to:
• obtain the Parcel Identifier (PID) Number(s), registered owner
information and legal description of the Property, or the details of
the manufactured home that correspond with the Property and to
endorse that information on this Amending Agreement with the intent
that the endorsement will form part of this Amending Agreement, and,
in the case of the manufactured home, endorse the information on the
financing change statement;
• register this Amending Agreement as a modification to the
registered charge created when the Deferment Agreement was
registered in the land title office, or, in the case of a
manufactured home, to register a financing change statement amending
the financing statement registered at the Personal Property Registry
when the Deferment Agreement was originally made.
