Local Government Act
Counter Petition Exemption Regulation
Section 334.1 (2) of the
Local Government Act
1
Section 334.1 (2)
[counter petition opportunity required for liabilities under
long term agreements]
of the
Local Government Act
does not apply to an agreement under which the municipality
acquires an interest or right in or with respect to land if the
liability in relation to which that provision would otherwise
apply is only one or both of
(a) an obligation that the municipality maintain the land, or
(b) an obligation that the municipality indemnify the grantor
of the interest or right being acquired by the municipality.
Section 828 (3) of the
Local Government Act
2
Section 828 (3)
[counter petition opportunity required for liabilities under
long term agreements]
of the
Local Government Act
does not apply to an agreement under which the regional district
acquires an interest or right in or with respect to land if the
liability in relation to which that provision would otherwise
apply is only one or both of
(a) an obligation that the regional district maintain the
land, or
(b) an obligation that the regional district indemnify the
grantor of the interest or right being acquired by the regional
district.
[Provisions of the
Local Government Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 323, relevant to the enactment of this
regulation: sections 251 and 799]
