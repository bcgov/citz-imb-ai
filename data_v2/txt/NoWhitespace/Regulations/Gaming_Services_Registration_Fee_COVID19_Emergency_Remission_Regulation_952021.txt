Financial Administration Act
Gaming Services Registration Fee (COVID-19
Emergency) Remission Regulation
Definitions
1
In this regulation:
"anniversary date"
,
(a)
in relation to a person described in section 2 (1) (a),
means an anniversary date of the person's registration in any of the
classes of registration referred to in section 31 (2) (a)
to (e) of the Regulation, and
(b)
in relation to a person described in section 2 (1) (b),
means an anniversary date of the person's registration in a class of
registration referred to in section 31 (4) (a)
or (b) of the Regulation;
"branch"
has the same meaning as in section 1 of the
Gaming Control Act
;
"Regulation"
means the Gaming Control Regulation, B.C. Reg. 208/2002.
Remission of fees
2
(1)
Subject to subsection (2), authorization is given for the
remission of the following fees:
(a)
a registration fee paid or payable to the branch under
section 31 (3) of the Regulation by a person who is
registered under section 65 of the
Gaming Control Act
as a gaming services provider in any of the classes of registration
referred to in section 31 (2) (a) to (e) of the Regulation;
(b)
a registration fee paid or payable to the branch under
section 31 (5) of the Regulation by a person who
(i)
is registered under section 67 of the
Gaming Control Act
as a gaming worker in a class of registration referred to in
section 31 (4) (a) or (b) of the Regulation, and
(ii)
is a director, or an officer or other employee, of a person described
in paragraph (a).
(2)
Subsection (1) applies in respect of a registration fee that, if
section 31.01 of the Regulation did not apply, would have been
required to be paid by a person described in
subsection (1) (a) or (b) on an anniversary date of the
person occurring after March 30, 2020 and before March 31,
2021.
