Local Government Act
Comox Valley Regional District Regulation
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 350/2010, December 6, 2010]
Regional growth strategy
1
(1)
The Comox Valley Regional District, except that part within the area
of the Islands Trust, is designated as an area for which a regional
growth strategy must be developed and adopted.
(2)
Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 350/2010.]
[am. B.C. Reg. 350/2010.]
Declared nuisances
2
The Comox Valley Regional District is granted the additional power to
exercise the powers conferred on the council of a municipality under
Division 12 of Part 3 of the
Community Charter
, except the powers conferred under sections 73 and 75, subject to the
terms and conditions set out in that Division.
[en. B.C. Reg. 215/2009, s. (b).]
