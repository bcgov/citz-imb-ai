School Act
School Tax Exemptions
(Wind Power Projects) Regulation
Definitions
1
In this regulation:
"Act"
means the
School Act
;
"wind power project"
means a power project that uses a wind turbine to generate or supply
electricity from the wind.
Approved class of alternative energy power project
2
Wind power projects are approved alternative energy power projects for
purposes of section 131.1 of the Act and are exempt from school taxes
to the extent set out in section 3 of the regulation.
What property is tax exempt?
3
The following improvements in respect of a wind power project are
exempt from school taxes:
(a)
any tower used to support a wind turbine;
(b)
the foundation for a tower referred to in paragraph (a).
