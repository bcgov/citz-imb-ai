Property Transfer Tax Act
Property Transfer Tax
Exemption Regulation No. 11
Exemption
1
The South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority is exempt
from payment of tax under the
Property Transfer Tax Act
in connection with its acquisition, under the
South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act
, of land for the construction of the Fraser River Crossing project as
defined in the
South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act
.
Retroactive effect
2
This regulation is retroactive to December 1, 2004, with effect on and
after that date.

Property Transfer Tax Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 378, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
