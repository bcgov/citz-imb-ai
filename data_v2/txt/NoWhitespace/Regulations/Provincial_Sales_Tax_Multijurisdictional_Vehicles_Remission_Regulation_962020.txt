Financial Administration Act
Provincial Sales Tax
(Multijurisdictional Vehicles)
Remission Regulation
Definitions
1
In this regulation:
"Act"
means the
Provincial Sales Tax Act
;
"purchase price"
has the same meaning as in section 1 of the Act;
"vehicle"
has the same meaning as in section 68 of the Act.
Remission of tax under section 72 or 72.1 of Act
2
Authorization is given for the remission of an amount of tax paid or
payable by a person under section 72 or 72.1 of the Act in respect of
the purchase price of a vehicle if
(a)
the vehicle was purchased before the date this section comes into
force, and
(b)
the circumstances giving rise to the tax liability under
section 72 or 72.1 of the Act arise after
March 10, 2020 but before October 1, 2020.
