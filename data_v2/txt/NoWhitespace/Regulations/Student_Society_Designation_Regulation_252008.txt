University Act
Student Society Designation Regulation
Simon Fraser University Graduate Student Society
1
The Simon Fraser University Graduate Student Society is a designated
student society under the
University Act
for the graduate student body of Simon Fraser University.
[Provisions of the
University Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 468, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
sections 27.1 (1) (b) and 71]
