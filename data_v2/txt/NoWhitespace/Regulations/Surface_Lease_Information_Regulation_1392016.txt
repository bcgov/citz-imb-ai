Petroleum and Natural Gas Act
Surface Lease Information Regulation
Definition
1
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Petroleum and Natural Gas Act
.
Prescribed information
2
The following information is prescribed for the purposes of section
178 (4) of the Act:
(a)
the legal description and size of the land subject to a surface lease;
(b)
a description of the nature of the interest conferred by a surface
lease;
(c)
the terms and conditions of a surface lease;
(d)
the date a surface lease is entered into and, if applicable, amended.
[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
Petroleum and Natural Gas Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 361, section 181 (e)]
