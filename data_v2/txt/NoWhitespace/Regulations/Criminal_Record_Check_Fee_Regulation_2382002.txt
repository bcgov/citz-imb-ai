Criminal Records Review Act
Criminal Record Check Fee Regulation
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 214/2013, November 30, 2013]
Fee payment
1
(1)
The fee payable for a criminal record check is $28.
(2)
The acceptable methods of paying the fee for a criminal record check
are certified cheque, money order and credit card.
