Election Act
Ballot Form Regulation
[Last amended May 23, 2023 by B.C. Reg. 130/2023]
Definition
1
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Election Act
.
Ordinary ballot
2
The form of ordinary ballot set out in Schedule 1 is prescribed for
the purposes of section 86 (2) of the Act.
Write-in ballots
3
(1)
A write-in ballot included in a mail-in voting package must be in the
form set out in Schedule 2 or 3.
(2)
If the form of write-in ballot set out in Schedule 2 is used, text
identifying the electoral district of the voter may be printed on the
reverse side of the ballot.
(3)
A write-in ballot used for any other alternative voting option or for
a voting opportunity must be in the form set out in Schedule 3.
