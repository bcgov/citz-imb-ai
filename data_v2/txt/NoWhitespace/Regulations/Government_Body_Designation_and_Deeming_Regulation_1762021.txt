Financial Administration Act
Government Body Designation
and Deeming Regulation
Definition
1
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Financial Administration Act
.
Public authorities designated as government bodies
2
Each of the following public authorities is designated, under
paragraph (d) of the definition of "government body" in
section 1 of the Act, as a government body for the purposes of
the Act:
(a)
the Greater Vancouver Sewerage and Drainage District;
(b)
the Greater Vancouver Water District;
(c)
an improvement district as defined in the
Local Government Act
.
Organizations deemed to be government bodies
3
The Providence Health Care Society is an organization deemed, under
section 88 (2) (b) of the Act, to be a government body
for the purposes of Parts 8, 9 and 9.1 of the Act.
