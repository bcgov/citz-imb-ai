Heritage Conservation Act
Application Regulation
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 219/2012, July 19, 2012]
Provisions applicable to heritage property
1
For the purposes of section 23 of the
Heritage Conservation Act
, sections 2, 3 (1), 8 (1) to (4), 9 and 14 to 27 of the
Park Act
apply to a Provincial heritage property.
[am. B.C. Reg. 219/2012, App. 1, s. 1.]
McAbee Fossil Beds Heritage Site
2
(1)
For the purposes of section 23 of the
Heritage Conservation Act
, in addition to the provisions listed in section 1 of this
regulation, sections 11, 29.1 and 30 (5) of the
Park Act
apply to the McAbee Fossil Beds Heritage Site.
(2)
Despite section 1 of this regulation, section 9 of the
Park Act
does not apply to the McAbee Fossil Beds Heritage Site.
[en. B.C. Reg. 219/2012, App. 1, s. 2.]
