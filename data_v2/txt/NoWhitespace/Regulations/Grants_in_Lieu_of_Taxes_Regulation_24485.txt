Local Government Act
Grants in Lieu of Taxes Regulation
Prorating of grants in lieu of taxes
1
Grants in Lieu of taxes received by a regional district from a
municipality or from the provincial surveyor of taxes with respect to
an electoral area, specified area or defined area pursuant to section
793 (5) and 793 (6) of the Act
1
shall be prorated by the regional district to the functions in which
the municipality, electoral area, specified area or defined area
participates in proportion to the amounts requisitioned from the
municipality, electoral area, specified area or defined area.
Prorated amount included in revenue of board
2
The amount so prorated under section 1 shall be included in the
revenue of the board under section 793 (2) (c) of the Act
1
for each function.
