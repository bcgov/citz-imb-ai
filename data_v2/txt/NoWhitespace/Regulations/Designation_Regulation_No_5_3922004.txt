Evidence Act
Designation Regulation No. 5
Conservation Officer Service members
1
Members of the Conservation Officer Service described in section 106
(2) of the
Environmental Management Act
are designated commissioners for taking affidavits for British
Columbia for the purpose of exercising the powers and performing the
duties of their office.
