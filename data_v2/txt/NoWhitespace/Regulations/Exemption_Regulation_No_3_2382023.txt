Environmental Assessment Act
Exemption Regulation (No. 3)
Section 31 (6) of
Environmental Assessment Act
not applicable in specified circumstances
1
(1)
Section 31 (6) of the
Environmental Assessment Act
does not apply in relation to Environmental Assessment Certificate #
E18-01 if, under section 31 (3) of that Act, the chief executive
assessment officer is conducting a review of an application made under
section 31 (2) of that Act by the holder of the certificate.
(2)
If, under section 31 (4) (a) of the
Environmental Assessment Act
, the chief executive assessment officer extends the deadline
specified in Environmental Assessment Certificate # E18-01, section 31
(6) of that Act does not apply in relation to the certificate until
the end of the period of the extension.
