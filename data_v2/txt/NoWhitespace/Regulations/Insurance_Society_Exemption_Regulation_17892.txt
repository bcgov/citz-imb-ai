Financial Institutions Act
Insurance Society Exemption Regulation
Interpretation
1
In this regulation
"Act"
means the
Financial Institutions Act
.
Exemption from section 196
2
Section 196 of the Act does not apply to a society, described in that
section, in respect of a contract between it and a member of the
society if each of the following conditions is fulfilled:
(a)
the society's assets exceed its liabilities by at least
$3 million;
(b)
the contract with the member
(i)
provides health care benefits or income replacement benefits covering
a period of disability because of illness or injury, and
(ii)
pays insurance money not exceeding $100 000 during a 24 month
benefit period.
[Provisions of the
Financial Institutions Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 141, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
section 289 (4) (o)]
