Resort Municipality of Whistler Act
Workers' Accommodation Regulation
Definition
1
For the purposes of section 8 (2) (g) of the
Resort Municipality of Whistler Act
,
"works and services"
means employee housing services and includes the acquisition and
improvement of land and the construction, operation and maintenance of
buildings for the accommodation of individuals who work within the
Resort Municipality of Whistler.

Resort Municipality of Whistler Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 407, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
