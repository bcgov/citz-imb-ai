Local Government Act
Cowichan Valley
Regional District Regulation
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 424/98]
9-1-1 Emergency Telephone Service
1
The Cowichan Valley Regional District is granted the additional power
to provide E 9-1-1 Emergency Telephone Service as an extended service.
Beekeeping regulation and control
2
The Cowichan Valley Regional District is granted the additional power
to regulate and control beekeeping as an extended service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 218/91.]
Transit
3
The Cowichan Valley Regional District is granted the additional power
to provide transit as a local service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 287/91].
Economic Development Grants-in-Aid
4
The Cowichan Valley Regional District is granted the additional power
to provide Economic Development Grants-in-Aid as an extended service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 23/92.]
Health Regulation
5
The Cowichan Valley Regional District is granted the additional power
of Health Regulation under section 523 (1) of the
Local Government Act
with respect to the control of smoking within all electoral areas and
municipalities, as an extended service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 404/92.]
Emergency Programs
6
The Cowichan Valley Regional District is granted the additional power
to provide Emergency Programs as an extended service under the
Emergency Program Act
.
[en. B.C. Reg. 155/93.]
Victims Assistance Program
7
The Cowichan Valley Regional District is granted the additional power
to provide Victims Assistance Program as an extended service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 424/98.]
