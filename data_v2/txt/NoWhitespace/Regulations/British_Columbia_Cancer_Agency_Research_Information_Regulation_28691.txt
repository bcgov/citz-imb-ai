Medical Research (BC Cancer Agency) and Health Status Registry Act
British Columbia Cancer Agency Research Information Regulation
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 215/2018, November 15, 2018]
Interpretation
1
In this regulation:
"Act"
means the
Medical Research (BC Cancer Agency) and Health Status Registry Act
;
"information"
means information or classes of information relevant to medical
research into the prevention, causes, diagnosis, treatment and
outcomes of cancer or its precursor lesions and includes records
containing that information.
[am. B.C. Reg. 215/2018, ss. (a) and (b).]
Information requests
2
Information that may be requested by the BC Cancer Agency under
section 2 (1) of the Act is limited to information listed in
Appendices 1 to 3.
[am. B.C. Reg. 215/2018, s. (c).]
Appendix 1
Personal Identification Information
1 Personal identifiers
(a) name;
(b) sex;
(c) birthdate and place;
(d) marital status;
(e) residence information.
2 Family identifiers
(a) spouse's name.
3 Health identifiers
(a) health insurance number;
(b) personal health number.
Appendix 2
Medical Information
1 Records from medical laboratories, imaging services, hospitals
and other health facilities and physicians.
2 Mortality and morbidity data including autopsy reports.
Appendix 3
Factors Which Influence the Occurrence of
Cancer in Human Populations
1 Intrinsic host factors
(a) ethnicity;
(b) medical history.
