Local Government Act
Strathcona Regional District Regulation
Declared nuisances
1
The Strathcona Regional District is granted the additional power to
exercise the powers conferred on the council of a municipality under
Division 12 of Part 3 of the
Community Charter
, except the powers conferred under sections 73 and 75, subject to the
terms and conditions set out in that Division.

Local Government Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 323, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
section 799]
