Definition
1
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Financial Institutions Act
.
Prescribed classes of insurance for which insurance licence may be
issued by the Insurance Council
2
For the purpose of section 174 (2) (b) of the Act, the prescribed
classes of insurance are the following:
(a)
general insurance;
(b)
life insurance as defined in section 1 (1) of the Classes of Insurance
Regulation;
(c)
accident and sickness insurance as defined in section 1 (1) of the
Classes of Insurance Regulation;
(d)
travel insurance as defined in the Insurance Licensing Exemption
Regulation.
