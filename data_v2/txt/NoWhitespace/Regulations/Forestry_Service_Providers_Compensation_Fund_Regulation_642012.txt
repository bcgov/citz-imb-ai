Forestry Service Providers Protection Act
Forestry Service Providers Compensation Fund Regulation
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 286/2016, December 7, 2016]
Definitions
1
In this regulation:
"Act"
means the
Forestry Service Providers Protection Act
;
"free growing stand"
has the same meaning as in the
Forest and Range Practices Act
.
[en. B.C. Reg. 286/2016, s. 1.]
Fund established
2
For the purposes set out in section 26 (1) of the Act, the Forestry
Service Providers Compensation Fund is established with an initial
balance of $5 million, as provided by the government.
Forestry service providers
3
(1)
For the purposes of the definition of "services" in section 1 (1) of
the Act, the following are prescribed:
(a)
activities relating to growing seedlings;
(b)
silviculture activities that are necessary, or are required under the
Forest and Range Practices Act
, to establish free growing stands.
(2)
For the purposes of Part 2 of the Act, the class of persons who
provide services for a holder of an agreement referred to in section
12 of the
Forest Act
are designated as forestry service providers.
[en. B.C. Reg. 3/2013, s. 2; am. B.C. Reg. 286/2016, s. 2.]
