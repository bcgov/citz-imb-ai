Workers Compensation Act

Boards of Review Regulation
B.C. Reg. 31/81

Workers Compensation Act



(1)

It is the responsibility of the person filing an Appearance to
notify the boards of review of any address change.



(2)

If disclosure of the claim file is desired, the attached
REQUEST FOR DISCLOSURE must be completed and returned to the
Workers' Compensation Board. There is a $10 fee which is
payable to the Workers' Compensation Board by money order and
must accompany this request if copies of documents are
required.
