
"Point in Time" Regulation Content

Land Title Act

Land Title Act Regulation
B.C. Reg. 334/79




NOTE
:

1.

The consent and grant of priority of charge holders may be in
separate counterparts of Form 35.




2.

The execution of all declaring or consenting parties must be
witnessed in accordance with Part 5 of the
Land Title Act
.




3.

The type and registration number of the charge must be typed
or printed immediately below the signature of the owner of the
charge.
