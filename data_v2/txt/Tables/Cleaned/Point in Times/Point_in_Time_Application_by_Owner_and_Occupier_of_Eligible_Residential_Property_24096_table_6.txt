Assessment Act


Application by Owner and Occupier of Eligible Residential Property

B.C. Reg. 240/96

Assessment Act




This form should be completed by the owner and occupier of the
property (as defined in the
Assessment Act
) and returned to the assessor of the assessment area where
the property is located (see above) by January 31 in each
year. If the form cannot be delivered by January 31, the
final deadline is March 15 (see
B.C. Reg. 30/93).
