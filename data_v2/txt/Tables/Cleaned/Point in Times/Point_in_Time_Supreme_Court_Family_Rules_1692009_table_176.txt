Court Rules Act

Supreme Court Family Rules
B.C. Reg. 169/2009




Note to Claimant AND Respondent:
you must file
financial information
(Form F8) if there is a claim by you or against you for
spousal support.

If you do not file the financial information that is required,
the court may attribute an amount of income to you and make a
support award against you, based on that income.
