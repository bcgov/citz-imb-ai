
Reviewable Projects Regulation

Environmental Assessment Act




Column 1

Project Category


Column 2

New Project


Column 3

Modification of Existing Project









1

Resort Developments (other than golf, marina or ski).









Criteria:


(1)

Subject to subsection (2), a new project that




(a)

is a resort development, with or without associated
recreation facilities,




(b)

is not a golf resort development, a marina resort
development or a ski resort development, and




(c)

has ≥ 2 000 bed units.



(2)

Assessment of a new project under subsection (1)
does not include the dismantling and abandonment phases.









Criteria:


(1)

Subject to subsection (2), modification of an
existing project if




(a)

the existing project, or the existing project after
modification, were it a new project, would meet the
criteria set out opposite in Column 2, and




(b)

the modification results in an increase in the number of
bed units at the project by ≥ 2 000.



(2)

Assessment of the modification of an existing project
described in subsection (1) does not include the
dismantling and abandonment phases.



(3)

Despite subsection (1), projects that are described in
section 1 of the All-Seasons Resort Policy (the
"policy"), dated March 28, 2019 and issued by the
Ministry of Forest, Lands, Natural Resource Operations
and Rural Development, only if the modification results
in




(a)

an increase in the number of bed units at the project by
≥ 2 000, and




(b)

an increase in the total number of bed units by
≥ 50% from what is approved in the Resort Master
Plan, as issued under the policy.











2
Golf Resorts.








Criteria:


(1)

Subject to subsection (2), a new project that




(a)

is a golf resort development, and




(b)

has a developed area of ≥ 200 ha, which
includes




(i)

≥ 100 ha of golf courses, and




(ii)

≥ 100 ha of area for the clubhouse, hotel,
restaurant, parking and ancillary services.



(2)

Assessment of a new project under subsection (1) does
not include the dismantling and abandonment phases.









Criteria:


(1)

Subject to subsection (2), modification of an
existing project if




(a)

the existing project, or the existing project after
modification, were it a new project, would meet the
criteria set out opposite in Column 2, and




(b)

the modification results in an increase in the developed
area of the project by ≥ 200 ha.



(2)

Assessment of the modification of an existing project
described in subsection (1) does not include the
dismantling and abandonment phases.











3
Marina Resorts.








Criteria:


(1)

Subject to subsection (2), a new project that




(a)

is a marina resort development, and




(b)

has a marina with ≥ 1 000 m of linear
moorage.



(2)

Assessment of a new project under subsection (1)
does not include the dismantling and abandonment phases.









Criteria:


(1)

Subject to subsection (2), modification of an
existing project if




(a)

the existing project, or the existing project after
modification, were it a new project, would meet the
criteria set out opposite in Column 2, and




(b)

the modification results in an increase in the moorage
at the project by ≥ 1 000 linear metres.



(2)

Assessment of the modification of an existing project
described in subsection (1) does not include the
dismantling and abandonment phases.











4
Ski Resorts.








Criteria:


(1)

Subject to subsection (2), a new project that




(a)

is a ski resort development, and




(b)

has ≥ 2 000 bed units.



(2)

Assessment of a new project under subsection (1)
does not include the dismantling and abandonment phases.









Criteria:


(1)

Subject to subsection (2), modification of an
existing project if




(a)

the existing project, or the existing project after
modification, were it a new project, would meet the
criteria set out opposite in Column 2, and




(b)

the modification results in an increase in the number of
the bed units at the project by ≥ 2 000.



(2)

Assessment of the modification of an existing project
described in subsection (1) does not include the
dismantling and abandonment phases.



(3)

Despite subsection (1), projects that are described in
section 1 of the All-Seasons Resort Policy (the
"policy"), dated March 28, 2019 and issued by the
Ministry of Forest, Lands, Natural Resource Operations
and Rural Development, only if the modification results
in




(a)

an increase in the number of bed units at the project by
≥ 2 000, and




(b)

an increase in the total number of bed units by
≥ 50% from what is approved in the Resort Master
Plan, as issued under the policy.
