Supreme Court Civil Rules
Court Rules Act
Implied undertaking to the court
Documents produced are not to be used by the other party(ies)
except for the purposes of this litigation unless and until the
scope of the undertaking is varied by a court order or other
judicial order, consent or statutory override or a situation of
immediate and serious danger emerges. This implied undertaking
continues despite settlement or completion of the litigation.
