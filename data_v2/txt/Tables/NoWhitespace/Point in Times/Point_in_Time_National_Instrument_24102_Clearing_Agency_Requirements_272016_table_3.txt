"Point in Time" Regulation Content
British Columbia Securities Commission
National Instrument 24-102 Clearing Agency Requirements
B.C. Reg. 27/2016
10.
Until six years after it has ceased to be a recognized or
exempted by the securities regulatory authority, the Clearing
Agency must file an amended submission to jurisdiction and
appointment of agent for service of process at least 30 days
before any change in the name or above address of the Agent.
