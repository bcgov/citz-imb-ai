Law and Equity Regulation


[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 74/2014, April 30, 2014]





Discount rates prescribed




1


Pursuant to section 56 of the

Law and Equity Act

, I hereby prescribe:




(a)

a discount rate of 1.5% per annum compound which shall be deemed to be
the future difference between the investment rate of interest and the
rate of increase of earnings due to inflation and general increases in
productivity, and




(b)

a discount rate of 2% per annum compound which shall be deemed to be
the future difference between the investment rate of interest and the
rate of general price inflation.

[am. B.C. Reg. 74/2014.]
