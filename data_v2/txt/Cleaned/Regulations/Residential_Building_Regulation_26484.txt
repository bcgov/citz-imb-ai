Assessment Act


'Residential Building' Regulation





Definition




1


In section 30 (2) of the

Assessment Act

,
"residential building"
means a building used or designed to be used in whole or in part for
residential purposes and includes an associated outbuilding of and
other improvement to a building used or designed to be used in whole
or in part for residential purposes, but does not include a floating
mobile home.



[Provisions of the

Assessment Act

, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 20, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
section 74 (2) (a)]
