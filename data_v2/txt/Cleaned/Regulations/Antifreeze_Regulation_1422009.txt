Environmental Management Act


Antifreeze Regulation






Definitions




1


In this regulation:


"antifreeze"
includes engine coolant;



"motor vehicle"

has the same meaning as in the

Motor Vehicle Act

.






Prohibition




2


Subject to section 3, a person must not sell antifreeze that contains
10% or more ethylene glycol by weight.






Exceptions




3


Section 2 does not apply to antifreeze




(a)

that contains at least 30 but no more than 50 parts per million by
weight of denatonium benzonate bittering agent,




(b)

contained within a motor vehicle for sale or lease, or




(c)

of a volume greater than 200 litres contained in a single container.






Offence and penalty




4


A person who contravenes section 2 commits an offence and is liable on
conviction to a fine not exceeding $200Â 000.
