Delegation to British Columbia Safety Authority




1


Except to the extent that the administration of the

Safety Standards Act

or regulations made under that Act is delegated to a local government,
the administration of the following enactments is delegated to the
British Columbia Safety Authority:




(a)

the

Safety Standards Act

, S.B.C. 2003, c. 39, except for sections 4, 5, 6, 8, 43 to 48, 51 to
62, 81, 83 to 85, 87 to 90 and 91.1;




(b)

the Electrical Safety Regulation;




(c)

the Elevating Devices Safety Regulation;




(d)

the Gas Safety Regulation;




(e)

the Safety Standards General Regulation;




(f)

the Power Engineers, Boiler, Pressure Vessel and Refrigeration Safety
Regulation;




(g)

the Alternative Safety Approaches Regulation.

[am. B.C. Regs. 234/2004, s. (a); 22/2011 s. (b).]

(2)

For the purposes of this section, the jurisdiction of the City of
Vancouver does not include the excluded port lands.




(3)

In this section,
"excluded port lands"
means that portion of the lands described in Schedule B, titled
"Vancouver Fraser Port Authority Description of Federal Real Property"
of the Letters Patent for the Vancouver Fraser Port Authority set out
in Order in Council P.C. 2007-1885 included within the boundaries of
the City of Vancouver.
