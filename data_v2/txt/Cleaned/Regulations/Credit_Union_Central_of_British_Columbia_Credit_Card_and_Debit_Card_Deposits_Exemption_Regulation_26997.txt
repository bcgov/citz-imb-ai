O.C. 932/97


Deposited August 1, 1997






This consolidation is current to March 5, 2024.


Financial Institutions Act



Credit Union Central of British Columbia

Credit Card and Debit Card Deposits

Exemption Regulation






Definitions




1


In this regulation:


"Act"
means the

Financial Institutions Act

;


"Central"
means Credit Union Central of British Columbia.






Exemption from section 82




2


Section 82 of the Act does not apply to Central in respect of
Mastercard credit card and Mastercard debit card deposits received by
Central from, or on behalf of, persons who are members of a credit
union incorporated in British Columbia.



[Provisions of the

Financial Institutions Act

, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 141, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
sections 289 (4) (o) and 82 (1) (i)]
