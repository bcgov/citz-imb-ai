[am. B.C. Reg. 427/2004, s. (a).]





Improvements prescribed




2

(1)


The following improvements on the community airports prescribed under
section 1 are exempted from property tax levies under any Act:




(a)

paved runways, paved taxiways and paved common-use aprons for
fixed-wing aircraft;




(b)

sidewalks and other paving not otherwise included under paragraph (a)
that are used for community airport purposes;




(c)

airport navigation aids, including runway lights and unenclosed
communication towers;




(d)

perimeter airport fencing;




(e)

lights and light standards.




(2)

As an exception, an exemption under subsection (1) does not apply to
improvements held or occupied by a person other than the operator of
the community airport.




(3)

For greater certainty, a structure or work that is a building designed
primarily for the shelter of people, living things, plant or personal
property is not an improvement to which an exemption under subsection
(1) may apply.
