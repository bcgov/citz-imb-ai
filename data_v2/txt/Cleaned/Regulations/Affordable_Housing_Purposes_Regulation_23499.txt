Ministry of Lands, Parks and Housing Act


Affordable Housing Purposes Regulation





Definition




1


For the purpose of section 8.1 of the

Ministry of Lands, Parks and Housing Act

,
"affordable housing purposes"
means providing housing for individuals who have low or moderate
incomes or a disability.

Ministry of Lands, Parks and Housing Act
