Community Charter



Conflicts of Interest Exceptions

(Sun Peaks Mountain Resort Municipality) Regulation


[Last amended March 11, 2021 by B.C. Reg. 64/2021]




Definitions




1


In this regulation:


"Act"
means the

Community Charter

;



"corporation"

means the Sun Peaks Resort Corporation;


"councillor"
means a councillor appointed to the council of Sun Peaks Mountain
Resort Municipality under section 25 (1) (b) (i)
[appointment by minister]
of the

Local Government Act

.

[am. B.C. Reg. 117/2018, s. 5.]





Nature of pecuniary interest




2


A pecuniary interest in the nature of remuneration, expenses,
benefits, dividends, profits or other amounts payable to a councillor
as a result of the councillor being a director, senior officer or
shareholder or an employee of the corporation, or of a subsidiary or
parent of the corporation, is prescribed for the purposes of section
104 (1) (e)
[exceptions from conflict provisions]
of the Act.






Exception to restriction on use of insider information




3


Section 108
[restrictions on use of insider information]
of the Act does not apply to a councillor in respect of a pecuniary
interest of the nature prescribed under section 2 during the term
of the councillor's appointment.

[am. B.C. Reg. 64/2021, s. 3.]
