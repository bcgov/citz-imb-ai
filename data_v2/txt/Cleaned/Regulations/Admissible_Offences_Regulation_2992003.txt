Offence Act


Admissible Offences Regulation





Offences prescribed




1


The following offences under the

Motor Vehicle Act

are prescribed for the purposes of section 15.3 (1) of the

Offence Act

:




(a)

section 140 — disobeying construction signs;




(b)

section 146 (1) — speeding in or outside a municipality;




(c)

section 146 (3) — speeding against a highway sign;




(d)

section 146 (7) — speeding against a municipal sign;




(e)

section 147 (1) — speeding in a school zone;




(f)

section 147 (2) — speeding in a playground zone.



[Provisions of the

Offence Act

, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 338, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
section 15.3 (4)]
