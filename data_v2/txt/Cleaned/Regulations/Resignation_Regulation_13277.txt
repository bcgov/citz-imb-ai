Procedure for employee resignations




1


Subject to the terms of a collective agreement or specific regulation
made under the

Public Service Act

, the procedure respecting employee resignations shall be




(a)

an employee wishing to resign should submit a resignation in writing
to the employing ministry through normal channels and is expected to
give 30Â days notice of resignation, or




(b)

where an employee does not submit a written resignation but is absent
without leave for 3 days immediately following an oral resignation,
the employee shall be deemed to have resigned on the date of the
employee's oral resignation.
