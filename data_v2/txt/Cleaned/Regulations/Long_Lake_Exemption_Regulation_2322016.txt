Definitions

means the

Utilities Commission Act

;

Long Lake Hydro Inc. and Premier Power Corp. on the date this
regulation comes into force, and

after the date this regulation comes into force, an extension or
reconstruction of the transmission system referred to in paragraph
(a);

Long Lake Hydro Inc. and its successors and assigns, and

Exemptions




2


The parties are exempt from Part 3 of the Act in respect of




(a)

the Long Lake transmission system, and




(b)

the provision of service respecting the transmission of electricity
from the authority's transmission system for the following mine
projects via the Long Lake Transmission system:




(i)

the Brucejack mine project;




(ii)

the Premier Gold mine project.

[am. B.C. Reg. 88/2022, s. (b).]
