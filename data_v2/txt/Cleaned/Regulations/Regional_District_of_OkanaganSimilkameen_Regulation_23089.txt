Local Government Act



Regional District of

Okanagan-Similkameen Regulation


[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 131/95]




9-1-1 emergency telephone system




1


The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the
additional power to establish and operate a 9-1-1 emergency telephone
system as an extended service.






Electrical power service




2


The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the power to
operate an electrical power service as a local service with all member
municipalities participating as authorized under section 1 (5) of the

Municipalities Enabling and Validating Amendment Act (No. 2), 1988


1

.

[en. B.C. Reg. 54/90.]





Economic development grant-in-aid




3


The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the
additional power to provide an economic development grant-in-aid as an
extended service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 161/90.]





Drainage Contribution




4


The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the
additional power to provide Drainage Contribution as a local service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 216/90.]





Electric power: Anarchist Mountain




5


The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the
additional power to provide, as a local service, electric power to
Anarchist Mountain.

[en. B.C. Reg. 432/90.]





Transit Systems




6


The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the
additional power to provide Transit Systems as a local service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 128/92.]





Emergency Programs




7


The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the
additional power to provide Emergency Programs as an extended service
under the

Emergency Program Act

.

[en. B.C. Reg. 131/95.]
