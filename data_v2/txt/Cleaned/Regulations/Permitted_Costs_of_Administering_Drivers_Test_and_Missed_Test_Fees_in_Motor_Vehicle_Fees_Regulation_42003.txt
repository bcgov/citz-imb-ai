Treasury Board


Deposited January 17, 2003

Insurance Corporation Act



Permitted Costs of Administering Drivers' Test

and Missed Test Fees in Motor Vehicle Fees Regulation






Fee under B.C. Reg. 334/91




1


For the purposes of section 17 (1) of the

Insurance Corporation Act

, the permitted costs of services in the Schedule to the Motor Vehicle
Fees Regulation, B.C. Reg. 334/91, are




(a)

the entire amount of the drivers' test fee the Insurance Corporation
of British Columbia collects under
item 4 (b) (ii) (B) for providing those services,




(b)

the entire amount of the drivers' test fee the Insurance Corporation
of British Columbia collects under
item 4 (b) (vii) (B) (II) for providing those
services, and




(c)

the entire amount of the missed test fee the Insurance Corporation of
British Columbia collects under item 4 (b) (vii)
to (xi) for providing those services.




Note:
this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 42/2000.
