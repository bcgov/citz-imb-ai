North Island-Coast Development Initiative Trust Act



North Island-Coast Development

Initiative Trust Regions Regulation



[Last amended February 16, 2021 by B.C. Reg. 37/2021]





Definitions




1


In this regulation:


"Act"
means the

North Island-Coast Development Initiative Trust Act

;



"electoral area"

has the same meaning as in the

Local Government Act

.






Regions defined




2


For the purposes of the Act:



"Central South Island region"

means that area of British Columbia comprising the following:




(a)

in the Powell River Regional District, electoral area E;




(b)

in the Regional District of Nanaimo, electoral areas A, B, C and E,
the City of Nanaimo, the City of Parksville and the District of
Lantzville;




(c)

the Cowichan Valley Regional District;




(d)

in the Capital Regional District, the Juan de Fuca Electoral Area, the
Saltspring Island Electoral Area and the Southern Gulf Islands
Electoral Area;



"North Island-Sunshine Coast region"

means that area of British Columbia comprising the following:




(a)

the Regional District of Mount Waddington;




(b)

the Regional District of Comox-Strathcona;




(c)

the Regional District of Alberni-Clayoquot;




(d)

in the Regional District of Nanaimo, electoral areas F, G and H, and
the Town of Qualicum Beach;




(e)

the Powell River Regional District, except electoral area E;




(f)

the Sunshine Coast Regional District.
