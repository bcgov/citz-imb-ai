Deposited November 19, 2021






Public Service Act


Public Service COVID-19 Vaccination Regulation





Definition




1


In this regulation,

"COVID-19 Vaccination Policy"

means the policy entitled "Human Resources Policy 25 —
COVID-19 Vaccination" that was issued under
section 5 (4) of the
Public Service Act
on November 1, 2021.






Term and condition of employment




2


The COVID-19 Vaccination Policy is a term and condition of
employment for employees.






Termination deemed to be for just cause




3


If an employee is terminated under the COVID-19 Vaccination
Policy, the employee is deemed to have been dismissed for just
cause.



[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
Public Service Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 385, s. 25.]
