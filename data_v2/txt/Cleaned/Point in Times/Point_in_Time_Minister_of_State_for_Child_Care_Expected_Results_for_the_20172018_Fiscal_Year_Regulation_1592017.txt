Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act



Minister of State for Child Care Expected Results for the
2017/2018 Fiscal Year Regulation






Application of this regulation




1


This regulation applies to the Minister of State for Child Care
for the 2017/2018 fiscal year.






Definition




2


In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act
.






Minister of State for Child Care




3


The expected results that the Minister of State for Child Care is
to achieve for the purposes of section 5 (3) of the Act are as
follows:




(a)

progress toward the development of an implementation plan for
universal child care that provides affordable, accessible and
high-quality care and learning to every child whose family wants
or needs it, starting with infant and toddler programs before
gradually expanding;




(b)

engage with all levels of government, child care providers and
private and not-for-profit sectors on the development of the
implementation plan referred to in paragraph (a);




(c)

submit to Cabinet a report on the results referred to in
paragraphs (a) and (b) on or before March 31, 2018.
