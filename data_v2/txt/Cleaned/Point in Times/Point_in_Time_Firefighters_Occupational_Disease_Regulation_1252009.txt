Section 2 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 32/2013, effective January 30,
2013.




Prescribed occupational diseases



2

Each of primary leukemia, primary non-Hodgkin's lymphoma, primary
site bladder cancer, primary site brain cancer, primary site
colorectal cancer, primary site kidney cancer, primary site
testicular cancer and primary site ureter cancer is prescribed as
an occupational disease for the purposes of section 6.1 (2) of the
Act.
