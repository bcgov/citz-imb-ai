Repealed




1


Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 448/2003, s. (b).]






Exclusions effective for the 2000 and subsequent taxation years




2


Bridges that carry track in place of a railway corporation are
excluded from the definition of "improvements" in section 1
of the
Assessment Act
for 2000 and subsequent taxation years except in the following
jurisdictions listed in Schedule 2.






Exclusion of tunnels




2.1


Tunnels that carry track in place of a railway corporation are
excluded from the definition of improvements in section 1 of the
Assessment Act
.

[en. B.C. Reg. 448/2003, s. (c).]





Exclusion for school tax purposes




3


Despite sections 1 and 2, in relation to taxes under the
School Act
, bridges that carry track in place of a railway corporation are
excluded from the definition of "improvements" in section 1
of the
Assessment Act
in 1997 and subsequent taxation years.
