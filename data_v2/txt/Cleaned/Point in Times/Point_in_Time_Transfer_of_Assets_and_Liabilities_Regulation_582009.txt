Community Services Interim Authorities Act


Transfer of Assets and Liabilities Regulation




Definitions



1

In this regulation:


"Act"
means the
Community Services Interim Authorities Act
;


"deferred contributions"
means contributions for expenditures that will be incurred in
future periods;


"Fraser Region Interim Aboriginal Authority"
means the authority known as the Fraser Region Interim Aboriginal
Authority, established by the Interim Authorities Regulation, B.C.
Reg. 277/2007;



"Vancouver Island Aboriginal Transition Authority"

means the authority known as the Vancouver Island Aboriginal
Transition Authority, established by the Interim Authorities
Regulation, B.C. Reg. 277/2007.





Transfer to government



2

Pursuant to section 14.1 of the Act, the following assets and
liabilities of the Fraser Region Interim Aboriginal Authority and
the Vancouver Island Aboriginal Transition Authority are
transferred to the government:

(a) current assets in the form of cash;

(b) current assets in the form of accounts receivable


(i)  that are owed to the Fraser Region Interim Aboriginal
Authority or the Vancouver Island Aboriginal Transition Authority
as of March 31 , 2009, and


(ii)  in the case of any amount in dispute on March 31, 2009,
that are anticipated by the Fraser Region Interim Aboriginal
Authority or the Vancouver Island Aboriginal Transition Authority
as being owed to the Fraser Region Interim Aboriginal Authority or
the Vancouver Island Aboriginal Transition Authority;


(c) capital assets consisting of information systems
development cost in the form of work in progress and computer
equipment;


(d) net assets consisting of investments in capital assets
and unrestricted net assets;

(e) records;

(f) liabilities in the form of accounts payable


(i)  that are owed by the Fraser Region Interim Aboriginal
Authority or the Vancouver Island Aboriginal Transition Authority
as of March 31, 2009, and


(ii)  in the case of any amount in dispute on March 31, 2009,
that are anticipated by the Fraser Region Interim Aboriginal
Authority or the Vancouver Island Aboriginal Transition Authority
as being owed by the Fraser Region Interim Aboriginal Authority or
the Vancouver Island Aboriginal Transition Authority;


(g) liabilities in the form of deferred contributions,
consisting of restricted contributions provided by the Ministry of
Children and Family Development for the development and purchase
of capital assets.



Note:
this regulation repeals B.C. Reg. 277/2007.
