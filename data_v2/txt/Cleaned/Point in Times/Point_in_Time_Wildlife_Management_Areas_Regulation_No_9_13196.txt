Wildlife Act


Wildlife Management Areas Regulation No. 9


[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 109/2002]





Columbia Wetlands Wildlife Management Area




1


The lands under the administration of the Minister of Environment
within the 200 year flood level of the Columbia River as is more
or less shown outlined in heavy black on the attached maps
(Schedule A) are designated as the Columbia Wetlands Wildlife
Management Area.

[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]



Schedule A


Map



The map is exempt from publication and may be inspected at the
offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Environment, Victoria,
B.C.
