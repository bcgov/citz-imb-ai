Wildlife Act



Cranberry Marsh/Starratt Wildlife Management Area Regulation






Designation of Cranberry Marsh/Starratt Wildlife Management Area




1


All those parcels of Crown land, together with all that foreshore
or land covered by water, under the administration of the Minister
of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, situated in the
Cariboo Land District and outlined in heavy black on the map with
the file name CranberryMarshStarrattwma5m.pdf, on file with GeoBC,
Ministry of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, are
designated as the Cranberry Marsh/Starratt Wildlife Management
Area, the whole containing approximately 319 hectares.
