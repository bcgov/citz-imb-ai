Section 1 definition of "faculty member" BEFORE amended by
2008-24-23, effective September 1, 2008 (BC Reg 220/2008).


"faculty member"
means a person employed by the university as an instructor, a
lecturer, an assistant professor, an associate professor, a
professor, or in an equivalent position designated by the university
council;





Section 1 definition of "senate" was added by 2008-24-24, effective
September 1, 2008 (BC Reg 220/2008).





Section 1 definition of "university council" BEFORE amended by
2008-24-24, effective September 1, 2008 (BC Reg 220/2008).


"university council"
means the university council established under section 8.
