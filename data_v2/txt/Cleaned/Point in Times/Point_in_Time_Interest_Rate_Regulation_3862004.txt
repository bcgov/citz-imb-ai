Land Tax Deferment Act


Interest Rate Regulation




Interest rate



1

For the purposes of sections 8 and 9 of the
Land Tax Deferment Act
, the interest rate for the six-month period beginning October 1,
2004, shall be 1.75 percent per annum.



Note:
this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 59/2004.

Land Tax Deferment Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 249, relevant to the enactment of this
regulation: sections 8 (3) and 9 (3)]
