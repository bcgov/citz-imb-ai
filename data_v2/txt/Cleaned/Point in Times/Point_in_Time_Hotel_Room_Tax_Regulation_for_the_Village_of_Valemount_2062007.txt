Hotel Room Tax Act; Provincial Sales Tax Act


Hotel Room Tax Regulation for the Village of Valemount




Definition



1

In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Hotel Room Tax Act
.





Applicability of tax



2

July 31, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of the
Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the Village
of Valemount.





Prescribed rate of tax payable



3

The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the Act is 2% of the
purchase price of accommodation purchased in the Village of
Valemount.

[am. B.C. Reg. 160/2012, s. 2.]




Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax



4

The Village of Valemount may spend the amount paid to it out of
the revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing, programs
and projects.

[am. B.C. Reg. 160/2012, s. 2.]




Repeal



5

This regulation is repealed effective August 1, 2017.

[am. B.C. Reg. 160/2012, s. 3.]
