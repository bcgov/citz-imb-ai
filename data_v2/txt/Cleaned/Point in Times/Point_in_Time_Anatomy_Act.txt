Section 6 BEFORE re-enacted by 2004-35-79, effective July 4, 2004

6

When the purpose for which a body received under section 4 (1) has
been served, the person who received the body is responsible, at
that person's expense, for its decent burial in accordance with
the recorded religious belief of the deceased person, and must
promptly notify the minister of the time and place of burial.
