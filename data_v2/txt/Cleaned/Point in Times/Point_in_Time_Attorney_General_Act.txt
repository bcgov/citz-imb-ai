Section 1 (1) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 27/2013 under RS1996-238-11,
effective January 30, 2013 (BC Reg 27/2013).


(1)  The Attorney General of British Columbia, appointed by the
Lieutenant Governor by Commission under the Great Seal, must preside
over the Ministry of Attorney General.





Section 1 (1) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 99/2018 under RS1996-238-11,
effective May 18, 2018 (BC Reg 99/2018).




(1)

The Attorney General of British Columbia, appointed by the
Lieutenant Governor by Commission under the Great Seal, must preside
over the Ministry of Justice.
