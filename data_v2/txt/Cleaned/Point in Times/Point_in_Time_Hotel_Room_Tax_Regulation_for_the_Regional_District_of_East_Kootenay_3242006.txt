Hotel Room Tax Act; Provincial Sales Tax Act



Hotel Room Tax Regulation for the Regional District of East
Kootenay





Definition



1

In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Hotel Room Tax Act
.





Applicability of tax



2

January 31, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of
the Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the City
of Fernie and in the portion of the East Kootenay Regional
District Electoral Area A outlined in Schedule A attached to the
Regional District of East Kootenay – Additional Hotel Room Tax
Bylaw No. 1909, 2006.





Prescribed rate of tax payable



3

The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the Act is 2% of the
purchase price of accommodation purchased in the City of Fernie
and in the portion of the East Kootenay Regional District
Electoral Area A outlined in Schedule A attached to the Regional
District of East Kootenay – Additional Hotel Room Tax Bylaw No.
1909, 2006.

[am. B.C. Reg. 12/2012, s. 2.]




Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax



4

The East Kootenay Regional District may spend the amount paid to
it out of the revenue collected from the tax for tourism
marketing, programs and projects.

[am. B.C. Reg. 12/2012, s. 3.]




Repeal



5

This regulation is repealed effective February 1, 2017.

[am. B.C. Reg. 12/2012, s. 4.]
