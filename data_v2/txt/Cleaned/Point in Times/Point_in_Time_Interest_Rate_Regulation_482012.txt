Land Tax Deferment Act


Interest Rate Regulation




Interest rate



1

(1)  For the purposes of sections 8 (2) and 9 (2) of the
Land Tax Deferment Act
, the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning April 1, 2012
is 1.00% a year.


(2)  For the purposes of sections 8 (3.1) and 9 (5) of the
Land Tax Deferment Act
, the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning April 1, 2012
is 3.00% a year.



Note: this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 149/2011.
