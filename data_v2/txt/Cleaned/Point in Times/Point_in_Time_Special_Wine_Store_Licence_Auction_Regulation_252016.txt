Special Wine Store Licence Auction Act


Special Wine Store Licence Auction Regulation

B.C. Reg. 25/2016





Section 8 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 291/2016, effective January
23, 2017.





Exception to forfeiture of deposit




8


For the purposes of section 12 of the Act, the deposit of a
successful bidder is not forfeited if the bidder's application for
a special wine store licence is refused under section 16 (1) (a)
of the
Liquor Control and Licensing Act
because, in the general manager's opinion, the bidder is not a fit
and proper person.
