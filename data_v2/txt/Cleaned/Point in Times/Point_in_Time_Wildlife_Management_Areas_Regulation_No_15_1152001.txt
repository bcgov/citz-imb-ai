Wildlife Act


Wildlife Management Areas Regulation No. 15


[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 109/2002]





Cluxewe Wildlife Management Area




1


The area of land under the administration of the Minister of
Environment and outlined in red on the attached map is designated
the Cluxewe Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing 137
hectares, more or less.

[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]



The map referred to in the text is exempt from publication and may
be inspected at the offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of
Environment, Victoria, B.C.



[Provisions of the
Wildlife Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 488, relevant to the enactment of this
