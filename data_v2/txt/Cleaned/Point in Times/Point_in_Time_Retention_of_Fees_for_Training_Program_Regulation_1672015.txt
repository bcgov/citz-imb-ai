Financial Administration Act


Retention of Fees for Training Program Regulation


[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 302/2016, January 23, 2017]





Authorization




1


The go2 Tourism HR Society is authorized to retain, as
remuneration for all services performed, the fees for training
programs set and collected under item 60 of Schedule 1 to the
Liquor Control and Licensing Regulation.

[am. B.C. Reg. 302/2016.]





Repeal




2


Unless earlier repealed, this regulation is repealed on September
30, 2017.
