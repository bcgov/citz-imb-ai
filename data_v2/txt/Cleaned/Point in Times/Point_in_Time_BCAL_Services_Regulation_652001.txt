Deposited March 28, 2001






Financial Administration Act


BCAL Services Regulation




Interpretation



1

In this regulation,
"IDSA Loan"
means the money owed or owing by Duke Point Development Limited
under a loan agreement between the government and Duke Point
Development Limited dated January 22, 1980 as amended November 7,
1980.





Remuneration



2

The government may pay the British Columbia Assets and Land
Corporation up to a total of $2 812 089 from the consolidated
revenue fund as remuneration for:


(a) collecting, managing and accounting for the IDSA Loan;


(b) the post-closing costs from the sale of BC Online to
MacDonald Dettwiler and Associates Ltd.;


(c) the costs from investigating the potential disposition of
LandData BC;


(d) the costs arising from the asset disposition and business
opportunity projects approved by Treasury Board on February 23,
2000.



Note:
this regulation repeals B.C. Reg. 41/2000.
