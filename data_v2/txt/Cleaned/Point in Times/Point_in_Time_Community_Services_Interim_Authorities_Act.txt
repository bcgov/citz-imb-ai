Section 13 (4) BEFORE amended by 2003-2-28, effective April 4, 2003
(BC Reg 160/2003).


(4)  At least once in every year, the accounts of each interim
authority must be audited by the Auditor General or by an auditor
appointed by the Lieutenant Governor in Council for that purpose,
and the costs of the audit must be paid by the interim authority.





Section 14 BEFORE amended by 2003-70-116, effective March 29, 2004
(BC Reg 64/2004).




14

(1)  Subject to subsection (2), the
Company Act
and the
Company Clauses Act
do not apply to an interim authority.


(2)  The Lieutenant Governor in Council, by order, may
declare that provisions of the
Company Act
or the
Society Act
apply to an interim authority.
