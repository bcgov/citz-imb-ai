Wildlife Act


South Okanagan Wildlife Management Area Regulation





Designation of South Okanagan Wildlife Management Area




1


All those parcels of Crown land, together with all that foreshore
or land covered by water, under the administration of the Minister
of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, situated in the
Similkameen Division of Yale Land District and outlined in heavy
black on the map with the file name south_okangan_wma5m.pdf, on
file with GeoBC, Ministry of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource
Operations, except the road survey as shown on Highways District
Plan RS67722, are designated as the South Okanagan Wildlife
Management Area, the whole containing approximately 903 hectares.
