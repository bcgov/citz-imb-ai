British Columbia Railway Act

Railway Property Designation and Form of Transfer Regulation No. 3

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 21/2010, January 18, 2010]

Railway property designated

1 The real property and rights, titles and interests described in Schedule A are designated as railway property for the purposes of section 45 of the British Columbia Railway Act .

Form of transfer

2 This regulation is the prescribed form of transfer for the purposes of section 45 (5) of the British Columbia Railway Act .

Subdividable property designated

3 The railway properties described in Schedule B are designated as subdividable properties for the purposes of section 45 of the British Columbia Railway Act .
