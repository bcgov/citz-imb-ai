[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 457/88]

Exemption

1 A person who

(a) is not a member of the Law Society of British Columbia, and

(b) appears as counsel or advocate in an international commercial arbitration under the International Commercial Arbitration Act or gives legal advice concerning that arbitration is, with respect to that appearance or legal advice, exempt from section 26 1 of the Legal Profession Act .

[am. B.C. Reg. 457/88.]

1. this is a reference to R.S.B.C. 1996-255-26; see now S.B.C. 1998-9-15
