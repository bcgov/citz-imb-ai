Railway property designated

1 The real property and rights, titles and interests described in the Schedule are designated as railway property for the purposes of section 45 of the British Columbia Railway Act .

Form of transfer

2 This regulation is the prescribed form of transfer for the purposes of section 45 (5) of the British Columbia Railway Act .
