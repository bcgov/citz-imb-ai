Section 17 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 343/2006 under RS1996-440-12, effective December 4, 2006 (BC Reg 343/06).

Investment of surplus funds

17 The minister may invest any funds raised under this Act in the hands of the company which it certifies are not immediately required for its purposes in investments permitted for a trust fund under section 36 (2) of the Financial Administration Act , and may sell the securities as and when the minister thinks expedient, but the authority does not extend to sinking funds provided under this Act.
