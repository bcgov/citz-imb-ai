Exemptions

3.1 A small system is exempt from section 6 of the Act if

(a) the system does not provide water for human consumption or food preparation purposes, and is not connected to a water supply system that provides water for human consumption and food preparation purposes, or

(b) each recipient of the water from the system has a point of entry or point of use treatment system that makes the water potable.

[en. B.C. Reg. 352/2005, s. 3.]
