Section 21 (1) BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 11/2021, effective February 5, 2021.

(1) In this section:

"agrologist" has the same meaning as in the Agrologists Act ;

"applied science technologist or technician" means a person who holds a certificate of registration under the Applied Science Technologists and Technicians Act ;

"professional biologist" has the same meaning as in the College of Applied Biology Act ;

"professional engineer" has the same meaning as in the Engineers and Geoscientists Act ;

"professional forester" has the same meaning as in the Foresters Act ;

"professional geoscientist" has the same meaning as in the Engineers and Geoscientists Act ;

"registered biology technologist" has the same meaning as in the College of Applied Biology Act ;

"registered forest technologist" has the same meaning as in the Foresters Act .

Section 21 (2) (a) and (b) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 11/2021, effective February 5, 2021.

(a) the individual is one of the following professionals:

(i) an agrologist;

(ii) an applied technologist or technician;

(iii) a professional biologist;

(iv) a professional engineer;

(v) a professional forester;

(vi) a professional geoscientist;

(vi.1) a registered biology technologist;

(vii) a registered forest technologist,

(b) the individual is registered and in good standing in British Columbia with the appropriate professional association constituted under an Act for the individual's profession, and
