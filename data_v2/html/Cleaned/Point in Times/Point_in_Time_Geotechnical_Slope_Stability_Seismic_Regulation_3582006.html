Local Government Act

Geotechnical Slope Stability (Seismic) Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "building" has the same meaning as in the British Columbia Building Code.

Seismic parameters

2 Where the intended use of land is the construction of a building, the geotechnical study and geohazard assessment for slope stability of that land must be based on ground motion values having a 10% probability of exceedance in 50 years.

[Provisions of the Local Government Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 323, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 692]
