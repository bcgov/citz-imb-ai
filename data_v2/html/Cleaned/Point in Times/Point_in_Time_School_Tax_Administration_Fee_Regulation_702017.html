School Act

School Tax Administration Fee Regulation

Administration fee

1 The administration fee allowed to each municipality for the purposes of section 124 (10) of the School Act is $2 000 plus 0.1% of the current year school tax amount contained in the notice issued to the municipal collector under section 119 (6) (b) of the School Act .

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: School Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 412, section 124 (10)]
