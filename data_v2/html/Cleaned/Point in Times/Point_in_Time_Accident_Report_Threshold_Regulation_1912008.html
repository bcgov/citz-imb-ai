Motor Vehicle Act

Accident Report Threshold Regulation

Prescribed amount

1 The prescribed amount for the purposes of section 249 is

(a) in the case of a vehicle other than a motorcycle, $1 000,

(b) in the case of a motorcycle, $600, and

(c) in the case of a cycle, $100.

[Provisions of the Motor Vehicle Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 318, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: sections 183, 210 (11) and 249]
