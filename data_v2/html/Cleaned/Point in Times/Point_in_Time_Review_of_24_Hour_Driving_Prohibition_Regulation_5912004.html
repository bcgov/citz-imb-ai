Section 2 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 273/2018, effective December 10, 2018.

Prescribed number of days

2 For the purpose of section 215.1 (1) of the Act, the prescribed number of days within which a person may apply for a review of a driving prohibition review served under section 215 (2) (b) is 7 days.

Section 3 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 264/2010, effective September 20, 2010.

Prescribed application fee

3 For the purposes of section 215.1 (1) (b) of the Act, the prescribed application fee is $50.
