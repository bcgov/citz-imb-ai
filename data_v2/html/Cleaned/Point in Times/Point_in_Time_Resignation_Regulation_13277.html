Public Service Act

Resignation Regulation

B.C. Reg. 132/77

Section 1 (b) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 64/2021, effective March 11, 2021.

(b) where an employee does not submit a written resignation but is absent without leave for 3 days immediately following an oral resignation, the employee shall be deemed to have resigned on the date of his oral resignation.
