Utilities Commission Act

Community-Based Biomass Call Exemption Regulation

Definitions

1 In this regulation:

"Act" means the Utilities Commission Act ;

"project proponent" means a person who enters into an energy supply contract with the authority as a result of the call referred to in section 2, or the successors or assigns of the person.

Exemption

2 The authority and all project proponents are exempt from section 71 of the Act with respect to the community-based biomass call for electricity that the authority is engaged in on the date this regulation comes into force.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Utilities Commission Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 473, sections 22 and 125.1 (4) (b)]
