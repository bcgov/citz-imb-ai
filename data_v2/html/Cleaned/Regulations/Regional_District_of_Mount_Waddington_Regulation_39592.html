Regional District of
Mount Waddington Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 225/93]

Emergency Programs

1 The Regional District of Mount Waddington is granted the additional power to provide Emergency Programs as an extended service under the Emergency Program Act .

E 9-1-1 Emergency Telephone Service

2 The Regional District of Mount Waddington is granted the additional power to provide E 9-1-1 Emergency Telephone Service as an extended service.
