Land Tax Deferment Act

Interest Rate Regulation

Interest rate

1 (1) For the purposes of sections 8 (2) and 9 (2) of the Land Tax Deferment Act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning April 1, 2016 is 0.70% a year.

(2) For the purposes of sections 8 (3.1) and 9 (5) of the Land Tax Deferment Act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning April 1, 2016 is 2.70% a year.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Land Tax Deferment Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 249, sections 8 and 9]
