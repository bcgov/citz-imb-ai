Tugboat Worker Lien Act

Tugboat Worker Lien Fee Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 536/95]

Definitions

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Tugboat Worker Lien Act .

Fees

2 There must be paid, at the time of filing a preliminary affidavit under section 5 of the Act, a fee of $60.
