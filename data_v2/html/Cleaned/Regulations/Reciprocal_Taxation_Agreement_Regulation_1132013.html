Financial Administration Act

Reciprocal Taxation Agreement Regulation

Approval for reciprocal taxation agreement

1 With the approval of the Lieutenant Governor in Council, the Minister of Finance, on behalf of the government, may enter into a reciprocal taxation agreement with the government of Canada.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Financial Administration Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 138, section 88]
