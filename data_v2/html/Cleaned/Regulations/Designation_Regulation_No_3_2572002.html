Evidence Act

Designation Regulation No. 3

Special Provincial Constables

1 Special Provincial Constables appointed under Section 9 of the Police Act are designated as commissioners for taking affidavits for British Columbia for the purposes of exercising the powers and performing the duties of their office.

[Provisions of the Evidence Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 124, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 60.1 (c)]
