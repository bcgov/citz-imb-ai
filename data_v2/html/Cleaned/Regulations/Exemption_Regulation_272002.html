BC Rail

3 The British Columbia Railway Company and its subsidiaries, as defined in section 1 of the British Columbia Railway Act ,

(a) are exempted from the application of sections 13 [service plans] and 14 [major capital plans] of the Budget Transparency and Accountability Act , and

(b) with respect to the 2010/2011 and subsequent fiscal years, are exempted from the application of section 16 [annual service plan reports] of the Budget Transparency and Accountability Act .

[en. B.C. Reg. 54/2010, s. (b).]

Limited exemption for Pacific Carbon Trust and Provincial Capital Commission

4 The following organizations are exempted from the application of section 13 [service plans] of the Budget Transparency and Accountability Act for the 2014/2015 fiscal year:

(a) Pacific Carbon Trust Inc.;

(b) Provincial Capital Commission.

[en. B.C. Reg. 17/2014.]

Limited exemption for Private Career Training Institutions Agency

5 The Private Career Training Institutions Agency is exempted from the application of section 13 [service plans] of the Budget Transparency and Accountability Act for the 2015/2016 fiscal year.

[en. B.C. Reg. 23/2015.]

Limited exemption for Real Estate Council of British Columbia

6 The Real Estate Council of British Columbia is exempted from the application of section 13 [service plans] of the Budget Transparency and Accountability Act for the 2017/2018 fiscal year.

[en. B.C. Reg. 168/2017, s. (c).]

Limited exemption for BC Infrastructure Benefits Inc.

7 BC Infrastructure Benefits Inc. is exempted from the application of sections 13 [service plans] and 16 [annual service plan reports] of the Budget Transparency and Accountability Act for the 2019/2020 fiscal year.

[en. B.C. Reg. 28/2019, s. (b).]
