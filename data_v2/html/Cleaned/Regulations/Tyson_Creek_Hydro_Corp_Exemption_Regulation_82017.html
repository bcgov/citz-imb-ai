Utilities Commission Act

Tyson Creek Hydro Corp. Exemption Regulation

Definitions

1 In this regulation:

"Act" means the Utilities Commission Act ;

"Tyson Creek Transmission System" means the transmission system owned by Tyson Creek Hydro Corp. on the date this regulation comes into force.

Exemption

2 The Tyson Creek Hydro Corp. and its successors and assigns are exempt from Part 3 of the Act in respect of

(a) the Tyson Creek Transmission System,

(b) the provision of service respecting the transmission of electricity from the Narrows Inlet Hydroelectric Project to the authorityâ€™s transmission system,

(c) extensions of the Tyson Creek Transmission System, and

(d) reconstruction of the Tyson Creek Transmission System.
