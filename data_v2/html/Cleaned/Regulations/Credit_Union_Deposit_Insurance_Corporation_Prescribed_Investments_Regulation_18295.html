Financial Institutions Act

Credit Union Deposit Insurance Corporation
Prescribed Investments Regulation

Definitions

1 In this regulation:

"Act" means the Financial Institutions Act ;

"pension plan" has the same meaning as in the Pension Benefits Standards Act .

Prescribed investments

2 For the purposes of section 272 (1) (d) of the Act but subject to section 272 (2) of the Act, the deposit insurance corporation may make any investment that a pension plan is capable of making under the Pension Benefits Standards Act .

[Provisions of the Financial Institutions Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 141, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: sections 272 and 289]
