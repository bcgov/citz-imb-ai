Municipalities Enabling and Validating Act (No. 3)

Point Grey Campus Lands Regulation

Prescribed geographical area

1 For the purposes of the definition of "Point Grey campus lands" in section 35 of the Municipalities Enabling and Validating Act (No. 3) , S.B.C. 2001, c. 44, the lands in the geographical area identified as the Point Grey campus lands on the attached map are the Point Grey campus lands.

Note: the map referred to in section 1 is exempt from publication and may be inspected at the Local Government Structure Branch of the Ministry of Community and Rural Development, 800 Johnson Street, Victoria, B.C.
