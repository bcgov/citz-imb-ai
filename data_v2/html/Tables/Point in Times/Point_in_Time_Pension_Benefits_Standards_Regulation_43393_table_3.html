B.C. Reg. 433/93

Pension Benefits Standards Act

AAFC = ASPC - (ED - D1)
where
AAFC = assumed age for calculation,
ASPC = age at subsequent pension commencement,
D1 = date of initial pension commencement, and
ED = effective date of pension suspension.
