Changes
1 Section 2 (1) All of the definitions are removed except for the definitions of "Act", "collision", "dangerous goods", "derailment", "grade-crossing collision", "railway company", "reportable accident", "reportable incident", "reportable railway accident", "reportable railway incident", "risk of collision", "serious injury", "special situation" and "track operator".
In the definition of "collision", the words "ships, rolling stock or aircraft, or between a ship, rolling stock or aircraft and another object" are removed and the words "rolling stock or between rolling stock and another object" are substituted.
In the definition of "reportable accident" the words "a reportable marine accident" and the words "a reportable commodity pipeline accident or a reportable aviation accident" are removed.
In the definition of "reportable incident" the words "a reportable marine incident" and the words "a reportable commodity pipeline incident or a reportable aviation incident" are removed.
In the definition of "risk of collision" the words "a ship" and "or aircraft" are removed.
