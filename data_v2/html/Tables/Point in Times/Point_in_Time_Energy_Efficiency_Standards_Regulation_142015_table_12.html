Energy Efficiency Standards Regulation

B.C. Reg. 14/2015

1 Electric storage-type water heaters having a top inlet and a rated storage capacity of 50 to 270 litres Sept 1, 2010 Products manufactured on or after Sept 2, 2010 Standby loss (in watts) must be ≤ 25 + (0.20 × V) CAN/CSA C191-04
2 Electric storage-type water heaters having a top inlet and a rated storage capacity of 271 to 454 litres Sept 1, 2010 Products manufactured on or after Sept 2, 2010 Standby loss (in watts) must be ≤ (0.472 × V) − 48.5 CAN/CSA C191-04
3 Electric storage-type water heaters having a bottom inlet and a rated storage capacity of 50 to 270 litres Sept 1, 2010 Products manufactured on or after Sept 2, 2010 Standby loss (in watts) must be ≤ 40 + (0.20 × V) CAN/CSA C191-04
4 Electric storage-type water heaters having a bottom inlet and a rated storage capacity of 271 to 454 litres Sept 1, 2010 Products manufactured on or after Sept 2, 2010 Standby loss (in watts) must be ≤ (0.472 × V) − 33.5 CAN/CSA C191-04
