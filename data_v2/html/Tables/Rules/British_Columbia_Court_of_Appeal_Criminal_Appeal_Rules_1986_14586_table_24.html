British Columbia Court of Appeal
Criminal Appeal Rules, 1986

Criminal Code (Canada)

(Part 1 - Evidence: This Part shall contain a transcription of the evidence stating the name of the witness, the party on whose behalf the witness was tendered and, on each page, whether the evidence was during examination in chief, cross examination in chief, cross examination, re-examination or as the case may be. Each question shall be preceded by the letter "Q" at the beginning of the question and the answer shall be preceded by the letter "A" at the beginning of the answer. Each page of the transcript shall have 47 lines and each line shall be consecutively numbered in the left margin.)
