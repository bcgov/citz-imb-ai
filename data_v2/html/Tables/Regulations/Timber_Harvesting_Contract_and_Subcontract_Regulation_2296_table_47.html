Timber Harvesting Contract
and Subcontract Regulation

Forest Act

1 The licence holder is not liable to the contractor for any failure to allocate to the contractor in any year the amount of work specified in this contract, as adjusted pursuant to a provision of this contract required or permitted by sections 20 or 21 of the regulation, if the failure results from changes in law, natural disasters, interference by a person who is not a party to the contract or any other event beyond the reasonable control of the licence holder, other than a change in the market price of logs.
2 The contractor is not liable to the licence holder for any failure to perform the amount of work allocated by the licence holder in any year if the failure results from changes in law, natural disasters, interference by a person who is not a party to this contract or any other event beyond the reasonable control of the contractor, other than a change in the market price of logs.
