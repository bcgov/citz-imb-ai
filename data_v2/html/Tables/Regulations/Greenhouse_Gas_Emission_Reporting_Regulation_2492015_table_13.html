Greenhouse Gas Emission Reporting Regulation

Greenhouse Gas Industrial Reporting and Control Act

Item Type of Biomass
1 Wood biomass, or the wood biomass component of mixed fuels, including
(a) wood residue within the meaning of the Forest Act ,
(b) wood-derived fuel, red liquor and black liquor from pulp and paper production processes, and
(c) woody matter from agricultural trimmings, tree thinning and orchard removals, but not including wood biomass that fails to meet the criteria, if any, for carbon neutrality established by the jurisdiction in which it was produced.
