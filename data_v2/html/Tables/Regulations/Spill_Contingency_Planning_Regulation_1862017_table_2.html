Spill Contingency Planning Regulation

Environmental Management Act

quantity = (detection time + shutdown time) x flow rate + line drainage
where
detection time = the maximum time that is likely to be required to detect an unintentional release from the pipeline;
shutdown time = the maximum time that is likely to be required to shut down the pipeline;
flow rate = the maximum flow rate of the pipeline;
line drainage = the maximum quantity that could be contained by the pipeline between any 2 shutoff valves.
