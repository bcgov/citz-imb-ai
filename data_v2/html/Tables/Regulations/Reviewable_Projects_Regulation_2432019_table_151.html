Reviewable Projects Regulation

Environmental Assessment Act

Criteria:
(1) Subject to subsection (2), a new project that
(a) is a golf resort development, and
(b) has a developed area of ≥ 200 ha, which includes
(i) ≥ 100 ha of golf courses, and
(ii) ≥ 100 ha of area for the clubhouse, hotel, restaurant, parking and ancillary services.
(2) Assessment of a new project under subsection (1) does not include the dismantling and abandonment phases.
