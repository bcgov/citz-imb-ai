Reviewable Projects Regulation

Environmental Assessment Act

Criteria:
(1) Subject to subsection (2), a new marine port project, other than a ferry terminal, if construction of the project entails dredging, filling or other direct physical disturbance of
(a) ≥ 1 000 m of linear shoreline, or
(b) ≥ 2 ha of foreshore or submerged land, or a combination of foreshore and submerged land, below the natural boundary of a marine coastline or marine estuary.
(2) Assessment of a new project under subsection (1) does not include the dismantling and abandonment phases.
