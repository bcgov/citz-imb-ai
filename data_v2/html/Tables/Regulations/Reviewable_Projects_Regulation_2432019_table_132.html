Reviewable Projects Regulation

Environmental Assessment Act

Criteria:
(1) Subject to subsection (2), a new project consisting of ≥ 20 continuous km of paved public highway with ≥ 2 lanes, if the highway is not alongside and contiguous to an area of land previously developed for a transmission line, transmission pipeline, public highway or railway.
(2) Assessment of a new project under subsection (1) does not include the dismantling and abandonment phases.
