Reviewable Projects Regulation

Environmental Assessment Act

Criteria:
(1) Subject to subsection (2), modification of an existing project that results in
(a) installing a new runway ≥ 1 500 m long,
(b) extending an existing runway that is < 1 500 m long by ≥ 1 500 m, or
(c) extending an existing runway that is ≥ 1 500 m long by ≥ 500 m.
(2) Assessment of the modification of an existing project described in subsection (1) does not include the dismantling and abandonment phases.
