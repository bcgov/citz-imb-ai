 Section 2 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 225/2016, effective September 20, 2016.

Requests for information

2   Owners of diagnostic facilities that are

(a) approved under section 33 of the Medicare Protection Act, and

(b) principally equipped for diagnostic services, studies, or procedures in relation to laboratory medicine

are prescribed for the purposes of section 6 [requests for information by authorized persons] of the Act.

 Section 3 was enacted by BC Reg 94/2017 effective March 7, 2017.

 Section 3 BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 201/2022 effective October 11, 2022.

Information-sharing agreements

3   The BC Clinical and Support Services Society is a prescribed body for the purposes of section 19 (1) (c) (viii) [information-sharing agreements required for disclosure] of the Act.
