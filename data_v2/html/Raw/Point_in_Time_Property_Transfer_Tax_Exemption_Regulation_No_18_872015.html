Property Transfer Tax Exemption Regulation No. 18

Exemption

1   Transfers to Kalum Land Holdings Corporation, Inc. No. BC 0881386, of the properties described below are exempt from tax payable under the Property Transfer Tax Act:

Block A, District Lot 8123, Range 5, Coast District, PID 029-355-575;

Block A, District Lot 8122, Range 5, Coast District, PID 029-355-567.

Retroactive effect

2   This regulation is retroactive to July 4, 2014, with effect on and after that date.
