B.C. Reg. 271/2016
O.C. 801/2016		Deposited November 10, 2016
effective November 10, 2016

South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act

First Investment Plan Regulation

Prescribed period for first investment plan

1   The prescribed period for the purposes of section 202.1 (1) (b) of the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act starts on November 1, 2016 and ends on September 1, 2017.

Repeal

2   This regulation is repealed on January 5, 2019.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act, S.B.C. 1998, c. 30, section 202.1]
