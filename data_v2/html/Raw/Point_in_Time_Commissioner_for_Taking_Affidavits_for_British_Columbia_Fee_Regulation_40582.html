Evidence Act

Commissioner for Taking Affidavits for British Columbia Fee Regulation

B.C. Reg. 405/82

 Section 1 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 57/2011, effective April 1, 2011.

 Amount of fee

1  A fee of $30 shall be paid to the Crown in right of the Province by a person who is appointed pursuant to section 56 of the Evidence Act to be a commissioner for taking affidavits for British Columbia.
