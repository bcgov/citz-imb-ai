 Section 2 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 218/2014, effective November 26, 2014.

Reserves established

2   A mineral reserve for all minerals and placer minerals is established over the parcels or tracts of land identified by a site number and name set out in Column 1 of the Table in the mining division and land district set out in Column 2 of the Table opposite the site number and name.

[en. B.C. Reg. 72/2010, s. 3.]

 Section 3 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 218/2014, effective November 26, 2014.

Prohibition

3   A free miner must not register a mineral or placer claim in a reserve established by section 2.

[am. B.C. Reg. 72/2010, s. 4.]
