B.C. Reg. 264/2002
M283/2002		Deposited September 27, 2002
effective September 30, 2002

Employment and Assistance Act and Employment and Assistance for Persons with Disabilities Act

Forms Regulation

 Forms prescribed

1  Effective September 30, 2002,

(a) the attached forms are prescribed for the purposes of the Employment and Assistance Act and the Employment and Assistance for Persons with Disabilities Act:

(i)  Enquiry;

(ii)  Application;

(iii)  Monthly Report;

(iv)  Review, and

(b) the attached Application for Income Assistance for Child in the Home of a Relative is prescribed for the purposes of the Employment and Assistance Act.

[en. B.C. Reg. 356/2003; am. B.C. Reg. 171/2004.]

[en. B.C. Reg. 154/2003; am B.C. Reg. 171/2004.]

Note: this regulation replaces: B.C. Reg. 59/2002 [BC Benefits (Income Assistance) Act]
B.C. Reg. 60/2002 [BC Benefits (Youth Works) Act]
B.C. Reg. 63/2002 [Disability Benefits Program Act].
