British Columbia Railway Act

Surplus Property Designation
Regulation No. 2

Designation of properties

1   Of the properties described in Schedule A, the following portions of those properties are designated as surplus property as those portions do not relate to the provision of revitalization rail services:

(a) Lots 1 and 2 shown on the map attached as Schedule B;

(b) the property to which PID 016-274-946 applies except that portion of the property shown as Lot 3 on the map attached as Schedule B.
