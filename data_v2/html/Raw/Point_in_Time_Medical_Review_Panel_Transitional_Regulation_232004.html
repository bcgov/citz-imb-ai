Workers Compensation Amendment Act (No. 2), 2002

Medical Review Panel Transitional Regulation

Appointment of panel chairs

1   If it is necessary for a chair of a medical review panel to be appointed in order to complete any proceedings referred to in section 36 (1) of the Workers Compensation Amendment Act (No. 2), 2002, the chair may be appointed by the Lieutenant Governor in Council on the terms and conditions established by the Lieutenant Governor in Council.

[Provisions of the Workers Compensation Amendment Act (No. 2), 2002, S.B.C. 2002, c. 66, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 44]
