(1)  Despite a regulation under section 108 (2) (n) or (o) of the Wildlife Act or a bylaw under section 728 (1) (a) of the Local Government Act, the chief provincial firearms officer may issue a permit authorizing a person to establish and maintain an indoor shooting range and to discharge a firearm in the indoor shooting range.
