B.C. Reg. 123/91
Treasury Board		Deposited April 23, 1991

Financial Administration Act

Motor Vehicle Act and Commercial Transport Act Retention of Fees Regulation

Interpretation

1   A commission as specified in the Schedule to this regulation may be retained by an agent of the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia or of the Superintendent of Motor Vehicles, as the case may be, authorized to conduct the transaction referred to in the Schedule to which the commission applies.

[am. B.C. Reg. 310/96.]
