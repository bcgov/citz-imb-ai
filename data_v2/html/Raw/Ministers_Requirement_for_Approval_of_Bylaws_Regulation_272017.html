Local Government Act

Minister's Requirement for Approval of Bylaws Regulation

North Coast Regional District

1   For the purposes of section 585 (1) [ministerial regulations requiring bylaw approval] of the Local Government Act, approval by the minister is required before North Coast Official Community Plan Bylaw No. 608, 2017, may be adopted by the North Coast Regional District.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Local Government Act, R.S.B.C. 2015, c. 1, s. 585]
