Police Act

Stl'atl'imx Tribal Police Service Regulation

Geographical area

1   The geographical area within which the designated policing unit and its designated constables for the Stl'atl'imx Tribal Police Service must operate is set out in the attached Schedule.

Powers, duties and functions of the board of the Stl'atl'imx Tribal Police Service

2   The powers, duties and functions of the board of the Stl'atl'imx Tribal Police Service include those set out in section 4.2 (2) (c) of the Police Act.

Schedule
