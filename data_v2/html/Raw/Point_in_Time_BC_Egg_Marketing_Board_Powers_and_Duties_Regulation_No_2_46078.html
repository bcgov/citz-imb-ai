Natural Products Marketing (BC) Act

B.C. Egg Marketing Board Powers and Duties Regulation No. 2

 Powers vested in board

1  The British Columbia Egg Marketing Board, established by B.C. Reg. 173/67, is vested with the powers described in section 11 (1) (o), (r), (s), (t) and (u) of the Natural Products Marketing (BC) Act.
