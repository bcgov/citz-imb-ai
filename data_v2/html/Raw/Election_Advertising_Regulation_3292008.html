Election Act

Election Advertising Regulation

Definitions

1   In this regulation, "Act" means the Election Act.

Advertising exempted from sponsor identification

2   Election advertising transmitted by any of the following articles is exempt from the requirements of section 231 (1) [sponsor identification] of the Act:

(a) clothing;

(b) novelty items, including wearable novelty items such as buttons, badges, wrist bands and necklaces;

(c) small items of nominal value that are intended for personal use.

[Provisions of the Election Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 106, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 283]
