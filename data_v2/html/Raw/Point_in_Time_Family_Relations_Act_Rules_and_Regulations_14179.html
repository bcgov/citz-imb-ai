 Spouse under 19 years

32  (1)  Where a spouse under the age of 19 years has capacity in law to sign and execute the form prescribed by subsection (2), the "Affidavit of Witness" or "For Maker of a Deed" for that spouse shall be amended to state his actual age in years.
