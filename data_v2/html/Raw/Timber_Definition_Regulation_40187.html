Forest Act

Timber Definition Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 393/97]

Definition

1   In this regulation, "timber" includes all special forest products designated from time to time by regulation under the Forest Act.

Application of definition

2   The expanded meaning of "timber" applies to the following provisions:

(a) the definitions "Crown timber" and "timber processing facility" in section 1 of the Forest Act;

(b) sections 5, 8, 14, 15, 23, 24, 30, 35, 45, 48, 49, 51, 57, 64, 70, 75, 84, 85, 89, 93, 94 (2), (4) and (5), 96 to 99, 103 to 105, 108, 110, 114, 117, 118, 120, 121, 128, 129, 131, 134, 136, 143 and 163 (b), (d) and (e) of the Forest Act.

[am. B.C. Reg.393/97, s. 5.]
