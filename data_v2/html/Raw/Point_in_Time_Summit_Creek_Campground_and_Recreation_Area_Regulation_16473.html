 Section 2 (4) BEFORE amended by BC Reg. 361/2012, effective March 18, 2013.

(4)  Upon the arrival of every motor vehicle or party to a designated campground, the management authority or its officer shall record the name and address of the head of the party, and of each other person with him as a member of his party, other than his wife and children, the number of persons in the party, together with the date of arrival, and, if the person or party is travelling by motor vehicle, the licence number thereof, and other identifying letters or characters appearing on the official number plate carried thereon, including the name of the province, state or country in which the number plate was issued.

 Section 2 (4) (a) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

(a) the name and address of the head of the party, and of each other person with the head of the party as a member of his or her party, other than his or her spouse and children,

 Section 2 (5) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

(5) Every person, upon arriving at a campground and applying for accommodation therein, shall furnish the management authority or its authorized officer the information necessary to complete his daily camping permit in accordance with subsection (4) and shall not be provided with a campsite until such information has been so furnished, and until a daily camping permit has been issued.

 Section 3 (14) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

(14) The holder of a season-use permit shall affix the permit to the inside of a window on the right-hand side of his motor vehicle, immediately upon issuance, and shall not be readmitted to a designated recreation area unless said permit is affixed as described above.

 Section 4 (2) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

(2) Except as may be authorized by a permit of the management authority, no person shall use, operate or have in his charge on any footpath, trail, protection road or other land in any designated campground or recreation area any motorcycle, motor scooter, snowmobile or motor vehicle or other mechanically powered conveyance, except on a road or parking area provided for public use or on an area specifically posted for such use.

 Section 9 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

Explosives

9   No person shall, without a management authority permit to do so, bring into or have in his possession within the management area any explosive or explosive substance, except lawfully carried firearms ammunition.

 Section 11 (1), (3), and (8) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

(1) Every person entering or seeking to enter, or being within or upon, a campground or recreation area shall give to any officer of the management authority such information regarding his name, address, destination, proposed activities and conduct within the campground or recreation area, and any matter pertaining to campground or recreation area occupancy, as the officer of the management authority may request.

(3) Any officer of the management authority may order any person who, in his opinion, behaves in contravention of subsection (2) to leave the campground or recreation area within a period specified by the officer of the management authority, and every person so ordered to leave under this subsection shall comply with the order of the officer of the management authority and leave the campground or recreation area within the specified period.

(8) No parent, guardian, custodian or person in charge of any minor shall permit or allow him to do in any campground or recreation area any act prohibited by an Act or regulation.

 Section 12 (1), (3), and (4) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

(1) No person shall cause or permit any animal owned by him or in his control to roam at large in any part of the management area, including campgrounds and recreation areas, except as may be authorized by a permit of the management authority.

(3) Any officer of the management authority may order any custodian in contravention of subsection (2) to remove any animal under his charge from the management area, or from any campground or recreation area, and every custodian shall obey every order issued under this section.

(4) The management authority or an officer of the management authority may define any area or areas within any campground or recreation area or any part of the management area in which the presence of dogs, cats or any other domestic animal is prohibited, and no person shall take into or have under his charge in any such area any prohibited domestic animal.

 Section 13 (2) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

(2) Except as provided in clause (1), no person shall have in his possession, hunt with or discharge any firearm or bow or crossbow in any designated campground or recreation area.

 Section 15 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

Exemptions

15   Every constable, peace officer, member, officer or agent of the management authority and member of the Armed Forces of Canada is exempt from the provisions of these regulations while in the performance of his duties.
