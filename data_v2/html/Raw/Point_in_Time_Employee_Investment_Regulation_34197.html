 Section 1 (3) definition of "associated group of persons" BEFORE amended by BC Reg 64/2021, effective March 11, 2021.

"associated group of persons" means a group of persons each of whom is associated one with another through one or more of the following relationships:

(a) as spouse, parent, grandparent, child, grandchild, brother or sister;

(b) as parent, grandparent, child, grandchild, brother or sister of a spouse;
