Financial Institutions Act

Credit Union Deposit Insurance Fund Protection Symbol Regulation

B.C. Reg. 318/90

 Section 1 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 219/2019, effective November 1, 2019.

Interpretation

1   In this regulation "fund protection symbol" means the symbol that under section 274 of the Financial Institutions Act is issued by the commission to each credit union for designating coverage under the fund.
