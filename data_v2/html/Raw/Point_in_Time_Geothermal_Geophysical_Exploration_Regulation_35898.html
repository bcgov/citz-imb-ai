Geothermal Resources Act

Geothermal Geophysical Exploration Regulation

B.C. Reg. 358/98

NOTE: Links below go to regulation content as it was prior to the changes made on the effective date. (PIT covers changes made from September 19, 2009 to "current to" date of the regulation.)
SECTIONEFFECTIVE DATE

March 31, 2017

 Section 1 BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 39/2017, effective March 31, 2017.

Definition

1   In this regulation, "commission" means the Oil and Gas Commission under the Petroleum and Natural Gas Act.

 Section 2 (1) and marginal note BEFORE amended by BC Reg 269/2010, effective October 4, 2010.

 Application of regulations under the Petroleum and Natural Gas Act

2  (1)  The Geophysical Exploration Regulation under the Petroleum and Natural Gas Act applies for the purposes of the Geothermal Resources Act to exploration for geothermal resources.

 Section 2 (1) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 39/2017, effective March 31, 2017.

(1) The Geophysical Exploration Regulation under the Oil and Gas Activities Act applies for the purposes of the Geothermal Resources Act to exploration for geothermal resources.

 Section 2 (2) BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 39/2017, effective March 31, 2017.

(2) References to the commission in the Geophysical Exploration Regulation, as it applies for the purposes of the Geothermal Resources Act, must be read as references to the minister responsible for the administration of the Geothermal Resources Act.

 Section 2 (3) was added by BC Reg 39/2017, effective March 31, 2017.
