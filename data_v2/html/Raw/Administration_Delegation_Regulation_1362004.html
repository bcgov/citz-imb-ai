Safety Standards Act

Administration Delegation Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 245/2018, November 27, 2018]

Delegation to British Columbia Safety Authority

1   Except to the extent that the administration of the Safety Standards Act or regulations made under that Act is delegated to a local government, the administration of the following enactments is delegated to the British Columbia Safety Authority:

(a) the Safety Standards Act, S.B.C. 2003, c. 39, except for sections 4, 5, 6, 8, 43 to 48, 51 to 62, 81, 83 to 85, 87 to 90 and 91.1;

(b) the Electrical Safety Regulation;

(c) the Elevating Devices Safety Regulation;

(d) the Gas Safety Regulation;

(e) the Safety Standards General Regulation;

(f) the Power Engineers, Boiler, Pressure Vessel and Refrigeration Safety Regulation;

(g) the Alternative Safety Approaches Regulation.

[am. B.C. Regs. 234/2004, s. (a); 22/2011 s. (b).]

Delegation to local government

(b) the Safety Standards General Regulation,
