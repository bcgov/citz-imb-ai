Interest Rate Regulation

B.C. Reg. 59/2009

 Note: This regulation has been placed in the Regulations Point in Time collection. This regulation is not repealed.

B.C. Reg. 59/2009
M64/2009		Deposited March 4, 2009

Land Tax Deferment Act

Interest Rate Regulation

 Interest rate

1  (1)  For the purposes of sections 8 (2) and 9 (2) of the Land Tax Deferment Act, the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning April 1, 2009 is 1.5% a year.

(2)  For the purposes of sections 8 (3.1) and 9 (5) of the Land Tax Deferment Act, the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning April 1, 2009 is 3.5% a year.

Note: this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 232/2008.

[Provisions of the Land Tax Deferment Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 249, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: sections 8 and 9]
