 Section 1 definition of "sawlog" BEFORE amended by BC Reg 287/2009, effective November 30, 2009.

"sawlog" has the same meaning as in the Scaling Regulation, B.C. Reg. 446/94, under the Schedule of Interior Timber Grades — All Species, and includes timber graded as Dead and Dry Sawlog, Grade Code 3;

 Section 1 definitions of "grade", "sawlog" and "special forest product" BEFORE amended by BC Reg 390/2012, effective December 7, 2012.

"grade" means

(a) with respect to a coastal area, the quality of cut timber as defined in the Schedule of Coast Timber Grades under the Scaling Regulation, B.C. Reg. 446/94, and

(b) with respect to an interior area, the quality of cut timber as defined in the Schedule of Interior Timber Grades — All Species under the Scaling Regulation, B.C. Reg. 446/94;

"sawlog" has the same meaning as in the Scaling Regulation, B.C. Reg. 446/94, under the Schedule of Interior Timber Grades — All Species;

"special forest product" means a product of cut timber as defined in the Special Forest Products Regulation, B.C. Reg. 241/2003, made under the Forest Act;

 Section 1 (1) definition of "valuation area" BEFORE amended by BC Reg 265/2015, effective December 23, 2015.

"valuation area" means

(a) with respect to a coastal area, the valuation areas numbered 1 to 4 on the map in Schedule A, and

(b) with respect to an interior area, the valuation areas numbered 5 to 9 on the map in Schedule A.
