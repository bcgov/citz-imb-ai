Emergency Communications Corporations Act

Additional Purpose Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 72/2016, March 15, 2016]

Additional purpose prescribed

1   For the purpose of section 3 (2) (b) of the Emergency Communications Corporations Act, an emergency communications corporation named in Column 1 of the following table is authorized to have the additional purpose set out opposite in Column 2:

Column 1Column 2
E-Comm Emergency Communications for British Columbia Incorporatedto enter into agreements with telephone services providers by which 9-1-1 telephone access to emergency services to members of the public will be provided by the telephone service providers

[am. B.C. Reg. 72/2016.]
