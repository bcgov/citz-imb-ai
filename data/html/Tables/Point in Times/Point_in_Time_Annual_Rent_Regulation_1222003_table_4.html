Annual Rent Regulation

Description of Licence,
Agreement or Permit 		Column 2
Rate of Annual Rent
1 Community forest agreement $0.37/m 3 of allowable annual cut determined for the community forest agreement area
2 First nations woodland licence $0.12/m 3 of allowable annual cut determined for the first nations woodland licence area
3 Forest licence, other than a supplemental forest licence $0.37/m 3 of allowable annual cut specified in the forest licence
4 Cutting permit issued under a supplemental forest licence $0.37/m 3 of volume of timber authorized to be harvested under the cutting permit
5 Timber licence $1.85/ha
6 Tree farm licence $0.57/m 3 of allowable annual cut determined for the tree farm licence area
7 Woodlot licence $0.60/m 3 of allowable annual cut determined for the woodlot licence area
