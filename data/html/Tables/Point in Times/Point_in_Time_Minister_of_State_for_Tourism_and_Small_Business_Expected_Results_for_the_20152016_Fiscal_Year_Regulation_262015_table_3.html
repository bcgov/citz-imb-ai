(a) paragraphs (a) to (f), and paragraph (j) to the extent that it applies to paragraphs (a) to (f), are transferred to the Minister of Small Business and Red Tape Reduction and Minister Responsible for the Liquor Distribution Branch, and
(b) paragraphs (g) to (i), and paragraph (j) to the extent that it applies to paragraphs (g) to (i), are transferred to the Minister of Jobs, Tourism and Skills Training and Minister Responsible for Labour.
