Building Act

Building Act General Regulation

B.C. Reg. 131/2016

Plumbing Level 1 Conformance with the building regulations, as they relate to one or more of the following:
(a) plumbing systems ;
(b) foundation drainage;
(c) hydronic heating systems;
of the following buildings :
(d) a Part 9 building ;
(e) a new Part 3 building , other than a building classified as a Group B, Division 2 major occupancy , that
(i) is no more than 4 storeys in building height , and
(ii) does not contain an advanced plumbing system ;
(f) an existing Part 3 building , if the work does not involve
(i) a change in occupancy to a Group B, Division 2 major occupancy , or
(ii) the installation of a new advanced plumbing system .
