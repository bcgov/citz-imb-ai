(Grease Trail) 		At the request of Nisg̱a'a Lisims Government/Nisg̱a'a Nation, British Columbia will designate as a provincial heritage site a second section of the historic Grease Trail near the site of Gitlax'aws (Grease Harbour) on Nisg̱a'a Lands.
