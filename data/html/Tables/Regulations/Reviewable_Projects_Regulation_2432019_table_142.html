Reviewable Projects Regulation

Environmental Assessment Act

Criteria:
(1) Subject to subsections (2) and (3), modification of an existing project, other than a ferry terminal, if
(a) the existing project or the project after modification, it they a new project, would meet the criteria set out opposite in Column 2, and
(b) the modification results in dredging, filling or other direct physical disturbance of
(i) ≥ 1 000 m of linear shoreline, or
(ii) ≥ 2 ha of foreshore or submerged land, or a combination of foreshore and submerged land, below the natural boundary of a marine coastline or marine estuary.
(2) Assessment of the modification of an existing project described in subsection (1) does not include the dismantling and abandonment phases.
