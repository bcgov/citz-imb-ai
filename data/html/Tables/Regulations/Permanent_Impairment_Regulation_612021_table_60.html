Permanent Impairment Regulation

Insurance (Vehicle) Act

Item Column 1
Alteration of consciousness 		Column 2
Percentage
1 Alteration of consciousness, including stupor, coma or another disorder or disturbance that prevents the insured from performing the activities of daily living to the extent that the insured requires continuous supervision in an institutional or controlled setting 100%
2 Alteration of consciousness that impairs the insured's ability to perform the activities of daily living to the extent that the insured requires periodic supervision in an institutional or controlled setting for 50% or more of the time 70%
3 Alteration of consciousness that impairs the insured's ability to perform the activities of daily living to the extent that the insured requires periodic supervision in an institutional or controlled setting for less than 50% of the time 40%
4 Alteration of consciousness that impairs the insured's ability to perform the activities of daily living to the extent that the insured requires supervision but not in an institutional or controlled setting 15%
5 Alteration of consciousness that impairs the insured's ability to perform the activities of daily living but not to the extent that the insured requires supervision 10%
