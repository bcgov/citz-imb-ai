Timber Harvesting Contract
and Subcontract Regulation

Forest Act

1 The licence holder may make a proposal to the contractor to reduce the amount of work specified in this contract or to terminate this contract in accordance with section 27 of the regulation if
(a) the allowable annual cut of the licence in respect of which the contractor is providing services under this contract is reduced, or
(b) a licence that is subject to a fibre basket agreement affecting this contract expires or is cancelled or surrendered.
2 The amount of work specified in this contract will be amended or this contract terminated in accordance with the resolution of a proposal made under section 1.
