Permanent Impairment Regulation

Insurance (Vehicle) Act

Item Column 1
Cerebral concussion or contusion 		Column 2
Percentage
1 Subject to subsections (2) and (3), a minor cerebral concussion or contusion, with
(a) post-traumatic amnesia of less than 30 minutes, or
(b) a loss of consciousness of less than 5 minutes 		0.5%
2 Subject to subsections (2) and (3), a moderate cerebral concussion or contusion, with

3 Subject to subsections (2) and (3), a severe cerebral concussion or contusion, with
(a) post-traumatic amnesia of 24 hours or more, or
(b) a loss of consciousness of 1 hour or more 		5%
