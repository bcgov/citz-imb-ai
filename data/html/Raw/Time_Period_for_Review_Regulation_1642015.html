Workers Compensation Act

Time Period for Review Regulation

[Last amended March 22, 2021 by B.C. Reg. 89/2021]

Definition

1   In this regulation, "Act" means the Workers Compensation Act.

Prescribed time period

2   For the purposes of section 270 (1) (b) [shorter time period for requesting review] of the Act, the time period to file a request for a review of any of the following is 45 days:

(a) a Board decision or order referred to in section 268 (1) (a) [decision or order respecting an occupational health or safety matter] of the Act;

(b) a Board decision referred to in section 268 (1) (c) (iii) (A) [decision respecting levy of contribution from specific employer] of the Act.

[en. B.C. Reg. 89/2021, Sch. 1, s. 5.]

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Workers Compensation Act, R.S.B.C. 2019, c. 1, s. 109 (3).]
