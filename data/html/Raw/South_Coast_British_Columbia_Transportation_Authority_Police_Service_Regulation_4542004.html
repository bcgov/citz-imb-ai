Interpretation

1   In this regulation:

"SCBCTAPS-DLEU" means the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Police Service Designated Law Enforcement Unit;

"SCBCTAPS-DPU" means the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Police Service Designated Policing Unit;

"transportation service region" has the same meaning as in the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act.

[en. B.C. Reg. 257/2008, s. 2 (b); am. B.C. Reg. 231/2022, App. 1, s. 1.]

Part 1 — Designated Policing Unit

Powers, duties and functions of the board

2   The powers, duties and functions of the board of the SCBCTAPS-DPU are those set out in section 4.2 (2) (c) of the Police Act.

[am. B.C. Regs. 257/2008, s. 2 (c); 231/2022, App. 1, s. 3.]

Geographical area

3   The geographical area within which the SCBCTAPS-DPU and its designated constables must operate is the transportation service region.

[am. B.C. Regs. 257/2008, s. 2 (c); 231/2022, App. 1, s. 3.]

Repealed

3.1   Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 454/2004, s. 3.1 (2).]

Part 2 — Designated Law Enforcement Unit

Enactments to be enforced

4   The following enactments are to be enforced by the SCBCTAPS-DLEU:

(a) the Greater Vancouver Transit Conduct and Safety Regulation, B.C. Reg. 87/99;

(b) the Fare Collection Regulation, B.C. Reg. 190/2012.

[en. B.C. Reg. 231/2022, App. 1, s. 5.]

Power, duties and functions of the board

5   The powers, duties and functions of the board of the SCBCTAPS-DLEU are those set out in section 18.2 (1) (d) of the Police Act.

[en. B.C. Reg. 231/2022, App. 1, s. 5.]

Geographical area

6   The geographical area within which the SCBCTAPS-DLEU and its enforcement officers must operate is the transportation service region.

[en. B.C. Reg. 231/2022, App. 1, s. 5.]

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Police Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 367, s. 4.2.]
