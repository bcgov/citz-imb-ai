Financial Administration Act

Government Body Designation Regulation

Government bodies designated

1   Each of the following is designated as a government body for the purposes of the Financial Administration Act:

(a) the Greater Vancouver Sewerage and Drainage District;

(b) the Greater Vancouver Water District;

(c) an improvement district as defined in the Local Government Act.

Note: this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 230/86.

[Provisions of the Financial Administration Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 138, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 1]
