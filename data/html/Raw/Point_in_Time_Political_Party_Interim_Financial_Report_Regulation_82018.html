Election Act

Political Party Interim
Financial Report Regulation

Definition

1   In this regulation, "Act" means the Election Act.

Interim financial reports by parties eligible for allowances

2   An interim financial report under section 207.01 [interim financial reports by parties eligible for allowances] of the Act for a registered political party must be in the form set out in the Schedule to this regulation.

Supplementary reports as "amendments" to original reports

3   (1) If a supplementary report is required by section 212 of the Act in relation to a report under section 207.01 of the Act, the supplementary report must be in the form under section 2 of this regulation.

(2) In the case of a first supplementary report, the form must be completed as "Amendment #1", and any further supplementary reports must be sequentially numbered as further amendments.
