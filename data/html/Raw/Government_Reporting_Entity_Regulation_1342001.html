Budget Transparency and Accountability Act

Government Reporting Entity Regulation

[Last amended February 14, 2020 by B.C. Reg. 20/2020]

Included organizations

1   The following organizations are included in the government reporting entity:

(a) Canadian Blood Services;

(b) Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 50/2004, s. 1 (a).]

(c) Legal Services Society;

(d)-(e) Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 82/2019, s. 1 (a).]

(f) Health Employers Association of British Columbia;

(g) Community Social Services Employers' Association of British Columbia (CSSEA);

(h) British Columbia Public School Employers' Association;

(i) The Post-Secondary Employers' Association;

(j) Crown Corporations Employers Association;

(k) Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 82/2019, s. 1 (a).]

(l) British Columbia Council for International Education;

(m) Forest Enhancement Society of British Columbia.

[am. B.C. Regs. 50/2004, s. 1 (a); 30/2008; 28/2009; 29/2011, Sch. s. 2; 23/2012, s. (a); 55/2012; 82/2019.]

Excluded organizations

2   The following organizations are excluded from the government reporting entity:

(a) C2T2 The Centre for Curriculum, Transfer and Technology Society;

(b) CEISS Centre for Education Information Standards and Services Society;

(c) Insurance Council of British Columbia;

(d) Workers' Compensation Board;

(e) to (g) Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 50/2004, s. 1 (b).]

(h) Credit Union Deposit Insurance Corporation of British Columbia;

(i) British Columbia Investment Management Corporation.

[am. B.C. Regs. 50/2004; 28/2007; 20/2020.]

.*See B.C. Reg. 141/2001 re deemed deposit date.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Budget Transparency and Accountability Act, S.B.C. 2000, c. 23, s. 24.]
