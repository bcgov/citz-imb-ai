Labour Mobility Act

Labour Mobility Regulation

[Last amended December 1, 2022 by B.C. Reg. 253/2022]

Definitions

1   For the purposes of this regulation:

"Act" means the Labour Mobility Act;

"SkilledTradesBC" means the corporation continued under section 2 (1) of the Skilled Trades BC Act.

[am. B.C. Reg. 253/2022, s. 10.]

Regulatory authority prescribed for section 3 (2) (a) of the Act

2   For the purposes of section 3 (2) (a) of the Act, SkilledTradesBC is a prescribed regulatory authority.

[am. B.C. Reg. 253/2022, s. 11.]

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Labour Mobility Act, S.B.C. 2009, c. 20, s. 9 (2).]
