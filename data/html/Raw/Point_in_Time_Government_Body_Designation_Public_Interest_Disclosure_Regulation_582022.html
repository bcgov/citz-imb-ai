 Section 2 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 131/2023, effective June 1, 2023.

Prescribed class of employees

2   For the purposes of the definition of "employee" in section 1 of the Public Interest Disclosure Act, members of government bodies designated in the Schedule who exercise an adjudicative function of a tribunal are prescribed.

[en. B.C. Reg. 90/2022.]
