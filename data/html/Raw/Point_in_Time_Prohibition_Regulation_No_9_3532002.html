O.C. 1107/2002		Deposited December 13, 2002

Land Act; Motor Vehicle (All Terrain) Act

Prohibition Regulation No. 9

Snowmobile prohibition — December 2002 to April 2003

1   The use of snowmobiles is prohibited from December 15, 2002 to April 16, 2003 within the area outlined in heavy black on the attached map, with the following exceptions:

(a) the trails shown in dashed lines;

(b) the shaded area.

Snowmobile prohibition — beginning in December 2003

2   The use of snowmobiles is prohibited, beginning in December 2003, from December 1 to April 16 within the area outlined in heavy black on the attached map, with the following exceptions:

(a) the trails shown in dashed lines;

(b) the shaded area.

Application of this regulation

3   This regulation does not apply to public officials engaged in the discharge of their duties.

Map

Map exempt from publication, may be inspected at the Kootenay Regional Office, Ministry of Environment, 401-333 Victoria Street, Nelson, B.C., V1L 4K3.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Land Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 245, sections 66 and 110; Motor Vehicle (All Terrain) Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 319, section 7]
