British Columbia Innovation Council Act

[RSBC 1996] CHAPTER 415

[Formerly titled Science Council Act. Science Council Act title changed to Innovation and Science Council Act by the Miscellaneous Statutes Amendment Act, 2003, SBC2003, c. 7, s. 51, effective March 12, 2003 (Royal Assent).
 
 
Innovation and Science Council Act title changed to British Columbia Innovation Council Act by the Miscellaneous Statutes Amendment Act, 2006, SBC2006, c. 15, s. 18, effective March 30, 2006 (Royal Assent).
 
 
British Columbia Innovation Council Act title changed to Innovate BC Act by the British Columbia Innovation Council Amendment Act, 2018, SBC2018, c. 2, s. 1, effective March 15, 2018 (Royal Assent).]
