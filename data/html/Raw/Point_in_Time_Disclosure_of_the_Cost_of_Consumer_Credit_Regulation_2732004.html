 Section 19 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 54/2017, effective March 1, 2017.

Access to statements by electronic means

19   For the purpose of section 92 (1) of the Act, a credit grantor may give a statement of account to a borrower by providing access to a copy of the statement by electronic means if

(a) when access is provided, the credit grantor provides the borrower with notice by electronic means that the statement is available, and

(b) the borrower consents in writing.

[en. B.C. Reg. 26/2014.]

 Section 20 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 54/2017, effective March 1, 2017.

Deemed receipt of statement

20   A statement of account given in accordance with section 19 is deemed to be received on the 3rd day after access is provided.

[en. B.C. Reg. 26/2014.]
