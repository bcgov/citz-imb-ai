O.C. 111/2019		Deposited March 11, 2019
effective April 1, 2019

Liquor Control and Licensing Act

Retention of Fees for Liquor Training Programs Regulation

Authorization

1   A person approved under section 184 (2) of the Liquor Control and Licensing Regulation to provide a training program prescribed under section 60 of the Liquor Control and Licensing Act who collects the fee prescribed under that Act for the training program is authorized to retain the fee as remuneration for providing the training program.

Repeal

2   This regulation is repealed on October 31, 2019.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Liquor Control and Licensing Act, S.B.C. 2015, c. 19, s. 83 (b)]
