Section 5 April 1, 2020
April 1, 2022

 Section 5 (2) table items 1 to 5 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 260/2019, effective April 1, 2020.

1For registration as a mortgage broker having only a single business address $1 000
2For registration as a mortgage broker having more than a single business address $1 000 payable on registration, plus $200 for each additional business address, payable on a one-time only basis at the time business is first commenced from that business address
3For registration as a submortgage broker $1 000
4For renewal of registration as a mortgage broker $500
5For renewal of registration as a submortgage broker $500

 Section 5 (2) table items 4 and 5 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 260/2019, effective April 1, 2022.

4For renewal of registration as a mortgage broker $1 250
