Ecological Reserve Act; Park Act

Application of Park Legislation to
Ecological Reserves Regulation

1   The Park, Conservancy and Recreation Area Regulation, B.C. Reg. 180/90, under the Park Act, and section 28 of the Park Act, apply to the ecological reserves listed in the Ecological Reserve Schedule as if they were parks as defined in the Park Act.
