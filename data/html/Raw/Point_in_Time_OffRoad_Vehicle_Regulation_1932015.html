 Section 1 (2) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

(2) In the Act and this regulation, "owner" includes a person in possession of an off-road vehicle under a contract by which he or she may become its owner on full compliance with the contract.

 Section 7 (1) (c) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 50/2016, effective September 1, 2016.

(c) a signed statement of a person stating the person is the current owner of the off-road vehicle.

 Section 10 (3) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.

(3) If the ownership of the off-road vehicle referred to in subsection (2) (a) passes to the deceased owner's spouse, the spouse may, on application in a form satisfactory to the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia and on payment of the fee set out in item 2 of the Schedule, have the off-road vehicle registered in his or her name and retain the number plate or number sticker on the off-road vehicle.
