Local Government Act

Regional District of
Okanagan-Similkameen Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 131/95]

9-1-1 emergency telephone system

1   The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the additional power to establish and operate a 9-1-1 emergency telephone system as an extended service.

Electrical power service

2   The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the power to operate an electrical power service as a local service with all member municipalities participating as authorized under section 1 (5) of the Municipalities Enabling and Validating Amendment Act (No. 2), 19881.

[en. B.C. Reg. 54/90.]

Economic development grant-in-aid

3   The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the additional power to provide an economic development grant-in-aid as an extended service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 161/90.]

Drainage Contribution

4   The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the additional power to provide Drainage Contribution as a local service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 216/90.]

Electric power: Anarchist Mountain

5   The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the additional power to provide, as a local service, electric power to Anarchist Mountain.

[en. B.C. Reg. 432/90.]

Transit Systems

6   The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the additional power to provide Transit Systems as a local service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 128/92.]

Emergency Programs

7   The Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen is granted the additional power to provide Emergency Programs as an extended service under the Emergency Program Act.

[en. B.C. Reg. 131/95.]

1.R.S.B.C. 1960-261-273 (5) as enacted by 1988-50-1, is intended.

[Provisions of the Local Government Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 323, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 799]
