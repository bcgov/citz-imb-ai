Wildlife Act

Wildlife Management Areas Regulation No. 16

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 109/2002]

Lazo Marsh-North East Comox Wildlife Management Area

1   The area of land under the administration of the Minister of Environment and outlined in red on the attached map is designated the Lazo Marsh-North East Comox Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing 127 hectares, more or less.

[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]

The map referred to in the text is exempt from publication and may be inspected at the offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Environment, Victoria, B.C.

[Provisions of the Wildlife Act, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 488, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 4]
