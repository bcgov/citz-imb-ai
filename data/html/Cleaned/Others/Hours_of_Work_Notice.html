Mines Act

Hours of Work Notice

Regulations setting out HOURS OF WORK substitutions for individual mines do not appear in this publication. They are published in the British Columbia Gazette, Part II, and a complete list may be found in the Index of Current B.C. Regulations.

The Health, Safety and Reclamation Code referred to in section 34 of the Mines Act is not a regulation for the purposes of the Regulations Act . Copies of the Code may be obtained from:
