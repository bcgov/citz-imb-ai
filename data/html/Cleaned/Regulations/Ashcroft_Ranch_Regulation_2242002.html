Ashcroft Ranch Regulation

Ashcroft Ranch

1 For the purposes of section 6 (1a) and 7 (2a) of the Greater Vancouver Sewerage and Drainage District Act , "Ashcroft Ranch" means

(a) the parcels of land described in Schedule 1,

(b) the water licences, appurtenant to those parcels, that are

(i) subsisting, and

(ii) listed in Schedule 2,

including any licence issued in amendment of, or in full or partial substitution for, those licences, and

(c) Grazing Licence RAN073068 dated for reference April 1, 1996.
