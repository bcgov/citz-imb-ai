Sustainable Environment Fund Act

Sustainable Environment Fund Revenue Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Sustainable Environment Fund Act .

Prescribed revenue

2 All revenue derived from fees payable under the Integrated Pest Management Act in respect the following is prescribed for the purposes of section 4 (g) of the Act:

(a) licences;

(b) examinations for and renewals of certificates;

(c) permits;

(d) confirmations.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Sustainable Environment Fund Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 445, section 6]
