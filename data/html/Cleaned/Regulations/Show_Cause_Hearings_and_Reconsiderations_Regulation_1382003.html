Motor Vehicle Act

Show Cause Hearings and
Reconsiderations Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Motor Vehicle Act .

Fees

2 (1) The fee for a show cause hearing or reconsideration under section 118.97 of the Act is $200, payable by the person applying to show cause or requesting a reconsideration.

(2) If the director finds in favour of the person at a show cause hearing or reconsideration, a fee paid under this section by that person must be refunded by the director.

[Provisions of the Motor Vehicle Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 318, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 118.992]
