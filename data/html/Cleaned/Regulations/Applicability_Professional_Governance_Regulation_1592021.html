Hydro and Power Authority Act

Applicability (Professional Governance) Regulation

Professional Governance Act

1 The Professional Governance Act applies to the British Columbia Hydro and Power Authority, including, without limitation, for the purposes of prescribing the British Columbia Hydro and Power Authority as a government registrant under that Act.
