Insurance Corporation Act

Permitted Costs of Administering Drivers' Test
and Missed Test Fees in Motor Vehicle Fees Regulation

Fee under B.C. Reg. 334/91

1 For the purposes of section 17 (1) of the Insurance Corporation Act , the permitted costs of services in the Schedule to the Motor Vehicle Fees Regulation, B.C. Reg. 334/91, are

(a) the entire amount of the drivers' test fee the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia collects under item 4 (b) (ii) (B) for providing those services,

(b) the entire amount of the drivers' test fee the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia collects under item 4 (b) (vii) (B) (II) for providing those services, and

(c) the entire amount of the missed test fee the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia collects under item 4 (b) (vii) to (xi) for providing those services.

Note: this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 42/2000.

[Provisions of the Insurance Corporation Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 228, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 17 (2)]
