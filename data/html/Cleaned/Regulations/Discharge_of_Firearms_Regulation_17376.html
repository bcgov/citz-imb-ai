Creston Valley Wildlife Act

Discharge of Firearms Regulation

No discharge without permit

1 No person shall discharge a firearm within the Creston Valley Wildlife Management Area except as authorized by a permit or an open general permit issued by the Management Authority.

[Provisions of the Creston Valley Wildlife Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 84, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 34]
