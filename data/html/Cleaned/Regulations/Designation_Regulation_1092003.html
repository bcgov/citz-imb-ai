Coastal Ferry Act

Designation Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 315/2012, November 9, 2012]

Properties described

1 (1) The properties described in Schedule A are designated as ferry terminal properties for the purposes of Division 2 of Part 3 of the Coastal Ferry Act .

(2) The properties described in Schedule B are designated as highway properties for the purposes of Division 2 of Part 3 of the Coastal Ferry Act .

(3) The properties described in Schedule C are designated as surplus highway properties for the purposes of Division 2 of Part 3 of the Coastal Ferry Act .

Schedules A to C

[am. B.C. Regs. 282/2003; 364/2003; 422/2003; 143/2004; 38/2006; 205/2009; 315/2012.]
