School Act

School Tax Administration Fee Regulation

Administration fee

1 The administration fee allowed to each municipality for the purposes of section 124 (10) of the School Act is $2 000 plus 0.1% of the total amount of general school taxes and additional school taxes to be raised for the current year as set out in the notice issued to the municipal collector under section 120.3 of that Act.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: School Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 412, s. 124 (10).]
