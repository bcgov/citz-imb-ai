Land Title Act

Sechelt Indian Band Designation Regulation

Band designated

1 The Sechelt Indian Band referred to in the Sechelt Indian Band Self-Government Act (Canada) is designated for the purpose of applying the Torrens system to Indian land of the band.

[Provisions of the Land Title Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 250, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 367 (2) (f)]
