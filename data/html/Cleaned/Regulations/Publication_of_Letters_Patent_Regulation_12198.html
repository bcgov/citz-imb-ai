Local Government Act

Publication of Letters Patent Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 118/2018, June 15, 2018]

Information to be published in Gazette

1 (1) This regulation applies to the notices respecting letters patent that the minister is required to publish under section 780 (1) (a) of the Local Government Act .

(2) The minister's notice in the Gazette must include the following information:

(a) the name of the municipality, regional district or improvement district to which the letters patent relate;

(b) the number and date of the order in council by which the letters patent were issued, and the effective date of letters patent if different than the date of enactment;

(c) in general terms, the purpose of the letters patent;

(d) the authorizing legislative provision for the letters patent;

(e) where the letters patent may be viewed;

(f) if the letters patent include a boundary description, one of the following may be used in the notice:

(i) a synopsis describing the area and where a map of the boundary description may be viewed;

(ii) the legal description of the parcels affected;

(iii) a metes and bounds description of the area affected.

(3) Subsection (2) does not limit the minister's authority to include any other information the minister considers necessary or advisable.

[am. B.C. Reg. 118/2018, s. 7.]
