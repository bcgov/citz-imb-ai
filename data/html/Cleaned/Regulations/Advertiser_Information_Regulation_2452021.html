Election Act

Advertiser Information Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Election Act .

Additional information that may be requested in notice to produce information

2 For the purposes of section 276.01 (3) (g) of the Act, a notice to produce information may require that an advertiser provide the following information to the chief electoral officer:

(a) if known by the advertiser, the contact information of the individual or organization that sponsored the election advertising message, including a mailing address, residential address, email address, telephone number and fax number of the individual or organization;

(b) a copy of any document used by the advertiser to verify the identity of the individual or organization referred to in section 276.01 (3) (b) of the Act;

(c) copies of financial transaction records in relation to payments referred to in section 276.01 (3) (d) of the Act;

(d) the means by which the election advertising message was transmitted;

(e) a copy or transcript of each version of the advertising message and the dates on which each version was transmitted.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Election Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 106, s. 283 (1).]
