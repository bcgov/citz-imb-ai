Utilities Commission Act

Energy Resources Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Utilities Commission Act .

Prescribed energy resources

2 The following types of energy resources are prescribed for the purposes of section 65 (2) (a) (ii) of the Act:

(a) petroleum, as defined in section 1 of the Petroleum and Natural Gas Act ;

(b) liquid hydrocarbons;

(c) petroleum products.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Utilities Commission Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 473, section 65 (2) (a) (ii)]
