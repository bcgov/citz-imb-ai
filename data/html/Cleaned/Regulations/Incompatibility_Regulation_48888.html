Land Title Act

Incompatibility Regulation

Incompatible provisions

1 Sections 18 to 29, 36 to 41, 53 to 60, 89 and 93 of the Indian Act (Canada) 1 are specified to be incompatible with the Torrens system.

1. provisions cited are those as of December 1988
