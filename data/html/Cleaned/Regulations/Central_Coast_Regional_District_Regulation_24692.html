Local Government Act

Central Coast Regional District Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 27/2000]

Airport Operation and Facilities

1 The Central Coast Regional District is granted the additional power for Airport Operation and Facilities as an extended service.

Local authority powers

2 The Central Coast Regional District is granted as an extended service the same power that a municipality has as a local authority under the Emergency Program Act for such electoral area or areas as the regional district may designate.
