Local Government Act

Regional District of
Fraser-Fort George Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 426/92]

Additional powers granted

1 The Regional District of Fraser-Fort George is granted the additional power

(a) to establish and operate an airport service as a local service,

(b) to operate an Enhanced 9-1-1 Emergency Response System as an extended service,

(c) to authorize the acquisition of Electric Power Capital Financing as a local service,

(d) to undertake the operation and maintenance of cemeteries as a local service,

(e) to authorize the acquisition of Telephone Capital Financing as a local service, and

(f) to authorize the acquisition of Natural Gas Capital Financing as a local service.

[am. B.C. Reg. 426/92.]
