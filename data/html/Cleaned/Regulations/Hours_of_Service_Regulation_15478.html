Public Service Act

Hours of Service Regulation

Hours of service

1 The normal hours of service in Provincial Government offices are from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Monday to Friday, inclusive, and all Provincial Government offices shall be open to the public continually during those hours.

[Provisions of the Public Service Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 385, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 25 (1)]
