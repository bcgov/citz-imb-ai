Information Sharing Agreement
Prescribed Enactments Regulation

[Last amended June 8, 2020 by B.C. Reg. 129/2020]

Prescribed enactments

1 Prescribed enactments for the purposes of section 5 (6) of the Medicare Protection Act are

(a) the Employment and Assistance Act ,

(b) the Insurance Corporation Act ,

(c) the Insurance (Vehicle) Act ,

(d) the Workers Compensation Act ,

(e) the Income Tax Act (Canada),

(f) the Employment and Assistance for Persons with Disabilities Act , and

(g) the Emergency Health Services Act .

[am. B.C. Regs. 223/2002; 110/2005; 158/2007, s. 1; 129/2020.]
