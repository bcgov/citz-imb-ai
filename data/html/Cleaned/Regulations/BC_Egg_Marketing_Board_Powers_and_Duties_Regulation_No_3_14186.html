Natural Products Marketing (BC) Act

B.C. Egg Marketing Board
Powers and Duties Regulation No. 3

Exercise of powers

1 Approval is given to the British Columbia Egg Marketing Board to perform the functions or duties, and to exercise the powers, imposed or conferred on it by the Canadian Egg Marketing Agency Egg Quota Grant of Administrative Authority, made under the Farm Products Agencies Act (Canada) [RS1985, c. F-4] and approved under that Act by Privy Council Order, P.C. 1985-3586, 12 December, 1985.

[Provisions of the Natural Products Marketing (BC) Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 330, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 5]
