Motion Picture Act

Fee Setting Criteria Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 104/2017, April 1, 2017]

Fees or charges payable in respect of licence

1 For the purposes of section 179 of the Business Practices and Consumer Protection Act , section 59 of the Cremation, Interment and Funeral Services Act and section 12.6 of the Motion Picture Act , the administrative authority must comply with a fee setting process that is in accordance with the following criteria:

(a) before setting fees, charges or other amounts under those sections, the authority must consult with

(i) the industry the authority considers affected, and

(ii) any parties the authority considers interested;

(b) the authority must give at least 90 days' notice to the affected industry before a fee, charge or other amount set by the authority has effect.

[am. B.C. Reg. 104/2017.]
