Human Resource Facility Act

Human Resource Facility Regulation

Designations of human resource facilities

1 The following are designated as human resource facilities:

(a) all facilities referred to in section 1 (a) to (d) of the Act;

(b) the following categories of human resource facilities or programs:

(i) child or youth mental health services;

(ii) youth justice services.
