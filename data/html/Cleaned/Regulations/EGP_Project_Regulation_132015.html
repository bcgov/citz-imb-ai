Utilities Commission Act

EGP Project Regulation

Definitions

1 In this regulation:

"Act" means the Utilities Commission Act ;

"EGP project" has the same meaning as in Direction No. 5 to the British Columbia Utilities Commission.

Exclusion

2 The EGP project is excluded from the operation of section 45 (1) of the Act.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Utilities Commission Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 473, section 45 (4)]
