Local Government Act

Powell River Regional District Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 29/94]

Paratransit

1 The Powell River Regional District is granted the additional power to provide para-transit as a local service.

Emergency Programs

2 The Powell River Regional District is granted the additional power to provide Emergency Programs as an extended service under the Emergency Program Act .
