Evidence Act

Commissioner for Taking Affidavits for
British Columbia Fee Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 57/2011, April 1, 2011]

Amount of fee

1 A fee of $50 shall be paid to the Crown in right of the Province by a person who is appointed pursuant to section 56 of the Evidence Act to be a commissioner for taking affidavits for British Columbia.
