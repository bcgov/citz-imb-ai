Motor Vehicle Act

Review of 24 Hour Driving
Prohibition Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 273/2018, December 10, 2018]

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Motor Vehicle Act .

Prescribed number of days

2 For the purpose of section 215.1 (1) of the Act, the prescribed number of days is 7 days.

[am. B.C. Reg. 273/2018.]

Prescribed application fee

3 For the purposes of section 215.1 (1) (b) of the Act, the prescribed application fee is $100.

[am. B.C. Reg. 264/2010.]

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Motor Vehicle Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 318, s. 210]
