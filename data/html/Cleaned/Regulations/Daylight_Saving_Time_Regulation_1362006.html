Interpretation Act

Daylight Saving Time Regulation

Daylight saving time

1 In British Columbia each year the time for general purposes shall be 7 hours behind Greenwich mean time during the period commencing at 2 o'clock in the forenoon on the second Sunday of March and ending at 2 o'clock in the forenoon on the first Sunday of November.

Note: this regulation repeals B.C. Reg. 80/2003.
