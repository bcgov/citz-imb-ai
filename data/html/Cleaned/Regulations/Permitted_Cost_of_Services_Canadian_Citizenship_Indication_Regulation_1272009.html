Insurance Corporation Act

Permitted Cost of Services (Canadian Citizenship Indication) Regulation

Canadian citizenship indication

1 For the purposes of section 17 (1) of the Insurance Corporation Act , the permitted costs of the services of the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia in the Schedule to the Motor Vehicle Fees Regulation, B.C. Reg. 334/91, are as follows:

(a) the fees collected by the corporation under item 4 (a.1) (i) and (ii);

(b) $35 of each fee collected by the corporation under item 4 (a.1) (iii) and (iv);

(c) the fees collected by the corporation under item 12.1 (a), (b) and (e);

(d) $35 of each fee collected by the corporation under item 12.1 (c).

[Provisions of the Insurance Corporation Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 228, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 17]
