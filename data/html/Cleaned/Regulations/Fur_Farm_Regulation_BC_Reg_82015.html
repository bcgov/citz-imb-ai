AMENDMENTS NOT IN FORCE

Animal Health Act

Fur Farm Regulation
B.C. Reg. 8/2015

amended by B.C. Reg. 295/2021

effective April 1, 2025

Schedule 5

1 Section 1 of the Fur Farm Regulation, B.C. Reg. 8/2015, is amended by repealing the definitions of "licence" and "mink pelt licence" .

2 Section 2 (2.1) is repealed and the following substituted:

(2.1) A person must not engage in fur farming of mink, including keeping or dealing with mink pelts.

3 Sections 2.1, 4.1, 4.2 and 4.3 are repealed.

4 Sections 5 and 6 (1) are amended by striking out " licence " wherever it appears and substituting " fur farm licence ".

5 Section 19 (b.1) is repealed.
