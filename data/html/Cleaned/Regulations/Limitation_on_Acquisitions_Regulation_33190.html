Financial Institutions Act

Limitation on Acquisitions Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 215/2012, November 1, 2012]

Interpretation

1 In this regulation "Act" means the Financial Institutions Act .

Prescribed percentages for section 21 of the Act

2 (1) For the purpose of section 21 (2) of the Act, the prescribed percentage is 50%.

(2) For the purpose of section 21 (3) (b) of the Act the prescribed percentage is 50%.
