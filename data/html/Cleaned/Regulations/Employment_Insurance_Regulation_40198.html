Public Service Act

Employment Insurance Regulation

Application of federal legislation

1 The consent of the government of the Province of British Columbia is given to the application of the Employment Insurance Act (1996) of Canada, as provided in section 5 (4) (d) of that Act, to all employees of the government of the Province.

[Provisions of the Public Service Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 385, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 25]
