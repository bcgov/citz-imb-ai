Land Owner Transparency Act

Transparency Exemption Regulation

Exemption from requirement to file

1 The following persons are exempt from the requirement to file a transparency report under the Land Owner Transparency Act :

(a) Society of Transition Houses;

(b) a corporation that is a member, as defined in the Societies Act , of the Society of Transition Houses at the time that it is required to file a transparency report;

(c) Ki-Low-Na Friendship Society;

(d) The Linda Krystina Housing Society.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Land Owner Transparency Act , S.B.C. 2019, c. 23, s. 101.]
