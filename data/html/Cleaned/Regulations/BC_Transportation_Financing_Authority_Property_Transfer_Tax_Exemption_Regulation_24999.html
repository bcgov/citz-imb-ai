Property Transfer Tax Act

BC Transportation Financing Authority Property Transfer Tax Exemption Regulation

Exemption

1 BC Transportation Financing Authority is exempt from payment of any tax and from the requirement to file any return in connection with its acquisition, from the government, of the lands described in the Schedule.

Schedule

Exempt from publication. The land descriptions may be seen at the offices of the Director, Properties Branch, Ministry of Transportation and Highways, 940 Blanshard Street, Victoria, B.C. V8W 3E6

[Provisions of the Property Transfer Tax Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 378, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 37 (1) and (2) (b)]
