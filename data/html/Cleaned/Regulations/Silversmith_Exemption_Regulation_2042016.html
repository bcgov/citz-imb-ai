Utilities Commission Act

Silversmith Exemption Regulation

Definitions

1 In this regulation:

"Act" means the Utilities Commission Act ;

"electricity purchase agreement" means the energy supply contract titled "Electricity Purchase Agreement", between Silversmith and the authority, that comes into effect August 1, 2016;

"Silversmith" means Silversmith Power and Light Corporation and its successors and assigns.

Exemption

2 Silversmith and the authority are exempt from section 71 of the Act in respect of the electricity purchase agreement.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Utilities Commission Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 473, section 22]
