Hydro and Power Authority Act

Hydro and Power Authority Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Hydro and Power Authority Act .

Purposes of authority

2 (1) For the purposes of section 12 (1.1) (c) [other purposes of authority] of the Act, to promote the use of electricity is prescribed as a purpose of the authority.

(2) For certainty, the purpose set out under subsection (1) includes promotion for the purpose of reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Hydro and Power Authority Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 212, s. 38.]
