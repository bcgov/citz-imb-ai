Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act

Minister Without Portfolio Expected Results for the 2022/2023 Fiscal Year Regulation

Application of this regulation

1 This regulation applies to the minister designated as an official without portfolio and without title in Order in Council 497/2022 and Order in Council 657/2022 for the 2022/2023 fiscal year.

Definition

2 In this regulation, "Act" means the Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act .

Expected results

3 The expected results that the minister designated as an official without portfolio and without title is to achieve for the purposes of section 5 (3) of the Act are as follows:

(a) continue to support and promote the priorities of government;

(b) continue to meet with key stakeholders to support diversity and inclusion in communities;

(c) promote community events that further reconciliation with Indigenous peoples;

(d) submit to Cabinet a report on the results referred to in paragraphs (a) through (c) on or before March 31, 2023.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act , S.B.C. 2001, c. 28, s. 9.]
