Youth Justice Act

Youth Justice Application of Legislation Regulation

Application of Youth Criminal Justice Act (Canada)

1 For the purposes of proceedings referred to in section 4 (1) of the Youth Justice Act , except if the proceedings are commenced by means of a violation ticket, section 17 of the Youth Criminal Justice Act (Canada) is prescribed.

[Provisions of the Youth Justice Act , S.B.C. 2003, c. 85, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 4]
