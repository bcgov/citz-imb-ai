Local Government Act

Additional Powers Joint Venture
Agreement Regulation

Joint venture agreement

1 The Regional District of Nanaimo, the City of Parksville and the Town of Qualicum Beach are granted the additional power to enter into and amend a joint venture agreement to establish a joint venture to design, construct, install, own, operate, maintain and replace a bulk water supply facility, and for all related purposes, within the boundaries of the Regional District of Nanaimo.

Condition

2 The Regional District of Nanaimo must, before entering into the joint venture agreement referred to in section 1, enact a bulk water supply local service establishment bylaw in respect of the rural areas to be served by the joint venture.

Amendments

3 An amendment to the joint venture agreement referred to in section 1 requires the approval of the inspector of municipalities.

[Provisions of the Local Government Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 323, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: sections 251 and 799]
