Financial Services Authority Act

Transfer to the BC Financial Services
Authority Regulation (No. 2)

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Appendix" means an appendix to the attached agreement.

Transfer of property

2 Authority is given for the transfer to and vesting in the BC Financial Services Authority, by agreement, of the following tangible and intangible property, subject to the terms and conditions that apply to the transfer of that property under an agreement between the minister and the Authority that is substantially in the form of the attached agreement:

(a) the property listed or described in Appendix 1;

(b) the websites, electronic or digital systems and copyrights described in Appendix 3;

(c) the property listed or described in Appendix 4.
