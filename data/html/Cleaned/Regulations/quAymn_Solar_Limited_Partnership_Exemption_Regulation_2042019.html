Utilities Commission Act

quA-ymn Solar Limited Partnership
Exemption Regulation

Definitions

1 In this regulation:

"Act" means the Utilities Commission Act ;

"quA-ymn" means quA-ymn Solar Limited Partnership, and its successors and assigns, owners of the solar generation facility described in section 2.

Exemption

2 The authority and quA-ymn are exempt from section 71 of the Act in respect of an energy supply contract that establishes the terms and conditions under which electricity generated at a solar generation facility with a plant capacity output that does not exceed 15 megawatts, located on the Highland Valley Copper Mine site, is sold to the authority.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Utilities Commission Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 473, s. 22.]
