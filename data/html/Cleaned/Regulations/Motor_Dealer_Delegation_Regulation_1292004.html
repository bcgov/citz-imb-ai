Motor Dealer Act

Motor Dealer Delegation Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 200/2017, April 1, 2018]

Delegation

1 (1) The administration of the following is delegated to the authority:

(a) the Motor Dealer Act ;

(b) the Motor Dealer Act Regulation;

(c) the Motor Dealer Consignment Sales Regulation;

(d) the Motor Dealer Customer Compensation Fund Regulation;

(e) Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 124/2006, s. 2.]

(f) the Salesperson Licensing Regulation;

(g) the Wholesaler Licensing Regulation;

(h) the Broker Licensing Regulation.

(2) Without limiting subsection (1), the authority may exercise the powers, functions or duties of the minister under section 2 of the Motor Dealer Act .

[am. B.C. Regs. 241/2004, Sch. 2; 124/2006, s. 2; 200/2017, Sch. 5.]
