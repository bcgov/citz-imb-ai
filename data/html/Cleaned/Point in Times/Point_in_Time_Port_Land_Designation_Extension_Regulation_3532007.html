Assessment Act

Port Land Designation Extension Regulation

Extension of date

1 The date referred to in section 20.3 (5) of the Assessment Act by which a regulation designating land that is eligible port land under section 20.3 (3) (a) of that Act must be in force in order to be effective for a taxation year is extended from October 31 to December 31 in the preceding year.

[Provisions of the Assessment Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 20, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: sections 20.3 and 74 (2) (i)]
