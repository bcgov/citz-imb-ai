Interpretation Act

Daylight Saving Time

Daylight saving time

1 In British Columbia each year the time for general purposes shall be 7 hours behind Greenwich mean time during the period commencing at 2 o'clock in the forenoon on the first Sunday of April and ending at 2 o'clock in the forenoon on the last Sunday of October.

[Provisions of the Interpretation Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 238, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 26]
