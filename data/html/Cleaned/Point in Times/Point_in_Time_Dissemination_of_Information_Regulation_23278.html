Assessment Act

Dissemination of Information Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 292/2007, September 24, 2007]

Information may be made available

1 The assessment authority may disclose and disseminate to any person information under the Assessment Act respecting the declared value and physical characteristics of any property.

[am. B.C. Reg. 292/2007, s. 2.]
