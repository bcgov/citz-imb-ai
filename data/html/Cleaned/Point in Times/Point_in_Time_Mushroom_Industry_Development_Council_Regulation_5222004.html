Farming and Fishing Industries Development Act

Mushroom Industry Development Council Regulation

Definitions

1 In this regulation:

"Act" means the Farming and Fishing Industries Development Act ;

"council" means the Mushroom Industry Development Council established by the minister under section 2 of the Act;

"levy" means the levy referred to in section 2;

"mushroom" means agaricus bisporous ;

"mushroom grower" means a person who operates a farm in British Columbia that produces mushrooms for sale.

Payment of levy

2 Each mushroom grower must pay to the council the levy established by the council under section 4 (1) of the Act.

Remittance of levy

3 The levy for each period set out in column 1 of the following table must be remitted by the due date set out in column 2 of the table.

Column 1
Levy Period 		Column 2
Due Date
January 31 to March 31 April 30
April 1 to June 30 July 31
July 1 to September 30 October 31
October 1 to December 31 January 31

Sunset of this regulation

4 This regulation is repealed effective January 1, 2010.
