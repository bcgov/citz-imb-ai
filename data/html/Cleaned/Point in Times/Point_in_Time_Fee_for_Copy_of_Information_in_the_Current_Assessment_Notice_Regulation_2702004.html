Assessment Act

Fee for Copy of Information
in the Current Assessment Notice Regulation

Fee for copy of notice

1 The fee, referred to in section 6 (5) of the Assessment Act , for a copy of the information contained in the current assessment notice is $6 per parcel of land referred to in the assessment notice.

Note: this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 61/81.

[Provisions of the Assessment Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 20, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 6 (5)]
