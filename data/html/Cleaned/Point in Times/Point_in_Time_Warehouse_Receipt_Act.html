Section 1, definition of "warehouse receipt" BEFORE amended by 2016-5-39,Sch 1, effective March 10, 2016 (Royal Assent).

"warehouse receipt" means an acknowledgment in writing by a warehouser of the receipt for storage of goods not owned by the warehouser;

Section 8 (1) BEFORE amended by 2023-10-1160, effective March 30, 2023 (Royal Assent).

(1) Except as provided in section 18, if a warehouser

(a) delivers goods for which the warehouser has issued a negotiable receipt, and

(b) fails to take up and cancel the receipt,

the warehouser is liable for failure to deliver the goods to anyone who purchases the receipt in good faith and for valuable consideration, whether he or she acquired title to the receipt before or after delivery of the goods by the warehouser.

Section 25 BEFORE amended by 2016-5-42,Sch 4, effective March 10, 2016 (Royal Assent).

Endorser not guarantor

25 The endorsement of a receipt does not make the endorser liable for any failure on the part of the warehouser or previous endorsers of the receipt to fulfil their respective obligations.
