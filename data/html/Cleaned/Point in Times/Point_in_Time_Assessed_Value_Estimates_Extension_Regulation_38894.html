Assessment Act

Assessed Value Estimates Extension Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 292/2007, September 24, 2007]

Extension of date

1 The date for the assessment authority to supply estimates of assessed values to each municipality under section 2 of the Assessment Act is extended from October 31 to January 5.

[am. B.C. Regs. 305/95; 196/96; 292/2007, s. 2.]
