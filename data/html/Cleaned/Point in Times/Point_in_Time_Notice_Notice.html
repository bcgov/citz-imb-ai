Notice BEFORE amended effective December 11, 2012.

Regulations setting out reserves made under this Act do not appear in this publication, but are published in The British Columbia Gazette, Part II, except

171/2011: Mineral and Coal Land Reserve (No Mineral Claim Registrations) Regulation;

280/2007: Mineral and Coal Land Reserve (No Mineral or Placer Mineral Registrations) Regulation;

395/2012: Coal Land Reserve Regulation.

Notice BEFORE amended effective September 6, 2016.

Regulations that create reserves made under this Act are not consolidated in this publication. They are published in the British Columbia Gazette Part II. See the Index of Current B.C. Regulations for a complete listing.

The following selected reserves are consolidated for online publication only and are available to view at http://www.bclaws.ca/ and http://www.qplegaleze.ca/default.htm:

B.C. Reg. 395/2012, Coal Land Reserve Regulation;
