Baynes Lake

10 (1) In that portion of Management Unit 4-22 in the area of Baynes Lake within the following described boundaries: DD5116 of DL 132, a person commits an offence if he or she

(a) uses or operates a motor vehicle,

(b) accesses the area by land or air during the period of April 15 to June 30, or

(c) accesses the area, during the period of April 1 to April 14 and from July 1 to August 1, with a dog that is not under the direct control of a person and restrained by a leash not longer than 2 metres.

(2) Despite subsection (1), that unsurveyed portion of Parcel 1 of DL 132, Kootenay District, Explanatory Plan DD5116, containing 0.433 hectares, more or less, tenured under the Land Act to Castle Rock Enterprises Inc. File #44003970 and that portion of the sand peninsula south of the boat launch at 49.1210° north latitude and 115.1255° west longitude are exempt.
