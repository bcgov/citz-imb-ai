Committees

1 The following committees are designated under section 12 (5) of the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act :

(a) Treasury Board;

(b) Environment and Land Use Committee;

(c)-(d) Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 275/2020, s. 1 (a).]

(e) Legislative Review Committee;

(f)-(j) Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 275/2020, s. 1 (a).]

(k) Appointment Orders Committee;

(l) Planning and Priorities Committee;

(m) Cabinet Committee on Economy;

(n) Cabinet Committee on Social Initiatives;

(o) Cabinet Working Group on Mental Health, Addictions and Homelessness;

(p) Cabinet Working Group on COVID;

(q) Government Caucus Committee on Economy;

(r) Government Caucus Committee on Social Initiatives.

[am. B.C. Regs. 241/2018; 275/2020; 45/2021.]
