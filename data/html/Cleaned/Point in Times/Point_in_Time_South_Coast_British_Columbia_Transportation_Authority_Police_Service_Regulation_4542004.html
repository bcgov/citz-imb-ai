Section 2 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 231/2022, effective November 25, 2022.

Powers, duties and functions of the board of the SCBCTAPS

2 The powers, duties and functions of the board of the SCBCTAPS are those set out in section 4.2 (2) (c) of the Police Act .

[am. B.C. Reg. 257/2008, s. 2 (c).]

Section 3 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 231/2022, effective November 25, 2022.

Geographical area

3 The geographical area within which the SCBCTAPS and its designated constables must operate is the transportation service region.

[am. B.C. Reg. 257/2008, s. 2 (c).]

Section 3.1 was enacted by BC Reg 231/2022, effective November 25, 2022.

Section 3.1 BEFORE self-repealed by BC Reg 454/2004, effective December 31, 2022.

Transition — former designated policing unit

3.1 (1) This regulation as it read immediately before its amendment on November 25, 2022 applies in relation to the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Police Service established by Ministerial Order 30/2013.

(2) This section is repealed on December 31, 2022.

[en. B.C. Reg. 231/2022, App. 1, s. 4.]
