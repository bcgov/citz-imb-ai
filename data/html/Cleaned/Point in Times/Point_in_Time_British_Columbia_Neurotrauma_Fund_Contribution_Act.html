Section 1 definitions of "British Columbia Neurotrauma Fund" and "Rick Hansen Man in Motion Foundation" BEFORE amended by 2015-18-260, effective November 28, 2016 (BC Reg 216/2015).

"British Columbia Neurotrauma Fund" means the British Columbia Neurotrauma Fund established by the Rick Hansen Man in Motion Foundation for the purposes of funding services and projects respecting neurotraumatic injury and victims of neurotraumatic injury;

"Rick Hansen Man in Motion Foundation" means the Rick Hansen Man in Motion Foundation incorporated under the Society Act ;
