Interpretation

In this Rule:

(a) "Act" means the Securities Act ;

(b) "NI 44-102" means National Instrument 44-102 Shelf Distributions ;

(c)Â a term that is defined or interpreted in the Act has the meaning ascribed to it in the Act.

Application

The exemptions established under this Rule are in addition to those established under NI 44-102.

Shelf Prospectus

Sections 68 to 70 of the Act do not apply to a distribution of securities made in compliance with NI 44-102.
