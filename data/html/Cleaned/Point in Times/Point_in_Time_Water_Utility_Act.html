Section 1 (a), (h) and (i) definition of "water utility" BEFORE amended by 2001-9-102, effective April 11, 2001 (Royal Assent).

(a) a person who owns or operates in British Columbia equipment or facilities for the diverting, developing, pumping, impounding, distributing or furnishing of water to or for 5 or more persons, or to a corporation, for compensation, and

(h) a person who supplies water by tanker truck, or

(i) a person who sells bottled water.

Section 1 (j) was added to definition of "water utility" by 2001-9-102, effective April 11, 2001 (Royal Assent).

Section 1 definition of "comptroller" BEFORE amended by 2014-15-216, effective February 29, 2016 (BC Reg 35/2016).

"comptroller" means the Comptroller of Water Rights under the Water Act;

Section 1 definition of "water utility" paragraph (f) BEFORE amended by 2014-15-216, effective February 29, 2016 (BC Reg 35/2016).

(f) an improvement district or water users' community under the Water Act,

Section 1 definition of "water utility" paragraph (f.1) was added by 2014-15-216, effective February 29, 2016 (BC Reg 35/2016).
