Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act

Cattery and Kennel Regulation

B.C. Reg. 96/2016

Section 4 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 34/2019, effective June 1, 2019.

4 The requirements and recommendations contained in A Code of Practice for Canadian Kennel Operations, Second Edition, as produced by the Canadian Veterinary Medical Association and published in 2007, are recognized as reasonable and generally accepted practices of engaging in the operation of a kennel for the purposes of section 24.02 (c) of the Act.
