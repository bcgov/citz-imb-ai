Forest Act

Master Licence to Cut Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Forest Act .

Prescribed purpose

2 Constructing or operating a pipeline under the National Energy Board Act (Canada) is a prescribed purpose for the purposes of section 47.4 (2) (b) (ii) of the Act.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Forest Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 157, section 151]
