Pharmacy Operations and Drug Scheduling Act

Prescribed Number of Days Regulation

Prescribed number of days

1 The prescribed number of days for the purposes of sections 21 (5) and 22 (2.1) of the Pharmacy Operations and Drug Scheduling Act is 60 days.

[Provisions of the Pharmacy Operations and Drug Scheduling Act , S.B.C. 2003, c. 77, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: sections 21 (5), 22 (2.1) and 34.1]
