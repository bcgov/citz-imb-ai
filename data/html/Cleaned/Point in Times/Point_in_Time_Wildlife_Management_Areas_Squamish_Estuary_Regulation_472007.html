Wildlife Act

Wildlife Management Areas (Squamish Estuary) Regulation

Designation of wildlife management area

1 All those parcels of Crown land, together with all that foreshore or land covered by water, under the administration of the Minister of Environment, situated in New Westminster District and contained within the described boundaries as shown on the Official Plan deposited in the Crown Land Registry as Plan 6 Tube 1977, are designated the Skwelwil'em Squamish Estuary Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing approximately 663 hectares.

[Provisions of the Wildlife Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 488, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 4 (2)]
