Wildlife Act

Habitat Conservation Trust Fund Regulation

Definitions

1 In this regulation:

"Act" means the Wildlife Act ;

"board" and "board member" have the same meanings as in Part 2 of the Act.

Notice

2 Notice of the date, time and location of any meeting of the board must be given to each board member at least 5 days before the meeting.

Remuneration

3 The amount of remuneration prescribed for the purposes of section 116 (1) (e) of the Act is,

(a) for the chair of the board, $250 for each day that he or she is engaged in meetings of the board for the purpose of exercising the board's powers and performing its duties under Part 2 of the Act, and

(b) for any other board member, $200 for each day that he or she is engaged in meetings of the board for the purpose of exercising the board's powers and performing its duties under Part 2 of the Act.

[Provisions of the Wildlife Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 488, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: sections 108, 114 (3) and 116 (1) (e)]
