Health Authorities Act

Vancouver Health Board Regulation

Designation as corporation

1 The City of Vancouver is designated a corporation in respect of section 26 (3) of the Health Act .

Powers of board

2 The Vancouver Health Board has the powers of the Council of the City of Vancouver, under section 26 (1) to (3) of the Health Act .

[Provisions of the Health Authorities Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 180, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: sections 19 (3) and 21 (2) (h) and (3)]
