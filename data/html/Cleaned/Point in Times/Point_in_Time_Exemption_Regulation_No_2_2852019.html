Environmental Assessment Act

Exemption Regulation (No. 2)

B.C. Reg. 285/2019

Section 1 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 2/2021, effective January 8, 2021.

Exemption from limitation on extension of certificate

1 Section 31 (4) (a) of the Environmental Assessment Act , as it limits the number of times that a deadline specified in an environmental assessment certificate may be extended, does not apply in relation to the reviewable project described in Environmental Assessment Certificate # M09-02 if the deadline is not extended beyond January 14, 2021.
