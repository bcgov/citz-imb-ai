Section 1 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 266/2020, effective September 20, 2020.

Delegation to British Columbia Safety Authority

1 The administration of the Railway Safety Act , except for

(a) sections 3, 11, 12, and 20 and Parts 4 and 5, and

(b) section 19 (0.1) of the Canadian Transportation Accident Investigation and Safety Board Act , adopted by section 5 of the adopting regulation

is delegated to the British Columbia Safety Authority, except to the extent that that administration is otherwise delegated by this regulation.

[am. B.C. Reg. 58/2006, s. 2.]
