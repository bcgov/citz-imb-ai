Section 3 BEFORE amended by 2003-70-211, effective March 29, 2004 (BC Reg 64/2004).

Company Act and Society Act not to apply

3 (1) Company Act and the Society Act do not apply to the society.

(2)  Despite subsection (1), the Lieutenant Governor in Council may order that one or more provisions of the Company Act apply to the society.

Section 3 (1) BEFORE amended by 2015-18-327, effective November 28, 2016 (BC Reg 216/2015).

(1) The Business Corporations Act and the Society Act do not apply to the society.

Section 9 (1) BEFORE amended by 2007-14-44(a), effective May 31, 2007 (Royal Assent).

(1)  The objects of the society are,

(a) subject to section 10 (3), to assist low income individuals to resolve their legal problems and facilitate access to justice for low income individuals,

(b) subject to section 10 (3), to establish and administer an effective and efficient system for providing legal aid to low income individuals in British Columbia, and

(c) to provide advice to the Attorney General respecting legal aid.

Section 9 (2) (a) BEFORE amended by 2007-14-44(b), effective May 31, 2007 (Royal Assent).

(a) the society is to identify and assess the legal needs of low income individuals in British Columbia;

Section 9 (2) (d) BEFORE amended by 2007-14-44(c), effective May 31, 2007 (Royal Assent).

(d) the society is to be flexible and innovative in the manner in which it provides legal aid.

Section 22 BEFORE re-enacted by 2003-2-38, effective April 4, 2003 (BC Reg 160/2003).

Audit

22 The Auditor General or a person designated by the Auditor General must, at least once in each fiscal year, audit the books, accounts and financial affairs of the society.
