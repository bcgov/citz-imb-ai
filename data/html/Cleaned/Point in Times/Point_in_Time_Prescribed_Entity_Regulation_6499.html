Section 3 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 2/2013, effective January 16, 2013.

Greater Vancouver Transportation Authority

3 The South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority established under the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act is a prescribed entity for the purpose of the definition of "entity" in section 1 of the Police Act .

[en. B.C. Reg. 180/2004; am. B.C. Reg. 257/2008, s. 3.]

Section 3 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 61/2016, effective March 2, 2016.

South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority

3 Each of the following is prescribed as an entity for the purposes of paragraph (d) of the definition of "entity" in section 1 of the Police Act :

(a) the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority established under the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act ;

(b) TransLink Security Management Ltd., a subsidiary of the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority established under the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act.

[en. B.C. Reg. 2/2013.]
