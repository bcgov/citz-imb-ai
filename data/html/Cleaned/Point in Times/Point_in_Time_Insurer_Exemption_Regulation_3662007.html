Extraprovincial insurance corporation — reinsurance

10 Parts 1 to 6 of the Act do not apply to an extraprovincial insurance corporation whose insurance business in British Columbia is confined to reinsurance of persons referred to in section 75 (a) to (c) or (e) to (i) of the Act or in sections 1 to 6 of this regulation.

[en. B.C. Reg. 299/2009, Sch. 2, s. 1 (b).]
