Section 3 (4) and (5) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 45/2022, effective February 24, 2022.

(4) Each director who votes shall have the number of votes that he or she would have had in voting on the matter at a regular meeting of the board.

(5) The rules under section 791 of the Act regarding the counting of votes at meetings of the board, except section 791 (10), apply to the counting of votes taken in accordance with this section.

Schedule, item "the Skeena-Queen Charlotte Regional District" was struck out by BC Reg 45/2022, effective February 24, 2022.

Schedule, item "the North Coast Regional District" was added by BC Reg 45/2022, effective February 24, 2022.
