Wildlife Act

Wildlife Management Areas Regulation No. 8

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 109/2002]

Boundary Bay Wildlife Management Area

1 The lands under the administration of the Minister of Environment outlined in red on the map in Schedule A are designated the Boundary Bay Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing 11 470 hectares, more or less.

[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]

Schedule A

Map

The map is exempt from publication and may be inspected at the offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Environment, Victoria, B.C.

[Provisions of the Wildlife Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 488, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 4]
