B.C. Reg. 174/2017
Treasury Board 		Deposited September 22, 2017

Financial Administration Act

Retention of Fees for Liquor Training Programs Regulation

Authorization

1 A person approved under section 184 (2) of the Liquor Control and Licensing Regulation to provide a training program prescribed under section 60 of the Liquor Control and Licensing Act who collects the fee prescribed under that Act for that training program is authorized to retain the fee as remuneration for all services performed.

Repeal

2 This regulation is repealed on March 31, 2019.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Financial Administration Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 138, section 14]
