Section 1 definition of "society" BEFORE amended by BC Reg 211/2015, effective November 28, 2016.

"society" means a society incorporated under the Society Act .

Section 2 (3) (b) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 211/2015, effective November 28, 2016.

(b) the society must not be prohibited under section 2 (2) of the Society Act from holding a community forest agreement.

Section 3 (3) (b) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 211/2015, effective November 28, 2016.

(b) the society must not be prohibited under section 2 (2) of the Society Act from holding a first nations woodland licence.

Section 4 (4) (b) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 211/2015, effective November 28, 2016.

(b) the society must not be prohibited under section 2 (2) of the Society Act from holding a replaceable forest licence.
