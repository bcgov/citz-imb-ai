Insurance Corporation Act

Permitted Cost of Services Regulation

Emission inspection centres

1 For the purpose of section 17 (1) of the Insurance Corporation Act , the permitted cost of services provided by the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia to operate emissions inspection centres anywhere in British Columbia under Division 40 of B.C. Reg. 26/58, the Motor Vehicle Act Regulations, is the entire amount of the fee it collects under Item 13 of the Schedule to B.C. Reg. 334/91, the Motor Vehicle Fees Regulation, for each vehicle emission inspection or vehicle emission reinspection.

Note: this regulation repeals B.C. Reg. 311/92.

[Provisions of the Insurance Corporation Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 228, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 17 (2)]
