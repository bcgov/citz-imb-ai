Section 1 definition of "faculty member" BEFORE amended by 2008-24-23, effective September 1, 2008 (BC Reg 220/2008).

"faculty member" means a person employed by the university as an instructor, a lecturer, an assistant professor, an associate professor, a professor, or in an equivalent position designated by the university council;

Section 1 definition of "senate" was added by 2008-24-24, effective September 1, 2008 (BC Reg 220/2008).

Section 1 definition of "university council" BEFORE amended by 2008-24-24, effective September 1, 2008 (BC Reg 220/2008).

"university council" means the university council established under section 8.

Section 2 (2) BEFORE amended by 2009-22-10, effective March 31, 2005 [retro from October 29, 2009 (Royal Assent)].

(2)  The university has the powers and capacity of a natural person of full capacity to exercise its powers and perform its duties and functions under this Act.
