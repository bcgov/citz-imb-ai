Special Accounts Appropriation and Control Act

Forfeited Crime Proceeds Fund Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Special Accounts Appropriation and Control Act .

Relevant provisions

2 (1)  For the purpose of section 7 (1) of the Act, the following provisions of the Criminal Code (Canada) are relevant provisions:

(a) section 83.14 (5);

(b) section 490.1 (1) and (2);

(c) section 490.2 (2).

(2)  For the purpose of section 7 (1) of the Act, the following provisions of the Controlled Drugs and Substances Act (Canada) are relevant provisions:

(a) section 16 (1) and (2);

(b) section 17 (2).
