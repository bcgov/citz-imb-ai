Insurance Premium Tax Act Classes of Insurance Regulation

Classes of insurance

1 For the purposes of the Insurance Premium Tax Act , "accident and sickness insurance" , "aircraft insurance" , "automobile insurance" , "hail insurance" , "life insurance" and "marine insurance" have the same meanings as in the Classes of Insurance Regulation under the Financial Institutions Act .
