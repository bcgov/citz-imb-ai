Local Government Act

Subdivision Parkland Valuation Regulation

B.C. Reg. 20/86

Section 1 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 98/2018, effective May 17, 2018.

Appraiser to determine value

1 Where an owner of land being subdivided and a local government do not agree on the market value of the land under section 941 (6), the value shall be determined by an appraiser appointed jointly by the owner and the local government.
