Section 2 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 14/2012, effective January 26, 2012.

Certification fee

2 The following are the prescribed certification fees for the purposes of section 30 (1) (b) [issuance of certificate] of the Act:

(a) $245 for an applicant who has a degree in education from a post-secondary institution established in Canada;

(b) $395 for an applicant who has a degree in education from a post-secondary institution established outside Canada;

(c) $245 for an applicant who applies for a certificate of qualification for which a degree in education from a post-secondary institution is not required by the certification standards or the independent school teaching certification standards.

Section 2.1 was enacted by BC Reg 14/2012, effective January 26, 2012.

Section 6 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 14/2012, effective January 26, 2012.

Annual practice fee

6 (1)  The following are the prescribed fees for the purposes of section 37 [annual practice fee] of the Act:

(a) the annual practice fee is $80 for a person who is issued a certificate of qualification between June 1 and November 30;

(b) the annual practice fee is $40 for a person who is issued a certificate of qualification between December 1 and May 31;

(c) the late fee under section 37 (2) of the Act is $30;

(d) the additional late fee under section 37 (3) (b) of the Act is $30.
