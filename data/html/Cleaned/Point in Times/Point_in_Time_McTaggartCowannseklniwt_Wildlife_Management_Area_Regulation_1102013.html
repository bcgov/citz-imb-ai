Wildlife Act

McTaggart-Cowan/nsƏk’łniw’t Wildlife Management Area Regulation

Designation of McTaggart-Cowan/nsƏk’łniw’t wildlife management area

1 All those parcels of Crown land, together with all that foreshore or land covered by water, under the administration of the Minister of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, situated in Similkameen Division Yale Land District and outlined in heavy black on the map with the file name mctaggart_cowan_wma6m.pdf, on file with GeoBC, Ministry of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, are designated as the McTaggart-Cowan/nsƏk’łniw’t Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing approximately 6 491 hectares.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Wildlife Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 488, section 4 (2)]
