Prohibition

3 A free miner must not register a mineral or placer claim in a reserve established by section 2.

[am. B.C. Regs. 271/2009, s. 1 (f).]

Coal land reserves

4 The coal land in each of the areas referred to in section 2 is established as a coal land reserve.
