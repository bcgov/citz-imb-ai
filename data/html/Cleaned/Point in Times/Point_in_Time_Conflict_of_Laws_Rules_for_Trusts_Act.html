Section 8 BEFORE amended by 2009-13-206, effective March 31, 2014 (BC Reg 148/2013).

Conflict with Wills Act

8 If there is a conflict between a provision of this Act and a provision of Part 3 of the Wills Act with respect to the law governing a trust created by a will or a severable aspect of such a trust, this Act prevails.
