Railway Act

Part XVI: Railway Fees Regulation

Certification and application fees

1 A person who receives an inspection, approval or other service in Column 1 of Schedule 1 must pay to the ministry the fee set out opposite in Column 2.

Annual inspection service fees

2 A person who operates a railway in a classification in Column 1 of Schedule 2 must, in each year, pay to the ministry the annual inspection service fee set out opposite in Column 2.
