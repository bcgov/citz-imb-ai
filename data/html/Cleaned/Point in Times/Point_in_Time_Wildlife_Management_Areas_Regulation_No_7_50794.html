Wildlife Act

Wildlife Management Areas Regulation No. 7

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 109/2002]

Coquitlam River Wildlife Management Area

1 The lands under the administration of the Minister of Environment described as:

Reserve No. 85293 covering parts of Lots 23 and 168, Group 1, New Westminster District, Plan 66109, and surveyed, vacant Crown Land described as District Lot 6429, Group 1, New Westminster Land District

are designated the Coquitlam River Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing 16.7 hectares, more or less.

[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]
