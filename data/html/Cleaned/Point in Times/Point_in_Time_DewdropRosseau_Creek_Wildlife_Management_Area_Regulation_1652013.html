Wildlife Act

Dewdrop-Rosseau Creek Wildlife Management Area Regulation

Designation of Dewdrop-Rosseau Creek wildlife management area

1 All those parcels of Crown land, together with all that foreshore or land covered by water, under the administration of the Minister of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, situated in Kamloops Division Yale Land District and outlined in heavy black on the map with the file name dewdrop-rosseau_creek_wma4m.pdf, on file with GeoBC, Ministry of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, except

(a) CN Right of Way Plan 1LOC26 and 5RY218, and

(b) a 30 metre right of way centred on Frederick Road, are designated as the Dewdrop-Rosseau Creek Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing approximately 5616 hectares.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Wildlife Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 488, section 4 (2)]
