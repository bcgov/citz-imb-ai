Criminal Asset Management Regulation

B.C. Reg. 275/2012

Schedule, section 8 A. and C. BEFORE amended by BC Reg 99/2018, effective May 18, 2018.

A. The information contained in this application or submitted in support of this application is true and if there is any change to the information after I have submitted this application I will contact the Director of Criminal Asset Management, Ministry of Justice, immediately.

C. I am aware that false or incomplete information or failure to notify the Director of Criminal Asset Management, Ministry of Justice, of any change in the information contained in this application or submitted in support of this application may result in the denial of the claim or liability for repayment.
