Section 2 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 352/2012, effective March 18, 2013.

Spouses

2 For the purposes of the Act, persons living and cohabiting with another person in a marriage-like relationship, including a marriage-like relationship between persons of the same gender, constitute a class of unmarried persons prescribed as spouses.
