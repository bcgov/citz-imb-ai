Section 4 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 330/2012, effective December 1, 2012.

Designated enactments

4 The following are designated enactments for the purposes of the Act:

(a) the Business Corporations Act and the regulations made under that Act;

(b) the Cooperative Association Act and the regulations made under that Act;

(c) the Hotel Room Tax Act and the regulations made under that Act;

(d) the Partnership Act and the regulations made under that Act;

(e) the Social Service Tax Act and the regulations made under that Act;

(f) the Society Act and the regulations made under that Act;

(g) the Tobacco Tax Act and the regulations made under that Act;

(h) the Workers Compensation Act and the regulations made under that Act;

(i) the Liquor Control and Licensing Act and the regulations made under that Act;

(j) the Liquor Distribution Act and the regulations made under that Act.

(k) the Consumption Tax Rebate and Transition Act and the regulations made under that Act.
