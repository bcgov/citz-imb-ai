Farming and Fishing Industries Development Act

Raspberry Industry Development Council Regulation

B.C. Reg. 484/98

Section 3 (2.1) BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 55/2023, effective March 1, 2023.

(2.1) On or before June 1, 1999, each first receiver must remit to the council the amount collected by the first receiver during the period January 1, 1998, to September 30, 1998, less any commissions retained under subsection (3), from the proceeds otherwise due to the raspberry grower for raspberries delivered during that period to the first receiver.

Section 3 (3) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 55/2023, effective March 1, 2023.

(3) A first receiver may retain a commission of 5% of the levy collected under subsection (1) or (2.1).
