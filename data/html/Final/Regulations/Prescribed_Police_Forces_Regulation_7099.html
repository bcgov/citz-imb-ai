Police Act

Prescribed Police Forces Regulation

[Last amended December 31, 2022 by B.C. Reg. 231/2022]

Prescribed police forces

1 The following are prescribed as police forces in British Columbia:

(a) Organized Crime Agency of British Columbia;

(b) Stl'atl'imx Tribal Police Service;

(c) South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Police Service Designated Policing Unit.
