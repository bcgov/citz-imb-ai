Creditor Assistance Act

Creditor Assistance Fee Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 536/95]

Definitions

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Creditor Assistance Act .

Fees

2 A fee of $30 must be paid to the district registrar

(a) for the filing of an affidavit of claim under section 8 (1) of the Act, or

(b) for the issue or renewal of a certificate.

[am. B.C. Reg. 536/95, s. 1.]
