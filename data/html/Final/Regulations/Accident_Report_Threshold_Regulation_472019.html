Motor Vehicle Act

Accident Report Threshold Regulation

Prescribed amount

1 The prescribed amount for the purposes of completing a written report under section 249 of the Motor Vehicle Act is,

(a) in the case of a vehicle, $10 000, and

(b) in the case of a cycle, $10 000.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Motor Vehicle Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 318, ss. 210 (11) and 249]
