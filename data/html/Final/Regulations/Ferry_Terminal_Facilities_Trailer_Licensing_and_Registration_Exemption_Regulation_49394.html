Motor Vehicle Act

Ferry Terminal Facilities Trailer
Licensing and Registration
Exemption Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 161/2007, June 1, 2007]

Power of corporation to exempt

1 (1) The Insurance Corporation of British Columbia is empowered to exempt trailers operated by British Columbia Ferry Services Inc. from the licensing and registration requirements of the Motor Vehicle Act .

(2) An exemption under subsection (1) may be given for a trailer only if the trailer

(a) has at least the minimum third party liability insurance coverage required under the Insurance (Vehicle) Act ,

(b) is operated only in and around a ferry terminal facility, and

(c) is operated in accordance with any other conditions that may be specified by the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia.

[am. B.C. Regs. 316/96; 140/2006; 161/2007, s. 3.]
