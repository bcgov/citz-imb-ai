Environmental Assessment Act

Required Consent (Eskay Creek Project) Regulation

Reviewable project agreement prescribed

1 For the purposes of section 7 (b) (ii) [required consent] of the Environmental Assessment Act , the Declaration Act Consent Decision-Making Agreement for Eskay Creek Project, negotiated and entered into under section 7 (1) (b) of the Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples Act and dated June 6, 2022, is prescribed.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Environmental Assessment Act , S.B.C. 2018, c. 51, s. 77 (2).]
