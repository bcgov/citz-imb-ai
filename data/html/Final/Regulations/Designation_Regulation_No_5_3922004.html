Evidence Act

Designation Regulation No. 5

Conservation Officer Service members

1 Members of the Conservation Officer Service described in section 106 (2) of the Environmental Management Act are designated commissioners for taking affidavits for British Columbia for the purpose of exercising the powers and performing the duties of their office.

[Provisions of the Evidence Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 124, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 60 (1)]
