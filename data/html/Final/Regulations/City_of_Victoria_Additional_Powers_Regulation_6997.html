Applicant may appeal

4 If an applicant for a development permit within an intensive residential development area is refused a development permit by the planning director or delegate responsible for issuing development permits or the applicant objects to a proposed provision of the development permit, the applicant may appeal the decision to council.

[am. B.C. Reg. 214/2009, s. (b).]

City may make different regulations

6 The City of Victoria may, under section 479 (1) of the Local Government Act , make regulations that are different, as specified in a bylaw, for different structures depending on the age of the structure.

[am. B.C. Reg. 118/2018, s. 2.]
