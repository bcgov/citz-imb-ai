Teachers Act

British Columbia Teachers' Council
Vacancy Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Teachers Act .

Appointment to fill vacancy on British Columbia Teachers' Council

2 For the purposes of section 11 (10) of the Act,

(a) before appointing a person to fill a vacancy, the minister must consult with the First Nations Education Authority, and

(b) a person must meet both of the following eligibility requirements to be appointed:

(i) the person must possess substantial knowledge of and experience in the First Nations education system in British Columbia;

(ii) the person must provide to the minister the person's written consent to be appointed.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Teachers Act , S.B.C. 2011, c. 19, s. 83 (2) (b.1).]
