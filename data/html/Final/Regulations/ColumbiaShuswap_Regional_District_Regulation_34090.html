Local Government Act

Columbia-Shuswap Regional District Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 189/2008, June 27, 2008]

Enhanced 9-1-1 Emergency Response System

1 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to operate an Enhanced 9-1-1 Emergency Response System as an extended service.

Economic Development Contribution

2 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to provide Economic Development Contribution as an extended service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 433/90.]

Emergency Programs

3 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to provide Emergency Programs as an extended service under the Emergency Program Act .

[en. B.C. Reg. 492/90.]

Flood protection

4 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to provide flood protection, including without limitation the powers of a district municipality under sections 552 and 599 of the Municipal Act 1 , 2 , as a local service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 74/91.]

Salmon Arm Airport

5 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to operate and maintain the Salmon Arm Airport as an extended service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 132/91.]

Transit

6 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to provide Transit as a local service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 219/92.]

Financial aid toward Golden Airport

7 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to contribute financial aid toward the cost of operation of the Golden Airport, as an extended service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 49/93.]

Financial aid toward Golden Cemetery

8 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to contribute financial aid toward the cost of the Golden Cemetery, as an extended service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 113/93.]

Agreement to form part of regional library district

9 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power of a municipality to enter into an agreement with the board of management of a library district to form part of a regional library district under Part 4 of the Library Act , as a local service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 310/93.]

Removal of dangerous buildings and other structures

10 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to exercise the powers conferred on the council of a municipality under section 727 of the Local Government Act .

[en. B.C. Reg. 295/2003.]

Firecrackers and fireworks

11 The Columbia-Shuswap Regional District is granted the additional power to, by bylaw, regulate, prohibit and impose requirements in relation to the use of firecrackers and fireworks.

[en. B.C. Reg. 189/2008.]
