Motor Vehicle Act

Approved Screening Device Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Motor Vehicle Act .

Approved screening devices

2 For the purposes of sections 215 and 215.41 (2) of the Act, the following devices are prescribed:

(a) Alcolmeter S-L2;

(b) Alco-Sensor IV DWF;

(c) Alco-Sensor FST.
