Sheriff Act

Fee Regulation

Fee for document service

1 The fee payable by a municipality to a sheriff for service of a document issued under a municipal bylaw is $10.

[Provisions of the Sheriff Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 425, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 11]
