Interpretation Act

Treaty Lands Regulation

Interpretation

1 Words and expressions used in this regulation in relation to a specific treaty first nation and defined in the final agreement of that treaty first nation have the same meaning as in that final agreement unless the context requires otherwise.

Definition of "treaty lands"

2 For the purposes of section 29.1 of the Interpretation Act , the treaty lands of a treaty first nation identified in Column 1 of the following table are the lands set out opposite in Column 2, as added to or subtracted from in accordance with the final agreement of the treaty first nation:
