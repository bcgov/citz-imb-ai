South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act

Maximum Ticketed Amount Regulation

Definitions

1 In this regulation:

"Act" means the South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act ;

"ticket" means a ticket issued under section 248 of the Act.

Maximum fine

2 For the purposes of section 245 (5) of the Act, the maximum ticketed amount payable in relation to a ticket for an infraction is $1 000.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: South Coast British Columbia Transportation Authority Act , S.B.C. 1998, c. 30, sections 16, 46, 245 and 263]
