Emergency Health Services Act

Emergency Medical Assistants
Licensing Board Regulation

Licensing board member

1 For the purpose of section 6 (2) of the Emergency Health Services Act , the emergency medical assistant to be appointed to the licensing board must be selected by the bargaining agent, as defined in section 1 of the Labour Relations Code , for emergency medical assistants.
