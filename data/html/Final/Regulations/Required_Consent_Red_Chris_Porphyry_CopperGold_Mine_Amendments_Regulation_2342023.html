Environmental Assessment Act

Required Consent (Red Chris Porphyry
Copper-Gold Mine Amendments) Regulation

Reviewable project agreement prescribed

1 For the purposes of section 7 (b) (ii) [required consent] of the Environmental Assessment Act , the Declaration Act Consent Decision-Making Agreement for Red Chris Porphyry Copper-Gold Mine Project, negotiated and entered into under section 7 (1) (b) of the Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples Act and dated November 1, 2023, is prescribed.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Environmental Assessment Act , S.B.C. 2018, c. 51, s. 7.]
