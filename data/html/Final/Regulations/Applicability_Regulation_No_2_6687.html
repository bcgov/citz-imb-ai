Hydro and Power Authority Act

Applicability Regulation No. 2

Provisions of Financial Administration Act applicable to authority

1 Paragraph (a) of the definition of "government corporation" in section 1 (1) of the Financial Administration Act and section 75 of that Act are made applicable to the British Columbia Hydro and Power Authority.

[Provisions of the Hydro and Power Authority Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 212, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 32 (6)]
