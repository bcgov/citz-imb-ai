Continuing Care Act

Continuing Care Programs Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 83/2019, April 15, 2019]

Programs prescribed

1 The programs named in the Schedule to this regulation are prescribed as continuing care programs under section 3 of the Continuing Care Act .

Schedule

[en. B.C. Reg. 83/2019, App. 2.]

1 The following programs administered by the ministry of the minister are prescribed as continuing care:

(a) Home Support Services;

(b) Adult Day Services;

(c) Meals Programs;

(d) Respite Services;

(e) Care Management;

(f) Long-Term Care Services;

(g) Palliative and End-of-Life Care Services;

(h) Home Oxygen Program;

(i) Assisted Living Services;

(j) Community Nursing Services;

(k) Community Rehabilitation Services.

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Continuing Care Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 70, s. 3]
