B.C. Rail Benefits (First Nations) Trust Act

Beneficiary First Nations Regulation

Beneficiary First Nations

1 The following are prescribed as Beneficiary First Nations:

(a) the Gitxsan Treaty Society;

(b) the following bands within the meaning of the Indian Act (Canada):

(i) Bridge River;

(ii) Canim Lake;

(iii) Fort Nelson First Nation;

(iv) Lheidli T'enneh;

(v) McLeod Lake;

(vi) Mount Currie;

(vii) Nak'azdli;

(viii) N'Quatqua;

(ix) Red Bluff;

(x) Saulteau First Nations;

(xi) Seton Lake;

(xii) Soda Creek;

(xiii) Squamish;

(xiv) T'it'q'et;

(xv) Takla Lake First Nation;

(xvi) Tl'azt'en Nation;

(xvii) Tsawwassen First Nation;

(xviii) West Moberly First Nations;

(xix) Williams Lake.
