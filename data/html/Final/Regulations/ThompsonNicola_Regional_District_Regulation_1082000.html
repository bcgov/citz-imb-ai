Local Government Act

Thompson-Nicola Regional District Regulation

Power under the Emergency Service Act

1 The Thompson-Nicola Regional District is granted as an extended service the same power that a municipality has as a local authority under the Emergency Program Act for such electoral area or areas the regional district may designate.

[Provisions of the Local Government Act , R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 323, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 799]
