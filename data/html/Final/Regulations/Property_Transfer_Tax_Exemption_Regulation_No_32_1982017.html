Property Transfer Tax Act

Property Transfer Tax Exemption
Regulation No. 32

Exemption

1 The transfer to Uchucklesaht Tribe of the property described below on March 28, 2017, is exempt from tax payable under the Property Transfer Tax Act :

District Lot 2239, Clayoquot District, PID 030-104-203.

Retroactive effect

2 This regulation is retroactive to March 28, 2017, with effect on and after that date.
