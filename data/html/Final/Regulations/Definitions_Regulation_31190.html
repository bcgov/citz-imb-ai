Definitions Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 211/2012, November 1, 2012]

Definition of "officer"

1 In the Credit Union Incorporation Act , the Financial Institutions Act and the Business Corporations Act , as it applies to a financial institution, "officer" of a corporation includes a credit officer, an employee of the corporation who has the authority to approve loans and an employee of the corporation who reports directly to the directors.

[en. B.C. Reg. 109/92; am. B.C. Reg. 211/2012.]

Definition of "carry on deposit business"

2 In the Financial Institutions Act "carry on deposit business" does not include raise money through the issue of security instruments for the purpose of business other than deposit business.

[en. B.C. Reg. 390/90.]
