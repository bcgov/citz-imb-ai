Environmental Assessment Act

Exemption Regulation (No. 2)

[Last amended January 8, 2021 by B.C. Reg. 2/2021]

Exemption from limitation on extension of certificate

1 Section 31 (4) (a) of the Environmental Assessment Act , as it limits the number of times that a deadline specified in an environmental assessment certificate may be extended, does not apply in relation to the reviewable project described in Environmental Assessment Certificate # M09-02 if the deadline is not extended beyond January 14, 2022.

[am. B.C. Reg. 2/2021.]

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Environmental Assessment Act , S.B.C. 2018, c. 51, s. 77.]
