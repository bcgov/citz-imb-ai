Local Government Act

Fraser Valley Regional District
Additional Power to Contract Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 118/2018, June 15, 2018]

Additional power to contract

1 The Fraser Valley Regional District is granted, to the extent permitted under section 296 of the Local Government Act , the power to enter into agreements for the acceptance, treatment and disposal of sewage to preserve trans-boundary ground water quality within the watershed of the Sumas River.

[am. B.C. Reg. 118/2018, s. 3.]
