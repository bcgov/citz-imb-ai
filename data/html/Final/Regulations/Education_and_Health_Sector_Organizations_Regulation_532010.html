Budget Transparency and Accountability Act

Education and Health Sector Organizations Regulation

[Last amended February 14, 2022 by B.C. Reg. 22/2022]

Included organizations

1 The following organizations are included in the definition of "education and health sector organization" in section 1 (1) of the Budget Transparency and Accountability Act :

(a) Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 17/2016, s. (a).]

(b) British Columbia Health Services Purchasing Organization;

(c) BC Clinical and Support Services Society;

(d) British Columbia Emergency Health Services;

(e) Provincial Health Services Authority;

(f) BC Health Care Occupational Health and Safety Society.

[am. B.C. Regs. 23/2012, s. (c); 145/2013, App. 2, s. 4; 17/2016; 22/2022.]

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Budget Transparency and Accountability Act , S.B.C. 2000, c. 23, s. 24.]
