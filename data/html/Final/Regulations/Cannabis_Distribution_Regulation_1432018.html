Cannabis Distribution Act

Cannabis Distribution Regulation

Definition

1 In this regulation, "Act" means the Cannabis Distribution Act .

Disclosure of cannabis records and information

2 For the purposes of section 14 (2) (a) of the Act, a cannabis official may disclose cannabis records and information as follows:

(a) for the purposes of the administration or enforcement of the Act, the Cannabis Control and Licensing Act , the Liquor Control and Licensing Act , the Liquor Distribution Act , the regulations under any of those Acts or an enactment of British Columbia or Canada relating to taxation;

(b) for the purposes of the administration of the Cannabis Act (Canada);

(c) in a proceeding before a court or a judicial or quasi-judicial tribunal;

(d) if an enactment of British Columbia or Canada requires or authorizes the disclosure;

(e) to the legal counsel of the person in possession of the cannabis records and information;

(f) to a law enforcement agency;

(g) for the purpose of selling non-confidential sales data;

(h) for the purpose of selling to a person sales data relating to the personâ€™s sales of cannabis;

(i) for the purpose of the compilation of statistical information by the government or the government of Canada.

Cannabis records and information supplied to the government in confidence

3 For the purpose of section 21 (1) (b) of the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act , the following classes of cannabis records and information, whether or not supplied to the government, are deemed to be supplied to the government in confidence:

(a) cannabis records and information related to the acquisition by the government of cannabis from a federal producer or other person who supplies cannabis to the government;

(b) cannabis records and information related to the acquisition by the government of cannabis accessories from a person who supplies cannabis accessories to the government;

(c) cannabis records and information related to the sale of cannabis or cannabis accessories acquired by the government from a person referred to in paragraph (a) or (b).

[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation: Cannabis Distribution Act , S.B.C. 2018, c. 28, ss. 21 and 23]
