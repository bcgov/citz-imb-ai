Property Transfer Tax Act




Property Transfer Tax

Exemption Regulation No. 5








Transactions exempted





1



For the purpose of implementing Replotting Scheme No. 101 of the Corporation of the District of North Vancouver






(a)


the transfers of land legally described under the heading "Existing Parcels" in the attached Schedule from the owners of the land to the corporation of the District of North Vancouver, and






(b)


the transfers of land legally described under the heading "New Parcels" in the attached Schedule from the Corporation of the District of North Vancouver to the owners of the land


are taxable transactions exempted under the


Property Transfer Tax Act


from tax.
