Forest Act




BC Timber Sales Account Regulation








Payment out of BC Timber Sales Account





1



For the purposes of section 109 (3) (e) of the


Forest          Act


, money in the BC Timber Sales Account may be expended for a
purpose related to an agreement, between the government and a holder of a licence or
agreement referred to in section 61 (1) of the


Forest          Act


, respecting a reduction under that section of the allowable annual
cut authorized in or for the licence or agreement to make timber available for disposition
under






(a)


a timber sales licence entered into under section 20 of the


Forest              Act


,






(b)


a forestry licence to cut entered into under section 47.6 (3) of the


Forest              Act


, or






(c)


a road permit granted for a licence referred to in paragraph (a) or (b) to the licence
holder,


to persons other than the holder of the licence or agreement referred to in section
61 (1) of the


Forest Act


.

(1)]
