Financial Institutions Act




Credit Union Deposit Insurance Corporation

Prescribed Investments    Regulation








Definitions





1



In this regulation:



"Act"

means the


Financial Institutions              Act


;



"pension plan"

has the same meaning as in the


Pension Benefits              Standards Act


.








Prescribed investments





2



For the purposes of section 272 (1) (d) of the Act but subject to section 272 (2) of
the Act, the deposit insurance corporation may make any investment that a pension plan is
capable of making under the


Pension Benefits Standards        Act


.
