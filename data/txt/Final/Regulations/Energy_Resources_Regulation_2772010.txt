Utilities Commission Act




Energy Resources Regulation








Definition





1



In this regulation,

"Act"

means the


Utilities Commission Act


.








Prescribed energy resources





2



The following types of energy resources are prescribed for the purposes of section 65 (2) (a) (ii) of the Act:






(a)


petroleum, as defined in section 1 of the


Petroleum and Natural Gas Act


;






(b)


liquid hydrocarbons;






(c)


petroleum products.
