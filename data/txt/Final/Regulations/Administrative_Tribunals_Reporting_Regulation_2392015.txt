Administrative Tribunals Act


Administrative Tribunals Reporting Regulation





Definitions




1


In this regulation:


"Act"
means the

Administrative Tribunals Act

;


"tribunal"
means a tribunal to which section 59.2
[reporting]
of the Act applies.






Reporting year




2

(1)


A tribunal may set one of the following as the date on which the
tribunal's reporting year begins:




(a)

January 1;




(b)

April 1.




(2)

A tribunal's reporting year ends at the end of the date before the
date set under subsection (1).




(3)

If a tribunal does not set a date under subsection (1), the tribunal's
reporting year begins on April 1.






When reports are due




3


A tribunal must submit, in writing, the information required by
section 59.2 of the Act to the minister responsible for the tribunal,
as soon as practicable after the end of the tribunal's reporting year.
