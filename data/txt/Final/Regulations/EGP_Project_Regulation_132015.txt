Utilities Commission Act




EGP Project Regulation








Definitions





1



In this regulation:



"Act"

means the


Utilities Commission Act


;



"EGP project"

has the same meaning as in Direction No. 5 to the British Columbia Utilities Commission.








Exclusion





2



The EGP project is excluded from the operation of section 45 (1) of the Act.
