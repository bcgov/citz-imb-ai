Financial Administration Act




Fiscal Agent for Universities Regulation








Definitions





1



In this regulation:



"minister"

means the Minister of Finance;



"university"

means a university as defined in the


College              and Institute Act


that is a government corporation as defined in
the


Financial Administration Act


.








Fiscal agent of a university





2



(1)


The minister is appointed the fiscal agent of each university.






(2)


With respect to the borrowing of money under the


University            Act


by a university, the minister, as fiscal agent of the
university, on the terms agreed to between that university and the minister,
may






(a)


provide advice or other assistance to the university, and






(b)


make agreements or other arrangements on behalf of the university.






(3)


The power and capacity described in subsection (2) of the minister as
fiscal agent is in addition to the other powers and capacities the minister
has.



[Provisions of the


Financial Administration          Act


, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 138, relevant to the enactment of this
regulation: section 75 (1) (b) and (c)]
