Forest Act




Annual Rent Regulation



[Last amended July 11, 2022 by B.C. Reg. 163/2022]









Definitions





1



In this regulation:



"Act"

means the


Forest Act


;



"reduced volume condition"

means a condition on a licence that, under section 14 (1) (g.2) of the Act, reduces a portion of the allowable annual cut available to the holder of the licence;



"supplemental forest licence"

has the same meaning as in section 14.2 (1) of the Act.


[en. B.C. Reg. 190/2014, Sch. 2, s. 1; am. B.C. Reg. 163/2022, s. (a).]
