Financial Institutions Act


Insurance Commission Exemption Regulation


[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 22/2013, January 30, 2013]





Interpretation




1


In this regulation
"Act"
means the

Financial Institutions Act

.






Exemption from section 178 (3)




2


A person described in section 2 (1) (b.1), (c), (g) and (i) and (2) of
the Insurance Licensing Exemptions Regulation, B.C. Reg. 328/90, while
acting only as described in those provisions, is a person prescribed
for the purposes of section 178 (3) of the Act.

[en. B.C. Reg. 22/2013, App. 2, s. 1.]
