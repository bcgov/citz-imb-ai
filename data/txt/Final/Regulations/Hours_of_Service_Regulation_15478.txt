Public Service Act


Hours of Service Regulation





Hours of service




1


The normal hours of service in Provincial Government offices are from
8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Monday to Friday, inclusive, and all
Provincial Government offices shall be open to the public continually
during those hours.
