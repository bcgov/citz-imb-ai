Resort Municipality of Whistler Act


Application Modification Regulation No. 1





Applicability of
Municipal Act
provision




1


The application of section 605 (1) of the

Municipal Act

to the municipality is modified to the extent that, instead of
obtaining the consent of the electors to the disposal of a gas
distribution system, the municipality may, before the disposal, obtain
the consent of at least 75% of the current customers of the gas
distribution system.
