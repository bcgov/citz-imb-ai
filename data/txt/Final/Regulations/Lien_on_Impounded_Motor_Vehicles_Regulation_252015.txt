Definitions




1


In this regulation:


"Act"
means the

Motor Vehicle Act

;



"claimed vehicle"

means a motor vehicle impounded under the Act and released to the
owner of the motor vehicle or a person authorized by the owner;



"unclaimed vehicle"

means a motor vehicle impounded under the Act and disposed of under
the Act or the

Warehouse Lien Act

.

Lien on impounded vehicle




3


The following fees, costs, charges and surcharges are prescribed for
the purposes of section 255 (2) (a)
[storage of impounded motor vehicle]
of the Act:




(a)

for storage,




(i)

$23.98 per day for impound lots in Zone 1,




(ii)

$22.89 per day for impound lots in Zone 2,




(iii)

$20.71 per day for impound lots in Zone 3, and




(iv)

$16.35 per day for impound lots in Zone 4;




(b)

for towing, the towing cost determined under section 4;




(c)

for administration,




(i)

$55.05 for a claimed vehicle, and




(ii)

$70.09 for an unclaimed vehicle.




(d)

Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 248/2021.]


[am. B.C. Regs. 26/2016, ss. 1 to 3; 91/2020; 125/2021, s. (b);
248/2021; 180/2022, s. 1.]






Towing cost




4

(1)


For the purposes of section 3 (b), the towing cost is the sum of the
following:




(a)

the basic towing cost determined in accordance with subsection (2) of
this section;




(b)

a fuel surcharge equal to 28% of the basic towing cost;




(c)

$35 if dollies are used.




(2)

The basic towing cost for a vehicle with a gross vehicle weight within
a range described in column 2, 3, 4 or 5 of the following table is the
cost set out in or determined in accordance with that column, opposite
the applicable towing distance described in column 1.




(3)

In determining an amount in accordance with columns 2 to 5 in item 2
of the table, the towing distance is to be expressed to the nearest
tenth of a kilometre.
