Utilities Commission Act




Community-Based Biomass Call Exemption Regulation








Definitions





1



In this regulation:



"Act"

means the


Utilities Commission Act


;



"project proponent"

means a person who enters into an energy supply contract with the authority as a result of the call referred to in section 2, or the successors or assigns of the person.








Exemption





2



The authority and all project proponents are exempt from section 71 of the Act with respect to the community-based biomass call for electricity that the authority is engaged in on the date this regulation comes into force.
