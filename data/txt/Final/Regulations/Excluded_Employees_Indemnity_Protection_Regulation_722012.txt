O.C. 209/2012


Deposited March 30, 2012






This consolidation is current to March 5, 2024.


Public Service Act



Excluded Employees Indemnity

Protection Regulation






Definition




1


In this regulation,
"excluded employee"
means




(a)

a person employed or appointed under the

Public Service Act

who is excluded from collective bargaining, and




(b)

a former excluded employee within the meaning of paragraph (a).






Indemnity protection




2


It is a term and condition of employment for excluded employees that
the government provide indemnity protection, in accordance with the
Excluded Employees (Legal Proceedings) Indemnity Regulation, in
respect of conduct that occurs in the performance of employment within
the meaning of that regulation.
