Local Government Act


Sunshine Coast Regional District Regulation

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 268/2001]




Commercial or industrial land development




1


The Sunshine Coast Regional District is granted the power to develop,
as an extended service, on its own behalf or as an agent of the
Province of British Columbia, land owned by the regional district or
Crown land, for use as a commercial or industrial development.






Central Dispatch Emergency Telephone Service




2


The Sunshine Coast Regional District is granted the additional power
to provide Central Dispatch Emergency Telephone Service as an extended
service.

[en. B.C. Reg. 319/91.]





Road improvement capital financing




3


The Sunshine Coast Regional District is granted the additional power
to provide, as a local service, capital financing and construction for
road improvements on Woodland and Chamberlain Roads.

[en. B.C. Reg. 318/95.]





Emergency Program Act




4


The Sunshine Coast Regional District is granted the same power that a
municipality has under the

Emergency Program Act

.

[en. B.C. Reg. 268/2001.]
