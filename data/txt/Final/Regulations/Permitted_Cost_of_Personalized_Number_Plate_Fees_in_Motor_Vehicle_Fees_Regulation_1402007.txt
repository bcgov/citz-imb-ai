Insurance Corporation Act




Permitted Cost of Personalized Number Plate

Fees in Motor Vehicle Fees Regulation








Fee under B.C. Reg. 334/91





1



For the purposes of section 17 (1) of the


Insurance Corporation Act


, the corporation's permitted costs of its services in the Schedule to the Motor Vehicle Fees Regulation, B.C. Reg. 334/91, are the following:






(a)


the entire amount of the personalized number plate fee the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia collects under item 9 (a) for any motor vehicle licensed under section 6 of the


Commercial Transport Act


and that has a licensed gross vehicle weight exceeding 4 500 kg;






(b)


the entire amount of the personalized number plate renewal fee the Insurance Corporation of British Columbia collects under item 9 (b) for any motor vehicle licensed under section 6 of the


Commercial Transport Act


and that has a licensed gross vehicle weight exceeding 4 500 kg.



[Provisions of the


Insurance Corporation Act


, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 228, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 17 (2)]
