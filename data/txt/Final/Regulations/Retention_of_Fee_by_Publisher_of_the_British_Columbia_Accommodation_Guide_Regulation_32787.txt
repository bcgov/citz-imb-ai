Financial Administration Act




Retention of Fee by Publisher of the British Columbia

Accommodation Guide    Regulation



[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 226/92]







Retention of fee





1



Where the person who has contracted with the Province to publish the British
Columbia Accommodation Guide has, on behalf of the Province, collected the registration fee
of $75 paid to register tourist accommodation pursuant to the Tourist Accommodation
Regulation enacted under the


Travel Regulation Act


, that person is        authorized to retain that registration fee, as remuneration for services
performed.
