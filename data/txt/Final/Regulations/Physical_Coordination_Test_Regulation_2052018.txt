Motor Vehicle Act




Physical Coordination Test Regulation








Definition





1



In this regulation,

"Act"

means the


Motor Vehicle Act


.








Prescribed physical coordination test





2



For the purposes of section 215 (8) (a) and (8.1) (a) of the Act, a prescribed physical coordination test consists of the following standard field sobriety tests, if administered together:






(a)


the horizontal gaze nystagmus test;






(b)


the walk-and-turn test;






(c)


the one-leg stand test.
