Community Charter




Regional District of Kootenay Boundary

Sanitary Sewage Dispute    Regulation








Prescribed matter





1



(1)


In this section,

"Regional District of Kootenay Boundary Sanitary Sewage
Dispute"

means a dispute between The Corporation of the City of Rossland, the
City of Trail and The Corporation of the Village of Warfield in the Regional District of
Kootenay Boundary in respect of interception, treatment and disposal of sanitary sewage
under Supplementary Letters Patent dated April 22, 1969.






(2)


For purposes of section 286 of the


Community              Charter


, the Regional District of Kootenay Boundary Sanitary
Sewage Dispute is a prescribed matter.
