Definition




1


In this regulation,
"Act"
means the

Teachers Act

.






Certification fee




2

(1)


The following are the prescribed fees for the purposes of section 30
(1) (b)
[issuance of certificate]
of the Act that must accompany an applicant's application for a
certificate of qualification or an independent school teaching
certificate:




(a)

$245 for an applicant who has a degree in education from a
post-secondary institution established in Canada;




(b)

$395 for an applicant who has a degree in education from a
post-secondary institution established outside Canada;




(c)

$245 for an applicant who applies for a certificate of qualification
or an independent school teaching certificate for which a degree in
education from a post-secondary institution is not required by the
certification standards or the independent school teaching
certification standards.




(2)

The following are the prescribed fees for the purposes of section 30
(1) (b) of the Act that an applicant must pay when the director of
certification issues a certificate of qualification or an independent
school teaching certificate:




(a)

$40 if the certificate of qualification or independent school teaching
certificate is issued on or after December 1 and on or before
May 31;




(b)

$80 if the certificate of qualification or independent school teaching
certificate is issued on or after June 1 and on or before
November 30.

[am. B.C. Reg. 14/2012, s. 1.]

Reconsideration fee




3


The prescribed reconsideration fee for the purposes of section 32 (3)
(b)
[reconsideration and appeal]
of the Act is $140.






Certification appeal fee




4


The certification appeal fee for the purposes of Division 5
[Certification Appeals]
of Part 6

[Disciplinary and Professional Conduct Inquiries and Certification
Appeals]

of the Act is $300.






Letter of permission fee




5


The prescribed letter of permission fee for the purposes of section 35
(1) (a)
[issuance of letter of permission]
of the Act is $200.






Annual practice fee




6


The following are the prescribed fees for the purposes of section 37
[annual practice fee]
of the Act:




(a)

the annual practice fee is $80;




(b)

the late fee under section 37 (2) of the Act is $30;




(c)

the additional late fee under section 37 (3) (b) of the Act
is $30.

[en. B.C. Reg. 14/2012, s. 3.]





Teacher education programs




7


The fee for determining if a teacher education program meets the
teacher education program approval standards is $12 000.
