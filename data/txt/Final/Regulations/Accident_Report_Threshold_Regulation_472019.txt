Motor Vehicle Act




Accident Report Threshold Regulation








Prescribed amount





1



The prescribed amount for the purposes of completing a written report under section 249 of the


Motor Vehicle Act


is,






(a)


in the case of a vehicle, $10 000, and






(b)


in the case of a cycle, $10 000.
