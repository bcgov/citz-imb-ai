Taxation (Rural Area) Act




Taxation (Rural Area)

Exemption Regulation








Waneta Dam power project partial exemption





1



(1)


Each of the following parcels of land, owned in part by the British Columbia Hydro
and Power Authority as a tenant in common, and the improvements on them are exempt from
taxation to the extent of the product obtained by multiplying their assessed value by the
fraction representing the fractional registered interest of the Authority in the
parcel:




Parcel Identifier: 016-344-502


Sublot 1, Township 7A, Kootenay District, Plan X66, Except Part Included in Plan
11116;


Parcel Identifier: 016-344-511


Sublot 2, Township 7A, Kootenay District, Plan X66;


Parcel Identifier: 027-434-915


Lot A, Township 7A, Kootenay District, Plan NEP86182, Except Plan
EPP9510;


Parcel Identifier: 027-434-923


Lot B, Township 7A, Kootenay District, Plan NEP86182, Except Plan
EPP9508.






(2)


This section is retroactive to the extent necessary to apply for the purposes of
the 2011 and 2012 taxation years.
