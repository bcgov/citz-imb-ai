Local Government Act



Regional District of

Kootenay Boundary Regulation


[Last amended June 20, 2022 by B.C. Reg. 141/2022]




Transit




1


The Regional District of Kootenay Boundary is granted the additional
power to provide transit as a local service.






Emergency Programs




2


The Regional District of Kootenay Boundary is granted the additional
power to provide Emergency Programs as an extended service under the

Emergency Program Act

.

[en. B.C. Reg. 148/99.]





Firecrackers and fireworks




3


The Regional District of Kootenay Boundary is granted the additional
power to, by bylaw, regulate, prohibit and impose requirements in
relation to the use of firecrackers and fireworks.

[en. B.C. Reg. 141/2022.]
