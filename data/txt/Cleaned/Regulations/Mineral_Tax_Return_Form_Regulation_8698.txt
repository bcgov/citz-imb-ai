Mineral Tax Act




Mineral Tax Return Form Regulation



[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 188/2015, December 1, 2015]







Form of returns





1



(1)


A return delivered by an operator of a mine to the commissioner under section 12 (1) of the


Mineral Tax Act


must be in the form set out in Schedule A.






(2)


A return delivered by an operator of a placer gold mine to the commissioner under section 12 (1.1) of the


Mineral Tax Act


must be in the form set out in Schedule C.






(3)


A return delivered by a quarry operator to the commissioner under section 12 (1.2) of the


Mineral Tax Act


must be in the form set out in Schedule D.






(4)


A return delivered for an operator's exploration account under section 12 (7) of the


Mineral Tax Act


must be in the form set out in Schedule E.
