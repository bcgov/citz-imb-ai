Utilities Commission Act




Nicola Lake Solar Project Exemption Regulation








Definitions





1



In this regulation:



"Act"

means the


Utilities Commission Act


;



"parties"

means






(a)


the Okanagan Nation Alliance and its successors and assigns, and






(b)


the Upper Nicola Band and its successors and assigns.








Exemption





2



The authority and the parties are exempt from section 71 of the Act in respect of an energy supply contract that establishes the terms and conditions under which electricity generated at a solar generation facility with a plant capacity output that does not exceed 15 megawatts, located at Nicola Lake on the Nicola LakeÂ 1 Indian Reserve, is sold to the authority.
