Railway Safety Act


Administration Delegation Regulation


[Last amended September 20, 2020 by B.C. Reg. 266/2020]






Definition




0.1


In this regulation,
"adopting regulation"
means the Railway Safety Adopted Provisions Regulation,
B.C. Reg. 210/2004.

[en. B.C. Reg. 58/2006, s. 1.]





Delegation to British Columbia Safety Authority




1

(1)


For the purposes of sections 2 and 6 of the

Railway Safety Act

, the administration of the

Railway Safety Act

is delegated to the British Columbia Safety Authority, except for




(a)

sections 3, 11, 12 and 20 and Parts 4 and 5, and




(b)

section 19 (0.1) of the


Canadian Transportation Accident Investigation and Safety Board
Act


, adopted by section 5 of the adopting regulation.




(2)

Despite subsection (1), the administration of provisions that is
otherwise delegated by this regulation is not delegated to the British
Columbia Safety Authority.

[en. B.C. Reg. 266/2020, s. 1.]





Delegation to the Canadian Transportation Accident Investigation and
Safety Board




2

(1)


In this section:


"board"
means the Canadian Transportation Accident Investigation and Safety
Board established by section 4 of the


Canadian Transportation Accident Investigation and Safety Board
Act


;



"common carrier"

means a railway that is neither a commuter railway nor an industrial
railway;



"commuter railway"

means a railway operated by or on behalf of the Greater Vancouver
Transportation Authority to transport passengers for a toll or fee;



"industrial railway"

means a railway authorized by a permit under section 11 of the

Railway Safety Act

to operate as an industrial railway.




(2)

Subject to section 1, the administration of the following provisions
is delegated to the board:




(a)

the provisions of the


Canadian Transportation Accident Investigation and Safety Board
Act


adopted by section 5 of the adopting regulation, except for
section 23 (1) of that Act;




(b)

the provisions of the Transportation Safety Board Regulations,
SOR/2014-37, adopted by section 6 of the adopting regulation, except
for section 5 of those regulations;




(c)

section 5 of the Transportation Safety Board Regulations, SOR/2014-37,
but only with respect to common carriers.


[en. B.C. Reg. 58/2006, s. 3; am. B.C. Reg. 266/2020, s. 2.]






Delegation to the Canadian Transportation Agency




3

(1)


In this section,
"agency"
means the Canadian Transportation Agency continued by section 7 (1) of
the

Canada Transportation Act

.




(2)

Subject to sections 1 and 2, the administration of the following
provisions is delegated to the agency:




(a)

the provisions of the

Canada Transportation Act

adopted by section 7 of the adopting regulation;




(b)

sections 16 (1), (3), (4), (4.1) and (6) and sections 26 (1), (3) and
(4) of the

Railway Safety Act

(Canada), adopted by section 3 of the adopting regulation.


[en. B.C. Reg. 284/2006; am. B.C. Reg. 266/2020, s. 3.]
