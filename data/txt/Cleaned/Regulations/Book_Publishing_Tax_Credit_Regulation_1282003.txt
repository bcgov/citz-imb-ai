Income Tax Act




Book Publishing Tax Credit Regulation








Carrying out business primarily in British Columbia





1



For the purposes of Part 8 of the


Income Tax          Act


, a book publishing corporation carries out its business primarily
through a permanent establishment in British Columbia if, in a taxation year,






(a)


the corporation has no permanent establishment outside of British Columbia,
or






(b)


the percentage determined for the corporation by the following formula exceeds
50%:





A

B


X 100%










where




A


is the total salaries and wages paid by the corporation in the
taxation year to employees of the permanent establishment in British Columbia,
and




B


is the total salaries and wages paid by the corporation in the
taxation year.

Income Tax        Act


, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 215, relevant to the enactment of this
regulation: sections 48 and 115]
