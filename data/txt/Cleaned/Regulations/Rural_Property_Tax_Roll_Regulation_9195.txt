Taxation (Rural Area) Act




Rural Property Tax Roll Regulation








Prescribed information





1



The Rural Property Taxation Roll is prescribed, for the purpose of section 21 (1) of the


Taxation (Rural Area) Act


, to contain the following        information:




1 Registered Owner(s) Name(s)


2 Mailing Address


3 Property Folio Number


4 Land District


5 Property Narrative Legal Description


6 Actual Use Code


7 Subdistrict Code


8 Tenure Equity Code


9 Common Owner Code


10 Property Location


11 Tax Exemption Code(s)


12 OIC Property Class Code(s)


13 Actual Assessed Value(s)


14 Description of Tax Charges


15 Tax Rates


16 Tax Amounts


17 Total Current Taxes Due

Taxation (Rural Area)          Act


, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 448, relevant to the enactment of this
regulation: section 21
(1)]
