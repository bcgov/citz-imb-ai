Financial Institutions Act



Credit Union Minimum Level

[Last amended August 1, 2021 by B.C. Reg. 208/2021]




Interpretation




1


In this regulation
"Act"
means the

Financial Institutions Act

.






Amount prescribed for section 277 (2) (d) of the Act




2


For the purpose of section 277 (2) (d) of the Act, the amount
prescribed is 5% of the calculated value of the credit union's risk
weighted assets.


[am. B.C. Regs. 597/2004, Sch. s. 1; 208/2021, App. 5, s. 2.]
