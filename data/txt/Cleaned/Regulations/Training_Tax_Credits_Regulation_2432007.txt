Definitions and interpretation





1



In this regulation:



"Act"

means the


Income Tax Act


;



"complete level 1"

, in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 1 requirements established by SkilledTradesBC and as amended from time to time for the program;



"complete level 2"

, in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 2 requirements established by SkilledTradesBC and as amended from time to time for the program;



"complete level 3"

, in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 3 requirements established by SkilledTradesBC and as amended from time to time for the program;



"complete level 4"

, in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 4 requirements established by SkilledTradesBC and as amended from time to time for the program;



"graduate"

means






(a)


in relation to an eligible recognized program set out in Schedule 1, to receive one of the following for completing the program:






(i)


a Certificate of Qualification;






(ii)


if a Certificate of Qualification is not granted for that program, a Certificate of Apprenticeship, and






(b)


in relation to an eligible training program set out in section 4 (5) or in Schedule 2, to receive one of the following for completing the program:






(i)


a Certificate of Qualification;






(ii)


if a Certificate of Qualification is not granted for that program, a Certificate of Apprenticeship;






(iii)


an Inter-Provincial Red Seal Endorsement;



"work-based hours"

, in relation to a number of work-based hours completed, means work-based hours shown as completed on a transcript from SkilledTradesBC.


[am. B.C. Reg. 253/2022, s. 16.]








Prescribed eligible recognized programs





2



The recognized programs set out in Column 1 of Schedule 1 are prescribed for the purposes of the definition of "eligible recognized program" in section 116 of the Act.








Prescribed eligible training programs





3



The accredited programs and recognized programs set out in Column 1 of Schedule 2 are prescribed for the purposes of the definition of "eligible training program" in section 116 of the Act.


[am. B.C. Reg. 253/2022, s. 17.]








Level requirements





4



(1)


The level 1 requirements, if any, established for an eligible recognized program itemized in Column 1 of Schedule 1 are the requirements set out opposite that item in Column 2.






(2)


The level 2 requirements, if any, established for an eligible recognized program itemized in Column 1 of Schedule 1 are the requirements set out opposite that item in Column 3.






(3)


The level 3 requirements, if any, established for an eligible training program itemized in Column 1 of Schedule 2 are the requirements set out opposite that item in Column 2.






(4)


The level 4 requirements, if any, established for an eligible training program itemized in Column 1 of Schedule 2 are the requirements set out opposite that item in Column 3.






(5)


The level 5 requirement established for the following eligible training programs is to graduate from the program:






(a)


hydraulic service mechanic.
