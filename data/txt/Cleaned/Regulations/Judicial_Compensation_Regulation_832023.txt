Judicial Compensation Act


Judicial Compensation Regulation





Definition




1


In this regulation,
"Act"
means the

Judicial Compensation Act

.






Setting higher amounts for purposes of the Act




2


For the purposes of section 7.1 (2) of the Act, the following
amounts are set:




(a)

for paragraph (a) of that subsection, $40 000;




(b)

for paragraph (b) of that subsection, 85% of the costs over
$40 000 but under $150 000.



[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation:

Judicial Compensation Act

, R.S.B.C. 2003, c. 59, s. 7.1 (3).]
