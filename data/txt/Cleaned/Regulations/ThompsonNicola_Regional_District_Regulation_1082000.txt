Local Government Act




Thompson-Nicola Regional District Regulation








Power under the

Emergency Service Act






1



The Thompson-Nicola Regional District is granted as an extended service the same power that a municipality has as a local authority under the


Emergency Program Act


for such electoral area or areas the regional district may designate.
