Work in lieu





5



(1)


Where a permittee has done or caused to be done road construction giving access to the permittee's location, that work shall be deemed to have been done on the location, and the permittee may






(a)


apply the road construction, in the year it is done, in lieu of up to one year's work requirement during the term of the permit, or






(b)


where the director has approved the work on the basis that it will benefit others than the permittee, apply the road construction in lieu of the work requirement.






(2)


Where the commissioner has approved a program of geological work or geophysical exploration, or both, that is to be done beyond the boundaries of a location or locations that work, on completion, may be applied by the permittee in lieu of the work requirement as if it had been done on the location or locations.


[am. B.C. Reg. 355/98, s. (c).]








Recording of work





6



All work shall be recorded with the director in the permit year in which it is done.


[am. B.C. Reg. 355/98, s. (d).]





Part 2 — Drilling Licences







Fee and rent on application





6.1



For the purposes of section 49.1 (4) (b) of the Act,






(a)


the fee for issue of a drilling licence is $500, and






(b)


the rent for the first year of a drilling licence is $3.50/ha/year.


[en. B.C. Reg. 33/2020, App. 2, s. 2.]

6.2

6.3



For the purposes of section 49.2 (2) (b) of the Act, the penalty is $500.


[en. B.C. Reg. 33/2020, App. 2, s. 2.]








Reinstatement fee





6.4



For the purposes of section 49.2 (3) (b) of the Act, the reinstatement fee is $500.


[en. B.C. Reg. 33/2020, App. 2, s. 2.]








Fees and rents on continuation





6.5



(1)


For the purposes of section 3 (5) (b) of the Petroleum and Natural Gas Drilling Licence and Lease Regulation,






(a)


the continuation fee is $500, and

For the purposes of section 3 (5.1) (a) and (5.3) (c) of the Petroleum and Natural Gas Drilling Licence and Lease Regulation, the rent is $3.50/ha for the period of the continuation.
