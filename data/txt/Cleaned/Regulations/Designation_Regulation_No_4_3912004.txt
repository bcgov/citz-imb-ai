Evidence Act


Designation Regulation No. 4





Employment Standards Branch officers




1


Officers of the Employment Standards Branch to whom powers under
section 117 of the

Employment Standards Act

have been delegated are designated commissioners for taking affidavits
for British Columbia for the purposes of exercising the powers and
performing the duties of their office.



[Provisions of the

Evidence Act

, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 124, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
section 60 (1)]
