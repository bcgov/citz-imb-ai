Financial Administration Act




Reciprocal Taxation Agreement Regulation








Approval for reciprocal taxation agreement





1



With the approval of the Lieutenant Governor in Council, the Minister of Finance, on behalf of the government, may enter into a reciprocal taxation agreement with the government of Canada.
