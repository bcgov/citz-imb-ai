Community Charter




Additional Municipal Powers -

Aeronautics Act Agreements Regulation








Application





1



This regulation applies to municipalities with which the federal minister
responsible for the


Aeronautics Act


(Canada) wishes to enter into an agreement        under section 5.81 of the


Aeronautics Act


(Canada).








Authority to enter into agreement and exercise powers





2



A municipality referred to in section 1 may






(a)


enter into agreements under section 5.81 of the


Aeronautics Act


(Canada) in relation to lands within the          municipality, and






(b)


exercise powers to regulate, prohibit and impose requirements in relation to
matters covered by the agreement, to the extent and in the manner contemplated by the
agreement.



[Provisions of the


Community        Charter


, S.B.C. 2003, c. 26, relevant to the enactment of this
regulation: section 281]
