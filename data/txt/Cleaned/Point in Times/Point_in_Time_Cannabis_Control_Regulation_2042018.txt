Section 5 BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 172/2021, effective June 30, 2021.







Sale outside British Columbia





5



For the purposes of section 15 (b) (iii) of the Act, a federal producer may sell cannabis to a person located in another province if the person is authorized, under the laws of that province and the

Cannabis Act

(Canada), to purchase and possess cannabis.







Section 7 BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 172/2021, effective June 30, 2021.







Supply outside British Columbia





7



For the purposes of section 17 (b) (iii) of the Act, a federal producer may supply cannabis to a person located in another province if the person is authorized, under the laws of that province and the

Cannabis Act

(Canada), to receive cannabis from the federal producer and possess it.

Section 19 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 22/2024, effective February 14, 2024.







No smoking or vaping on public patios





19



For the purposes of section 67 of the Act, a patio that is a public place is prescribed as a place in which a person must not smoke or vape cannabis.

Section 35 BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 172/2021, effective June 30, 2021.







Holding oneself out as licensee





35



A person must not hold themselves out as a licensee unless the person is a licensee.







Section 36 BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 172/2021, effective June 30, 2021.







Advertisement of cannabis sales by unauthorized person





36



Unless authorized to sell cannabis under section 15 of the Act, a person must not use any words, phrases, designs, domain names or other branding elements or indicia that could indicate that the person owns or operates a business that sells cannabis.







Section 37 BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 22/2024, effective February 14, 2024.







Marketing, advertisement or promotion of a place to consume cannabis





37



A person must not market, advertise or promote any place as a place to consume cannabis or to spend time after consuming cannabis.
