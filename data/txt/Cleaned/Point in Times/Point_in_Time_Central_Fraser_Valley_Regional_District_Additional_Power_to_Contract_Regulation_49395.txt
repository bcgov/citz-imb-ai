




"Point in Time" Regulation Content

Local Government Act


Central Fraser Valley Regional District Additional Power to Contract
Regulation

B.C. Reg. 493/95





Section 1 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 118/2018, effective June 15,
2018.





Additional power to contract




1


The Central Fraser Valley Regional District is granted, to the
extent permitted under section 799 of the
Local Government Act
, the power to enter into agreements for the provision of sanitary
sewer treatment and disposal services by the Central Fraser Valley
Regional District to areas outside the Central Fraser Valley
Regional District within the township of Langley east of 256
Street.
