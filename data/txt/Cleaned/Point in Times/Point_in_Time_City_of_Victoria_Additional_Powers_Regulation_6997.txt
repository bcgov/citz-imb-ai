Local Government Act


City of Victoria Additional Powers Regulation

B.C. Reg. 69/97





Section 6 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 118/2018, effective June 15,
2018.





City may make different regulations




6


The City of Victoria may, under section 903 (1) of the
Local Government Act
, make regulations that are different, as specified in a bylaw,
for different structures depending on the age of the structure.
