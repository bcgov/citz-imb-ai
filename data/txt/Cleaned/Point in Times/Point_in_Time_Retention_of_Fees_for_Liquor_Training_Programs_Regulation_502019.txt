Liquor Control and Licensing Act


Retention of Fees for Liquor Training Programs Regulation





Authorization




1


A person approved under section 184 (2) of the Liquor Control and
Licensing Regulation to provide a training program prescribed
under section 60 of the
Liquor Control and Licensing Act
who collects the fee prescribed under that Act for the training
program is authorized to retain the fee as remuneration for
providing the training program.






Repeal




2


This regulation is repealed on October 31, 2019.
