Wildlife Act




Wildlife Management Areas Regulation No. 13



[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 109/2002]







Sturgeon Bank Wildlife Management Area





1



The lands under the administration of the Minister of Environment as outlined in heavy black on the attached map are designated the Sturgeon Bank Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing 5 152 hectares, more or less.


[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]





Map



The map is exempt from publication and may be inspected at the offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Environment, Victoria, B.C.
