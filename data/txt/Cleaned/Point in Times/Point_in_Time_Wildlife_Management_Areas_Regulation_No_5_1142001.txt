Wildlife Act




Wildlife Management Areas Regulation No. 5






Parksville-Qualicum Beach Wildlife Management Area




1


The following area of land, under the administration of the Minister of Environment, is designated the Parksville-Qualicum Beach Wildlife Management Area:


Firstly: All those unencumbered and unalienated foreshore lands situated in Nanaimo District contained within the following described area:


Commencing at a point on the natural boundary of Strait of Georgia on the westerly shore thereof at a point due east of the northwest corner of District Lot 73, Nanoose District, approximately 1 km northwest of Little Qualicum River; thence in a general southeasterly direction approximately 17 km along the natural boundary of Strait of Georgia to the most northwesterly point of Madrona Point on the natural boundary of Craig Bay on the easterly shore thereof including all intervening territory between the mean high water mark and the mean low water mark, and including the following parcels of land situated in Nanoose District:


(a) Lot 1, District Lot 1, Nanoose District, Plan 36317;


(b) Lot 1, District Lot 1, Nanoose District, Plan 14755;


(c) Lot 2, District Lots 1 and 181, Nanoose District, Plan 10257, except those parts thereof within the boundaries of plans 13008, 14755, 15551, 16971, 17062, 17277, 18660, 18661, 19158, 19342, 21228, 21387, 21715, 27632, 29928, 30958 and 36317;


(d) Lot "A", District Lot 181, Nanoose District, Plan 17062;


(e) Lot 1, District Lots 2 and 50, Nanoose District, Plan VIP 55660;


the said area more particularly outlined in heavy black on the attached maps nos. 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5, containing 873 hectares, more or less;


Secondly: all those unencumbered and unalienated foreshore lands or lands covered by water being part of the beds of Englishman River and Morrison Creek; from the estuary of Englishman River 14 kilometres upstream to the northerly boundary of Englishman River Falls Provincial Park and Morrison Creek; thence 5 kilometres upstream from the confluence of Morrison Creek and Englishman River and Lot A of Blocks 544 and 583, Plan 33535; shown in red on the attached map no. 6, all of Nanaimo District, containing 156 hectares, more or less.


[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]




The maps referred to in the text following paragraph (e) are exempt from publication and may be inspected at the offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Environment, Victoria, B.C.




Note:

this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 117/93.
