Hotel Room Tax Act; Provincial Sales Tax Act




Hotel Room Tax Regulation for the City of Surrey






Definition




1


In this regulation,

"Act"

means the

Hotel Room Tax Act

.






Applicability of tax




2


February 28, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of the Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the City of Surrey.






Prescribed rate of tax payable




3


The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the Act is 2% of the purchase price of accommodation purchased in the City of Surrey.


[am. B.C. Reg. 30/2012, s. 2.]






Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax




4


The City of Surrey may spend the amount paid to it out of the revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing, programs and projects.


[am. B.C. Reg. 30/2012, s. 2.]






Repeal




5


This regulation is repealed effective March 1, 2017.


[am. B.C. Reg. 30/2012, s. 3.]
