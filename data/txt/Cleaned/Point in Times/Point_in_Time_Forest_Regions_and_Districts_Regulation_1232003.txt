Forest Act




Forest Regions and Districts Regulation

Forest regions established





1



(1)


The Coast forest region is established as shown on the attached map titled, "Coast forest region".






(2)


The Northern Interior forest region is established as shown on the attached map titled, "Northern Interior forest region".






(3)


The Southern Interior forest region is established as shown on the attached map titled, "Southern Interior forest region".








Forest districts established





2



(1)


The following forest districts are established as shown on the attached map titled "Coast forest region": Campbell River forest district; Chilliwack forest district; North Coast forest district; North Island-Central Coast forest district; Haida Gwaii forest district; South Island forest district; Squamish forest district; Sunshine Coast forest district.






(2)


The following forest districts are established as shown on the attached map titled "Northern Interior forest region": Fort Nelson forest district; Fort St. James forest district; Kalum forest district; Mackenzie forest district; Nadina forest district; Peace forest district; Prince George forest district; Skeena Stikine forest district, Vanderhoof forest district.






(3)


The following forest districts are established as shown on the attached map titled "Southern Interior forest region": Arrow Boundary forest district; Cascades forest district; Central Cariboo forest district; Chilcotin forest district; Columbia forest district; Headwaters forest district; Kamloops forest district; Kootenay Lake forest district; Okanagan Shuswap forest district; Quesnel forest district; Rocky Mountain forest district; 100 Mile House forest district.
