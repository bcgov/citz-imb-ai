Wildlife Act




Wildlife Management Areas Regulation No. 12



[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 109/2002]







Hamling Lakes Wildlife Management Area





1



The lands under the administration of the Minister of Environment as outlined in heavy black on the attached map are designated the Hamling Lakes Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing 30 572 hectares, more or less.


[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]





Map



The map is exempt from publication and may be inspected at the offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Environment, Victoria, B.C.
