Financial Administration Act


Land Tax Deferment Administration Fee Remission Regulation




Remission of fees



1

Authorization is given for the remission of application and
renewal fees payable in respect of a deferral agreement requested
by a person who meets the requirements of section 5 (2.1) of the
Land Tax Deferment Act
for the 2009 or 2010 taxation year.
