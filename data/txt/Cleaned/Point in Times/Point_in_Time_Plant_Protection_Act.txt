Powers and duties of inspectors



3

To carry out duties under this Act and the regulations, an
inspector may do one or more of the following:


(a) enter premises at any reasonable time for an inspection
under this Act or regulations;


(b) inspect the premises, a plant, root medium or container;


(c) detain or order the detention of a plant, root medium or
container;


(d) carry out or direct the carrying out of treatment;


(e) on the order of the minister, confiscate or destroy a
plant, root medium or container;


(f) carry out other duties and exercise other powers
prescribed in the regulations.






Section 6 BEFORE amended by 2007-14-213,Sch, effective December 1,
2007 (BC Reg 354/2007).




Appeal



6

A person adversely affected by an act or decision of an inspector
under this Act or regulations may appeal to the minister in the
prescribed manner.
