Refund on ceasing to be a member





23



(1)

(2)


If a member to whom this Part applies ceases to be a member having served for 7 years or for more than 2 Parliaments, he or she may at option, on application,






(a)


after reaching the age of 55 years, be granted a superannuation allowance in accordance with section 20 or 21, as the case may be, or






(b)


irrespective of age, receive as a refund the total of his or her contributions, including interest accrued, but in this event he or she is not entitled to any other benefits under this Part unless his or her account is reinstated under subsection (3).






(3)
