Schedule 1, rule 1 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.





1


After receiving a ballot from the returning officer, the voter must, in the polling booth or space provided, mark it with an "X" in the square provided opposite to the division for which he or she desires to vote, fold the ballot and, in the presence of the returning officer or deputy returning officer and scrutineers, if any, deposit the ballot in the box and then leave the premises.
