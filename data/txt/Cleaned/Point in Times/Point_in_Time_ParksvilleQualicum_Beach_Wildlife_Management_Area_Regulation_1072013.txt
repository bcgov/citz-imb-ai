Wildlife Act



Parksville-Qualicum Beach Wildlife Management Area Regulation






Designation of Parksville-Qualicum Beach Wildlife Management Area




1


All those parcels of Crown land, together with all that foreshore
or land covered by water, under the administration of the Minister
of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, situated in
Nanaimo Land District, Nanoose Land District and Newcastle Land
District and outlined in heavy black on the map with the file name
APPROVED_parksville_qualicum_beach_wma3m.pdf, on file with GeoBC,
Ministry of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, are
designated as the Parksville-Qualicum Beach Wildlife Management
Area, the whole containing approximately 1 245 hectares.
