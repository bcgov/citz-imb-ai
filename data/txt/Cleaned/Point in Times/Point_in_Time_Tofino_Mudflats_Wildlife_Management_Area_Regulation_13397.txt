Wildlife Act




Tofino Mudflats Wildlife Management Area Regulation



[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 109/2002]







Area designated





1



The lands under the administration of the Minister of Environment, as outlined in heavy black on the attached map, are designated the Tofino Mudflats Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing 1 645 hectares, more or less.


[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]




Note:

The map attached is exempt from publication under section 6 of the Regulations Act. It may be inspected at the offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Environment.
