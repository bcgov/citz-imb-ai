Todagin Wildlife Management Area



All those parcels or tracts of Crown land, together with all that foreshore or land covered by water, situated in Cassiar District and contained within the described boundaries as shown on the Official Plan deposited in the Crown Land Registry as Plan 15 Tube 1942.


The whole wildlife management area containing approximately 122 506 hectares.
