Definitions




1


In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Pharmacy Operations and Drug Scheduling Act
.






Prescribed health care professions




2


The following classes of persons are prescribed for the purposes
of paragraph (b) (i) of the definition of
"practitioner"
in section 1 of the Act:




(a)

midwives;




(b)

nurses practising nursing as nurse practitioners, registered
nurses or psychiatric registered nurses;




(c)

optometrists;




(d)

pharmacists, for the purposes only of prescribing the following
drugs for emergency contraception:




(i)

ethinyl estradiol;




(ii)

norgestrel;




(iii)

progestin;




(e)

naturopathic physicians.

[am. B.C. Regs. 163/2009; 228/2015.]
