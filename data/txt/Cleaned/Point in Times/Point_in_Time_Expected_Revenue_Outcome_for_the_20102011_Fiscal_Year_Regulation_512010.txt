Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act



Expected Revenue Outcome for the 2010/2011 Fiscal Year Regulation





Revenue targets



1

For the purposes of section 5 (2) of the
Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act
,


(a) the sources of actual revenue for the 2010/2011 fiscal
year are specified as audit and revenue compliance related
activities in respect of taxes, resource royalties and other
revenues payable under enactments administered by the revenue
minister, and


(b) the amount of actual revenue in the 2010/2011 fiscal year
from the revenue sources referred to in paragraph (a) is specified
as $185 325 000.



Note:
this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 164/2009.
