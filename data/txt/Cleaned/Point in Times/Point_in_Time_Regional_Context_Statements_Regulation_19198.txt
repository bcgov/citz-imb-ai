Section 1 definitions of "facilitator" and "neutral" BEFORE amended by BC Reg 98/2018, effective May 17, 2018.



"facilitator"

means a facilitator as defined in section 848 of the Act;



"neutral"

means a person appointed as a member of a peer panel under section 861 (1) of the Act or as a single arbitrator under section 861 (2) or (3) of the Act;
