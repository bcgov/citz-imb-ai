Section 1 definition of "average monthly rent" BEFORE amended by BC Reg 94/2023, effective April 3, 2023.



"average monthly rent"

, in relation to a calendar year, means the average rent reported by CMHC for a one-month period in the preceding calendar year;




Section 1 definition of "census area" BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 94/2023, effective April 3, 2023.



"census area"

means a geographic area classified by Statistics Canada as a census subdivision or a census metropolitan area for the purposes of taking a census of population;
