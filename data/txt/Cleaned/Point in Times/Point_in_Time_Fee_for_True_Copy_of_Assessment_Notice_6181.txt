Assessment Act




Fee for True Copy of Assessment Notice






Fee for copy of notice




1


The fee, referred to in section 6 (5) of the

Assessment Act

, for a true copy of an assessment notice shall be $6 per parcel of land referred to in the assessment notice.



[Provisions of the

Assessment Act

, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 20, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: sections 6 (5) and 74]
