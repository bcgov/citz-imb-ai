B.C. Reg. 101/92


O.C. 501/92



Deposited April 2, 1992







Financial Institutions Act




Non-Reporting Company Exemption Regulation






Interpretation




1


In this regulation

"Act"

means the

Financial Institutions Act

.






Certain

Company Act

requirements disapplied




2


Sections 111, 153 and 154 of the

Company Act

, as made applicable to trust companies and insurance companies generally by Part 1 of the Act, do not apply to or in respect of


(a) a trust company, or


(b) an insurance company


that is not a reporting company as defined in section 1 of the

Company Act

.



[Provisions of the

Financial Institutions Act

, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 141, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 289 (4) (o)]
