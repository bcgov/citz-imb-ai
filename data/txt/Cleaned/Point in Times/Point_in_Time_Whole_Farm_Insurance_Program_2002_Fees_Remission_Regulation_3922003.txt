B.C. Reg. 392/2003


O.C. 1024/2003



Deposited October 31, 2003







Financial Administration Act




Whole Farm Insurance Program 2002 Fees Remission Regulation






Definition




1


In this regulation,

"program"

means the Whole Farm Insurance Program (WFIP) farm income plan established under the

Farm Income Insurance Act

.






Remission authorized




2


Approval is given for the remission of the obligation to pay the application fee under the program for the 2002 claim year.






Repeal




3


This regulation is repealed on December 31, 2003.

Financial Administration Act

, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 138, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 19]
