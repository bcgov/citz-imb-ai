Place of business



11

An employee venture capital corporation must establish a place of
business in British Columbia within 30 days after being
registered, and must afterwards maintain a place of business in
British Columbia.






Section 12 BEFORE amended by 2003-4-3, effective March 12, 2003
(Royal Assent).




Limits on equity capital



12

(1)  The equity capital of an employee venture capital
corporation, including the equity capital referred to in section
14, must not exceed


(a) $5 million if it is not a reporting issuer as defined in
the
Securities Act
, or


(b) $20 million if it is a reporting issuer as so defined.


(2)  If the Lieutenant Governor in Council considers it to be
in the public interest, the Lieutenant Governor in Council may by
order and with or without conditions, in a particular case, exempt
an employee venture capital corporation from the limits of equity
capital set out in subsection (1) and specify other limits that
are to apply in that particular case.
