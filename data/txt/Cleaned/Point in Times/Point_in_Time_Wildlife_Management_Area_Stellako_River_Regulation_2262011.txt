Wildlife Act


Wildlife Management Area (Stellako River) Regulation





Designation of Stellako River wildlife management area




1


All those parcels of Crown lands, together with all that foreshore
or land covered by water, under the administration of the Minister
of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, situated in
Range 5, Coast District and contained within the described
boundaries as shown on the Official Plan deposited in the Crown
Land Registry as Plan 3 Tube 2004, except




(a)

DL 6854, and




(b)

Francois Lake Road as shown on Plan 5 Tube 1435 and Plan 14 Tube
1633,


are designated the Stellako River Wildlife Management Area, the
whole containing approximately 503 hectares.
