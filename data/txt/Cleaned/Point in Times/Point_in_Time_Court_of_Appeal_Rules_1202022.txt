Section 92 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 187/2022, effective September 20, 2022.







Bringing cross appeals in relation to pre-existing applications for leave to appeal





92



A respondent who has been served a pre-existing application for leave to appeal may bring a cross appeal by complying with Rule 8 and, for that purpose, a reference in Rule 8 to "notice of appeal" must be read as though it were a reference to "pre-existing application for leave to appeal".
