Definitions Regulation
B.C. Reg. 311/90





Section 1 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 211/2012, effective November 1,
2012.




Definition of "officer"



1

In the
Credit Union Incorporation Act
, the
Financial Institutions Act
and the
Company Act
as it applies for the purposes of those 2 Acts,
"officer"
of a corporation includes a credit officer, an employee of the
corporation who has the authority to approve loans and an employee
of the corporation who reports directly to the directors.

[en. B.C. Reg. 109/92.]
