Community Charter




Size of Council Regulation






Exception to size of council




1


(1) 							This regulation applies to a municipality that has, on the day this regulation comes into force,


(a) a population of more than 50 000, and


(b) a council consisting of a mayor and less than 8 councillors.


(2)
As an exception to section 118 (4) of the

Community Charter

, a municipality described under subsection (1) may obtain the approval of the electors respecting retention of the size of its council by holding an alternative approval process.






Repeal of regulation




2


This regulation is repealed effective April 30, 2005.



[Provisions of the

Community Charter

, S.B.C. 2003, c. 26, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 281 (1) (b) and (2 (d)]
