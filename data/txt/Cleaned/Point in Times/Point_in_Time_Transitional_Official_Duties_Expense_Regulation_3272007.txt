Financial Administration Act


Transitional Official Duties Expense Regulation





Definitions



1

In this regulation:


"amended regulation"
means the Official Duties Expense Regulation, BC Reg 226/2001, as
amended on October 30, 2007;


"official"
means an official as defined in the amended regulation;


"official duties"
means official duties as defined in the amended regulation.





Transitional expenses



2

(1)  The amended regulation applies with respect to claims
made under that regulation by an official on or after October 30,
2007 for


(a) expenses incurred by the official on or after October 30,
2007 or allowances payable in relation to official duties
performed on or after October 30, 2007,


(b) expenses incurred by the official on or after July 1,
2007 but before October 30, 2007, if the official would have been
entitled to have those expenses reimbursed under the amended
regulation if that regulation had been in force on July 1, 2007,
and


(c) allowances payable in relation to official duties
performed on or after July 1, 2007 but before October 30, 2007, if
the official would have been entitled to have those allowances
paid under the amended regulation if that regulation had been in
force on July 1, 2007.


(2)  If an official received compensation under the Official
Duties Expense Regulation before October 30, 2007for expenses
incurred on or after July 1, 2007 but before that regulation was
amended on October 30, 2007, the official may not claim an amount
for those expenses under the amended regulation that is more than
the difference between the amount paid and the amount permitted
under the amended regulation.


(3)  If an official received an allowance under the Official
Duties Expense Regulation before October 30, 2007 for official
duties performed on or after July 1, 2007 but before that
regulation was amended on October 30, 2007, the official may not
claim an allowance under the amended regulation with respect to
those official duties that is more than the difference between the
amount paid and the amount permitted under the amended regulation.


(4)  Subsections (2) and (3) apply only if the compensation
or allowance paid to an official before October 30, 2007 is less
than the amount that is payable in the same circumstances under
the amended regulation.
