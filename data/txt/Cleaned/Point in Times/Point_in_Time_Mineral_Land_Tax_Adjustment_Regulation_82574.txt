Section 5 (1) and (2) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective
March 30, 2022.




(1)

Where an owner surrenders all of his mineral land included in a
notice of mineral land tax assessment, the administration shall
cancel all taxes and interest due with respect to the mineral land
surrendered to the Crown in right of the Province.




(2)

Where an owner surrenders part of his mineral land included in a
notice of mineral land tax assessment, the administrator shall
cancel the notice of mineral land tax assessment for that year and
issue a supplementary notice of mineral land tax assessment covering
the mineral land retained by the owner, provided that the mineral
land included in the original notice was not subject to forfeiture
