Interpretation Act


Daylight Saving Time




Daylight saving time



1

In British Columbia each year the time for general purposes shall
be 7 hours behind Greenwich mean time during the period commencing
at 2 o'clock in the forenoon on the first Sunday of April and
ending at 2 o'clock in the forenoon on the last Sunday of October.
