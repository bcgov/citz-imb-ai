




"Point in Time" Regulation Content



Business Practices and Consumer Protection Act



Government Cheque Cashing Fees Regulation


B.C. Reg. 127/2018







Section 1 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 169/2019, effective September 15, 2019.







Definition





1



In this regulation,

"government assistance cheque"

means a government cheque that is issued






(a)


to a recipient under the

Employment and Assistance Act

to provide income assistance, hardship assistance or a supplement under that Act, or






(b)


to a recipient under the

Employment and Assistance for Persons with Disabilities Act

to provide disability assistance, hardship assistance or a supplement under that Act.
