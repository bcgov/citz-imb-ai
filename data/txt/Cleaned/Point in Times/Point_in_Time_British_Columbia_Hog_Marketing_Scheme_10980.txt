Section 7 (1), (2) and (3) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.






(1)


Where a commercial producer applies to be registered as a commercial producer, and the commission is satisfied that he qualifies under section 8, it shall enter his name and address in the register as a commercial producer.






(2)


Where the commission is satisfied that a person has ceased to qualify as a commercial producer, it shall amend the register to reflect that fact, but unless the person affected requests the amendment the commission shall first mail a notice by registered post to him at his last registered address advising him of the proposed amendment.






(3)


Where a commercial producer






(a)


is in arrears of his licence fees, or






(b)


fails to comply with a provision of the scheme or an order from


the commission, the commission may strike his name from the register.






Section 8 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 76/2022, effective March 30, 2022.








8



A person qualifies to be registered as a commercial producer if he operates a farm






(a)


from which he markets, or






(b)


on which there are hog production facilities capable of producing for market


300 or more hogs annually, for processing.
