Section 1 (a) and (b) (i) and (ii) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 134/2010, effective June 7, 2010.


(a) if the application is for a new home to be built by an owner builder, a document certifying under the seal of the office that the new home may be built by the owner builder named in the document;


(b) if the application is for a new home to be built by a residential builder, a document


(i)  certifying under the seal of the office that the residential builder is licensed under the Act to build the new home, and


(ii)  certifying under the seal of a warranty provider that the new home is covered by home warranty insurance;






Section 1 (c) BEFORE amended by BC Reg 69/2016, effective September 1, 2016.


(c) if the application is for a new home that is exempt under section 2 of the Homeowner Protection Act Regulation, B.C. Reg. 29/99, a declaration by the builder of the new home or the owner of the property on which the new home is to be built that the new home is exempt from the requirement to be covered by home warranty insurance.
