Approved medical services and health care plans




1


For the purposes of the definition of "taxable premium" in section
1 of the
Insurance Premium Tax Act
, a medical services or health care plan that meets the following
criteria is approved:




(a)

the plan is established and managed by an insurance company or an
extraprovincial insurance corporation that




(i)

is authorized to carry on insurance business under the
Financial Institutions Act
, and




(ii)

is a non-profit corporation;




(b)

the plan provides for the payment of benefits directly to one or
more of the following:




(i)

a person entitled to practice a designated health profession under
the
Health Professions Act
;




(ii)
