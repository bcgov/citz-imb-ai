Assessment Act




Fee for Copy of Information

in the Current Assessment Notice Regulation








Fee for copy of notice





1



The fee, referred to in section 6 (5) of the

Assessment Act

, for a copy of the information contained in the current assessment notice is $6 per parcel of land referred to in the assessment notice.




Note:

this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 61/81.
