Human Rights Code




Human Rights Code Regulation






Delegation by the commissioner of investigation and mediation




1


The following classes of persons are designated for the purposes of section 46 (3) (b)


1


of the

Human Rights Code

:


(a) human rights officers;


(b) those persons to whom human rights officers report for the purposes of investigations and mediations under the

Human Rights Code

.






Annual report




2


The annual report under section 18


1


of the

Human Rights Code

must include a statement for the reporting period of the median times taken


(a) from filing a complaint until a human rights officer is assigned under section 23 (2) of the

Human Rights Code

,


(b) from assignment of a human rights officer under section 23 (2) of the

Human Rights Code

until the human rights officer submits a report under section 23 (3),


(c) from submission of the investigation report until the complaint is disposed of under section 26 of the

Human Rights Code

, and


(d) in cases where it is determined a complaint can be disposed of without an investigation, from filing a complaint until the complaint is disposed of under section 26 of the

Human Rights Code

.


(e) to (g) Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 148/98.]


[am. B.C. Reg. 148/98.]






1.



Sections 46 (3) and 18 of the

Human Rights Code

, as referred to in sections 1 and 2 of this regulation, were repealed, effective March 31, 2003, by S.B.C. 2002, chapter 62, sections 24 and 5 respectively.

this regulation repeals B.C. Regs. 151/75 and 239/92.
