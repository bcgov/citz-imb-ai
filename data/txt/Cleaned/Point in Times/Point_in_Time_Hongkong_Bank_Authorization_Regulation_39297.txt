Deposited December 5, 1997







Financial Institutions Act




Hongkong Bank Authorization Regulation






Interpretation




1


In this regulation:



"guarantee"

means the deed of guarantee, dated October 1, 1996, given by HSBC Holdings plc in respect of the deposit liabilities of the bank;



"the bank"

means the Hongkong Bank of Canada.






Authorization




2


Until November 1, 2000, while the guarantee remains in effect, the bank is by this regulation authorized, in the course of conducting, soliciting or advertising deposit business, to describe, in a truthful manner, deposits in the bank by its customers as guaranteed by HSBC Holdings plc.




Note:

this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 344/94.
