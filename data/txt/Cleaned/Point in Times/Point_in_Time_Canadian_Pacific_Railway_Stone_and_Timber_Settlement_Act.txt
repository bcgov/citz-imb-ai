Regulations to amend Schedule




11

(1)


The Lieutenant Governor in Council may, by regulation, do one or
more of the following:




(a)

prescribe additional railway lands by adding items to the
Schedule;




(b)

amend the district lot numbers set out in Column 1 of the
Schedule;




(c)

amend the land district names, or the division names of the land
districts, set out in Column 2 of the Schedule.




(2)

A regulation made under this section may be made retroactive to
the date this section comes into force or a later date, and if
made retroactive is deemed to have come into force on the
specified date.




(3)

This section is repealed 2 years after the date this section comes
into force.
