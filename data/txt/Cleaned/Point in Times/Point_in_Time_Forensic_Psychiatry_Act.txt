Section 7 BEFORE amended by 2013-12-26, effective June 1, 2013 (BC
Reg 160/2013).




Staff



7

(1)  Subject to the
Public Service Act
, the commission may appoint officers and employees the commission
considers necessary for this Act, and may determine their duties.


(2)  The Lieutenant Governor in Council may appoint an
executive director of the commission, to be appointed during
pleasure, and may fix his or her remuneration and may declare that
some or all of the
Public Service Act
applies to the executive director.


(3)  Despite the
Public Service Act
but with the approval of the minister, the commission may engage
and retain persons it considers necessary as consultants, experts
or specialists, and may fix their remuneration.
