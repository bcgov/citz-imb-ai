Lazo Marsh-North East Comox Wildlife Management Area





1



The area of land under the administration of the Minister of Environment and outlined in red on the attached map is designated the Lazo Marsh-North East Comox Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing 127 hectares, more or less.


[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]




The map referred to in the text is exempt from publication and may be inspected at the offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Environment, Victoria, B.C.
