Section 1 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 4/2010, effective January 14,
2010.




Covenant re land disposition



1

Upon the issuance of a Crown grant under section 48 of the
Land Act
, the Minister of Sustainable Resource Management may require the
grantee to enter into a covenant that he and his successors in
title will not dispose of the land granted separately from another
designated parcel already owned by the grantee.

[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 2.]
