Section 2 (1) definitions of "comply", "non-compliance date" and
"penalty date" BEFORE amended by BC Reg 279/2019, effective April 6,

"comply"
means comply with a provision of Part 3 of the Act, or the
regulations, as specified in section 3 of this regulation;



"non-compliance date"

means the date the Board, under section 196.1 (1) of the Act, is
satisfied an employer has failed to comply;



"penalty date"

means the date of the order by which the Board imposes an
administrative penalty under section 196.1 (1) of the Act.
