Section 21 (1) BEFORE repealed by BC Reg 11/2021, effective February 5, 2021.






(1)


In this section:



"agrologist"

has the same meaning as in the

Agrologists Act

;



"applied science technologist or technician"

means a person who holds a certificate of registration under the

Applied Science Technologists and Technicians Act

;



"professional biologist"

has the same meaning as in the

College of Applied Biology Act

;



"professional engineer"

has the same meaning as in the

Engineers and Geoscientists Act

;



"professional forester"

has the same meaning as in the

Foresters Act

;



"professional geoscientist"

has the same meaning as in the

Engineers and Geoscientists Act

;



"registered biology technologist"

has the same meaning as in the

College of Applied Biology Act

;



"registered forest technologist"

has the same meaning as in the

Foresters Act

.
