




"Point in Time" Regulation Content



Workers Compensation Act



Time Period for Review Regulation


B.C. Reg. 164/2015







Section 2 BEFORE re-enacted by BC Reg 89/2021, effective March 22, 2021.







Prescribed time period





2



For the purposes of section 96.2 (3) (a) of the Act, the prescribed time period is 45 days for the following:






(a)


a Board decision referred to in section 96.2 (1) (b) of the Act respecting a payment under section 73 (1) of the Act;






(b)


a Board decision or order referred to in section 96.2 (1) (c) of the Act.
