[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 284/91]





Prescribed capacity




1


The prescribed capacity for the purpose of paragraph (l) in the
definition of "improvements" in section 1 (2) of the
Assessment Act
is




(a)

for vessels in wineries, 20 000 or more gallons, and




(b)

for vessels not in wineries,




(i)

if aboveground,




(A)

5 000 or more gallons, or




(B)

800 or more cubic feet, and




(ii)

if underground,




(A)

3 975 or more gallons, or




(B)

635 or more cubic feet.

[am. B.C. Reg. 284/91.]


Note:
this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 394/88.
