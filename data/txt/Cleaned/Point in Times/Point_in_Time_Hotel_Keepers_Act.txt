Section 6 BEFORE amended by 2003-7-30, effective March 28, 2003 (BC Reg 130/2003).





Expulsion of undesirables




6


An innkeeper or the innkeeper's representative may require a person, not registered as a guest, boarder or lodger, whom the innkeeper or the innkeeper's representative considers undesirable to leave the inn, and if that person fails to leave, may eject the person from the inn premises.
