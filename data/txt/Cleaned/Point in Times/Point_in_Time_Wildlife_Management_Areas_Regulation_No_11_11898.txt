Wildlife Act

[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 109/2002]





Midge Creek Wildlife Management Area




1


The lands under the administration of the Minister of Environment
as outlined in heavy black on the attached map are designated the
Midge Creek Wildlife Management Area, the whole containing 14 757
hectares, more or less.

[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2002, s. 1.]



Map



The map is exempt from publication and may be inspected at the
offices of the Wildlife Branch, Ministry of Environment, Victoria,
B.C.
