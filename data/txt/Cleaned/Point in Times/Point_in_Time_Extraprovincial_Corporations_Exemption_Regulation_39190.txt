Financial Institutions Act




Extraprovincial Corporations Exemption Regulation






Interpretation




1


In this regulation

"Act"

means the

Financial Institutions Act

.






Repealed




2


Repealed. [B.C. Reg. 597/2004, Sch. s. 2.]






Extraprovincial societies




3


(1) 							In this section

"extraprovincial society"

means an extraprovincial society as defined in section 1 of the

Society Act

that under this Act has a business authorization for insurance business.


(2)
Sections 297, 299, 300 (1), 301, 304 to 310, 312, 313 and 315 of the

Company Act

, as they apply for the purposes of this Act, do not apply to an extraprovincial society that


(a) immediately before the date this regulation comes into force is registered as an extraprovincial society under Part 8 of the

Society Act

, and


(b) continues to be so registered on and after that date.


[en. B.C. Reg. 32/93.]

Financial Institutions Act

, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 141, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 289 (4) (o)]
