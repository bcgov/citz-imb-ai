




Fireworks Act
[RSBC 1996] CHAPTER 146






Definition




1


In this Act,
"fireworks"
includes cannon crackers, fireballs, firecrackers, mines, Roman
candles, skyrockets, squibs, torpedoes and any other explosive
designated as a firework by regulation.






Application of Act




2


This Act applies to




(a)

a municipality that, by bylaw, declares that this Act applies, and




(b)

a rural area designated by regulation.






Selling, giving or setting off fireworks




3


Except between October 24 and November 1 in any year, a person must
not sell, give, fire or set off fireworks.






Sale to minor




4


The fire commissioner may designate fireworks that may not be sold to
a minor without the written permission of the parent or guardian of
the minor.






Permission for public display




5


Fireworks may be sold to and discharged by a person or organization at
a public display if the public display is held with the written
permission of




(a)

the fire commissioner, or




(b)

a local assistant of the fire commissioner who has jurisdiction in the
fire district where the public display is held.
