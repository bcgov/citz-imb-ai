




Skagit Environmental Enhancement Act
[RSBC 1996] CHAPTER 426






Definitions




1


In this Act:


"agreement"
means the agreement dated March 30, 1984 between the City of
Seattle in the State of Washington, United States of America and the
government;


"commission"
means the Commission established under Appendix D to the
agreement;


"fund"
means the Skagit Environmental Endowment Fund established under
Appendix D to the agreement.






Powers of commission




2


The commission has the powers and rights of a corporation in British
Columbia including the right to acquire and dispose of real property.






No taxation




3


The commission and the fund are not liable to taxation.






Not government agent




4


The commission is not an agent of the government.






Acts not applicable




5

(1)


Sections 17 (2) and 18 of the

Interpretation Act

do not apply to the commission.




(2)

The

Business Corporations Act

does not apply to the commission, but the Lieutenant Governor in
Council may order that one or more of the provisions apply.
