EXPLANATORY NOTE


Legislative citations have the format of "year-chapter-section".



Amendments Not in Force:

Any legislative changes to the Act that are not in force as of July 20, 2016 are identified
in

italics

at the beginning of the historical table. The "Section" column identifies the affected
provisions of the Act. The "Citation" column identifies the amending legislation by
its citation.





Legislative History:

The second part of the table provides a legislative history of each section of the
Act between the 1996 general Statute Revision and the 2016 limited revision of this
Act. The "Section" column identifies all sections of the Act in force on July 20,
2016. The "History" column for each section begins with the citation of the section
immediately before the 2016 limited revision of this Act. This is followed by a list
of citations for the legislation that enacted or amended the section between the 1996
general Statute Revision and July 20, 2016. If a section was repealed and replaced
during that period, these last citations begin at the most recent replacement. If
a section was spent or repealed and not replaced before the Act's revision, the citations
are excluded from this table.
