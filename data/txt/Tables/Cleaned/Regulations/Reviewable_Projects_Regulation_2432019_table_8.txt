
Reviewable Projects Regulation

Environmental Assessment Act



Criteria:


(1)

Subject to subsection (2), a new manufacturing facility,
regardless of size.



(2)

If refining processes, precious metal distillate
processes or ore roasting processes are integrated with
ore milling operations at, or in the vicinity of, a mine
site and are dedicated to the mining operations at the
mine site, those processes are not reviewable as part of
a project that is a reviewable project under subsection
(1).
