
Medical and Health Care Services Regulation

Medicare Protection Act




Item



Column 1

Practitioner




Column 2

Diagnostic Services That May Be Requested






1

Enrolled medical practitioner


All diagnostic services



2
Nurse practitioner

Those diagnostic services associated with the scope of practice
of a nurse practitioner



3

RN (certified) or RPN (certified)


Those diagnostic services associated with the scope of practice
of a RN (certified) or RPN (certified), if requested without an
order having been made by a practitioner referred to in any of
items 1, 2, 4, 5 or 6



4
Enrolled dentist

Those diagnostic services associated with the scope of practice
of a dentist



5

Enrolled podiatrist


Those diagnostic services associated with the scope of practice
of a podiatrist



6
Enrolled midwife

Those diagnostic services associated with the scope of practice
of a midwife
