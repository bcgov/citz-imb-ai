
Supreme Court Family Rules

Court Rules Act




[ ] by Spouse 1 and Spouse 2 jointly [

attach joint affidavit, or affidavit of each spouse, respecting
the grounds for divorce under section 7 (1) of the Civil
Marriage Act (Canada)

]




[ ] by Spouse 1 with the consent of Spouse 2 [

attach affidavit of Spouse 1 respecting the grounds for divorce
under section 7 (1) of the Civil Marriage Act (Canada) and an
affidavit of Spouse 2 providing evidence of consent to the
proceedings

]




[ ] by Spouse 1 with an order referred to in section 7 (2) of the
Civil Marriage Act
(Canada) [

attach affidavit of Spouse 1 respecting the grounds for divorce
under section 7 (1) of the Civil Marriage Act (Canada) and a
copy of that order

]
