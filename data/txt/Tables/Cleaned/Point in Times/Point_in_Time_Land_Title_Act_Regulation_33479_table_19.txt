Land Title Act

Land Title Act Regulation
B.C. Reg. 334/79




NOTE
:

1.

Certain classes of charge (e.g. mortgages) must be extended by
an appropriate instrument executed by the fee simple owner
(see section 131 (1) (b)) to coincide with the boundaries of
the new parcel.




2.

The consent of charge holders may be on separate Form(s) 14.
