Business Corporations Act and Cooperative Association Act

Cooperative Association Regulation
B.C. Reg. 391/2000




Note:


The following information must be provided for each
subscriber. Each subscriber must sign his or her full name in
the presence of a witness on two original copies of these
Rules. Only one witness is required, providing that he or she
witnesses the signature of each subscriber, in which case the
signature, name, and address of the witness may appear after
the signatures of the subscribers.
