1
Compact audio products
Jan 1, 2013

Products manufactured on or after Jan 2, 2013


Must be capable of entering each of the following modes that
are applicable to the individual product:

(a) a standby mode with a power consumption of ≤ 1 watt, with
information or status display active;

(b) a standby mode with a power consumption of ≤ 0.5 watts,
with information or status display inactive;

(c) a standby mode with a power consumption of ≤ 0.5 watts,
without information or status display;

(d) an off mode with a power consumption of ≤ 0.5 watts.


CAN/CSA C62301-07 tested at 115 volts, regardless of the
nominal voltage of the energy device
