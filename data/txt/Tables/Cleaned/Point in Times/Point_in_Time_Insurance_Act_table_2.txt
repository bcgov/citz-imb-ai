
“Point in Time” Act Content
INSURANCE ACT
[RSBC 2012]  CHAPTER 1





Insurer to furnish forms for proof of claim




6.

The insurer must furnish forms for proof of claim within
15 days after receiving notice of claim, but if the
claimant has not received the forms within that time the
claimant may submit his or her proof of claim in the form of a
written statement of the cause or nature of the accident,
sickness or disability giving rise to the claim and of the
extent of the loss.
