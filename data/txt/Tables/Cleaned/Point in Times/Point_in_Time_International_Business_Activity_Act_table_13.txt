
“Point in Time” Act Content
INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS ACTIVITY ACT

(formerly titled International Financial Activity Act)

[SBC 2004] CHAPTER 49



foreign dividends
=

the amount included under section 12 (1) (k) of the federal
Act in computing the IFB income of the international financial
business for the taxation year in respect of which the
corporation makes a deduction under section 113 of the federal
Act;



inducements
=

the amount included under section 12 (1) (x) of the federal
Act in computing the IFB income of the international financial
business for the taxation year.
