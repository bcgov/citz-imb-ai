Manufactured Home Act

Manufactured Home Regulation
B.C. Reg. 51/78

Manufactured Home Act




1.


In addition to a fee marked by an asterisk, a further operator
fee of $1.50, plus any G.S.T. applicable to the operator fee,
may be charged for any transaction done by electronic means
from a location outside a government office or at a government
office by a person who is not a government employee.
