WORKERS COMPENSATION ACT

[RSBC 1996] CHAPTER 492



(a)

Carcinoma of the lung when associated with:




(i)
asbestosis or

Where there is exposure to airborne asbestos dust.



(ii)

bilateral diffuse pleural thickening or fibrosis, over 5 mm
thick and extending over more than a quarter of the chest wall


Where there is exposure to airborne asbestos dust and the
claimant has not previously suffered collagen disease, chronic
uremia, drug-induced fibrosis, tuberculosis or other infection
or trauma capable of causing pleural thickening or fibrosis.
