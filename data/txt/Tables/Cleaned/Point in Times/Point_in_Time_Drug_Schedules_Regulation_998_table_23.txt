
"Point in Time" Regulation Content

Pharmacy Operations and Drug Scheduling Act

Drug Schedules Regulation
B.C. Reg. 9/98



1

Nicotine and its salts, for human use, except

(a) in natural substances;

(b) in the form of a chewing gum containing 4 mg or less of
nicotine per dosage unit;

(c) in the form of a transdermal patch with a delivery rate of
22 mg or less of nicotine per day;

(d) in a form to be administered orally by means of an
inhalation device delivering 4 mg or less of nicotine per
dosage unit; or

(e) in the form of a lozenge containing 4 mg or less of
nicotine per dosage unit
