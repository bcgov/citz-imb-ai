Reviewable Projects Regulation
Environmental Assessment Act
Criteria:
(1)
Subject to subsection (2), modification of an existing
facility that results in the addition of ≥ 2 lanes of
paved public highway to an existing paved public highway
over a continuous distance of ≥ 20 km on land
that is not alongside and contiguous to an area of land
previously developed for a transmission line, transmission
pipeline, public highway or railway.
(2)
Assessment of the modification of an existing project
described in subsection (1) does not include the
dismantling and abandonment phases.
