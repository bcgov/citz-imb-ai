Reviewable Projects Regulation
Environmental Assessment Act
Criteria:
(1)
Subject to subsection (2), a new railway project that
consists of
(a)
≥ 20 continuous km of developed track, if the railway is
not alongside and contiguous to an area of land previously
developed for a transmission line, transmission pipeline,
public highway or railway, or
(b)
a rail line designed to accommodate high-speed trains with
a design speed of ≥ 200 km/hour.
(2)
Assessment of a new project under subsection (1) does not
include the dismantling and abandonment phases.
