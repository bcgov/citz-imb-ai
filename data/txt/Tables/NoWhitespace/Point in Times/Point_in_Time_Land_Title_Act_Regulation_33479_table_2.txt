B.C. Reg. 334/79
Land Title Act
[s. 75 (3)]
Consent
Pursuant to section 15 (2) of B.C. Reg. 334/79, consent is
given to this plan of subdivision. The giving of this consent
shall not imply an obligation of the Crown in right of the
Province to improve, maintain or repair the forest service
road shown on this plan.
