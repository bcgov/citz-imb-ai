"Purchase Obligation" means an agreement to purchase goods or
services that is enforceable and legally binding on your
company that specifies all significant terms, including: fixed
or minimum quantities to be purchased; fixed, minimum or
variable price provisions; and the approximate timing of the
transaction.

"Other Long Term Obligations" means other long term
liabilities reflected on your company's balance sheet.
