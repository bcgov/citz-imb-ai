"Point in Time" Regulation Content
Private Training Act
Fees and Student Tuition Protection Fund Regulation
B.C. Reg. 140/2016
PC = 100 x (NCPFY / SEPFY)
where
PC
=
the percentage of approved claims against the fund in
relation to total students enrolled;
NCPFY
=
the number of claims against the fund filed by students of
the institution approved by the trustee under section 24
[authority of trustee in dealing with claims]
of the Act during the institution's previous fiscal year;
SEPFY
=
the number of students enrolled in approved programs of
instruction during the institution's previous fiscal year.
