COMMUNITY CHARTER
[SBC 2003] CHAPTER 26
Applicable provision
Period of disqualification
(1) (a)
[failure to take oath]
until the next general local election;
(1) (b)
[unexcused absence]
until the next general local election;
(1) (c)
[conflict]
until the next general local election;
(1) (d)
[unauthorized use of money]
for 3 years from the date of the vote to which the
disqualification relates;
(1) (e)
[election disqualifications]
in accordance with Part 3
[Electors and Elections]
of the
Local Government Act
.
