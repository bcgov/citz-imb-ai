Fireworks act [rsbc 1996] chapter 146 Definition
1 In this act, "fireworks" Includes cannon crackers, fireballs, firecrackers, mines, roman Candles, skyrockets, squibs, torpedoes and any other explosive Designated as a firework by regulation.
Application of act 2 This act applies to (a) A municipality that, by bylaw, declares that this act applies, and (b) A rural area designated by regulation.
Selling, giving or setting off fireworks 3 Except between october 24 and november 1 in any year, a person must Not sell, give, fire or set off fireworks.
Sale to minor 4 The fire commissioner may designate fireworks that may not be sold to A minor without the written permission of the parent or guardian of The minor.
Permission for public display 5 Fireworks may be sold to and discharged by a person or organization at A public display if the public display is held with the written Permission of (a) The fire commissioner, or (b) A local assistant of the fire commissioner who has jurisdiction in the Fire district where the public display is held.
