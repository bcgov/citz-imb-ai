Family day act [sbc 2012] chapter 24 Assented to may 31, 2012 Family day established as public holiday 1 The day in february prescribed under this act is a public holiday to Be observed as "family day".
Day set by regulation 2 The lieutenant governor in council may by regulation prescribe a day In february to be observed as "family day".
