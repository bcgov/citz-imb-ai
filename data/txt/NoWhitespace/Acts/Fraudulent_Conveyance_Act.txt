Fraudulent conveyance act [rsbc 1996] chapter 163 Fraudulent conveyance to avoid debt or duty of others 1 If made to delay, hinder or defraud creditors and others of their just And lawful remedies (a) A disposition of property, by writing or otherwise, (b) A bond, (c) A proceeding, or (d) An order Is void and of no effect against a person or the person's assignee or Personal representative whose rights and obligations are or might be Disturbed, hindered, delayed or defrauded, despite a pretence or other Matter to the contrary.
Application of act 2 This act does not apply to a disposition of property for good Consideration and in good faith lawfully transferred to a person who, At the time of the transfer, has no notice or knowledge of collusion Or fraud.
