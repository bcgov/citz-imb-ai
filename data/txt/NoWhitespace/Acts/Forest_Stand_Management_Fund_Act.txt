Forest stand management fund act [rsbc 1996] chapter 161 Stumpage levy 1 (1) By regulation, the lieutenant governor in council may impose a levy on Stumpage bearing timber for which stumpage is determined and payable Under the Forest act .
(2) If a regulation imposing a levy is made, the Forest act Applies to the enforcement and collection of the levy as if it were Stumpage payable under that act.
(3) [repealed 2004-36-135.] Power to make regulations 2 The lieutenant governor in council may make regulations referred to in Section 41 of the Interpretation act .
