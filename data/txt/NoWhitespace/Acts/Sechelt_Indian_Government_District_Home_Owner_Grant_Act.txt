Sechelt Indian Government District Home Owner Grant Act
[RSBC 1996] CHAPTER 417
Definition
1
In this Act,
"district"
means the Sechelt Indian Government District recognized by section 17
of the
Sechelt Indian Band Self-Government Act
(Canada).
Minister may pay grant
2
(1)
The minister may pay to the district, out of money appropriated for
that purpose, a grant in place of benefits payable under the
Home Owner Grant Act
.
(2)
The minister may attach conditions to a grant paid under subsection
(1) with respect to
(a)
the persons to whom the district must pass on the benefit of the
grant, and
(b)
how
(i)
the persons who are entitled to the benefit will be determined, and
(ii)
the amount of the benefits to them will be determined and calculated.
(3)
For the purposes of subsection (2), the minister may attach different
conditions for different classes of persons.
(4)
If the district fails to comply with a condition of the minister under
subsection (2) or (3), the minister may
(a)
withhold all or part of the grant appropriated for the purposes of
this Act in any subsequent year, and
(b)
order that a municipal benefit referred to in section 4 (1) of the
Sechelt Indian Government District Enabling Act
, not be paid or conferred.
(5)
If the minister makes an order under subsection (4) (b), the person
who is authorized to make the payment or confer the benefit must
comply with the minister's order.
