Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Election act Advertiser information regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Election act .
Additional information that may be requested in notice to produce Information
2 For the purposes of section 276.01 (3) (g) of the act, a notice to Produce information may require that an advertiser provide the Following information to the chief electoral officer:
(a) If known by the advertiser, the contact information of the individual Or organization that sponsored the election advertising message, Including a mailing address, residential address, email address, Telephone number and fax number of the individual or organization; (b) A copy of any document used by the advertiser to verify the identity Of the individual or organization referred to in Section 276.01 (3) (b) of the act; (c) Copies of financial transaction records in relation to payments Referred to in section 276.01 (3) (d) of the act; (d) The means by which the election advertising message was transmitted; (e) A copy or transcript of each version of the advertising message and The dates on which each version was transmitted.
