Link to point in time Recall and initiative act Advertising sponsor Disclosure report regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 16/2010, january 15, 2010] Advertising sponsor disclosure report forms 1 The following reports under the act must be provided in the form Attached as the schedule to this regulation:
(a) An initiative advertising disclosure report under section 101 of the Act;
(b) A recall advertising disclosure report under section 148 of the act.
Classes of advertising 2 A report referred to in section 1 of this regulation must separately Report the value of the following classes of advertising:
(a) Brochures, pamphlets, flyers and similar forms of advertising; (b) Newspaper, magazine, journal and similar forms of advertising; (c) Radio;
(d) Signs, such as lawn signs and billboards, and similar forms of Advertising;
(e) Television;
(f) Any other forms of advertising.
Supplementary reports as "amendments" to original reports 3 (1) If a supplementary report is required by section 101 (4) or 148 (5) of The act, the supplementary report must be in the form attached as the Schedule to this regulation.
(2) In the case of a first supplementary report, the form must be Completed as "amendment #1", and any further supplementary Reports must be sequentially numbered as further amendments.
