Land act Fossil definition regulation Definition
1 For the purposes of the Land act , "fossil" Means the preserved remains, traces or imprints of organisms from the Geological past, but does not include (a) Human remains or artifacts, or (b) The following substances:
(i) Limestone;
(ii) Dolomite;
(iii) Coal;
(iv) Petroleum;
(v) Natural gas.
