1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Election act .
Ordinary ballot 2 The form of ordinary ballot set out in schedule 1 is prescribed for The purposes of section 86 (2) of the act.
Write-in ballots 3 (1) A write-in ballot included in a mail-in voting package must be in the Form set out in schedule 2 or 3.
(2) If the form of write-in ballot set out in schedule 2 is used, text Identifying the electoral district of the voter may be printed on the Reverse side of the ballot.
(3) A write-in ballot used for any other alternative voting option or for A voting opportunity must be in the form set out in schedule 3.
