Motor Vehicle Act
Quality Assurance Vehicle
Initiative Regulation
Definitions
1
In this regulation:
"Act"
means the
Motor Vehicle Act
;
"authorized person"
has the same meaning as in section 25.01
[interpretation and application]
of the Motor Vehicle Act Regulations;
"designated constable"
has the same meaning as in section 1
[definitions]
of the
Police Act
;
"designated inspection facility"
has the same meaning as in section 25.01 of the Motor Vehicle Act
Regulations;
"designated policing unit"
has the same meaning as in section 1 of the
Police Act
;
"municipal constable"
has the same meaning as in section 1 of the
Police Act
;
"provincial constable"
has the same meaning as in section 1 of the
Police Act
;
"quality assurance vehicle initiative"
means an initiative designed to determine whether a designated
inspection facility or authorized person complies with the
requirements under the Act respecting vehicle inspection and
maintenance.
Participation in quality assurance vehicle initiative
2
(1)
For the purposes of the participation of persons, vehicles and
equipment in a quality assurance vehicle initiative, the director may
exempt from the provisions specified in section 4
(a)
the persons specified in section 3, and
(b)
all vehicles and equipment operated by those persons.
(2)
An exemption under subsection (1) may be unconditional or on any
condition the director considers desirable.
Persons who may be exempted
3
For the purposes of section 2, the following persons, and all vehicles
and equipment operated by those persons, are specified, when those
persons are acting in the course of their duties:
(a)
a person employed by or under contract to the ministry of the minister
responsible for the administration of the
Transportation Act
;
(b)
a provincial constable or a municipal constable;
(c)
a designated constable of a designated policing unit established under
the
Police Act
.

4
