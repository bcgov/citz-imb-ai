Local government act Regional district of Bulkley-nechako regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 193/2001] Economic development 1 The regional district of bulkley-nechako is granted the additional Power to provide economic development as an extended service.
Para-transit 2 The regional district of bulkley-nechako is granted the additional Power to provide para-transit as a local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 241/91.] Cemetery grant-in-aid 3 The regional district of bulkley-nechako is granted the additional Power to provide cemetery grant-in-aid as a local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 169/92.] Electric power distribution 4 The regional district of bulkley-nechako is granted the additional Power to provide, as a local service, electric power distribution to Birch bay on francois lake.
[en. b.c. reg. 418/92.] Telephone capital financing 5 The regional district of bulkley-nechako is granted the additional Power to authorize the acquisition of telephone capital financing as a Local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 436/92.] Water diversion works and maintenance 6 The regional district of bulkley-nechako is granted the additional Power to establish a water diversion works and maintenance service Specifically for the glacier gulch project, including the powers of a Municipality under sections 551 and 552 of the Municipal act 1 , as a local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 34/95.] Emergency program act 7 The regional district of bulkley-nechako is granted the same power That a municipality has as a local authority under the Emergency program act .
