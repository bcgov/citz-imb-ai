Financial administration act Collection and loan management branch regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 231/2001] Definition
1 In this regulation, "branch" Means the collection and loan management branch, ministry of Provincial revenue.
[am. b.c. reg. 231/2001.] Role of branch 2 The branch provides collection and loan administration services to the Government.
Branch may collect certain information 3 For the purpose of providing the services referred to in section 2 and Without limiting any other authority under which the branch may Collect information, the branch may collect the following information About persons who owe money to the government:
(a) Particulars of amounts owing; (b) Name, including any alias or business name; (c) Current and former addresses, including any e-mail addresses; (d) Birth date; (e) Social insurance number; (f) Name of spouse or next of kin; (g) Occupation;
(h) Name of employer; (i) Residence and business telephone numbers; and (j) Banking and credit information.
