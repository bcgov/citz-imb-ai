Offence act Admissible offences regulation Offences prescribed 1 The following offences under the Motor vehicle act Are prescribed for the purposes of section 15.3 (1) of the Offence act :
(a) Section 140 — disobeying construction signs; (b) Section 146 (1) — speeding in or outside a municipality; (c) Section 146 (3) — speeding against a highway sign; (d) Section 146 (7) — speeding against a municipal sign; (e) Section 147 (1) — speeding in a school zone; (f) Section 147 (2) — speeding in a playground zone.
