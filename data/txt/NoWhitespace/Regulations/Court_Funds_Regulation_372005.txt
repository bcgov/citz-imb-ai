Unclaimed property act Court funds regulation Definition
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Unclaimed property act ; "judicial officer" Means,
(a) In the case of funds in the court of appeal, a justice or registrar, (b) In the case of funds in the supreme court, a judge, master or Registrar, and (c) In the case of funds in the provincial court, a judge or registrar.
Court funds 2 For the purposes of the act, "unclaimed court funds" Means funds, other than judicial interim release funds, that have been Paid into court in a proceeding and that (a) Have remained in court for at least 5 years, and (b) Have been determined by a judicial officer for that court to be Unclaimed court funds.
