Open fires in outdoor stoves 19 (1) The circumstances in which a person described in section 5 (1) or 6 (1) of the act may light, fuel or use an open fire that is in an Outdoor stove, and is in or within 1 km of forest land or grass land, Are as follows:
(a) The person is not prohibited from doing so under another enactment; (b) To do so is safe and is likely to continue to be safe; (c) The person takes reasonable precautions to ensure the fire is Contained in the stove; (d) Before leaving the area, the person ensures that the fire is Extinguished.
(2) Without limiting subsection (1), a person who lights, fuels or uses an Open fire referred to in that subsection must ensure that the fire Does not spread beyond the outdoor stove.
