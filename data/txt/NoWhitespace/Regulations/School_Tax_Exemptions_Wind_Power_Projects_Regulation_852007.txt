School act School tax exemptions (wind power projects) regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the School act ; "wind power project" Means a power project that uses a wind turbine to generate or supply Electricity from the wind.
Approved class of alternative energy power project 2 Wind power projects are approved alternative energy power projects for Purposes of section 131.1 of the act and are exempt from school taxes To the extent set out in section 3 of the regulation.
What property is tax exempt?
3 The following improvements in respect of a wind power project are Exempt from school taxes:
(a) Any tower used to support a wind turbine; (b) The foundation for a tower referred to in paragraph (a).
