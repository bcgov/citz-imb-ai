Resort municipality of whistler act Workers' accommodation regulation Definition
1 For the purposes of section 8 (2) (g) of the Resort municipality of whistler act , "works and services" Means employee housing services and includes the acquisition and Improvement of land and the construction, operation and maintenance of Buildings for the accommodation of individuals who work within the Resort municipality of whistler.
