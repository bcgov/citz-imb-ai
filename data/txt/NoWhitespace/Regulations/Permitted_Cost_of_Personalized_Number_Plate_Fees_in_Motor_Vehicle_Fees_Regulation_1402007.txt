Insurance corporation act Permitted cost of personalized number plate Fees in motor vehicle fees regulation Fee under b.c. reg. 334/91 1 For the purposes of section 17 (1) of the Insurance corporation act , the corporation's permitted costs of its services in the schedule to The motor vehicle fees regulation, b.c. reg. 334/91, are the Following:
(a) The entire amount of the personalized number plate fee the insurance Corporation of british columbia collects under item 9 (a) for any Motor vehicle licensed under section 6 of the Commercial transport act And that has a licensed gross vehicle weight exceeding 4 500 kg; (b) The entire amount of the personalized number plate renewal fee the Insurance corporation of british columbia collects under item 9 (b) For any motor vehicle licensed under section 6 of the Commercial transport act And that has a licensed gross vehicle weight exceeding 4 500 kg.
