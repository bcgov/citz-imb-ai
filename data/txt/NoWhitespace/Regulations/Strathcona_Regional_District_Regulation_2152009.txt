Local government act Strathcona regional district regulation Declared nuisances 1 The strathcona regional district is granted the additional power to Exercise the powers conferred on the council of a municipality under Division 12 of part 3 of the Community charter , except the powers conferred under sections 73 and 75, subject to the Terms and conditions set out in that division.
