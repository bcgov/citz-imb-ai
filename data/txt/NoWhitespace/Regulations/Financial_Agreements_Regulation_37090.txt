Financial administration act Financial agreements regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 100/99] Prescribed classes 1 The prescribed classes of financial agreements for the purposes of Section 76 (1) (c) of the Financial administration act Are:
(a) Options to enter into any of the agreements referred to in section 76 (1) (b) of the Financial administration act ; (b) Forward security purchase or sale agreements; (c) Options to enter into forward security purchase or sale agreements; (d) Equity-index linked futures agreements; (e) Equity-index linked exchange agreements; (f) Options to enter into equity-index linked futures agreements or Equity-index linked exchange agreements; (g) Options to purchase or sell any security.
