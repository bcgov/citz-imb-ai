Insurance corporation act Permitted costs of administering drivers' test And missed test fees in motor vehicle fees regulation Fee under b.c. reg. 334/91 1 For the purposes of section 17 (1) of the Insurance corporation act , the permitted costs of services in the schedule to the motor vehicle Fees regulation, b.c. reg. 334/91, are (a) The entire amount of the drivers' test fee the insurance corporation Of british columbia collects under Item 4 (b) (ii) (b) for providing those services, (b) The entire amount of the drivers' test fee the insurance corporation Of british columbia collects under Item 4 (b) (vii) (b) (ii) for providing those Services, and (c) The entire amount of the missed test fee the insurance corporation of British columbia collects under item 4 (b) (vii) To (xi) for providing those services.
Note:
This regulation replaces b.c. reg. 42/2000.
