Link to point in time Business practices and consumer protection act, Cremation, interment and funeral services act and Motion picture act Fee setting criteria regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 104/2017, april 1, 2017] Fees or charges payable in respect of licence 1 For the purposes of section 179 of the Business practices and consumer protection act , section 59 of the Cremation, interment and funeral services act And section 12.6 of the Motion picture act , the administrative authority must comply with a fee setting process That is in accordance with the following criteria:
(a) Before setting fees, charges or other amounts under those sections, The authority must consult with (i) The industry the authority considers affected, and (ii) Any parties the authority considers interested; (b) The authority must give at least 90 days' notice to the affected Industry before a fee, charge or other amount set by the authority has Effect.
