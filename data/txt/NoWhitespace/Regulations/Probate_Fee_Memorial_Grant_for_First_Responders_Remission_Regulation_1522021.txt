Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Financial administration act Probate fee (memorial grant for first Responders) remission regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Probate fee act ; "memorial grant program" Means the memorial grant program for first responders established by The government of canada.
Remission of probate fee 2 Authorization is given for the remission of a fee paid or payable by a Person under section 2 of the act in respect of an amount Provided to the estate of a deceased under the memorial grant program If the fee is paid or becomes payable on or after July 1, 2021.
