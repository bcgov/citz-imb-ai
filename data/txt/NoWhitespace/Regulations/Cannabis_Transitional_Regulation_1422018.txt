B.c. reg. 142/2018 O.c. 358/2018 Deposited july 9, 2018 Cannabis control and licensing act and cannabis distribution act Cannabis transitional regulation Authority for government to perform certain activities 1 (1) In this section:
"cannabis" Means fresh marihuana, dried marihuana and cannabis oil, as those Terms are defined in section 1 (1) of the access to cannabis for Medical purposes regulations made under the Controlled drugs and substances act (canada) and marihuana plants or seeds, within the meaning of Those regulations; "provide" Has the same meaning as in section 2 (1) of the Controlled drugs and substances act (canada).
(2) The government is authorized to possess, provide, send, deliver And transport cannabis and offer to perform any of those Activities.
Restriction on sale 2 For the purposes of section 3 of the Cannabis distribution act , the government may not sell cannabis.
Repeal
3 This regulation is repealed when section 204 (1) of the Cannabis act (canada) comes into force.
