Local government act Regional district of Fraser-fort george regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 426/92] Additional powers granted 1 The regional district of fraser-fort george is granted the additional Power
(a) To establish and operate an airport service as a local service, (b) To operate an enhanced 9-1-1 emergency response system as an extended Service,
(c) To authorize the acquisition of electric power capital financing as a Local service, (d) To undertake the operation and maintenance of cemeteries as a local Service,
(e) To authorize the acquisition of telephone capital financing as a local Service, and (f) To authorize the acquisition of natural gas capital financing as a Local service.
