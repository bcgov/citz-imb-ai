1 In this regulation, "stps" Means the stl'atl'imx tribal police service.
Police (disposal of property) regulation 2 The provisions of b.c. reg. 87/91, the police (disposal of property) Regulation, apply to the stps as though each reference in b.c. reg.
87/91 to (a) A "commissioner" were a reference to the chief officer of the stps, (b) "provincial police force" were a reference to the stps, and (c) "province" were a reference to the band councils of the stl'atl'imx Nation.
