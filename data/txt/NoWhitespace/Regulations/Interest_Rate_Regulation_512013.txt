Land tax deferment act Interest rate regulation Interest rate 1 (1) For the purposes of sections 8 (2) and 9 (2) of the Land tax deferment act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning april 1, 2013 is 1.00% a year.
(2) For the purposes of sections 8 (3.1) and 9 (5) of the Land tax deferment act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning april 1, 2013 is 3.00% a year.
