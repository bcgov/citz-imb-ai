Human resource facility act Human resource facility regulation Designations of human resource facilities 1 The following are designated as human resource facilities:
(a) All facilities referred to in section 1 (a) to (d) of the act; (b) The following categories of human resource facilities or programs:
(i) Child or youth mental health services; (ii) Youth justice services.
