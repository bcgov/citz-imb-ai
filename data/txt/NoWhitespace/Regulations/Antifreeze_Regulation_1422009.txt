Environmental management act Antifreeze regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"antifreeze" Includes engine coolant; "motor vehicle" Has the same meaning as in the Motor vehicle act .
Prohibition
2 Subject to section 3, a person must not sell antifreeze that contains 10% or more ethylene glycol by weight.
Exceptions
3 Section 2 does not apply to antifreeze (a) That contains at least 30 but no more than 50 parts per million by Weight of denatonium benzonate bittering agent, (b) Contained within a motor vehicle for sale or lease, or (c) Of a volume greater than 200 litres contained in a single container.
Offence and penalty 4 A person who contravenes section 2 commits an offence and is liable on Conviction to a fine not exceeding $200Â 000.
