Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Financial administration act Retention of driver's licence fees regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "corporation" Means the insurance corporation of british columbia.
Retention of fees 2 The corporation is authorized to retain, as remuneration for services Performed by agents who are retained by or enter into an agreement With the corporation under section 9 (2) of the Insurance corporation act To provide driver licensing and related services, up to $13 of each Fee collected by the corporation under item 4 (a) (i) Of the schedule to the motor vehicle fees regulation.
