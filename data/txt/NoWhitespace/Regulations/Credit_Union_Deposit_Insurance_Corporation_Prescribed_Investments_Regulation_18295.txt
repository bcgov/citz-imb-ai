Financial institutions act Credit union deposit insurance corporation Prescribed investments regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Financial institutions act ; "pension plan" Has the same meaning as in the Pension benefits standards act .
Prescribed investments 2 For the purposes of section 272 (1) (d) of the act but subject to Section 272 (2) of the act, the deposit insurance corporation may make Any investment that a pension plan is capable of making under the Pension benefits standards act .
