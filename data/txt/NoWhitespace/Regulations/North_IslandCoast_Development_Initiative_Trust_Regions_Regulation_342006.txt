1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the North island-coast development initiative trust act ; "electoral area" Has the same meaning as in the Local government act .
Regions defined 2 For the purposes of the act:
"central south island region" Means that area of british columbia comprising the following:
(a) In the powell river regional district, electoral area e; (b) In the regional district of nanaimo, electoral areas a, b, c and e, The city of nanaimo, the city of parksville and the district of Lantzville;
(c) The cowichan valley regional district; (d) In the capital regional district, the juan de fuca electoral area, the Saltspring island electoral area and the southern gulf islands Electoral area; "north island-sunshine coast region" Means that area of british columbia comprising the following:
(a) The regional district of mount waddington; (b) The regional district of comox-strathcona; (c) The regional district of alberni-clayoquot; (d) In the regional district of nanaimo, electoral areas f, g and h, and The town of qualicum beach; (e) The powell river regional district, except electoral area e; (f) The sunshine coast regional district.
