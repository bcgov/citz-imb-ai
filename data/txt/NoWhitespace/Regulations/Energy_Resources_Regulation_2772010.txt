Utilities commission act Energy resources regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Utilities commission act .
Prescribed energy resources 2 The following types of energy resources are prescribed for the Purposes of section 65 (2) (a) (ii) of the act:
(a) Petroleum, as defined in section 1 of the Petroleum and natural gas act ; (b) Liquid hydrocarbons; (c) Petroleum products.
