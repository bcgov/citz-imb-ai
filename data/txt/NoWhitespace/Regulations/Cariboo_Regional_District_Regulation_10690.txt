Local government act Cariboo regional district regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 27/93] Grant-in-aid: south cariboo home support service association 1 The cariboo regional district is granted the additional power to Provide a contribution (grant-in-aid) to the south cariboo home Support service association as an additional extended service.
Grant-in-aid: cariboo tourist association 2 The cariboo regional district is granted the additional power to Provide a contribution (grant-in-aid) to the cariboo tourist Association as an additional extended service.
[en. b.c. reg. 141/90.] Paratransit
3 The cariboo regional district is granted the additional power to Provide paratransit as a local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 22/92.] Economic development 4 The cariboo regional district is granted the additional power to Provide economic development as an extended service.
[en. b.c. reg. 55/92.] Economic development grants-in-aid 5 The cariboo regional district is granted the additional power to Provide economic development grants-in-aid as an extended service.
[en. b.c. reg. 72/92.] Natural gas capital financing 6 The cariboo regional district is granted the additional power to Authorize the acquisition of natural gas capital financing as a local Service.
