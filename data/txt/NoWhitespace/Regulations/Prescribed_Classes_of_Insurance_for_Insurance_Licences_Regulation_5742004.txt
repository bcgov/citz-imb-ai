Link to point in time Financial institutions act Prescribed classes of insurance for Insurance licences regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 204/2011, july 1, 2012] Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Financial institutions act .
Prescribed classes of insurance for which insurance licence may be Issued by the insurance council 2 For the purpose of section 174 (2) (b) of the act, the prescribed Classes of insurance are the following:
(a) General insurance; (b) Life insurance as defined in section 1 (1) of the classes of insurance Regulation;
(c) Accident and sickness insurance as defined in section 1 (1) of the Classes of insurance regulation; (d) Travel insurance as defined in the insurance licensing exemption Regulation.
