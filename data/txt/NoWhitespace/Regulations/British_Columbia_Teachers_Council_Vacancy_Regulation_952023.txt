1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Teachers act .
Appointment to fill vacancy on british columbia teachers' council 2 For the purposes of section 11 (10) of the act, (a) Before appointing a person to fill a vacancy, the minister must Consult with the first nations education authority, and (b) A person must meet both of the following eligibility requirements to Be appointed:
(i) The person must possess substantial knowledge of and experience in the First nations education system in british columbia; (ii) The person must provide to the minister the person's written consent To be appointed.
