Utilities commission act Egp project regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Utilities commission act ; "egp project" Has the same meaning as in direction no. 5 to the british columbia Utilities commission.
Exclusion
2 The egp project is excluded from the operation of section 45 (1) of The act.
