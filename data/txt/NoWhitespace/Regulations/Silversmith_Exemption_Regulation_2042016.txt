Utilities commission act Silversmith exemption regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Utilities commission act ; "electricity purchase agreement" Means the energy supply contract titled "electricity purchase Agreement", between silversmith and the authority, that comes into Effect august 1, 2016; "silversmith" Means silversmith power and light corporation and its successors and Assigns.
Exemption
2 Silversmith and the authority are exempt from section 71 of the act in Respect of the electricity purchase agreement.
