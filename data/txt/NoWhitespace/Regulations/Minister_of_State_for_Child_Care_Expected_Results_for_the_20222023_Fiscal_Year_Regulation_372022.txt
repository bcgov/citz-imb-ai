Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Balanced budget and ministerial accountability act Minister of state for child care Expected results for the 2022/2023 Fiscal year regulation Application of this regulation 1 This regulation applies to the minister of state for child care for The 2022/2023 fiscal year.
Definition
2 In this regulation, "act" Means the Balanced budget and ministerial accountability act .
Minister of state for child care 3 The expected results that the minister of state for child care is to Achieve for the purposes of section 5 (3) of the act are as follows:
(a) Continue to implement initiatives that support government's universal Child care plan that provides affordable, accessible, inclusive and High-quality care and early learning for every child whose family Wants or needs it; (b) Engage with the following to further inform implementation of Universal and inclusive child care:
(i) All levels of government; (ii) Indigenous governing bodies and other entities representing indigenous Peoples;
(iii) School boards, child care providers and other sector stakeholders; (c) Continue to support the transfer of responsibility for child care Delivery to the ministry of education with the goal of completing the Transfer by 2023; (d) Continue to monitor the implementation of the multilateral early Learning and child care framework agreement with the federal Government;
(e) Submit to cabinet a report on the results referred to in paragraphs (a) through (d) on or before march 31, 2023.
