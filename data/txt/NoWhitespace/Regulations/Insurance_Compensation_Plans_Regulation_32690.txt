Financial institutions act Insurance compensation plans regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 83/91] Interpretation
1 In this regulation "act" Means the Financial institutions act .
Designated compensation plan 2 For the purposes of section 66 (2) of the act, the following are Designated as insurance compensation plans:
(a) Property and casualty insurance compensation plan, administered by the Property and casualty insurance compensation corporation; (b) Canadian life and health insurance compensation plan, administered by The canadian life and health insurance compensation corporation.
