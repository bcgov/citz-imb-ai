Financial Institutions Act
Insurance Compensation Plans Regulation
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 83/91]
Interpretation
1
In this regulation
"Act"
means the
Financial Institutions Act
.
Designated compensation plan
2
For the purposes of section 66 (2) of the Act, the following are
designated as insurance compensation plans:
(a)
Property and Casualty Insurance Compensation Plan, administered by the
Property and Casualty Insurance Compensation Corporation;
(b)
Canadian Life and Health Insurance Compensation Plan, administered by
the Canadian Life and Health Insurance Compensation Corporation.
[en. B.C. Reg. 83/91.]
[Provisions of the
Financial Institutions Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 141, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
sections 66 (2), 160 (2) (c) (ii) and 289]
