Assessment act 'residential building' regulation Definition
1 In section 30 (2) of the Assessment act , "residential building" Means a building used or designed to be used in whole or in part for Residential purposes and includes an associated outbuilding of and Other improvement to a building used or designed to be used in whole Or in part for residential purposes, but does not include a floating Mobile home.
