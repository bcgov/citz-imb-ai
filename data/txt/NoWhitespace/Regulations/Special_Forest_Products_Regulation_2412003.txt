Forest act Special forest products regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 104/2009, march 13, 2009] Special forest products 1 The following are designated to be special forest products under Section 1 of the Forest act :
(a) Christmas trees; (b) Firewood;
(c) Mining timbers; (d) Stakes and sticks; (e) Cants;
(f) Posts and rails (split and round); (g) Shake and shingle bolts, blocks and blanks; (h) Shakes;
(i) Woodchips;
(j) Hogged tree material.
[am. b.c. reg. 104/2009, s. 1.] Note:
This regulation replaces b.c. reg. 355/87.
