Ecological reserve entry, use and occupation restricted 2 (1) Subject to this section, a person must not enter, use or occupy an Ecological reserve unless authorized by a permit issued under Section 5.1 (1) of the act.
(2) A person may, without a permit, enter and travel in or through, by Foot or by non-motorized boat, an ecological reserve other than an Ecological reserve set out in the schedule.
(3) Subsection (2) does not authorize entry to an ecological reserve If a heat or rainfall warning issued by environment and climate change Canada is in effect in relation to an area that encompasses, overlaps With or abuts the ecological reserve.
(4) A permittee must comply with the terms and conditions included in the Permittee's permit under section 5.1 (5) of the act.
Notice of conditions 3 The minister must, if satisfied that the circumstances set out in Section 2 (3) exist in relation to an ecological reserve, Provide public notice, in the manner the minister considers Appropriate, that entry to the ecological reserve is prohibited under Section 2 unless authorized by a permit.
Application for permit 4 (1) For the purposes of section 5.1 (1) of the act, a person may Apply to the minister for a permit authorizing the use and occupation Of an ecological reserve for ecological scientific research or Educational purposes.
(2) An application for a permit must include the following:
(a) A description of the proposed scientific research or educational uses And activities; (b) A description and map of the areas of land that would be affected by The uses and activities; (c) A description of the proposed means of entry to and access through the Ecological reserve; (d) The proposed duration of the uses and activities; (e) The expected ecological impact of the uses and activities; (f) The number of individuals proposed to enter the ecological reserve And, if known, their names; (g) The name of the individual proposed to direct the uses and activities.
Hearing
5 (1) The minister is not required to hold a hearing before suspending, Modifying or cancelling a permit.
(2) If the minister holds a hearing, it may be held in writing, Electronically or in person, or by any combination of written, Electronic or in-person hearings.
Prohibitions
6 (1) A person must not do or attempt to do any of the following in an Ecological reserve:
(a) Prospect or explore for, or extract, coal, petroleum or natural gas; (b) Drill for or divert water or dam a waterway.
(2) A person must not do or attempt to do any of the following unless Authorized to do so under a permit issued under Section 5.1 (1) of the act:
(a) Introduce a plant or animal species in an ecological reserve; (b) Operate a vehicle or a motorized boat, or conduct a take-off or Landing of an aircraft or a drone, in an ecological reserve; (c) Deposit waste in an ecological reserve; (d) Allow or cause waste to flow onto or seep into land in an ecological Reserve or to flow or seep into water in an ecological reserve; (e) Allow or cause waste to be emitted into the air from within an Ecological reserve; (f) Mine or quarry earth, soil, peat, marl, sand, gravel, rock or another Mineral resource in an ecological reserve; (g) Make an alteration to land on or beneath its surface in an ecological Reserve;
(h) Damage, destroy or take a sample of a natural resource in, or remove a Natural resource from, an ecological reserve; (i) Disturb an animal in an ecological reserve; (j) Hunt or trap in, catch fish in, or take fish or wildlife from, an Ecological reserve; (k) Touch or feed wildlife in an ecological reserve or entice wildlife in An ecological reserve to approach by any means, including by holding Out or setting out a decoy, lure, food or bait or by using a wildlife Caller;
(l) Light, fuel or use a fire in an ecological reserve; (m) Allow a domesticated animal to graze in an ecological reserve; (n) Cut or fell timber in an ecological reserve; (o) Construct or modify a road or trail in an ecological reserve; (p) Camp in an ecological reserve; (q) Establish, construct or modify a building or other structure in an Ecological reserve; (r) Smoke cannabis, or hold lighted cannabis, in an ecological reserve; (s) Use or hold, in an ecological reserve, an activated e-cigarette that Is vapourizing cannabis; (t) Smoke tobacco, or hold lighted tobacco, in an ecological reserve; (u) Use or hold, in an ecological reserve, an activated e-cigarette that Is vapourizing an e-substance other than cannabis.
(3) This section does not apply in relation to a use or activity carried Out
(a) Under section 18 of the Wildfire act , or (b) By or on behalf of the minister for the purposes of one or more of the Following:
(i) Restoring an ecosystem; (ii) Protecting a rare or endangered native plant or animal; (iii) Carrying out ecological scientific research or education.
Offences
7 A person who contravenes any of the following commits an offence:
(a) Section 2 (1) or (4); (b) Section 6 (1) or (2).
