1 The regional district of kootenay boundary is granted the additional Power to provide transit as a local service.
Emergency programs 2 The regional district of kootenay boundary is granted the additional Power to provide emergency programs as an extended service under the Emergency program act .
[en. b.c. reg. 148/99.] Firecrackers and fireworks 3 The regional district of kootenay boundary is granted the additional Power to, by bylaw, regulate, prohibit and impose requirements in Relation to the use of firecrackers and fireworks.
