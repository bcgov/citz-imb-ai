Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Financial administration act Provincial sales tax (readymix Delivery charge) remission regulation Definitions
1 (1) In this regulation:
"act" Means the Provincial sales tax act ; "specified delivery charge" Means a standby or waiting time charge included by section 10 (2) (g) Of the act in the purchase price of ready-mixed concrete.
(2) The definitions in section 1 of the act apply to this regulation.
Remission of penalty 2 Authorization is given for the remission of a penalty under section 203 (1) of the act imposed on a collector who has not levied tax on The portion of the purchase price of ready-mixed concrete that is a Specified delivery charge, if the sale occurred on or after april 1, 2013 and on or before october 14, 2022.
