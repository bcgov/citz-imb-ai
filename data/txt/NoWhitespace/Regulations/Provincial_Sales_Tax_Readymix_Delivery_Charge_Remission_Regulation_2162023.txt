Financial Administration Act
Provincial Sales Tax (Readymix
Delivery Charge) Remission Regulation
Definitions
1
(1)
In this regulation:
"Act"
means the
Provincial Sales Tax Act
;
"specified delivery charge"
means a standby or waiting time charge included by section 10 (2) (g)
of the Act in the purchase price of ready-mixed concrete.
(2)
The definitions in section 1 of the Act apply to this regulation.
Remission of penalty
2
Authorization is given for the remission of a penalty under section
203 (1) of the Act imposed on a collector who has not levied tax on
the portion of the purchase price of ready-mixed concrete that is a
specified delivery charge, if the sale occurred on or after April 1,
2013 and on or before October 14, 2022.
