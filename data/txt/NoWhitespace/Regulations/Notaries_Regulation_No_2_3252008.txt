Notaries act Notaries regulation no. 2 Special fund amount 1 For the purposes of sections 20 (2) and (7) of the act, the prescribed Amount is $3 million.
Notice of suspension or termination 2 For the purpose of section 40 (a) of the act, notice of the suspension Or termination of membership must be published (a) On the society's website, and (b) In a newspaper having general circulation in each area in which the Member or former member maintained an office at the time of suspension Or termination.
