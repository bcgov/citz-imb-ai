Sechelt indian government district enabling act Sechelt indian government district Municipal benefits regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 224/95] Municipal benefits 1 The sechelt indian government district is entitled to or eligible for The following municipal benefits:
(a) Grants under the Local government grants act ; (b) Subject to an agreement being entered into under section 2 of the Provincial-municipal partnership act 1 , incentives for economic development and participation in economic Development programs of the government under that act.
