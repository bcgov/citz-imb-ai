Designation of transit-oriented areas under the Vancouver charter 2 (1) For the purposes of section 630 [transition — interim designation of transit-oriented areas] Of the Vancouver charter , the area shown shaded in yellow on the attached map referred to in Column 2 of the table in schedule 2 is designated as a Transit-oriented area in respect of the city to the extent the area is Located within the city.
(2) The transit station set out in column 1 of the table in schedule 2 is The transit station in relation to which the transit-oriented area Referred to in subsection (1) is measured.
