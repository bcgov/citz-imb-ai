1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Southern interior development initiative trust act ; "electoral area" Has the same meaning as in the Local government act .
Regions defined 2 For the purposes of the act:
"columbia-kootenay region" Means that area of british columbia comprising the following:
(a) The regional district of kootenay boundary; (b) The regional district of central kootenay; (c) In the columbia-shuswap regional district, electoral areas a and b, The city of revelstoke and the town of golden; "thompson-okanagan region" Means that area of british columbia comprising the following:
(a) In the thompson-nicola regional district, electoral areas a, b, j, l, M, n, o and p, the city of kamloops, the city of merritt and the Village of chase; (b) In the fraser valley regional district, electoral areas a and b and The district of hope; (c) The regional district of okanagan-similkameen; (d) The regional district of central okanagan; (e) The regional district of north okanagan; (f) In the columbia shuswap regional district, electoral areas c, d, e and F, the city of salmon arm and the district of sicamous.
