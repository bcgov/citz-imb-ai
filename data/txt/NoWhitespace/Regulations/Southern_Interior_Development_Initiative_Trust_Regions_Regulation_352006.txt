Southern Interior Development Initiative Trust Act
Southern Interior Development Initiative Trust Regions Regulation
Definitions
1
In this regulation:
"Act"
means the
Southern Interior Development Initiative Trust Act
;
"electoral area"
has the same meaning as in the
Local Government Act
.
Regions defined
2
For the purposes of the Act:
"Columbia-Kootenay region"
means that area of British Columbia comprising the following:
(a)
the Regional District of Kootenay Boundary;
(b)
the Regional District of Central Kootenay;
(c)
in the Columbia-Shuswap Regional District, electoral areas A and B,
the City of Revelstoke and the Town of Golden;
"Thompson-Okanagan region"
means that area of British Columbia comprising the following:
(a)
in the Thompson-Nicola Regional District, electoral areas A, B, J, L,
M, N, O and P, the City of Kamloops, the City of Merritt and the
Village of Chase;
(b)
in the Fraser Valley Regional District, electoral areas A and B and
the District of Hope;
(c)
the Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen;
(d)
the Regional District of Central Okanagan;
(e)
the Regional District of North Okanagan;
(f)
in the Columbia Shuswap Regional District, electoral areas C, D, E and
F, the City of Salmon Arm and the District of Sicamous.
[Provisions of the
Southern Interior Development Initiative Trust Act
, S.B.C. 2005, c. 39, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
sections 1 and 25]
