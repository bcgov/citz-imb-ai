1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Financial administration act .
Public authorities designated as government bodies 2 Each of the following public authorities is designated, under Paragraph (d) of the definition of "government body" in Section 1 of the act, as a government body for the purposes of The act:
(a) The greater vancouver sewerage and drainage district; (b) The greater vancouver water district; (c) An improvement district as defined in the Local government act .
Organizations deemed to be government bodies 3 The providence health care society is an organization deemed, under Section 88 (2) (b) of the act, to be a government body For the purposes of parts 8, 9 and 9.1 of the act.
