Sustainable environment fund act Sustainable environment fund revenue regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Sustainable environment fund act .
Prescribed revenue 2 All revenue derived from fees payable under the Integrated pest management act In respect the following is prescribed for the purposes of section 4 (g) of the act:
(a) Licences;
(b) Examinations for and renewals of certificates; (c) Permits;
(d) Confirmations.
