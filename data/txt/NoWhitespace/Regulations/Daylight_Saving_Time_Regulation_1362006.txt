B.c. reg. 136/2006 O.c. 349/2006 Deposited may 18, 2006 Effective january 1, 2007 This consolidation is current to march 5, 2024.
Interpretation act Daylight saving time regulation Daylight saving time 1 In british columbia each year the time for general purposes shall be 7 Hours behind greenwich mean time during the period commencing at 2 O'clock in the forenoon on the second sunday of march and ending at 2 O'clock in the forenoon on the first sunday of november.
Note:
This regulation repeals b.c. reg. 80/2003.
