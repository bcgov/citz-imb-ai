1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Community charter ; "corporation" Means the sun peaks resort corporation; "councillor" Means a councillor appointed to the council of sun peaks mountain Resort municipality under section 25 (1) (b) (i) [appointment by minister] Of the Local government act .
[am. b.c. reg. 117/2018, s. 5.] Nature of pecuniary interest 2 A pecuniary interest in the nature of remuneration, expenses, Benefits, dividends, profits or other amounts payable to a councillor As a result of the councillor being a director, senior officer or Shareholder or an employee of the corporation, or of a subsidiary or Parent of the corporation, is prescribed for the purposes of section 104 (1) (e) [exceptions from conflict provisions] Of the act.
Exception to restriction on use of insider information 3 Section 108
[restrictions on use of insider information] Of the act does not apply to a councillor in respect of a pecuniary Interest of the nature prescribed under section 2 during the term Of the councillor's appointment.
