Election act Election advertising regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Election act .
Advertising exempted from sponsor identification 2 Election advertising transmitted by any of the following articles is Exempt from the requirements of section 231 (1) [sponsor identification] Of the act:
(a) Clothing;
(b) Novelty items, including wearable novelty items such as buttons, Badges, wrist bands and necklaces; (c) Small items of nominal value that are intended for personal use.
