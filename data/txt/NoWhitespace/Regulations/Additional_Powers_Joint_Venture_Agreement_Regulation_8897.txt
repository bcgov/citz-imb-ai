Local government act Additional powers joint venture Agreement regulation Joint venture agreement 1 The regional district of nanaimo, the city of parksville and the town Of qualicum beach are granted the additional power to enter into and Amend a joint venture agreement to establish a joint venture to Design, construct, install, own, operate, maintain and replace a bulk Water supply facility, and for all related purposes, within the Boundaries of the regional district of nanaimo.
Condition
2 The regional district of nanaimo must, before entering into the joint Venture agreement referred to in section 1, enact a bulk water Supply local service establishment bylaw in respect of the rural areas To be served by the joint venture.
Amendments
3 An amendment to the joint venture agreement referred to in section 1 Requires the approval of the inspector of municipalities.
