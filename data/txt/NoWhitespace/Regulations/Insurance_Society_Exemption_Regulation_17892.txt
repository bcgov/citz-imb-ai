Financial institutions act Insurance society exemption regulation Interpretation
1 In this regulation "act" Means the Financial institutions act .
Exemption from section 196 2 Section 196 of the act does not apply to a society, described in that Section, in respect of a contract between it and a member of the Society if each of the following conditions is fulfilled:
(a) The society's assets exceed its liabilities by at least $3 million; (b) The contract with the member (i) Provides health care benefits or income replacement benefits covering A period of disability because of illness or injury, and (ii) Pays insurance money not exceeding $100 000 during a 24 month Benefit period.
