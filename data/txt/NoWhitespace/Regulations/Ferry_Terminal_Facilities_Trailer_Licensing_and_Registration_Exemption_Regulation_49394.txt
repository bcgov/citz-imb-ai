Motor vehicle act Ferry terminal facilities trailer Licensing and registration Exemption regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 161/2007, june 1, 2007] Power of corporation to exempt 1 (1) The insurance corporation of british columbia is empowered to exempt Trailers operated by british columbia ferry services inc. from the Licensing and registration requirements of the Motor vehicle act .
(2) An exemption under subsection (1) may be given for a trailer only if The trailer (a) Has at least the minimum third party liability insurance coverage Required under the Insurance (vehicle) act , (b) Is operated only in and around a ferry terminal facility, and (c) Is operated in accordance with any other conditions that may be Specified by the insurance corporation of british columbia.
