Link to point in time Health authorities act Hospital transfer regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 211/2015, november 28, 2016] Transfer of assets and liabilities 1 The assets and liabilities of a society that has been designated as a Hospital under the Hospital act May be transferred to a board or council by (a) The society agreeing to the transfer by special resolution as defined In section 1 of the Societies act , and (b) The board or council agreeing to assume the assets and liabilities by Bylaw passed by a majority of not less than 75% of the votes of the Members of the board or council voting in person or by proxy after Receiving at least 14 days' notice of the proposed bylaw.
