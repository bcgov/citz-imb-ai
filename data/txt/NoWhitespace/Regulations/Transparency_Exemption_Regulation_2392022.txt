Land owner transparency act Transparency exemption regulation Exemption from requirement to file 1 The following persons are exempt from the requirement to file a Transparency report under the Land owner transparency act :
(a) Society of transition houses; (b) A corporation that is a member, as defined in the Societies act , of the society of transition houses at the time that it is required To file a transparency report; (c) Ki-low-na friendship society; (d) The linda krystina housing society.
