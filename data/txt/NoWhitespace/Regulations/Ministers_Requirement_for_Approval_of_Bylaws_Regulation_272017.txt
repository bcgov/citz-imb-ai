Local government act Minister's requirement for approval of bylaws regulation North coast regional district 1 For the purposes of section 585 (1) [ministerial regulations requiring bylaw approval] Of the Local government act , approval by the minister is required before north coast official Community plan bylaw no. 608, 2017, may be adopted by the north coast Regional district.
