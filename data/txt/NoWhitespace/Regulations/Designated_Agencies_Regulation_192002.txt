Adult guardianship act Designated agencies regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 38/2007, february 26, 2007] Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Adult guardianship act ; "health region" Means an area that under the regional health boards regulation, b.c.
Reg. 293/2001, constitutes a region for a regional health board; "regional health board" Means a board continued or designated under the regional health boards Regulation, b.c. reg. 293/2001.
Community living services for adults program 2 (1) For the purpose of performing functions under part 3 of the act in Relation to adults resident anywhere in british columbia who are Eligible to receive community living support under the Community living authority act , community living british columbia is designated as an agency.
(2) A designation under any other section of this regulation does not Authorize or require another designated agency to perform functions Under part 3 of the act in relation to adults who are eligible to Receive community living support under the Community living authority act .
[am. b.c. reg. 238/2005.] Health regions 3 For the purpose of performing functions under part 3 of the act in Relation to adults resident in a health region for which the following Regional health boards are responsible, but excluding adults referred To in section 4 of this regulation, each regional health board is Designated as an agency for its region:
(a) Fraser health authority; (b) Interior health authority; (c) Northern health authority; (d) Vancouver coastal health authority; (e) Vancouver island health authority.
[am. b.c. reg. 38/2007, s. (a).] Providence health care 4 Providence health care society is designated as an agency for the Purposes of performing functions under part 3 of the act in relation To adults who are patients or persons in care at hospitals or Facilities owned and operated by providence health care society.
[en. b.c. reg. 38/2007, s. (b).] Note:
