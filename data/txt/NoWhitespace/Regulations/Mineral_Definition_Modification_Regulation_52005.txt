Mineral tenure act Mineral definition modification regulation Fossil exclusion 1 For the purpose of paragraph (g) of the definition of "mineral" in Section 1 of the Mineral tenure act , a fossil, defined as follows, is not a mineral:
"fossil" Does not include limestone, dolomite, coal, petroleum or natural gas.
