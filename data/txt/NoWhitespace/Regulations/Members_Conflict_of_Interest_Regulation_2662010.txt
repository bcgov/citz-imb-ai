1 In this regulation:
"insider" Means a person who owns more than 10% of the voting shares or is a Director or senior officer of a public corporation; "material change" Means an acquisition or disposition, whether in whole or in part, Occurring after a member files a disclosure statement, of any asset, Liability, financial interest or source of income of a member, the Member's spouse or a minor child of the member, or of a private Corporation controlled by any of them, if the change or event would Reasonably be expected to have a significant effect on the information Previously disclosed; "minor child" Means a child under the age of 19 years; "public corporation" Means a corporation whose shares are publicly traded; "self-directed" , when used in relation to an investment, means an investment in which A person or the person's investment dealer or agent invests as Selected or directed by the person.
