Public Service Act
Executive Staff Maximum Salary Regulation
Maximum salaries
1
(1)
The salary payable to a person who is appointed Chief of Staff, Office
of the Premier, by the Lieutenant Governor in Council, is not to
exceed $299 215.05.
(2)
The salary payable to a person who is appointed Deputy Chief of Staff,
Office of the Premier, by the Lieutenant Governor in Council, is not
to exceed $165 000.
(3)
The salary payable to a person who is appointed as an Executive
Director, Operations and Strategic Initiatives, Office of the Premier
(Vancouver), by the Lieutenant Governor in Council, is not to exceed
$165 000.
