Utilities commission act Jimmie creek exemption regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Utilities commission act ; "toba montrose transmission system" Means the transmission system owned by the toba montrose general Partnership on the date this regulation comes into force.
Exemption
2 The toba montrose general partnership and its successors and assigns Are exempt from part 3 of the act in respect of (a) The toba montrose transmission system, and (b) The provision of service respecting the transmission of electricity From the hydroelectric project at jimmie creek to the authority's Transmission system.
