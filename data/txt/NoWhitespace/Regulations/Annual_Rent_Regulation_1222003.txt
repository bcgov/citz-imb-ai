1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Forest act ; "reduced volume condition" Means a condition on a licence that, under section 14 (1) (g.2) of the Act, reduces a portion of the allowable annual cut available to the Holder of the licence; "supplemental forest licence" Has the same meaning as in section 14.2 (1) of the act.
