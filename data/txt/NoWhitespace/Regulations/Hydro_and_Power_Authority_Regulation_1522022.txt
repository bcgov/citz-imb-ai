Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Hydro and power authority act Hydro and power authority regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hydro and power authority act .
Purposes of authority 2 (1) For the purposes of section 12 (1.1) (c) [other purposes of authority] Of the act, to promote the use of electricity is prescribed as a Purpose of the authority.
(2) For certainty, the purpose set out under subsection (1) includes Promotion for the purpose of reducing greenhouse gas emissions.
