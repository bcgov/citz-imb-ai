Hydro and Power Authority Act
Hydro and Power Authority Regulation
Definition
1
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Hydro and Power Authority Act
.
Purposes of authority
2
(1)
For the purposes of section 12 (1.1) (c)
[other purposes of authority]
of the Act, to promote the use of electricity is prescribed as a
purpose of the authority.
(2)
For certainty, the purpose set out under subsection (1) includes
promotion for the purpose of reducing greenhouse gas emissions.
