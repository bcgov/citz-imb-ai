1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Judicial compensation act .
Setting higher amounts for purposes of the act 2 For the purposes of section 7.1 (2) of the act, the following Amounts are set:
(a) For paragraph (a) of that subsection, $40 000; (b) For paragraph (b) of that subsection, 85% of the costs over $40 000 but under $150 000.
