Forest and Range Practices Act
Invasive Plants Regulation
Application of sections 16 (2) and 37 (2) of the Act
1
Until June 1, 2004, this regulation applies immediately, despite
sections 16 (2) and 37 (2) of the Act, to a forest stewardship plan,
woodlot licence plan, range use plan, and range stewardship plan, as
applicable.
Invasive plant species specified
2

Forest and Range Practices Act
, the prescribed species of invasive plants are as follows:
