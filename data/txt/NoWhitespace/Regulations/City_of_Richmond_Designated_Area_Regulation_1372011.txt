Municipalities enabling and validating act (no. 4) City of richmond Designated area regulation Designated area 1 For the purposes of section 2 (3) (a) (i) of the Municipalities enabling and validating act (no. 4) , the designated portion of the area known as richmond city centre is That area within the following boundaries:
Commencing at the point of intersection of the south shoreline of the Middle arm of the fraser river and the west boundary of the right of Way for no. 4 road; Thence southerly 453 metres along the right of way for no. 4 road; Thence west 86 metres; Thence south 200 metres; Thence east 86 metres to no. 4 road; Thence southerly along no. 4 road to bridgeport road; Thence westerly along bridgeport road and sea island way to garden City road; Thence southerly along garden city road to alderbridge way; Thence easterly along alderbridge way to no. 4 road; Thence southerly along no. 4 road to blundell road; Thence westerly along blundell road; Thence northerly 1 492 metres along gilbert road; Thence west 110 metres; Thence north 110 metres to westminster highway; Thence westerly along westminster highway to no. 2 road; Thence northerly along no. 2 road to the south shoreline of the middle Arm of the fraser river; Thence along the south shoreline of the middle arm of the fraser river In a northeasterly direction to the point of commencement.
