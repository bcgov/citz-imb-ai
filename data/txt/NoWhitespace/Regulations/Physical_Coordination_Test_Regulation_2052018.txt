Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Motor vehicle act Physical coordination test regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Motor vehicle act .
Prescribed physical coordination test 2 For the purposes of section 215 (8) (a) and (8.1) (a) of the act, a Prescribed physical coordination test consists of the following Standard field sobriety tests, if administered together:
(a) The horizontal gaze nystagmus test; (b) The walk-and-turn test; (c) The one-leg stand test.
