Financial administration act Land tax deferment administration fee remission regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Land tax deferment act .
Remission of fees 2 Authorization is given for the remission of application and renewal Fees payable in respect of a deferral agreement requested by (a) A person who meets the requirements of section 5 (2.1) of the act for The 2009 or 2010 taxation year, or (b) A person who meets the requirements of section 5 (2.3) of the act for The 2010 or a subsequent taxation year.
Note:
This regulation replaces b.c. reg.102/2009.
