Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Environmental assessment act Exemption regulation (no. 3) Section 31 (6) of Environmental assessment act Not applicable in specified circumstances 1 (1) Section 31 (6) of the Environmental assessment act Does not apply in relation to environmental assessment certificate # E18-01 if, under section 31 (3) of that act, the chief executive Assessment officer is conducting a review of an application made under Section 31 (2) of that act by the holder of the certificate.
(2) If, under section 31 (4) (a) of the Environmental assessment act , the chief executive assessment officer extends the deadline Specified in environmental assessment certificate # e18-01, section 31 (6) of that act does not apply in relation to the certificate until The end of the period of the extension.
