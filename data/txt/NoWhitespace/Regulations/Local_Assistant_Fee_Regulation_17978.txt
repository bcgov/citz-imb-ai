Fire services act Local assistant fee regulation Local assistant fee 1 The sum to be paid under authority of section 12 of the Fire services act Out of the consolidated revenue fund to every local assistant for each Fire investigated and reported by him to the satisfaction of the fire Commissioner be set at the sum of $3 in addition to the necessary Expense of obtaining any requisite information.
