Link to point in time Forestry service providers protection act Forestry service providers compensation fund regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 286/2016, december 7, 2016] Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Forestry service providers protection act ; "free growing stand" Has the same meaning as in the Forest and range practices act .
[en. b.c. reg. 286/2016, s. 1.] Fund established 2 For the purposes set out in section 26 (1) of the act, the forestry Service providers compensation fund is established with an initial Balance of $5 million, as provided by the government.
Forestry service providers 3 (1) For the purposes of the definition of "services" in section 1 (1) of The act, the following are prescribed:
(a) Activities relating to growing seedlings; (b) Silviculture activities that are necessary, or are required under the Forest and range practices act , to establish free growing stands.
(2) For the purposes of part 2 of the act, the class of persons who Provide services for a holder of an agreement referred to in section 12 of the Forest act Are designated as forestry service providers.
