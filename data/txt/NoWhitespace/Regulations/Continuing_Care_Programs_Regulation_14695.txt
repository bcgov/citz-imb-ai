[en. b.c. reg. 83/2019, app. 2.] 1 The following programs administered by the ministry of the minister Are prescribed as continuing care:
(a) Home support services; (b) Adult day services; (c) Meals programs; (d) Respite services; (e) Care management; (f) Long-term care services; (g) Palliative and end-of-life care services; (h) Home oxygen program; (i) Assisted living services; (j) Community nursing services; (k) Community rehabilitation services.
