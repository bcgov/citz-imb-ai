B.c. reg. 352/81 Regulation of the chief justice Filed august 25, 1981 This consolidation is current to march 5, 2024.
Link to point in time Law and equity act Law and equity regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 74/2014, april 30, 2014] Discount rates prescribed 1 Pursuant to section 56 of the Law and equity act , i hereby prescribe:
(a) A discount rate of 1.5% per annum compound which shall be deemed to be The future difference between the investment rate of interest and the Rate of increase of earnings due to inflation and general increases in Productivity, and (b) A discount rate of 2% per annum compound which shall be deemed to be The future difference between the investment rate of interest and the Rate of general price inflation.
