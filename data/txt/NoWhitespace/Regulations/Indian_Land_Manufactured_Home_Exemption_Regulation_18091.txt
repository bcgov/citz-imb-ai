Manufactured home tax act Indian land manufactured home Exemption regulation Exemption from act 1 A manufactured home, to which an exemption under section 11 (1) of the Indian self government enabling act Would apply if the manufactured home were an interest in real Property, is exempted from the Manufactured home tax act .
