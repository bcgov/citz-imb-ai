Manufactured Home Tax Act
Indian Land Manufactured Home
Exemption Regulation
Exemption from Act
1
A manufactured home, to which an exemption under section 11 (1) of the
Indian Self Government Enabling Act
would apply if the manufactured home were an interest in real
property, is exempted from the
Manufactured Home Tax Act
.

Manufactured Home Tax Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 281, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
section 9 (2) (c)]
