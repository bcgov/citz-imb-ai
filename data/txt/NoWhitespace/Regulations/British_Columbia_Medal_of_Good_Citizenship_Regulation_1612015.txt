Provincial symbols and honours act British columbia medal of good citizenship regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Provincial symbols and honours act .
Prescribed letters 2 For the purposes of section 19.6 (1) of the act, the prescribed Letters are "m.g.c.".
Form of medal 3 For the purposes of section 19.6 (2) (a) of the act, the prescribed Form of the medal is as follows:
(a) The shape of the medal is circular; (b) The colour of the medal is silver; (c) The size of the medal is 36 mm in diameter; (d) The obverse of the medal bears the following:
(i) Along the rim, the inscription "good citizenship british columbia"; (ii) In the centre, the shield of arms of british columbia; (e) The reverse of the medal bears the following:
(i) Along the rim, the inscription "generosity, service, selflessness"; (ii) In the centre, a stylized flowering dogwood.
Additional insignia of medal 4 For the purposes of section 19.6 (2) (b) of the act, the additional Insignia of the medal are the following:
(a) A ribbon that (i) Is worn with the medal referred to in section 3, (ii) Is 32 mm wide, and (iii) Has 7 vertical stripes that are coloured, in order from left to right, Blue, red, yellow, white, yellow, red and blue; (b) A miniature replica of the medal referred to in section 3; (c) A lapel pin that is (i) In the form of the shield of arms of british columbia, and (ii) Silver in colour.
