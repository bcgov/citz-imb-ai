When money can be collected and retained by operator other than
government
1
(1)
For the purposes of section 3 (2) (a) of the
BC Online Act
, an operator of BC Online other than the government may collect money
payable to the government or to a public officer for a service or
information if both of the following apply:
(a)
the money is payable to the government or to a public officer under an
Act listed in the Schedule, or a regulation under the Act;
(b)
the Act or regulation describes the money in any of the following
ways:
(i)
the money is payable to the operator;
(ii)
the money is an operator fee or operator charge;
(iii)
the money is payable, under the
Environmental Management Act
or a regulation under that Act, to the electronic data distributor.
(2)
For the purposes of section 3 (2) (a) of the
BC Online Act
, an operator of BC Online other than the government may retain the
whole or a part of money payable to the government or to a public
officer for a service or information if both of the following apply:
(a)
the operator may collect the money under subsection (1);
(b)
an agreement referred to in section 2 (1) of the
BC Online Act
authorizes the operator to retain the whole or the part of the money.
When money can be collected and retained by government as operator
2
For the purposes of section 3 (2) (b) of the
BC Online Act
, if the government is the operator of BC Online, money payable for a
service or information under an Act listed in the Schedule, or a
regulation under the Act, is payable to the government as operator of
BC Online if the Act or regulation describes the money in any of the
following ways:
(a)
the money is payable to the operator;
(b)
the money is an operator fee or operator charge;
(c)
the money is payable, under the
Environmental Management Act
or a regulation under that Act, to the electronic data distributor.
