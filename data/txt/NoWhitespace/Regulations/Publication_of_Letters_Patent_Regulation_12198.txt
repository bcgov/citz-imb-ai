Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Link to point in time Local government act Publication of letters patent regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 118/2018, june 15, 2018] Information to be published in gazette 1 (1) This regulation applies to the notices respecting letters patent that The minister is required to publish under section 780 (1) (a) of the Local government act .
(2) The minister's notice in the gazette must include the following Information:
(a) The name of the municipality, regional district or improvement District to which the letters patent relate; (b) The number and date of the order in council by which the letters Patent were issued, and the effective date of letters patent if Different than the date of enactment; (c) In general terms, the purpose of the letters patent; (d) The authorizing legislative provision for the letters patent; (e) Where the letters patent may be viewed; (f) If the letters patent include a boundary description, one of the Following may be used in the notice:
(i) A synopsis describing the area and where a map of the boundary Description may be viewed; (ii) The legal description of the parcels affected; (iii) A metes and bounds description of the area affected.
(3) Subsection (2) does not limit the minister's authority to include any Other information the minister considers necessary or advisable.
