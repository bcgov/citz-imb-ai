Taxation (rural area) act Rural property tax roll regulation Prescribed information 1 The rural property taxation roll is prescribed, for the purpose of Section 21 (1) of the Taxation (rural area) act , to contain the following information:
1 registered owner(s) name(s) 2 mailing address 3 property folio number 4 land district 5 property narrative legal description 6 actual use code 7 subdistrict code 8 tenure equity code 9 common owner code 10 property location 11 tax exemption code(s) 12 oic property class code(s) 13 actual assessed value(s) 14 description of tax charges 15 tax rates 16 tax amounts 17 total current taxes due [provisions of the Taxation (rural area) act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 448, relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
Section 21 (1)]
