Link to point in time Criminal records review act Criminal record check authorizations regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 128/2011, october 1, 2011] Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"acquired injury care facility" Means a facility that is a licensed community care facility under the Community care and assisted living act And provides residential care for persons whose physical, intellectual Or cognitive abilities are limited primarily due to an injury, Including persons suffering from brain injuries or injuries sustained In accidents; "act" Means the Criminal records review act ; "community living care facility" Means a facility that is a licensed community care facility under the Community care and assisted living act And provides residential care to persons with developmental Disabilities;
"mental health and substance abuse care facility" Means a facility that is a licensed community care facility under the Community care and assisted living act And provides residential care for persons who are in care primarily Due to a mental disorder, substance dependence or both.
