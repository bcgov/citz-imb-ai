Manufactured home tax act Travel manufactured home Exemption regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 254/97] Interpretation
1 In this regulation "travel trailer" Means a vehicle that is (a) Capable of being towed on its own permanent wheels and undercarriage By a motor vehicle, (b) Licensed, or able to be licensed, as a trailer under the Motor vehicle act For use on highway, and (c) Not used as a principal residence.
[en. b.c. reg. 254/97.] Exemptions
2 The
Manufactured home tax act Does not apply to campers, motor homes or travel trailers.
