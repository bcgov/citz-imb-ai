Local government act Central coast regional district regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 27/2000] Airport operation and facilities 1 The central coast regional district is granted the additional power For airport operation and facilities as an extended service.
Local authority powers 2 The central coast regional district is granted as an extended service The same power that a municipality has as a local authority under the Emergency program act For such electoral area or areas as the regional district may Designate.
