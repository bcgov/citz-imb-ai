1 In this regulation:
"agriculture income stabilization trust fund" Means the trust fund that (a) Was established under the authority of order in council 403/98 by the Minister charged with the administration of the Financial administration act , and (b) Is renamed under section 4 of this regulation; "federal provincial agreement" Means, in relation to a specified farm income plan, the federal Provincial agreement that establishes the terms and conditions for the Plan, as set out in section 3; "specified farm income plan" Means a plan continued under section 2 or established under section 3 Or 3.1;
"trustees" Means the individuals designated in writing by the minister charged With the administration of the Financial administration act As the trustees of the agriculture income stabilization trust fund.

[en. b.c. reg. 241/2017, s. 3; am b.c. reg. 13/2020.] Agriculture income stabilization trust fund 4 (1) The whole farm insurance program trust fund authorized by order in Council 403/98 is renamed the agriculture income stabilization trust Fund.
(2) The trustees may be authorized to pay money out of the agriculture Income stabilization trust fund for the following purposes:
(a) To make payments to eligible farmers in accordance with the terms and Conditions of the specified farm income plans; (b) To pay, in accordance with the terms and conditions of a specified Farm income plan, the administrators retained to administer the Specified farm income plan; (c) To pay for the administration and management of the trust fund; (d) To pay reinsurance premiums; (e) To repay advances to the trust fund made by contributors in excess of Their required contributions; (f) To repay money paid into the trust fund in error.
[am. b.c. regs. 203/2011, ss. 2 and 3; 241/2017, s. 4.] Appeals
5 The trustees' failure or refusal to pay an appropriate amount of money Under a specified farm income plan is appealable in accordance with The procedures set out in the terms and conditions for the specified Farm income plan.
