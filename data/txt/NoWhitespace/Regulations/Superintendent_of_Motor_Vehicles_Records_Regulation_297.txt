Motor Vehicle Act
Superintendent of Motor Vehicles Records Regulation
[Last amended June 26, 2020 by B.C. Reg. 154/2020]
Interpretation
1
In this regulation
"Act"
means the
Motor Vehicle Act
.
Records prescribed for section 82 (11) of the Act
2
For the purpose of section 82 (11) of the Act, the records described
below are prescribed:
(a)
those records, each of which sets out or describes, with respect to a
person, the information required by section 95 (4) of the Act;
(b)
those records, each of which sets out or describes, with respect to a
person, particulars of or pertaining to a prohibition from operating a
motor vehicle imposed under section 91, 92, 93, 94.2, 98, 99,
100, 215, 215.43 or 251 (4) of the Act or
section 7 (2) (f) or (3) of the
Young Offenders (British Columbia) Act
;
(c)
those records, each of which sets out or describes, with respect to a
person, particulars of or pertaining to a suspension imposed before
September 1, 1982 under section 29, 90, 94, 95 or 102 of the
Act;
(d)
those records, each of which sets out or describes, with respect to a
person, particulars of or pertaining to vehicle impoundments imposed
under section 215.46 or 251 of the Act.
[am. B.C. Reg. 154/2020.]
