1 In this regulation "act" Means the Motor vehicle act .
Records prescribed for section 82 (11) of the act 2 For the purpose of section 82 (11) of the act, the records described Below are prescribed:
(a) Those records, each of which sets out or describes, with respect to a Person, the information required by section 95 (4) of the act; (b) Those records, each of which sets out or describes, with respect to a Person, particulars of or pertaining to a prohibition from operating a Motor vehicle imposed under section 91, 92, 93, 94.2, 98, 99, 100, 215, 215.43 or 251 (4) of the act or Section 7 (2) (f) or (3) of the Young offenders (british columbia) act ; (c) Those records, each of which sets out or describes, with respect to a Person, particulars of or pertaining to a suspension imposed before September 1, 1982 under section 29, 90, 94, 95 or 102 of the Act;
(d) Those records, each of which sets out or describes, with respect to a Person, particulars of or pertaining to vehicle impoundments imposed Under section 215.46 or 251 of the act.
