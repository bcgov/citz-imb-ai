"act" Means the Income tax act ; "complete level 1" , in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 1 Requirements established by skilledtradesbc and as amended from time To time for the program; "complete level 2" , in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 2 Requirements established by skilledtradesbc and as amended from time To time for the program; "complete level 3" , in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 3 Requirements established by skilledtradesbc and as amended from time To time for the program; "complete level 4" , in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 4 Requirements established by skilledtradesbc and as amended from time To time for the program; "graduate" Means
(a) In relation to an eligible recognized program set out in schedule 1, To receive one of the following for completing the program:
(i) A certificate of qualification; (ii) If a certificate of qualification is not granted for that program, a Certificate of apprenticeship, and (b) In relation to an eligible training program set out in section 4 (5) Or in schedule 2, to receive one of the following for completing the Program:
(i) A certificate of qualification; (ii) If a certificate of qualification is not granted for that program, a Certificate of apprenticeship; (iii) An inter-provincial red seal endorsement; "work-based hours" , in relation to a number of work-based hours completed, means Work-based hours shown as completed on a transcript from Skilledtradesbc.

(3) The level 3 requirements, if any, established for an eligible training Program itemized in column 1 of schedule 2 are the requirements set Out opposite that item in column 2.
(4) The level 4 requirements, if any, established for an eligible training Program itemized in column 1 of schedule 2 are the requirements set Out opposite that item in column 3.
