1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Teachers act .
Certification fee 2 (1) The following are the prescribed fees for the purposes of section 30 (1) (b) [issuance of certificate] Of the act that must accompany an applicant's application for a Certificate of qualification or an independent school teaching Certificate:
(a) $245 for an applicant who has a degree in education from a Post-secondary institution established in canada; (b) $395 for an applicant who has a degree in education from a Post-secondary institution established outside canada; (c) $245 for an applicant who applies for a certificate of qualification Or an independent school teaching certificate for which a degree in Education from a post-secondary institution is not required by the Certification standards or the independent school teaching Certification standards.
(2) The following are the prescribed fees for the purposes of section 30 (1) (b) of the act that an applicant must pay when the director of Certification issues a certificate of qualification or an independent School teaching certificate:
(a) $40 if the certificate of qualification or independent school teaching Certificate is issued on or after december 1 and on or before May 31;
(b) $80 if the certificate of qualification or independent school teaching Certificate is issued on or after june 1 and on or before November 30.
