Heritage conservation act Application regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 219/2012, july 19, 2012] Provisions applicable to heritage property 1 For the purposes of section 23 of the Heritage conservation act , sections 2, 3 (1), 8 (1) to (4), 9 and 14 to 27 of the Park act Apply to a provincial heritage property.
[am. b.c. reg. 219/2012, app. 1, s. 1.] Mcabee fossil beds heritage site 2 (1) For the purposes of section 23 of the Heritage conservation act , in addition to the provisions listed in section 1 of this Regulation, sections 11, 29.1 and 30 (5) of the Park act Apply to the mcabee fossil beds heritage site.
(2) Despite section 1 of this regulation, section 9 of the Park act Does not apply to the mcabee fossil beds heritage site.
