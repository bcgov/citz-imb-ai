Local government act Grants in lieu of taxes regulation Prorating of grants in lieu of taxes 1 Grants in lieu of taxes received by a regional district from a Municipality or from the provincial surveyor of taxes with respect to An electoral area, specified area or defined area pursuant to section 793 (5) and 793 (6) of the act 1 Shall be prorated by the regional district to the functions in which The municipality, electoral area, specified area or defined area Participates in proportion to the amounts requisitioned from the Municipality, electoral area, specified area or defined area.
Prorated amount included in revenue of board 2 The amount so prorated under section 1 shall be included in the Revenue of the board under section 793 (2) (c) of the act 1 For each function.
