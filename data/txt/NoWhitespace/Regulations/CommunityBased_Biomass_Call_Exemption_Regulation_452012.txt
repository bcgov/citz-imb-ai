Utilities commission act Community-based biomass call exemption regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Utilities commission act ; "project proponent" Means a person who enters into an energy supply contract with the Authority as a result of the call referred to in section 2, or the Successors or assigns of the person.
Exemption
2 The authority and all project proponents are exempt from section 71 of The act with respect to the community-based biomass call for Electricity that the authority is engaged in on the date this Regulation comes into force.
