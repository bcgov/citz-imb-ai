1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Motor vehicle act ; "claimed vehicle" Means a motor vehicle impounded under the act and released to the Owner of the motor vehicle or a person authorized by the owner; "unclaimed vehicle" Means a motor vehicle impounded under the act and disposed of under The act or the Warehouse lien act .
