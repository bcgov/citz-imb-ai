Insurance corporation act Permitted cost of services (canadian citizenship indication) Regulation
Canadian citizenship indication 1 For the purposes of section 17 (1) of the Insurance corporation act , the permitted costs of the services of the insurance corporation of British columbia in the schedule to the motor vehicle fees regulation, B.c. reg. 334/91, are as follows:
(a) The fees collected by the corporation under item 4 (a.1) (i) and (ii); (b) $35 of each fee collected by the corporation under item 4 (a.1) (iii) And (iv); (c) The fees collected by the corporation under item 12.1 (a), (b) and (e); (d) $35 of each fee collected by the corporation under item 12.1 (c).
