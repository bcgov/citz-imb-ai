Local government act Columbia-shuswap regional district regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 189/2008, june 27, 2008] Enhanced 9-1-1 emergency response system 1 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To operate an enhanced 9-1-1 emergency response system as an extended Service.
Economic development contribution 2 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To provide economic development contribution as an extended service.
[en. b.c. reg. 433/90.] Emergency programs 3 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To provide emergency programs as an extended service under the Emergency program act .
[en. b.c. reg. 492/90.] Flood protection 4 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To provide flood protection, including without limitation the powers Of a district municipality under sections 552 and 599 of the Municipal act 1 , 2 , as a local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 74/91.] Salmon arm airport 5 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To operate and maintain the salmon arm airport as an extended service.
[en. b.c. reg. 132/91.] Transit
6 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To provide transit as a local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 219/92.] Financial aid toward golden airport 7 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To contribute financial aid toward the cost of operation of the golden Airport, as an extended service.
[en. b.c. reg. 49/93.] Financial aid toward golden cemetery 8 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To contribute financial aid toward the cost of the golden cemetery, as An extended service.
[en. b.c. reg. 113/93.] Agreement to form part of regional library district 9 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power Of a municipality to enter into an agreement with the board of Management of a library district to form part of a regional library District under part 4 of the Library act , as a local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 310/93.] Removal of dangerous buildings and other structures 10 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To exercise the powers conferred on the council of a municipality Under section 727 of the Local government act .
[en. b.c. reg. 295/2003.] Firecrackers and fireworks 11 The columbia-shuswap regional district is granted the additional power To, by bylaw, regulate, prohibit and impose requirements in relation To the use of firecrackers and fireworks.
