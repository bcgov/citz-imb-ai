Property Transfer Tax Act
Property Transfer Tax Exemption Regulation No. 33
Exemption
1
The transfers to Toquaht Nation of the properties described below on
August 12, 2020, are exempt from tax payable under the
Property Transfer Tax Act
:
Section 36, Clayoquot District except part in Plan VIP 75649, PID
000-913-871;
Section 38, Clayoquot District except part in Plan VIP 75650, PID
009-389-873;
Section 40, Clayoquot District, PID 008-947-147;
Section 41, Clayoquot District except part in Plan VIP 84395, PID
008-947-163;
Section 90, Alberni District (situated in Clayoquot District) except
part in Plan VIP 75648, PID 008-947-201.
Retroactive effect
2
This regulation is retroactive to August 12, 2020, with effect on and
after that date.
