Income tax act Book publishing tax credit regulation Carrying out business primarily in british columbia 1 For the purposes of part 8 of the Income tax act , a book publishing corporation carries out its business primarily Through a permanent establishment in british columbia if, in a Taxation year, (a) The corporation has no permanent establishment outside of british Columbia, or (b) The percentage determined for the corporation by the following formula Exceeds 50%:

B
X 100%
Where
A

Is the total salaries and wages paid by the corporation in the Taxation year.
