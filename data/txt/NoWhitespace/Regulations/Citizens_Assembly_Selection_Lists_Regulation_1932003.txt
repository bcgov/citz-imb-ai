Election act Citizens' assembly selection lists regulation Definitions
1 The definitions in section 1 of the Election act Apply for the purposes of this regulation.
Use of personal information 2 Personal information (a) As defined in the Freedom of information and protection of privacy act , and (b) Contained in the provincial list of voters May be used for a purpose described in section 3 or 4.
Use of the provincial list of voters 3 The chief electoral officer may use the provincial list of voters to Provide the chair of the citizens' assembly with one or more selection Lists for use under section 4 by the chair of the citizens' Assembly.
Use of a selection list 4 The chair of the citizens' assembly may identify and contact potential Members of the citizens' assembly by using a selection list received Under section 3 from the chief electoral officer.
