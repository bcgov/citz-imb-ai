Local government act Regional district of Okanagan-similkameen regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 131/95] 9-1-1 emergency telephone system 1 The regional district of okanagan-similkameen is granted the Additional power to establish and operate a 9-1-1 emergency telephone System as an extended service.
Electrical power service 2 The regional district of okanagan-similkameen is granted the power to Operate an electrical power service as a local service with all member Municipalities participating as authorized under section 1 (5) of the Municipalities enabling and validating amendment act (no. 2), 1988 1 .
[en. b.c. reg. 54/90.] Economic development grant-in-aid 3 The regional district of okanagan-similkameen is granted the Additional power to provide an economic development grant-in-aid as an Extended service.
[en. b.c. reg. 161/90.] Drainage contribution 4 The regional district of okanagan-similkameen is granted the Additional power to provide drainage contribution as a local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 216/90.] Electric power: anarchist mountain 5 The regional district of okanagan-similkameen is granted the Additional power to provide, as a local service, electric power to Anarchist mountain.
[en. b.c. reg. 432/90.] Transit systems 6 The regional district of okanagan-similkameen is granted the Additional power to provide transit systems as a local service.
[en. b.c. reg. 128/92.] Emergency programs 7 The regional district of okanagan-similkameen is granted the Additional power to provide emergency programs as an extended service Under the Emergency program act .
