Link to consolidated regulation (pdf) Link to point in time Medical research (bc cancer agency) and health status registry act British columbia cancer agency research information regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 215/2018, november 15, 2018] Interpretation
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Medical research (bc cancer agency) and health status registry act ; "information" Means information or classes of information relevant to medical Research into the prevention, causes, diagnosis, treatment and Outcomes of cancer or its precursor lesions and includes records Containing that information.
[am. b.c. reg. 215/2018, ss. (a) and (b).] Information requests 2 Information that may be requested by the bc cancer agency under Section 2 (1) of the act is limited to information listed in Appendices 1 to 3.
[am. b.c. reg. 215/2018, s. (c).] Appendix 1
Personal identification information 1 personal identifiers (a) name; (b) sex; (c) birthdate and place; (d) marital status; (e) residence information.
2 family identifiers (a) spouse's name.
3 health identifiers (a) health insurance number; (b) personal health number.
Appendix 2
Medical information 1 records from medical laboratories, imaging services, hospitals And other health facilities and physicians.
2 mortality and morbidity data including autopsy reports.
Appendix 3
Factors which influence the occurrence of Cancer in human populations 1 intrinsic host factors (a) ethnicity; (b) medical history.
