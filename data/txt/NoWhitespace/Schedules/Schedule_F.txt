BISHOP BAY – MONKEY BEACH CORRIDOR CONSERVANCY
All those parcels or tracts of Crown land, together with all that
foreshore or land covered by water, situated in Range 4, Coast District
and contained within the described boundaries as shown on the Official
Plan deposited in the Crown Land Registry as Plan 11 Tube 1975;
except (1) those parts required for the ancillary facilities required
for timber harvesting in lands excepted from or beyond the conservancy
in the Monkey Beach Development; and (2) that part lying beyond a line
drawn parallel to and 200 metres perpendicularly distant from the
natural boundary of the sea.
The whole conservancy containing approximately 13 hectares
(5 hectares of upland and 8 hectares of foreshore).
