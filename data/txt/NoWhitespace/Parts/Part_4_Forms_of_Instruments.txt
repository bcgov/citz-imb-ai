Order in council transferring government rights in highway 40 (1) A certified copy of an order of the lieutenant governor in council Releasing or waiving the right of the government in a highway vested In the crown has the same effect as if a crown grant had been issued By the government to the person named in the order in council as Transferee, and must be so dealt with by the registrar.
(2) Unless an order in council under subsection (1) otherwise provides, The following are deemed to be excepted from its operation:
(a) Minerals and placer minerals as defined in the Mineral tenure act ; (b) Coal;
(c) Petroleum as defined in the Petroleum and natural gas act ; (d) Gas or gases.
