Mountain resort associations act Section 1 definition of "municipality" before amended by 2003-52-450, effective january 1, 2004 (bc reg 465/2003, repealing Bc reg 428/2003).
"municipality" Means a municipality as defined in the Local government act ; Section 1 definition of "mountain resort area" before repealed by 2007-6-34, effective march 29, 2007.
"mountain resort area" Means a mountain resort area established under section 2; Section 1 definition of "resort promotion area" was added by 2007-6-34, effective march 29, 2007.
Section 1 definition of "resort land" before amended by 2007-6-35, Effective march 29, 2007.
"resort land" Means the land in a mountain resort area that is specified by the Minister as resort land and includes a lot, strata lot or other Parcel into which the land is subdivided.
