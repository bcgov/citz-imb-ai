Definition of "officer" 1 In the Credit union incorporation act , the Financial institutions act And the Company act As it applies for the purposes of those 2 acts, "officer" Of a corporation includes a credit officer, an employee of the Corporation who has the authority to approve loans and an employee Of the corporation who reports directly to the directors.
