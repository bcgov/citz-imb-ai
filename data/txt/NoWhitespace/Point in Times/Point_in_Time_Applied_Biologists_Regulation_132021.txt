Reserved practice 4 For the purposes of section 51 (1) (b) [reserved practice] Of the act, the practice of applied biology is a reserved practice That may only be carried out by or under the supervision of a Registrant, if the practice relates to providing advice or another Service for a biology objective that, having regard to the Protective purposes, requires the experience or technical Knowledge of a registered professional biologist or registered Biology technologist.
[en. b.c. reg. 239/2021, app. 3, s. 2.]
