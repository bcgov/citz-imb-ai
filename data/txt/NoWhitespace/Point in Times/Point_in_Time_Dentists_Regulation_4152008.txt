Section 1 definitions of "dental appliance" and "fit" before amended By bc reg 32/2020, effective february 24, 2020.
"dental appliance" Means an appliance or a device designed or offered for a dental Condition or other condition of the orofacial complex, excluding a Ready-to-use or self-adapted mouthguard designed or offered for Temporary protection against injury during sporting activities; "fit" Means to perform any intra-oral procedure related to dispensing, Including the making of impressions; Section 1 definition of "dispense", paragraph (b) before amended by Bc reg 32/2020, effective february 24, 2020.
(b) In respect of a dental appliance, means to fabricate or alter; Section 1 definition of "prescribe", paragraph (b) before amended by Bc reg 32/2020, effective february 24, 2020.
(b) In respect of a dental appliance, to issue an authorization to Dispense for use by a named individual; Section 1 definition of "college" before repealed by bc reg 172/2022, effective september 1, 2022 "college" Means the college of dental surgeons of british columbia continued Under section 15.1 of the act; Section 1.1 was enacted by bc reg 172/2022, effective september 1, 2022 Section 2 (1) (e) was added by bc reg 189/2014, effective october 21, 2014.
