Section 1 definitions of "dental appliance" and "fit" BEFORE amended
by BC Reg 32/2020, effective February 24, 2020.
"dental appliance"
means an appliance or a device designed or offered for a dental
condition or other condition of the orofacial complex, excluding a
ready-to-use or self-adapted mouthguard designed or offered for
temporary protection against injury during sporting activities;
"fit"
means to perform any intra-oral procedure related to dispensing,
including the making of impressions;
