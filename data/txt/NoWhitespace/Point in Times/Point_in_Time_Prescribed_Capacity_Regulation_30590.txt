B.c. reg. 305/90 O.c. 1376/90 Deposited september 7, 1990 Assessment act Prescribed capacity regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 284/91] Prescribed capacity 1 The prescribed capacity for the purpose of paragraph (l) in the Definition of "improvements" in section 1 (2) of the Assessment act Is
(a) For vessels in wineries, 20 000 or more gallons, and (b) For vessels not in wineries, (i) If aboveground, (a) 5 000 or more gallons, or (b) 800 or more cubic feet, and (ii) If underground, (a) 3 975 or more gallons, or (b) 635 or more cubic feet.
[am. b.c. reg. 284/91.] Note:
This regulation replaces b.c. reg. 394/88.
