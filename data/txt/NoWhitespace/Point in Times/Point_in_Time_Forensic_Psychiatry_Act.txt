Section 7 before amended by 2013-12-26, effective june 1, 2013 (bc Reg 160/2013).
Staff
7 (1)  subject to the Public service act , the commission may appoint officers and employees the commission Considers necessary for this act, and may determine their duties.
(2)  the lieutenant governor in council may appoint an Executive director of the commission, to be appointed during Pleasure, and may fix his or her remuneration and may declare that Some or all of the Public service act Applies to the executive director.
(3)  despite the Public service act But with the approval of the minister, the commission may engage And retain persons it considers necessary as consultants, experts Or specialists, and may fix their remuneration.
