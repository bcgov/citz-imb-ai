B.c. reg. 159/2017 Treasury board Deposited august 23, 2017 Balanced budget and ministerial accountability act Minister of state for child care expected results for the 2017/2018 fiscal year regulation Application of this regulation 1 This regulation applies to the minister of state for child care For the 2017/2018 fiscal year.
Definition
2 In this regulation, "act" Means the Balanced budget and ministerial accountability act .
Minister of state for child care 3 The expected results that the minister of state for child care is To achieve for the purposes of section 5 (3) of the act are as Follows:
(a) Progress toward the development of an implementation plan for Universal child care that provides affordable, accessible and High-quality care and learning to every child whose family wants Or needs it, starting with infant and toddler programs before Gradually expanding; (b) Engage with all levels of government, child care providers and Private and not-for-profit sectors on the development of the Implementation plan referred to in paragraph (a); (c) Submit to cabinet a report on the results referred to in Paragraphs (a) and (b) on or before march 31, 2018.
