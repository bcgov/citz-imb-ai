Application of section 67 (1) of the Act
1
(1)
In this section, "Act" means the
Speculation and Vacancy Tax Act
.
(2)
For the purpose of applying section 67 (1) of the Act for the 2018
or a subsequent calendar year,
(a)
paragraph (b) of that section is to be read as if the reference to
"on" were a reference to "within 6 years after", and
(b)
subparagraphs (b) (i) and (ii) of that section are to be read as
if the reference to "the date that is 6 years after" were
excluded.
