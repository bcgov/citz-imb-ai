Application of act 2 Subject to section 3 (4), and sections 4 and 9, this act Determines the care that an occupier is required to show toward Persons entering on the premises in respect of dangers to them, or To their property on the premises, or to the property on the Premises of persons who have not themselves entered on the Premises, that are due to the state of the premises, or to Anything done or omitted to be done on the premises, and for which The occupier is responsible by law.
