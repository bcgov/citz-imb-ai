B.c. reg. 184/2005 O.c. 372/2005 Deposited march 31, 2005 Local government act Skeena-queen charlotte regional district requirements modification Regulation
Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Local government act .
Local government act Requirements modified 2 (1)  subject to subsection (3), the requirements established Under sections 805 (1) and 806 (1) of the act are modified such That the date by which compliance with those requirements is Required is modified from april 10 to april 30.
(2)  subject to subsection (3), the requirement established Under section 815 (1) of the act is modified such that the date by Which compliance with that requirement is required is modified From march 31 to april 30.
(3)  the modification of requirements effected by subsections (1) and (2) applies only in relation to the skeena-queen charlotte Regional district and only for the year 2005.
[provisions of the Local government act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 323, relevant to the enactment of this Regulation: section 799]
