B.c. reg. 258/2005 O.c. 545/2005 Deposited july 28, 2005 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Hotel room tax regulation for the city of langley Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hotel room tax act .
Applicability of tax 2 August 31, 2005 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of The act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the city Of langley.
Prescribed rate of tax payable 3 The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the act is 2% of the Purchase price of accommodation purchased in the city of langley.
[am. b.c. reg. 116/2011, s. 12.] Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax 4 The city of langley may spend the amount paid to it out of the Revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing.
[am. b.c. reg. 116/2011, s. 12.] Repeal
5 This regulation is repealed effective july 1, 2017.
