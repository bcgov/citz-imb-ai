B.c. reg. 3/2020 Treasury board Deposited january 22, 2020 Balanced budget and ministerial accountability act Minister of state for child care expected results for the 2020/2021 fiscal year regulation Application of this regulation 1 This regulation applies to the minister of state for child care For the 2020/2021 fiscal year.
Definition
2 In this regulation, "act" Means the Balanced budget and ministerial accountability act .
Minister of state for child care 3 The expected results that the minister of state for child care is To achieve for the purposes of section 5 (3) of the act are as Follows:
(a) Continue to implement initiatives that support government's Universal child care plan that provides affordable, accessible, Inclusive and high-quality care and early learning for every child Whose family wants or needs it; (b) Together with the federal government, engage in activities aimed At renewing the bilateral early learning and child care framework Agreement;
(c) Continue to engage with all levels of government, child care Providers and the private and not-for-profit sectors to further Inform implementation of the plan for universal and inclusive Child care; (d) Submit to cabinet a report on the results referred to in Paragraphs (a) through (c) on or before march 31, 2021.
