Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act
Minister of State for Child Care Expected Results for the
2020/2021 Fiscal Year Regulation
Application of this regulation
1
This regulation applies to the Minister of State for Child Care
for the 2020/2021 fiscal year.
Definition
2
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act
.
Minister of State for Child Care
3
The expected results that the Minister of State for Child Care is
to achieve for the purposes of section 5 (3) of the Act are as
follows:
(a)
continue to implement initiatives that support government's
universal child care plan that provides affordable, accessible,
inclusive and high-quality care and early learning for every child
whose family wants or needs it;
(b)
together with the federal government, engage in activities aimed
at renewing the Bilateral Early Learning and Child Care Framework
agreement;
(c)
continue to engage with all levels of government, child care
providers and the private and not-for-profit sectors to further
inform implementation of the plan for universal and inclusive
child care;
(d)
submit to Cabinet a report on the results referred to in
paragraphs (a) through (c) on or before March 31, 2021.
[Provisions relevant to the enactment of this regulation:
Balanced Budget and Ministerial Accountability Act
, S.B.C. 2001, c. 28, s. 9.]
