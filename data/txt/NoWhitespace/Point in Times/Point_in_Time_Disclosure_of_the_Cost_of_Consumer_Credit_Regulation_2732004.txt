Access to statements by electronic means 19 For the purpose of section 92 (1) of the act, a credit grantor may Give a statement of account to a borrower by providing access to a Copy of the statement by electronic means if (a) When access is provided, the credit grantor provides the borrower With notice by electronic means that the statement is available, And
(b) The borrower consents in writing.
