Hotel Room Tax Act; Provincial Sales Tax Act
Hotel Room Tax Regulation
for the District of Tofino
Definition
1
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Hotel Room Tax Act
.
Applicability of tax
2
May 31, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of the
Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the District
of Tofino.
Prescribed rate of tax payable
3
The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the Act is 2% of the
purchase price of accommodation purchased in the District of
Tofino.
[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2012, s. 2.]
Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax
4
The District of Tofino may spend the amount paid to it out of the
revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing, programs and
projects.
[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2012, s. 2.]
Repeal
5
This regulation is repealed effective June 1, 2017.
[am. B.C. Reg. 109/2012, s. 3.]
