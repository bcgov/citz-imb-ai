Deposited december 8, 2011 Wildlife act Wildlife management area (pemberton wetlands) regulation Designation of pemberton wetlands wildlife management area 1 All those parcels of crown lands, together with all that foreshore Or land covered by water, under the administration of the minister Of forests, lands and natural resource operations, situated in Lillooet district and contained within the described boundaries as Shown on the official plan deposited in the crown land registry as Plan 3 tube 2009, except (a) Airport road, a 20 metre wide gazetted road, (b) A 30 metre right of way centred on the ministry of forests, lands And natural resource operations fsr 7979-01-0, and (c) A 30 metre right of way centred on the ministry of forests, lands And natural resource operations road r09288, Are designated the pemberton wetlands wildlife management area, The whole containing approximately 753 hectares.
