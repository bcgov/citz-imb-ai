Deposited march 28, 2013 Wildlife act Cranberry marsh/starratt wildlife management area regulation Designation of cranberry marsh/starratt wildlife management area 1 All those parcels of crown land, together with all that foreshore Or land covered by water, under the administration of the minister Of forests, lands and natural resource operations, situated in the Cariboo land district and outlined in heavy black on the map with The file name cranberrymarshstarrattwma5m.pdf, on file with geobc, Ministry of forests, lands and natural resource operations, are Designated as the cranberry marsh/starratt wildlife management Area, the whole containing approximately 319 hectares.
