Prescribed tax 2 (1) For the purposes of section 15 (2) (b) of the British columbia transit act , prescribed tax rates for the fiscal year starting april 1, 2018 Are
(a) $0.1816 per $1 000 of the net taxable value of land and Improvements of property in classes 1, 3, 8 and 9, and (b) $0.8808 per $1 000 of the net taxable value of land and Improvements of property in classes 2, 4, 5, 6 and 7.
(2) The references to classes of property in subsection (1) are to the Classes of property under the prescribed classes of property Regulation.
