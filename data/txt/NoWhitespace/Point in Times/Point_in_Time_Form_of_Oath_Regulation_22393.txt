B.c. reg. 223/93 O.c. 867/93 Deposited july 2, 1993 Land surveyors act Form of oath regulation Form of oath 1 An oath under section 46 (1) of the land surveyors act must be in The following form:
I, ...................., swear/affirm that (a) i am a canadian citizen or permanent resident of canada, And
(b) i will faithfully and without partiality perform my Duties as a land surveyor and member of the corporation of land Surveyors of british columbia according to law.
[provisions of the Land surveyors act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 248, relevant to the enactment of this Regulation: section 46 (1)]
