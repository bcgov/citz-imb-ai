Financial Administration Act
Retention of Fees for Liquor Training Programs Regulation
Authorization
1
A person approved under section 184 (2) of the Liquor Control and
Licensing Regulation to provide a training program prescribed
under section 60 of the
Liquor Control and Licensing Act
who collects the fee prescribed under that Act for that training
program is authorized to retain the fee as remuneration for all
services performed.
Repeal
2
This regulation is repealed on March 31, 2019.
