B.c. reg. 193/2004 O.c. 433/2004 Deposited april 30, 2004 Special accounts appropriation and control act Forfeited crime proceeds fund regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Special accounts appropriation and control act .
Relevant provisions 2 (1)  for the purpose of section 7 (1) of the act, the Following provisions of the Criminal code (canada) are relevant provisions:
(a) section 83.14 (5); (b) section 490.1 (1) and (2); (c) section 490.2 (2).
(2)  for the purpose of section 7 (1) of the act, the Following provisions of the Controlled drugs and substances act (canada) are relevant provisions:
(a) section 16 (1) and (2); (b) section 17 (2).
