B.c. reg. 309/87 Regulation of the minister Deposited september 1, 1987 Local services act Community planning area no. 28 Distribution of notices of public hearing Delivery of notice 1 Pursuant to section 892 (4) (b) of the Municipal act , the Local services act And order in council 1171 dated june 1, 1959, where an application To amend the zoning regulations applicable in community planning Area no. 28 would alter a permitted use or the density of any Area, the notice of public hearing to be held under section 890 (1) of the Municipal act Shall be mailed or otherwise delivered to all owners and tenants In occupation of all parcels any part of which is within 100 Metres of any part of the area that is subject to the regulation Alteration.
