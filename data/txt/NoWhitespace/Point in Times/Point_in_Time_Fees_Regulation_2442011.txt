Certification fee 2 The following are the prescribed certification fees for the Purposes of section 30 (1) (b) [issuance of certificate] Of the act:
(a) $245 for an applicant who has a degree in education from A post-secondary institution established in canada; (b) $395 for an applicant who has a degree in education from A post-secondary institution established outside canada; (c) $245 for an applicant who applies for a certificate of Qualification for which a degree in education from a Post-secondary institution is not required by the certification Standards or the independent school teaching certification Standards.

Section 6 before re-enacted by bc reg 14/2012, effective january 26, 2012.
Annual practice fee 6 (1)  the following are the prescribed fees for the purposes Of section 37 [annual practice fee] Of the act:
(a) the annual practice fee is $80 for a person who is issued A certificate of qualification between june 1 and november 30; (b) the annual practice fee is $40 for a person who is issued A certificate of qualification between december 1 and may 31; (c) the late fee under section 37 (2) of the act is $30; (d) the additional late fee under section 37 (3) (b) of the Act is $30.
