B.c. reg. 70/97 O.c. 256/97 Deposited march 6, 1997 Local government act District of esquimalt additional powers regulation Areas for intensive residential development 1 The district of esquimalt is granted the additional powers (a) to designate areas for intensive residential development In its official community plan or plans for the purposes of Section 920 of the Local government act , and (b) to define the meaning of the term "intensive residential Development".
Section 920 (8) applies 2 Section 920 (8) of the Local government act Applies to the authority of the district of esquimalt to designate Areas of intensive residential development.
