Section 1 definition of "sawlog" before amended by bc reg 287/2009, Effective november 30, 2009.
"sawlog" Has the same meaning as in the scaling regulation, b.c. reg. 446/94, Under the schedule of interior timber grades — all species, and Includes timber graded as dead and dry sawlog, grade code 3; Section 1 definitions of "grade", "sawlog" and "special forest Product" before amended by bc reg 390/2012, effective december 7, 2012.
"grade" Means
(a) with respect to a coastal area, the quality of cut timber As defined in the schedule of coast timber grades under the scaling Regulation, b.c. reg. 446/94, and (b) with respect to an interior area, the quality of cut timber As defined in the schedule of interior timber grades — all species Under the scaling regulation, b.c. reg. 446/94; "sawlog" Has the same meaning as in the scaling regulation, b.c. reg. 446/94, Under the schedule of interior timber grades — all species; "special forest product" Means a product of cut timber as defined in the special forest Products regulation, b.c. reg. 241/2003, made under the Forest act ; Section 1 (1) definition of "valuation area" before amended by bc Reg 265/2015, effective december 23, 2015.
"valuation area" Means
(a) With respect to a coastal area, the valuation areas numbered 1 to 4 On the map in schedule a, and (b) With respect to an interior area, the valuation areas numbered 5 to 9 on the map in schedule a.
