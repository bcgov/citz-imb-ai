B.c. reg. 286/99 Treasury board Filed september 8, 1999 Insurance corporation act Permitted cost of services regulation Emission inspection centres 1 For the purpose of section 17 (1) of the Insurance corporation act , the permitted cost of services provided by the insurance Corporation of british columbia to operate emissions inspection Centres anywhere in british columbia under division 40 of B.c. reg. 26/58, the motor vehicle act regulations, is the Entire amount of the fee it collects under item 13 of the Schedule to b.c. reg. 334/91, the motor vehicle fees Regulation, for each vehicle emission inspection or vehicle Emission reinspection.
Note:
This regulation repeals b.c. reg. 311/92.
