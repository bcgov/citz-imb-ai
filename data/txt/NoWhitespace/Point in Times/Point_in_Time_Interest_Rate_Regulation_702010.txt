B.c. reg. 70/2010 M62/2010
Deposited march 10, 2010 Land tax deferment act Interest rate regulation Interest rate 1 (1)  for the purposes of sections 8 (2) and 9 (2) of the Land tax deferment act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning april 1, 2010 Is 0.25% a year.
(2)  for the purposes of sections 8 (3.1) and 9 (5) of the Land tax deferment act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning april 1, 2010 Is 2.25% a year.
Note:
This regulation replaces b.c. reg. 181/2009.
