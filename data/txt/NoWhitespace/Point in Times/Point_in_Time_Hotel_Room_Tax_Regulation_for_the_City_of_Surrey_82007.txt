B.c. reg. 8/2007 O.c. 31/2007 Deposited february 2, 2007 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Hotel room tax regulation for the city of surrey Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hotel room tax act .
Applicability of tax 2 February 28, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of The act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the city Of surrey.
Prescribed rate of tax payable 3 The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the act is 2% of the Purchase price of accommodation purchased in the city of surrey.
[am. b.c. reg. 30/2012, s. 2.] Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax 4 The city of surrey may spend the amount paid to it out of the Revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing, programs and Projects.
[am. b.c. reg. 30/2012, s. 2.] Repeal
5 This regulation is repealed effective march 1, 2017.
