Certain
Company act Requirements disapplied 2 Sections 111, 153 and 154 of the Company act , as made applicable to trust companies and insurance companies Generally by part 1 of the act, do not apply to or in respect of (a) a trust company, or (b) an insurance company That is not a reporting company as defined in section 1 of the Company act .
