B.c. reg. 331/2012 Chief electoral officer Deposited deposited february 2, 2018 Election act Political party interim Financial report regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Election act .
Interim financial reports by parties eligible for allowances 2 An interim financial report under section 207.01 [interim financial reports by parties eligible for allowances] Of the act for a registered political party must be in the form Set out in the schedule to this regulation.
Supplementary reports as "amendments" to original reports 3 (1) If a supplementary report is required by section 212 of the act in Relation to a report under section 207.01 of the act, the Supplementary report must be in the form under section 2 of this Regulation.
(2) In the case of a first supplementary report, the form must be Completed as "amendment #1", and any further supplementary reports Must be sequentially numbered as further amendments.
