B.c. reg. 164/2009 Treasury board Deposited june 4, 2009 Balanced budget and ministerial accountability act Expected revenue outcome for the 2009/2010 fiscal year regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Balanced budget and ministerial accountability act .
Revenue targets 2 For the purposes of section 5 (2) of the act, (a) the sources of actual revenue for the 2009/2010 fiscal Year are specified as audit and revenue compliance related Activities in respect of taxes, resource royalties and other Revenues payable under enactments administered by the revenue Minister, and (b) the amount of actual revenue in the 2009/2010 fiscal year From the revenue sources referred to in paragraph (a) is specified As $211 100 000.
