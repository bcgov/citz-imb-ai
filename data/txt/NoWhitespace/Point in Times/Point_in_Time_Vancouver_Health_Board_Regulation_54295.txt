B.c. reg. 542/95 O.c. 1545/95 Deposited december 15, 1995 Effective january 1, 1996 Health authorities act Vancouver health board regulation Designation as corporation 1 The city of vancouver is designated a corporation in respect of Section 26 (3) of the Health act .
Powers of board 2 The vancouver health board has the powers of the council of the City of vancouver, under section 26 (1) to (3) of the Health act .
