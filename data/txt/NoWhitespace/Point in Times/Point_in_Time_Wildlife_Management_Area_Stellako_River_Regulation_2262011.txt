Deposited december 8, 2011 Wildlife act Wildlife management area (stellako river) regulation Designation of stellako river wildlife management area 1 All those parcels of crown lands, together with all that foreshore Or land covered by water, under the administration of the minister Of forests, lands and natural resource operations, situated in Range 5, coast district and contained within the described Boundaries as shown on the official plan deposited in the crown Land registry as plan 3 tube 2004, except (a) Dl 6854, and (b) Francois lake road as shown on plan 5 tube 1435 and plan 14 tube 1633, Are designated the stellako river wildlife management area, the Whole containing approximately 503 hectares.
