Cattle or effects of debtor may be detained 3 A keeper of a livery, boarding or sale stables and a person who Feeds and cares for cattle for compensation may detain in the Keeper's custody and possession, before they have been removed out Of his or her custody and possession, but not afterwards, any Cattle, vehicle, harness, furnishings or other gear, or personal Effects of another person who is indebted to him or her for Stabling, boarding or caring for the cattle.
