B.c. reg. 383/2004 Chief electoral officer Deposited august 6, 2004 Effective september 1, 2004 Election act Voter registration regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 330/2008, november 25, 2008] Definitions
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Election act .
Information that may be requested by voter registration form 2 An application form referred to in section 35 [application for registration] Of the act may request the following information:
(a) The applicant's gender; (b) The applicant's driver's licence number; (c) The last 6 digits of the applicant's social insurance number; (d) A telephone number at which the applicant may be contacted.
Required information for online voter registration 3 If an application referred to in section 35 of the act is made Online through the elections bc website, the application must Include
(a) The applicant's driver's licence number, or (b) The last 6 digits of the applicant's social insurance number.
Required information for voter registration by telephone 4 If an application referred to in section 35 of the act is made Orally by telephone, the applicant must provide (a) The applicant's driver's licence number, or (b) The last 6 digits of the applicant's social insurance number.
