B.c. reg. 152/97 O.c. 465/97 Deposited april 17, 1997 Local government act Village of tahsis regulation Internet provide service 1 The village of tahsis is granted the additional power to (a) provide, by bylaw, for the establishment, operation, Maintenance, repair, extension, improvement or alteration, of an Internet provider service for the inhabitants of the village and Adjacent localities, and (b) set, by bylaw, the rates and terms under which the Internet service may be supplied and used, and to impose service Connection fees and set the terms of payment.
[provisions of the Local government act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 323, relevant to the enactment of this Regulation: section 251]
