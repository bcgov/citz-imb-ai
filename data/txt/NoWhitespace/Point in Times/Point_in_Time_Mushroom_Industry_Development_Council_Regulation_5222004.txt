B.c. reg. 522/2004 O.c. 1139/2004 Deposited december 3, 2004 Effective january 1, 2005 Farming and fishing industries development act Mushroom industry development council regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Farming and fishing industries development act ; "council" Means the mushroom industry development council established by the Minister under section 2 of the act; "levy" Means the levy referred to in section 2; "mushroom" Means
Agaricus bisporous ; "mushroom grower" Means a person who operates a farm in british columbia that Produces mushrooms for sale.
Payment of levy 2 Each mushroom grower must pay to the council the levy established By the council under section 4 (1) of the act.
Remittance of levy 3 The levy for each period set out in column 1 of the following Table must be remitted by the due date set out in column 2 of the Table.
Column 1
Levy period Column 2
Due date January 31 to march 31 April 30
April 1 to june 30 July 31
July 1 to september 30 October 31
October 1 to december 31 January 31
Sunset of this regulation 4 This regulation is repealed effective january 1, 2010.
