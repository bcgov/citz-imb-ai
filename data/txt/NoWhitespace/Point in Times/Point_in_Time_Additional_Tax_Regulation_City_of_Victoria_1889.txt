B.c. reg. 18/89 O.c. 92/89 Deposited february 9, 1989 Effective january 26, 19891 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Additional tax regulation (city of victoria) Applicability
1 Section 3 (1) of the Hotel room tax act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 207, applies in respect of accommodation Purchased within the city of victoria.
Additional tax payable 2 The additional tax payable under that section is 2% of the Purchase price of the accommodation.
Purpose for tax expenditure 3 The purposes for which the amount paid to the municipality out of The revenue collected from the tax may be expended are convention Centre marketing and tourism promotion, projects and programs.
