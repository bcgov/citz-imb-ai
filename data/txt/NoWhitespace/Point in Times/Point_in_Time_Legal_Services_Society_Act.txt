Section 9 (1) BEFORE amended by 2007-14-44(a), effective May 31,
2007 (Royal Assent).
(1)  The objects of the society are,
(a) subject to section 10 (3), to assist low income individuals
to resolve their legal problems and facilitate access to justice for
low income individuals,
(b) subject to section 10 (3), to establish and administer an
effective and efficient system for providing legal aid to low income
individuals in British Columbia, and
(c) to provide advice to the Attorney General respecting legal
aid.

must, at least once in each fiscal year, audit the books, accounts
and financial affairs of the society.
