Section 6 before re-enacted by bc reg 13/2011, effective september 1, 2011.
Notifying others of outcome of assessment 6 On completing the assessment, the team of assessors, or, if there Is only one assessor under section 2 (1) (b), that assessor, must, In writing, inform the following of the details and results of the Assessment and must provide them with a completed copy of the Incapability assessment report in form 1:
(a) the adult; (b) the adult's legal counsel; (c) if present during the assessment, the adult's spouse, Near relative or friend; (d) the designated agency that asked for the assessment.
