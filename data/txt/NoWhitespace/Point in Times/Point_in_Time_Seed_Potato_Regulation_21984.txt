Section 20 before amended by bc reg 112/2014, effective june 18, 2014.
Vacancy
20 (1) In the event of any casual vacancy in the membership of the Committee the remaining members of the committee shall constitute The committee until the vacancy is filled.
(2) Where a member of the committee is absent from the province for 6 Months or fails to attend to his duties for a like period, the Other members of the committee may declare his office vacant.
(3) A member of the committee ceases to hold office where (a) He is convicted of an offence under the act or regulations, (b) By notice in writing to the committee he resigns his office, or (c) His office is declared vacant by the other members of the Committee.
(4) Where a vacancy occurs in the membership of the committee by Reason of death or through a member ceasing to hold office, the Remaining members of the committee, if constituted a quorum, may, Subject to the approval of the minister, fill the vacancy, and the Person appointed to fill the vacancy shall hold office for the Unexpired term of the member whose office he fills.
