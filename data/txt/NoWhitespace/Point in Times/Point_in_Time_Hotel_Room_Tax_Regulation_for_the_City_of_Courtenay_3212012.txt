B.c. reg. 321/2012 O.c. 736/2012 Deposited november 9, 2012 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Hotel room tax regulation for the city of courtenay Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hotel room tax act .
Applicability of tax 2 March 1, 2013 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of the Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the city of Courtenay.
Prescribed rate of tax payable 3 The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the act is 2% of the Purchase price of accommodation purchased in the city of Courtenay.
Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax 4 The city of courtenay may spend the amount paid to it out of the Revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing, programs and Projects.
Repeal
5 This regulation is repealed effective march 1, 2018.
