(1)
The collector must invoice, on a monthly basis, each producer for an
amount equal to the aggregate of the following calculations:
(a)
the marketable gas levy rate applicable in the billing month
multiplied by the volume of marketable gas produced by that producer
in the preceding production month;
(b)
the petroleum levy rate applicable in the billing month multiplied
by the volume of petroleum produced by that producer in the
preceding production month.
