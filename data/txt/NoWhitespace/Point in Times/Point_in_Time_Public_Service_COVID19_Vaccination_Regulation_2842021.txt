B.c. reg. 284/2021 O.c. 627/2021 Deposited november 19, 2021 Public service act Public service covid-19 vaccination regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "covid-19 vaccination policy" Means the policy entitled "human resources policy 25 — Covid-19 vaccination" that was issued under Section 5 (4) of the Public service act On november 1, 2021.
Term and condition of employment 2 The covid-19 vaccination policy is a term and condition of Employment for employees.
Termination deemed to be for just cause 3 If an employee is terminated under the covid-19 vaccination Policy, the employee is deemed to have been dismissed for just Cause.
