Lymantria moth eradication regulation Section 1 definition of "lymantria moth" before amended by bc reg 109/2023, effective april 24, 2023.
"lymantria moth" Means the following species of moth:
(a) Lymantria albescens ; (b) Lymantria dispar ; (c) Lymantria dispar dispar ; (d) Lymantria umbrosa ; Section 2 (1) (b) before amended by bc reg 109/2023, effective april 24, 2023.
(b) To enter on land in a treatment zone to do any of the following:
(i) Search for and remove lymantria moth eggs; (ii) Search for lymantria moths; (iii) Set or retrieve traps for lymantria moths; Schedule 1 before re-enacted by bc reg 109/2023, effective april 24, 2023.
