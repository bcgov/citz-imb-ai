Deposited april 8, 2013 Wildlife act South okanagan wildlife management area regulation Designation of south okanagan wildlife management area 1 All those parcels of crown land, together with all that foreshore Or land covered by water, under the administration of the minister Of forests, lands and natural resource operations, situated in the Similkameen division of yale land district and outlined in heavy Black on the map with the file name south_okangan_wma5m.pdf, on File with geobc, ministry of forests, lands and natural resource Operations, except the road survey as shown on highways district Plan rs67722, are designated as the south okanagan wildlife Management area, the whole containing approximately 903 hectares.
