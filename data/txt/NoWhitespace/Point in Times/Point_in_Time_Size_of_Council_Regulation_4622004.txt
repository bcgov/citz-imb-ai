B.c. reg. 462/2004 O.c. 1012/2004 Deposited october 22, 2004 Community charter Size of council regulation Exception to size of council 1 (1)  this regulation applies to a municipality that has, on The day this regulation comes into force, (a) a population of more than 50 000, and (b) a council consisting of a mayor and less than 8 Councillors.
(2)  as an exception to section 118 (4) of the Community charter , a municipality described under subsection (1) may obtain the Approval of the electors respecting retention of the size of its Council by holding an alternative approval process.
Repeal of regulation 2 This regulation is repealed effective april 30, 2005.
