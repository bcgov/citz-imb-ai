B.c. reg. 70/2017 O.c. 87/2017 Deposited march 1, 2017 Effective march 1, 2017 School act School tax administration fee regulation Administration fee 1 The administration fee allowed to each municipality for the Purposes of section 124 (10) of the School act Is $2 000 plus 0.1% of the current year school tax amount Contained in the notice issued to the municipal collector under Section 119 (6) (b) of the School act .
