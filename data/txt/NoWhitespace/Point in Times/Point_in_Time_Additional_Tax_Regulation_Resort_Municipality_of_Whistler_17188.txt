B.c. reg. 171/88 O.c. 759/88 Deposited april 22, 1988 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Additional tax regulation (resort municipality of whistler) Applicability
1 Section 3 (1) of the Hotel room tax act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 207, applies in respect of accommodation Purchased within the resort municipality of whistler.
Additional tax payable 2 The additional tax payable under that section is 2% of the Purchase price of the accommodation.
Purpose for tax expenditure 3 The purposes for which the amount paid to the municipality out of The revenue collected from the tax may be expended are tourism Promotion, projects and programs.
