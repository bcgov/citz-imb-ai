B.c. reg. 7/2007 O.c. 30/2007 Deposited february 2, 2007 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Hotel room tax regulation for the city of kimberley Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hotel room tax act .
Applicability of tax 2 March 31, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of the Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the city of Kimberley.
Prescribed rate of tax payable 3 The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the act is 2% of the Purchase price of accommodation purchased in the city of Kimberley.
[am. b.c. reg. 65/2012, s. 2.] Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax 4 The city of kimberley may spend the amount paid to it out of the Revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing, programs and Projects.
[am. b.c. reg. 65/2012, s. 2.] Repeal
5 This regulation is repealed effective april 1, 2017.
