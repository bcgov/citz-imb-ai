Section 2 (1) before repealed by bc reg 206/2016, effective july 28, 2016.
(1) The owners of the land within a bare land strata plan may apply to The registrar to cancel the plan.
Section 2 (2) (part) before amended by bc reg 206/2016, effective July 28, 2016.
(2) An application to cancel a bare land strata plan must be in the form Required by the registrar, and must include Section 2 (3) and (4) before amended by bc reg 206/2016, effective July 28, 2016.
(3) An application to cancel a bare land strata plan must be accompanied By
(a) A certified copy of an unanimous resolution of the strata Corporation that the plan be cancelled, (b) Subject to subsection (4), a written consent to the cancellation From each owner of a charge or encumbrance against the land in the Plan,
(c) All documents that are required to carry out the proposals for Vesting or other disposition of the land in the plan, and (d) An affidavit of an owner of land in the plan, verifying the contents Of the application.
(4) The registrar may accept an application to cancel a bare land strata Plan that has not been consented to by all the owners of charges Against the land in the plan if the interests of the owners who have Not consented to the cancellation will not be affected by the Cancellation.
Section 2.1 was enacted by bc reg 206/2016, effective july 28, 2016.
Section 3 before amended by bc reg 206/2016, effective july 28, 2016.
Prohibition
3 (1) Subject to subsection (2), no bare land strata plan shall be Cancelled unless (a) The documents referred to in section 2 (3) (c) comply with the Requirements of the Land title act , (b) The debts and liabilities of the strata corporation have been Extinguished, and (c) All taxes, assessments and charges levied on the owners of land Within the plan have been paid.
(2) Where the documents referred to in section 2 (3) (c) include a Subdivision plan, the registrar may receive but not deposit the Subdivision plan prior to it being approved by an approving Officer.
