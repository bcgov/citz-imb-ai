B.c. reg. 324/2006 O.c. 877/2006 Deposited december 4, 2006 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Hotel room tax regulation for the regional district of east Kootenay
Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hotel room tax act .
Applicability of tax 2 January 31, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of The act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the city Of fernie and in the portion of the east kootenay regional District electoral area a outlined in schedule a attached to the Regional district of east kootenay – additional hotel room tax Bylaw no. 1909, 2006.
Prescribed rate of tax payable 3 The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the act is 2% of the Purchase price of accommodation purchased in the city of fernie And in the portion of the east kootenay regional district Electoral area a outlined in schedule a attached to the regional District of east kootenay – additional hotel room tax bylaw no.
1909, 2006.
[am. b.c. reg. 12/2012, s. 2.] Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax 4 The east kootenay regional district may spend the amount paid to It out of the revenue collected from the tax for tourism Marketing, programs and projects.
[am. b.c. reg. 12/2012, s. 3.] Repeal
5 This regulation is repealed effective february 1, 2017.
