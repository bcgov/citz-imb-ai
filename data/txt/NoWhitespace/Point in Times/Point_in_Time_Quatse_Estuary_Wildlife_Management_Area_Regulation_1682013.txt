Wildlife Act
Quatse Estuary Wildlife Management Area Regulation
Designation of Quatse Estuary Wildlife Management Area
1
All those parcels of Crown land, together with all that foreshore
or land covered by water, under the administration of the Minister
of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, situated in
Rupert District and outlined in heavy black on the map with the
file name quatse_estuary_wma5m.pdf, on file with GeoBC, Ministry
of Forests, Lands and Natural Resource Operations, are designated
as the Quatse Estuary Wildlife Management Area, the whole
containing approximately 157 hectares.
