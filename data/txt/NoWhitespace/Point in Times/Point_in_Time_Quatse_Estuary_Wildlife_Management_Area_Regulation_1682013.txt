Deposited april 8, 2013 Wildlife act Quatse estuary wildlife management area regulation Designation of quatse estuary wildlife management area 1 All those parcels of crown land, together with all that foreshore Or land covered by water, under the administration of the minister Of forests, lands and natural resource operations, situated in Rupert district and outlined in heavy black on the map with the File name quatse_estuary_wma5m.pdf, on file with geobc, ministry Of forests, lands and natural resource operations, are designated As the quatse estuary wildlife management area, the whole Containing approximately 157 hectares.
