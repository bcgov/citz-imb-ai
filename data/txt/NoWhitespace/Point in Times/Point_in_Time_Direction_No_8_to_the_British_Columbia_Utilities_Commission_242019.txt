Basis for establishing authority revenue requirements 3 In regulating and setting rates for the authority for f2020 and F2021, the commission must ensure that those rates allow the Authority to collect sufficient revenue in each fiscal year to Enable the authority to achieve an annual rate of return on deemed Equity that would yield a distributable surplus of $712 million.
