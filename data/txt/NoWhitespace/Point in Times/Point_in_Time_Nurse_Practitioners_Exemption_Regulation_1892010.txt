B.c. reg. 189/2010 O.c. 411/2010 Deposited june 25, 2010 Effective june 30, 2010 Labour mobility act Nurse practitioners exemption regulation Definition
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Labour mobility act ; "occupation of nurse practitioner" Means the set of jobs that is the same or substantially similar to The set of jobs that constitutes the practice of nursing as a Nurse practitioner under the Health professions act .
Nurse practitioner exemption 2 The occupation of nurse practitioner is exempt from the Application of the act.
Repeal
3 Unless earlier repealed, this regulation is repealed september 30, 2011.
