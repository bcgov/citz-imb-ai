B.c. reg. 80/2003 O.c. 214/2003 Deposited march 14, 2003 Interpretation act Daylight saving time Daylight saving time 1 In british columbia each year the time for general purposes shall Be 7 hours behind greenwich mean time during the period commencing At 2 o'clock in the forenoon on the first sunday of april and Ending at 2 o'clock in the forenoon on the last sunday of october.
