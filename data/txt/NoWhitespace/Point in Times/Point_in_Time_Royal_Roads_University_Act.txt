(5)  the Company act And the Society act Do not apply to the university, but the lieutenant governor in Council may, by order, direct that the Company act Or the Society act , or any provision of either, applies to the university.

(5)  the Company act Does not apply to the university, but on the recommendation of the Minister, the minister of finance, by regulation, may declare that All or part of that act applies to the university.
Section 5.1 was enacted by 2012-7-32, effective march 29, 2012 (royal assent).
Section 5.1 before repealed by 2018-36-2, effective october 31, 2018 (royal assent).
Persons not eligible 5.1 A person is not eligible to be or to remain a member of the board If the person is (a) An employee of the university, and (b) A voting member of the executive body of, or an officer of, an Academic or non-academic staff association of the university who Has the responsibility, or joint responsibility with others, to (i) Negotiate with the board, on behalf of the academic or Non-academic staff association of the university, the terms and Conditions of service of members of that association, or (ii) Adjudicate disputes regarding members of the academic or Non-academic staff association of the university.
