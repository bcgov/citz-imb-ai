Persons not eligible
5.1
A person is not eligible to be or to remain a member of the board
if the person is
(a)
an employee of the university, and
(b)
a voting member of the executive body of, or an officer of, an
academic or non-academic staff association of the university who
has the responsibility, or joint responsibility with others, to
(i)
negotiate with the board, on behalf of the academic or
non-academic staff association of the university, the terms and
conditions of service of members of that association, or
(ii)
adjudicate disputes regarding members of the academic or
non-academic staff association of the university.
