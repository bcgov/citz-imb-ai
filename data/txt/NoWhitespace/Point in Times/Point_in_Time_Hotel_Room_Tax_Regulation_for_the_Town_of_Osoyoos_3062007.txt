B.c. reg. 306/2007 O.c. 656/2007 Deposited october 5, 2007 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Hotel room tax regulation for the town of osoyoos Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hotel room tax act .
Applicability of tax 2 November 30, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of The act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the town Of osoyoos.
Prescribed rate of tax payable 3 The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the act is 2% of the Purchase price of accommodation purchased in the town of osoyoos.
[am. b.c. reg. 320/2012, s. 2.] Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax 4 The town of osoyoos may spend the amount paid to it out of the Revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing, programs and Projects.
[am. b.c. reg. 320/2012, s. 2.] Repeal
5 This regulation is repealed effective december 1, 2017.
