Section 1 before amended by bc reg 4/2010, effective january 14, 2010.
Covenant re land disposition 1 Upon the issuance of a crown grant under section 48 of the Land act , the minister of sustainable resource management may require the Grantee to enter into a covenant that he and his successors in Title will not dispose of the land granted separately from another Designated parcel already owned by the grantee.
