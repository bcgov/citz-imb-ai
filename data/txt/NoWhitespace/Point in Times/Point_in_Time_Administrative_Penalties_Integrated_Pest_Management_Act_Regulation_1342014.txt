Section 12 (1) and (2) before amended by bc reg 164/2020, effective June 29, 2020.
(1) A person who contravenes section 70 or 72 (1) (a) of the integrated Pest management regulation is liable to an administrative penalty Not exceeding $40 000.
(2) A person who contravenes section 31, 34 (1), 35, 36, 37, 38, 39 (1), 40, 41 (1),(2), (5) or (6), 42 (1), (2), (5) or (6), 48 (b), 49 (1) (c), 54 (d), 57 (1) (d), 59 (2), 65 (1), 66, 72 (1) (c), 75 (2) or 83 (1) or (2) (b) of the integrated pest management regulation is Liable to an administrative penalty not exceeding $10 000.

(1) A person who contravenes section 59.1, 59.2 (1), 59.3, 70 or 72 (1) (a) of the integrated pest management regulation is liable to an Administrative penalty not exceeding $40 000.
