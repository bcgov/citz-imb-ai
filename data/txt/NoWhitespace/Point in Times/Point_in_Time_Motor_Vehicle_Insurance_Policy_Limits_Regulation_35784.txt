B.c. reg. 357/84 O.c. 2237/84 Deposited december 21, 1984 Effective january 1, 1985 Insurance act Motor vehicle insurance policy limits regulation Prescribed limits under section 151 of the act 1 In respect of motor vehicle liability insurance policies issued or Renewed on and after january 1, 1985, the prescribed amounts under Section 151 of the act are (a) at least $200 000 for each of subsections (1) and (2), (b) $180 000 for subsection (3) (a), (c) $20 000 for subsection (3) (b), (d) at least $200 000 for the first reference to a prescribed Amount in subsection (4), and (e) at least $20 000 for the second reference to a prescribed Amount in subsection (4).
[provisions of the Insurance act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 226, relevant to the enactment of this Regulation: sections 151 and 192]
