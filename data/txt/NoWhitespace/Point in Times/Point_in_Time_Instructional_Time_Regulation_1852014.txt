B.c. reg. 185/2014 O.c. 546/2014 Deposited october 6, 2014 School act Instructional time regulation Definition
1 In this regulation, "board" Includes a francophone education authority.
Deemed provision of hours of instruction 2 A board that has scheduled for a school in its district a number Of hours of instruction for a day between september 2, 2014 and September 30, 2014 is deemed to have provided the scheduled number Of hours of instruction for that day and to have complied with Section 87.03 of the School act If the school is closed as a result of a strike or lockout in Respect of some or all of the employees of the board assigned to The school.
