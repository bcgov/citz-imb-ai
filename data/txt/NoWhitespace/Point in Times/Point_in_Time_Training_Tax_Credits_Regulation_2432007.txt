"complete level 1" , in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 1 Requirements established by the industry training authority and as Amended from time to time for the program; "complete level 2" , in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 2 Requirements established by the industry training authority and as Amended from time to time for the program; "complete level 3" , in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 3 Requirements established by the industry training authority and as Amended from time to time for the program; "complete level 4" , in relation to a program, means the completion of the level 4 Requirements established by the industry training authority and as Amended from time to time for the program; "work-based hours" , in relation to a number of work-based hours completed, means Work-based hours shown as completed on a transcript from the Industry training authority.

Other prescribed requirements — basic tax credit for individuals 5 For the purposes of section 118(1)(d) of the act, the prescribed Requirement is that, while the individual was registered in an Eligible recognized program, the individual was not (a) enrolled under section 3 of the School act , (b) registered under section 13 of the School act , or (c) attending an independent school, as defined in the Independent school act , or an educational institution operated by the government of Canada or by a band, as defined in the Indian act (canada).
