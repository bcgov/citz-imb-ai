B.c. reg. 324/96 O.c. 1327/96 Deposited november 18, 1996 Assessment act Adjustment factors relating to the valuation of railway Corporation property regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 402/98] Interpretation
1 In this regulation:
"general tax base" Means the taxable assessed values on which property taxes under The
Municipal act Are levied; "hospital tax base" Means the taxable assessed values on which property taxes under The
Hospital district act Are levied; "school tax base" Means the taxable assessed values on which property taxes under The
School act Are levied.
Application of the regulation 2 This regulation does not apply to railway corporation property Situated north of 59 degrees north latitude.
1997 tax year 3 In the 1997 taxation year the following adjustment factors apply:
(a) For taxes levied on the school tax base, the adjustment factor for Each of the categories of railway corporation property listed in This paragraph is set out to the right of the name for the Category:
Category of property Adjustment factor Right of way 0.56 Track in place 0.56 (b) Subject to paragraph (c), for taxes levied on the general and the Hospital tax bases, the adjustment factor for each of the Categories of railway corporation property listed in this Paragraph is set out beside the name for the category:
Category of property Adjustment factor Right of way in incorporated areas 0.55 Right of way in unincorporated areas 0.85 Track in place in incorporated areas 0.55 Track in place in unincorporated areas 0.85 Bridges
0.75 (c) For taxes levied on the general and hospital tax base in each of The jurisdictions listed in schedule 1, the adjustment Factors for track in place, right of way and bridges respectively Are set out to the right of the name of the jurisdiction.
