Interpretation
1
In this regulation:
"general tax base"
means the taxable assessed values on which property taxes under
the
Municipal Act
are levied;
"hospital tax base"
means the taxable assessed values on which property taxes under
the
Hospital District Act
are levied;
"school tax base"
means the taxable assessed values on which property taxes under
the
School Act
are levied.
Application of the regulation
2
This regulation does not apply to railway corporation property
situated north of 59 degrees north latitude.
