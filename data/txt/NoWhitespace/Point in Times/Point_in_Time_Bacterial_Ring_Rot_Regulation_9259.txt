Section 3 before amended by bc reg 112/2014, effective june 18, 2014.
Potatoes must be inspected 3 No person shall sell or have in his possession for sale within the Province any imported potatoes unless such potatoes have been Inspected and released by an inspector as being apparently free From bacterial ring rot.
