Deposited october 19, 1990 Effective november 1, 1990 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Additional tax regulation (town of smithers) Applicability
1 Section 3 (1) of the Hotel room tax act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 207, applies in respect of accommodation Purchased within the town of smithers.
Additional tax payable 2 The additional tax payable under that section is 2% of the Purchase price of the accommodation.
Purpose for tax expenditure 3 The purposes, for which the amount paid to the municipality out of The revenue collected from the tax may be expended, are tourism Promotion, projects and programs.
