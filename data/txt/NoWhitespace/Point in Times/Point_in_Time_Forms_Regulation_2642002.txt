Deposited september 27, 2002 Effective september 30, 2002 Employment and assistance act and employment and assistance for Persons with disabilities act Forms regulation Forms prescribed 1 Effective september 30, 2002, (a) the attached forms are prescribed for the purposes of the Employment and assistance act And the Employment and assistance for persons with disabilities act :
(i)  enquiry; (ii)  application; (iii)  monthly report; (iv)  review, and (b) the attached application for income assistance for child In the home of a relative is prescribed for the purposes of the Employment and assistance act .
