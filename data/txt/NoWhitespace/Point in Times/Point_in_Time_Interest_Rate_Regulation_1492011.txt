Deposited august 9, 2011 Land tax deferment act Interest rate regulation Interest rate 1 (1)  for the purposes of sections 8 (2) and 9 (2) of the Land tax deferment act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning october 1, 2011 is 1.00% a year.
(2)  for the purposes of sections 8 (3.1) and 9 (5) of the Land tax deferment act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning october 1, 2011 is 3.00% a year.
Note:
This regulation replaces b.c. reg. 30/2011.
