B.c. reg. 133/97 O.c. 425/97 Deposited april 9, 1997 Wildlife act Tofino mudflats wildlife management area regulation [includes amendments up to b.c. reg. 109/2002] Area designated 1 The lands under the administration of the minister of environment, As outlined in heavy black on the attached map, are designated the Tofino mudflats wildlife management area, the whole containing 1 645 hectares, more or less.
[am. b.c. reg. 109/2002, s. 1.] Note:
The map attached is exempt from publication under section 6 of the Regulations act. it may be inspected at the offices of the wildlife Branch, ministry of environment.
