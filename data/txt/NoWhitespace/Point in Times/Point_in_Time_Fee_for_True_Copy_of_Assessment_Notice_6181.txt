B.c. reg. 61/81 O.c. 391/81 Filed february 23, 1981 Assessment act Fee for true copy of assessment notice Fee for copy of notice 1 The fee, referred to in section 6 (5) of the Assessment act , for a true copy of an assessment notice shall be $6 per parcel Of land referred to in the assessment notice.
