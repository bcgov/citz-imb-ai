The account consists of 1/2 of the amounts that are paid into the
consolidated revenue fund under section 8 of the

Expenditures
3
On the written authorization of the Minister of Health, money may
be paid out of the account for the administration, operation and
delivery of health care, health research, health promotion and
health education services.
