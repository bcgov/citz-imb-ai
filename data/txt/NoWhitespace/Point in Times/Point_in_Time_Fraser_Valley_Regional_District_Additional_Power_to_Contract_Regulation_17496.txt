"point in time" regulation content Local government act Fraser valley regional district additional power to contract Regulation
B.c. reg. 174/96 Section 1 before amended by bc reg 118/2018, effective june 15, 2018.
Additional power to contract 1 The fraser valley regional district is granted, to the extent Permitted under section 799 of the Local government act , the power to enter into agreements for the acceptance, treatment And disposal of sewage to preserve trans-boundary ground water Quality within the watershed of the sumas river.
