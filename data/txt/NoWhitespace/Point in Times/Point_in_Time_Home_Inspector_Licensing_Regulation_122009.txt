Section 1 definition of "home inspection" before repealed by bc reg 70/2016, effective september 1, 2016.
"home inspection" Means an opinion as to the condition of a residential dwelling or Property based on a visual examination of readily accessible Features and components of the dwelling or property; Section 3 before re-enacted by bc reg 70/2016, effective september 1, 2016.
Qualifications for licence 3 (1) An applicant for a licence as a home inspector must meet the Qualifications set by an association or authority that, in the Opinion of the director, is appropriate to assess the Qualifications of home inspectors.
(2) The director may require a home inspector to take additional Training in a program related to home inspection.

Reporting changes to director 7 A licensee must submit the following information to the director Within 14 days of the occurrence of:
(a) A change in the name of the licensee; (b) A change in the location from which the licensee conducts Business.
