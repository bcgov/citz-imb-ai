Exemptions
3.1 A small system is exempt from section 6 of the act if (a) the system does not provide water for human consumption Or food preparation purposes, and is not connected to a water Supply system that provides water for human consumption and food Preparation purposes, or (b) each recipient of the water from the system has a point Of entry or point of use treatment system that makes the water Potable.
