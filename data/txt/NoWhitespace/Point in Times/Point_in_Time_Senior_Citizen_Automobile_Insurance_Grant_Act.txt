Section 1 (1) before amended by 2003-94-80, effective june 1, 2007 (bc reg 166/2006).
(1)  the lieutenant governor in council may, by regulation, Establish a program to provide financial assistance by way of Government grant for the purpose of assisting persons aged 65 or Older to pay basic premiums for universal compulsory automobile Insurance under the Insurance (motor vehicle) act For pleasure use of automobiles.
