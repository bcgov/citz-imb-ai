"point in time" regulation content Business practices and consumer protection act Government cheque cashing fees regulation B.c. reg. 127/2018 Section 1 before re-enacted by bc reg 169/2019, effective september 15, 2019.
Definition
1 In this regulation, "government assistance cheque" Means a government cheque that is issued (a) To a recipient under the Employment and assistance act To provide income assistance, hardship assistance or a supplement Under that act, or (b) To a recipient under the Employment and assistance for persons with disabilities act To provide disability assistance, hardship assistance or a Supplement under that act.
