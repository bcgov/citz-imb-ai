B.c. reg. 75/93 O.c. 731/90 Deposited march 16, 1993 Effective may 10, 1990 1 Local government act Property class percentages regulation Prescribed percentages 1 For the purposes of the definition of "converted value of land and Improvements" in section 5 of the Local government act The following percentages are prescribed:
(a) 10% in respect of property class 1; (b) 35% in respect of property class 2; (c) 40% in respect of property class 3; (d) 34% in respect of property class 4; (e) 34% in respect of property class 5; (f) 24.5% in respect of property class 6; (g) 30% in respect of property class 7; (h) 10% in respect of property class 8; (i) 10% in respect of property class 9.
1.
See b.c. reg. 93/93 in the b.c. gazette part ii, march 30, 1993, p. 147 [provisions of the Local government act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 323, relevant to the enactment of this Regulation: section 6.6 (3) (b.1)]
