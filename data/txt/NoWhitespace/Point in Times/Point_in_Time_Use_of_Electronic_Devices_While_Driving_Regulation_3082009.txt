Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Motor vehicle act .
Section 1 definitions "commercial motor vehicle", "driving time", "electronic logging device" and "record of duty status" were added By bc reg 39/2023, effective february 13, 2023.
Section 1 definition of "electronic logging device" or "eld" before Amended by bc reg 39/2023, effective august 1, 2023.
"electronic logging device" Or
"eld" Means a device or technology that (a) Automatically records a driver's driving time, (b) Facilitates the recording of the driver's record of duty status, and (c) Is certified by an accredited certification body under Section 79.1 of the commercial vehicle drivers hours of service Regulations (canada); Section 1 definitions of "driving time" and "record of duty status" Before repealed by bc reg 39/2023, effective august 1, 2023.
"driving time" Has the same meaning as in section 37.01 of the motor vehicle Act regulations, b.c. reg. 26/58; "record of duty status" Means a record in which a driver records the information required Under section 77 or 82 of the commercial vehicle drivers hours Of service regulations (canada).

Persons engaged in prescribed activity — commercial motor vehicle With eld 5.1 For the purposes of section 214.5 (b) of the act, a Person who is driving or operating a commercial motor vehicle, if The commercial motor vehicle is equipped with an electronic Logging device that is mounted in a fixed position during the Operation of the commercial motor vehicle and is visible to the Driver when the driver is in the normal driving position, is Engaged in a prescribed activity and the person is exempt from the Prohibition in section 214.2 of the act in respect of the use Of the electronic logging device.
