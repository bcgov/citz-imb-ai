B.c. reg. 373/96 O.c. 1436/96 Deposited december 13, 1996 Effective january 1, 1997 Human rights code Human rights code regulation Delegation by the commissioner of investigation and mediation 1 The following classes of persons are designated for the purposes Of section 46 (3) (b) 1 Of the Human rights code :
(a) human rights officers; (b) those persons to whom human rights officers report for The purposes of investigations and mediations under the Human rights code .
Annual report 2 The annual report under section 18 1 Of the Human rights code Must include a statement for the reporting period of the median Times taken (a) from filing a complaint until a human rights officer is Assigned under section 23 (2) of the Human rights code , (b) from assignment of a human rights officer under section 23 (2) of the Human rights code Until the human rights officer submits a report under section 23 (3), (c) from submission of the investigation report until the Complaint is disposed of under section 26 of the Human rights code , and (d) in cases where it is determined a complaint can be Disposed of without an investigation, from filing a complaint Until the complaint is disposed of under section 26 of the Human rights code .
(e) to (g) repealed. [b.c. reg. 148/98.] [am. b.c. reg. 148/98.] 1.
Sections 46 (3) and 18 of the Human rights code , as referred to in sections 1 and 2 of this regulation, were Repealed, effective march 31, 2003, by s.b.c. 2002, chapter 62, sections 24 and 5 respectively.
Note:
This regulation repeals b.c. regs. 151/75 and 239/92.
