Section 1 definition of "qualifying practitioner" before amended by Bc reg 265/2009, effective january 31, 2010.
"qualifying practitioner" Means a person who is (a) either (i)  a psychologist, or (ii)  a psychological associate Registered with the college of psychologists of british columbia and Licensed to practice in british columbia, or (b) a psychologist certified by the british columbia Association of school psychologists.
