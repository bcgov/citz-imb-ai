Section 1 definition "livestock dealer" BEFORE amended by 2009-34-4,
effective November 26, 2009 (Royal Assent).
"livestock dealer"
means a person who, directly or as an agent, buys or sells livestock
or possesses livestock for sale but does not include a person who
resides in British Columbia and who transacts business that arises
directly from the person's ranching or farming operation;
Section 1 definition of "game" BEFORE amended by 2014-16-110,
effective January 19, 2015 (BC Reg 7/2015).
"game"
means game as defined in the
Game Farm Act
that is being raised for agricultural purposes under a licence
issued under that Act;
