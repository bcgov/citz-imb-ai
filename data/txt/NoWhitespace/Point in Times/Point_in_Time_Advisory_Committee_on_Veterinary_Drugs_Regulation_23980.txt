Veterinary Drugs Act
Advisory Committee on Veterinary Drugs Regulation
Establishment of committee
1
A committee called the Advisory Committee on Veterinary Drugs is
established which shall consist of 5 members as follows:
(a)
one member nominated by the College of Pharmacists of British
Columbia, who shall be the chairman of the committee;
(b)
one member nominated by the British Columbia Veterinary Medical
Association;
(c)
2 members from the Ministry of Agriculture and Lands, one of whom
shall be the secretary of the committee;
(d)
one member from the Ministry of Health.
Duties
2
(1)
The chairman may appoint sub-committees comprising members or
non-members of the committee, and he shall designate the
responsibilities and duties of each sub-committee.
(2)
The secretary shall keep proper minutes and record the proceedings
of each meeting of the committee.
(3)
The committee shall
(a)
make recommendations to the Minister of Agriculture and Lands
(i)
on the sufficiency of present legislation dealing with veterinary
drugs, and
(ii)
as to the enactment of new legislation or changes in existing
legislation, and
(b)
carry out such investigations as may be requested by the Minister
of Agriculture and Lands.

Veterinary Drugs Act
