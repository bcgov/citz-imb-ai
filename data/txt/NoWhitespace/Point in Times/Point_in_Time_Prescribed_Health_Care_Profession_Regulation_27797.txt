B.c. reg. 277/97 O.c. 961/97 Deposited august 1, 1997 Pharmacists, pharmacy operations and drug scheduling act Prescribed health care profession regulation Midwifery
1 Midwifery is a prescribed health care profession for the purposes Of the definition of "practitioner" in section 1 of the Pharmacists, pharmacy operations and drug scheduling act .
Pharmacy
2 Pharmacy is a prescribed health care profession for the purposes Of the definition of "practitioner" in section 1 of the Pharmacists, pharmacy operations and drug scheduling act Only for the purposes of prescribing the following drugs for use For emergency contraception:
(a) ethinyl estradiol; (b) repealed. [b.c. reg. 384/2007.] (c) norgestrel; (d) progestin.
[en. b.c. reg. 354/2000; am. b.c. reg. 384/2007.] Nursing as a nurse practitioner 3 Nursing as a nurse practitioner is a prescribed health care Profession for the purposes of the definition of "practitioner" in Section 1 of the Pharmacists, pharmacy operations and drug scheduling act .
