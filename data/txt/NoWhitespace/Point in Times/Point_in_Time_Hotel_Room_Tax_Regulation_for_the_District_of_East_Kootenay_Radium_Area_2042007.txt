B.c. reg. 204/2007 O.c. 460/2007 Deposited june 21, 2007 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Hotel room tax regulation for the district of east kootenay (radium area) Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hotel room tax act .
Applicability of tax 2 July 31, 2007 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of the Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the village Of radium hot springs and in the portions of the east kootenay Regional district electoral area g shown in schedules a and b, Attached to the regional district of east kootenay – additional Hotel room tax bylaw no. 1973, 2007 (radium area).
Prescribed rate of tax payable 3 The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the act is 2% of the Purchase price of accommodation purchased in the village of radium Hot springs and in the portions of the east kootenay regional District electoral area g shown in schedules a and b, attached to The regional district of east kootenay – additional hotel room tax Bylaw no. 1973, 2007 (radium area).
[am. b.c. reg. 158/2012, s. 2.] Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax 4 The east kootenay regional district may spend the amount paid to It out of the revenue collected from the tax for tourism Marketing, programs and projects.
[am. b.c. reg. 158/2012, s. 2.] Repeal
5 This regulation is repealed effective august 1, 2017.
