Deposited may 17, 2002 Motor vehicle act Inspection standards (safety and repair) regulation Prescribed standards 1 The standards of safety and repair set out in the schedule are Prescribed as the standards applicable to those classes of Vehicles which require to be inspected under division 25 of b.c.
Reg. 26/58, the motor vehicle act regulations.
