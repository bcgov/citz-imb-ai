B.c. reg. 196/2005 O.c. 415/2005 Deposited march 31, 2005 Wildlife act Habitat conservation trust fund regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Wildlife act ; "board" And
"board member" Have the same meanings as in part 2 of the act.
Notice
2 Notice of the date, time and location of any meeting of the board Must be given to each board member at least 5 days before the Meeting.
Remuneration
3 The amount of remuneration prescribed for the purposes of section 116 (1) (e) of the act is, (a) for the chair of the board, $250 for each day that he or She is engaged in meetings of the board for the purpose of Exercising the board's powers and performing its duties under part 2 of the act, and (b) for any other board member, $200 for each day that he or She is engaged in meetings of the board for the purpose of Exercising the board's powers and performing its duties under part 2 of the act.
[provisions of the Wildlife act , r.s.b.c. 1996, c. 488, relevant to the enactment of this Regulation: sections 108, 114 (3) and 116 (1) (e)]
