B.c. reg. 141/2005 O.c. 247/2005 Deposited march 18, 2005 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Hotel room tax regulation for the city of kamloops Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hotel room tax act .
Applicability of tax 2 June 30, 2005 is the prescribed date after which section 3 of the Act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the city of Kamloops.
Prescribed rate of tax payable 3 The tax payable under section 3 (1) of the act is 2% of the Purchase price of accommodation purchased in the city of kamloops.
[am. b.c. reg. 116/2011, s. 12.] Prescribed purpose for expenditure of tax 4 The city of kamloops may spend the amount paid to it out of the Revenue collected from the tax for tourism marketing.
[am. b.c. reg. 116/2011, s. 12.] Repeal
5 This regulation is repealed effective july 1, 2017.
