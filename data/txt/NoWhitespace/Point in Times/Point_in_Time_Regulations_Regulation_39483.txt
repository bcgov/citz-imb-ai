Reference to consolidated regulations of british columbia 7 A reference in an enacting regulation to a previously enacted Regulation that at the time of the enactment of the enacting Regulation is published in the looseleaf consolidated regulations Of british columbia shall, unless a contrary intention appears in The enacting regulation, be construed to be a reference to the Previously enacted regulation as published in that consolidation.
[en. b.c. reg. 45/85, s. 3.] Section 8 definition of "enacting authority", paragraphs (a) and (b) Before amended by bc reg 76/2022, effective march 30, 2022.
(a) The person who has the authority under an enactment to enact the Regulation to be revised, or a person designated by him, or (b) In the case of a regulation that can be enacted by the lieutenant Governor in council, the minister responsible for administration of The enactment that is authority for enacting the regulation to be Revised, or a person designated by him, Section 11 before amended by bc reg 76/2022, effective march 30, 2022.
Submission to registrar 11 If the enacting authority approves the revised regulation he shall Endorse it as approved and deposit it with the registrar, and the Act, except sections 2 and 3 (2), applies to the revised Regulation the same as if the regulation had been originally Enacted.
