Section 21 (1) before repealed by bc reg 11/2021, effective february 5, 2021.
(1) In this section:
"agrologist" Has the same meaning as in the Agrologists act ; "applied science technologist or technician" Means a person who holds a certificate of registration under the Applied science technologists and technicians act ; "professional biologist" Has the same meaning as in the College of applied biology act ; "professional engineer" Has the same meaning as in the Engineers and geoscientists act ; "professional forester" Has the same meaning as in the Foresters act ; "professional geoscientist" Has the same meaning as in the Engineers and geoscientists act ; "registered biology technologist" Has the same meaning as in the College of applied biology act ; "registered forest technologist" Has the same meaning as in the Foresters act .
Section 21 (2) (a) and (b) before amended by bc reg 11/2021, Effective february 5, 2021.
(a) The individual is one of the following professionals:
(i) An agrologist; (ii) An applied technologist or technician; (iii) A professional biologist; (iv) A professional engineer; (v) A professional forester; (vi) A professional geoscientist; (vi.1) A registered biology technologist; (vii) A registered forest technologist, (b) The individual is registered and in good standing in british Columbia with the appropriate professional association constituted Under an act for the individual's profession, and Section 21 (2) (c) (iii) before amended by bc reg 11/2021, effective February 5, 2021.
(iii) Under the code of ethics of the appropriate professional association And is subject to disciplinary action by that professional Association.
