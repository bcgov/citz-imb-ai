B.c. reg. 421/95 Chief electoral officer Deposited october 11, 1995 Election act Voter registration regulation Definitions
1 In this regulation:
"act" Means the Election act .
Notes for assistance in cross-reference 2 In this regulation, if a reference to a provision of the act is Followed by italicized words in square brackets indicating the Subject matter of the provision, the words in square brackets are Provided for convenience of reference only and are not to be Considered an interpretation of the provision.
Form of voter registration application 3 An application referred to in section 35 [application for registration] Of the act must be in the form attached as schedule 1 to this Regulation and may require or request information as indicated on That form.
