Section 3 (5) before amended by 2010-6-95,sch 5, effective july 1, 2010.
(5)  despite subsection (1), a sale or transfer of the logs or Timber on which a lien is claimed under this act during the time Limited for the filing of the statement of claim, and previous to The filing of it, or after the filing of it and during the time Limited for the enforcement of it, does not in any way affect the Lien, which remains in force against the logs or timber in whose Possession they are found, except sawn timber sold in the ordinary Course of business.
Section 4 (4) before amended by 2010-6-89, effective july 1, 2010.
(4)  there must be attached to or endorsed on the writ of Summons in the action a copy of the statement of lien filed under Section 3, and no other statement of claim or particulars is Necessary unless ordered by the court.
Section 5 (2) and (3) before amended by 2010-6-94,sch 4, effective July 1, 2010.
(2)  the writ of summons in the action must be in the form, as Nearly as may be, in use in the court in which it is issued, and the Practice must follow, as nearly as may be, the practice of the Court.
(3)  a writ of summons may be served anywhere in british Columbia in the same manner as in other cases.
Section 7 (1) before amended by 2010-6-94,sch 4, effective july 1, 2010.
(1)  whether commenced by writ of summons or attachment, the Court may direct that any proceeding must be disposed of summarily Without waiting for the regular sittings of the court, on the terms As to notice and otherwise that the order provides.
Section 8 (1) and (2) before amended by 2010-6-90, effective july 1, 2010.
(1)  if an attachment issues in the first instance, the Statement of claim and defence or dispute note, and proceedings to Judgment, may be the same as provided in sections 3 to 7.
(2)  if an action has been commenced by writ of summons, and if An attachment issues after proceedings have been begun by writ of Summons, the proceedings must continue and be carried to judgment Under the writ of summons, except those that must be taken under the Attachment.
Section 11 (1) before amended by 2010-6-94,sch 4, effective july 1, 2010.
(1)  the writ of attachment must also, if no writ of summons Has issued, summon the defendant to appear before the court out of Which the attachment has issued.
