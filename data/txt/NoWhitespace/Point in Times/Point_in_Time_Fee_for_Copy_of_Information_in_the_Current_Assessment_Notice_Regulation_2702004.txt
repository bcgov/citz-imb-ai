B.c. reg. 270/2004 Regulation before repealed by bc reg 52/2016, effective march 2, 2016.
B.c. reg. 270/2004 O.c. 633/2004 Deposited june 18, 2004 Assessment act Fee for copy of information In the current assessment notice regulation Fee for copy of notice 1 The fee, referred to in section 6 (5) of the Assessment act , for a copy of the information contained in the current Assessment notice is $6 per parcel of land referred to in the Assessment notice.
Note:
This regulation replaces b.c. reg. 61/81.
