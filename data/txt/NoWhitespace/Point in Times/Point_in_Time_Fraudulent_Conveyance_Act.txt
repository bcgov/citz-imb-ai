Section 1 before amended by 2012-6-5, effective march 29, 2012 (royal assent).
Fraudulent conveyance to avoid debt or duty of others 1 If made to delay, hinder or defraud creditors and others of their Just and lawful remedies (a) a disposition of property, by writing or otherwise, (b) a bond, (c) a proceeding, or (d) an order Is void and of no effect against a person or the person's assignee Or personal representative whose rights and obligations by Collusion, guile, malice or fraud are or might be disturbed, Hindered, delayed or defrauded, despite a pretence or other matter To the contrary.
