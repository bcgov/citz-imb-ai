B.c. reg. 59/2009 M64/2009
Deposited march 4, 2009 Land tax deferment act Interest rate regulation Interest rate 1 (1)  for the purposes of sections 8 (2) and 9 (2) of the Land tax deferment act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning april 1, 2009 Is 1.5% a year.
(2)  for the purposes of sections 8 (3.1) and 9 (5) of the Land tax deferment act , the interest rate for the 6-month period beginning april 1, 2009 Is 3.5% a year.
Note:
This regulation replaces b.c. reg. 232/2008.
