B.c. reg. 255/2008 O.c. 690/2008 Deposited september 19, 2008 Hotel room tax act; provincial sales tax act Additional tax regulation for the city of langford Definition
1 In this regulation, "act" Means the Hotel room tax act .
Applicability of additional tax 2 October 31, 2008, is the prescribed date after which section 3 of The act applies in respect of accommodation purchased in the city Of langford.
Prescribed rate of additional tax payable 3 The additional tax payable under section 3 (1) of the act is 2% of The purchase price of accommodation purchased in the city of Langford.
Prescribed purpose for expenditure of additional tax 4 The city of langford may spend the amount paid to it out of the Revenue collected from the additional tax for tourism marketing, Programs and projects.
Repeal
5 This regulation is repealed effective november 1, 2013.
