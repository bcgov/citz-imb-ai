Section 7 before repealed by 2010-2-91, effective march 31, 2010 (royal assent).
Amendment of 2004 tax rates 7 Despite the Community charter And the Vancouver charter , in relation to a regulation under section 2 that applies to the 2004 taxation year, a municipality may, at any time in that Taxation year, amend its municipal property value tax rates for That taxation year in order to comply with that section.
