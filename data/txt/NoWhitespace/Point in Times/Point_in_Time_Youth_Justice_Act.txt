Section 30 BEFORE amended by 2023-10-1195, effective March 30, 2023

Care of youth admitted to custody centre
30
A young person who is admitted to a youth custody centre is
subject to the care, control and custody of the government, and
all rights and powers of care, control and custody of a parent or
guardian over the young person cease until that young person is
released on completion of his or her term of custody under the
court order.
