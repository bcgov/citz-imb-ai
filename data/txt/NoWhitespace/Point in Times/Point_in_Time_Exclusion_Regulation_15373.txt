Authority for other classes of insurance 2 In addition to any class of automobile insurance and reinsurance, All other classes of insurance and reinsurance defined in the Insurance act Are designated and approved as classes of insurance and Reinsurance in which the insurance corporation of british columbia Is authorized to engage in and carry on.
