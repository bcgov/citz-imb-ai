Manufactured Home Tax Act
Travel Manufactured Home
Exemption Regulation
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 254/97]
Interpretation
1
In this regulation
"travel trailer"
means a vehicle that is
(a)
capable of being towed on its own permanent wheels and undercarriage by a motor vehicle,
(b)
licensed, or able to be licensed, as a trailer under the
Motor Vehicle Act
for use on highway, and
(c)
not used as a principal residence.
[en. B.C. Reg. 254/97.]
Exemptions
2
The
Manufactured Home Tax Act
does not apply to campers, motor homes or travel trailers.
[Provisions of the
Manufactured Home Tax Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 281, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 4 (d)]
