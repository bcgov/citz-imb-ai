Local Government Act
Fraser Valley Regional District Additional Power to Contract Regulation
B.C. Reg. 174/96
Section 1 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 118/2018, effective June 15, 2018.
Additional power to contract
1
The Fraser Valley Regional District is granted, to the extent permitted under section 799 of the
Local Government Act
, the power to enter into agreements for the acceptance, treatment and disposal of sewage to preserve trans-boundary ground water quality within the watershed of the Sumas River.
