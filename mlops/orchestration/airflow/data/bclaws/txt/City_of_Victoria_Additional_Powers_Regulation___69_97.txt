Applicant may appeal
4
If an applicant for a development permit within an intensive residential development area is refused a development permit by the planning director or delegate responsible for issuing development permits or the applicant objects to a proposed provision of the development permit, the applicant may appeal the decision to council.
[am. B.C. Reg. 214/2009, s. (b).]
