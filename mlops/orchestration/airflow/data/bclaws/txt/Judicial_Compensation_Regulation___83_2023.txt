Judicial Compensation Act
Judicial Compensation Regulation
[Last amended March 4, 2024 by B.C. Reg. 39/2024]
Definition
1
In this regulation,
"Act"
means the
Judicial Compensation Act
.
Setting higher amounts for purposes of the Act
2
For the purposes of section 7.1 (2) of the Act, the following amounts are set:
(a)
for paragraph (a) of that subsection, $40 000;
(b)
for paragraph (b) of that subsection, 85% of the costs over $40 000 but under $150 000.
2022 commission
3
(1)
This section applies in respect of the costs of participation in the 2022 commission established under section 2 of the Act.
(2)
Despite section 2, the amount for the purposes of section 7.1 (2) of the Act is 100% of costs to a maximum of $116 000.
(3)
This section is repealed on December 31, 2024.
