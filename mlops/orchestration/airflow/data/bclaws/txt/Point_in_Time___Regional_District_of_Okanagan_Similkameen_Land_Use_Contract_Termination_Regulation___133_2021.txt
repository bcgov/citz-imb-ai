Land Use Contract Termination Regulation
Definitions
1
In this regulation:
"Act"
means the
Local Government Act
;
"land use contract"
means Land Use Contract No. LU-6-D, authorized on February 22, 1973 by Regional District Amendment Bylaw No. 169, 1973 and entered into on March 9, 1973;
"Regional District"
means the Regional District of Okanagan-Similkameen.
Exception — section 547 of Act
2
As an exception to section 547 (2) of the Act, the deadline for the Regional District to adopt a zoning bylaw that will, on June 30, 2024, apply to the land that is subject to the land use contract is extended to June 30, 2024.
Exception — section 548 of Act
3
(1)
As an exception to section 548 (3) (a) of the Act, the deadline for the Regional District to adopt a bylaw under section 548 (2) of the Act to terminate the land use contract is extended to June 30, 2024.
(2)
Section 548 (3) (b) (i) of the Act does not apply to a bylaw described in subsection (1).
Repeal
4
This regulation is repealed on June 30, 2024.
