Emergency Program Act
COVID-19 (Limitation Periods in Court Proceedings) Regulation
Limitation periods in court proceedings
1
(1)
Subject to subsection (2), every mandatory limitation period and any other mandatory time period that is established in an enactment or law of British Columbia within which a civil or family action, proceeding, claim or appeal must be commenced in any of the following courts is suspended:
(a)
the Court of Appeal;
(b)
the Supreme Court;
(c)
the Provincial Court.
(2)
Subsection (1) does not apply to a mandatory limitation period and any other mandatory time period established under the following enactments:
(a)
the
Builders Lien Act
;
(b)
Division 5
[Builders Liens and Other Charges]
of Part 5
[Property]
of the
Strata Property Act
.
