Local Government Act
Cariboo Regional District Regulation
[includes amendments up to B.C. Reg. 27/93]
Grant-in-aid: South Cariboo Home Support Service Association
1
The Cariboo Regional District is granted the additional power to provide a contribution (grant-in-aid) to the South Cariboo Home Support Service Association as an additional extended service.
Grant-in-aid: Cariboo Tourist Association
2
The Cariboo Regional District is granted the additional power to provide a contribution (grant-in-aid) to the Cariboo Tourist Association as an additional extended service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 141/90.]
Paratransit
3
The Cariboo Regional District is granted the additional power to provide Paratransit as a local service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 22/92.]
Economic Development
4
The Cariboo Regional District is granted the additional power to provide Economic Development as an extended service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 55/92.]
Economic Development Grants-in-aid
5
The Cariboo Regional District is granted the additional power to provide Economic Development Grants-in-aid as an extended service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 72/92.]
Natural Gas Capital Financing
6
The Cariboo Regional District is granted the additional power to authorize the acquisition of Natural Gas Capital Financing as a local service.
[en. B.C. Reg. 27/93.]
