Mineral Tenure Act
Mineral Definition Modification Regulation
Fossil exclusion
1
For the purpose of paragraph (g) of the definition of "mineral" in section 1 of the
Mineral Tenure Act
, a fossil, defined as follows, is not a mineral:
"fossil"
does not include limestone, dolomite, coal, petroleum or natural gas.

Mineral Tenure Act
