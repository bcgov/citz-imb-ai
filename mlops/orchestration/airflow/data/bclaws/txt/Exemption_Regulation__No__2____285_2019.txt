Exemption Regulation (No. 2)

Exemption from limitation on extension of certificate
1
Section 31 (4) (a) of the
Environmental Assessment Act
, as it limits the number of times that a deadline specified in an environmental assessment certificate may be extended, does not apply in relation to the reviewable project described in Environmental Assessment Certificate # M09-02 if the deadline is not extended beyond January 14, 2022.
