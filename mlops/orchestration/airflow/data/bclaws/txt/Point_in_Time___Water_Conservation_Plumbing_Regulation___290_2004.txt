Identification
3
(1)
Watersaver water closets must be marked with
(a) WS,
(b) 13.25 LPF,
(c) WS/13.25 LPF, or
(d) x LPF which indicates the actual confirmed test value (in litres per flush) and where x is equal to or less than 13.25.
(2)
Low Consumption water closets must be marked with
(a) LC,
(b) 6 LPF,
(c) LC/6 LPF, or
(d) x LPF which indicates the actual confirmed test value (in litres per flush) and where x is equal to or less than 6.
Flush tanks
4
The water supply to urinal flush tanks equipped for automatic flushing must be controlled with a timing device in order to limit operation to the time period during which the building is normally occupied.
Note:
this regulation replaces B.C. Reg. 294/98.
