Section 5 BEFORE repealed by 2000-23-42, effective March 28, 2002.
Annual report
5
(1)
The minister must make and submit to the Lieutenant Governor in Council an annual report of the work performed by the ministry.
(2)
The report under subsection (1) must be laid before the Legislative Assembly during the session next following the end of the year for which the report is made.
