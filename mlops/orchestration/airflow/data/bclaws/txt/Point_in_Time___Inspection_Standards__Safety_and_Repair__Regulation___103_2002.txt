Motor Vehicle Act
Inspection Standards (Safety and Repair) Regulation
Prescribed standards
1
The Standards of Safety and Repair set out in the Schedule are prescribed as the standards applicable to those classes of vehicles which require to be inspected under Division 25 of B.C. Reg. 26/58,   the Motor Vehicle Act Regulations.
