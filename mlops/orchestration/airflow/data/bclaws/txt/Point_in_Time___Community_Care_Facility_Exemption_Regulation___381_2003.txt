Motor Carrier Act
Community Care Facility Exemption Regulation
Exemption of motor vehicles
1
A motor vehicle is exempt from the application of the
Motor Carrier Act
if
(a) the
Motor Carrier Act
would otherwise apply to it because it is used by a community care facility as defined in section 1 of the
Community Care Facility Act
, and
(b) the motor vehicle is used in no other way to which the
Motor Carrier Act
applies except by the community care facility.
Exemption of persons
2
A person is exempt from the application of the
Motor Carrier Act
if the
Motor Carrier Act
would otherwise apply to the person solely because of a motor vehicle described in section 1, assuming section 1 did not exempt the motor vehicle from the
Motor Carrier Act
.
Repeal
3
This regulation is repealed on the day the
Community Care and Assisted Living Act
, S.B.C. 2002, c. 75, comes into force.

Motor Carrier Act
, R.S.B.C. 1996, c. 315, relevant to the enactment of this regulation: section 2 (2)]
