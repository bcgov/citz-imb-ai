Section 2 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 82/2012, effective April 18, 2012.
Reserve established
2
A mineral reserve for all minerals is established over each parcel or tract of land identified by a site number and name set out in column 1 of the Table, within the mining division and land district set out in column 2 of the Table opposite the site number and name.
Section 3 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 82/2012, effective April 18, 2012.
Conditional registration
3
A free miner may register a mineral claim in a reserve established by section 2 subject to the conditions listed in section 4.
Section 4 BEFORE amended by BC Reg 82/2012, effective April 18, 2012.
Conditions
4
A free miner must not obstruct, endanger or interfere with, or allow any other person to obstruct, endanger or interfere with, the construction, operation or maintenance of a transmission line, pipeline or other work, structure or activity that is in a reserve established under section 2 and set out in column 3 of the Table opposite the site number and name of the reserve.
