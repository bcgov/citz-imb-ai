Notice
3
(1)
The regulator may send the following by mail or email:
(a)
an acknowledgment under section 61.2 (a) of the Act;
(b)
a report under section 61.3 (1) of the Act;
(c)
a statement under section 61.4 (2) of the Act.
(2)
The number prescribed for the purposes of section 99 (1) (j.2) of the Act is one.
(3)
If the regulator receives more than one application in respect of the same alleged contravention, the regulator's obligation under the following sections may be met as indicated:
(a)
under section 61.3 (1) of the Act, by publishing the reports on a publicly accessible website maintained by or on behalf of the regulator;
(b)
under section 61.4 (2) of the Act, by publishing the statement on a publicly accessible website maintained by of on behalf of the regulator.
